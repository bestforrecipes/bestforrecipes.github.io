##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Scampi:

1.  Take 3 tbsp Butter And Olive Oil
2.  Make ready 4 clove Minced Garlic
3.  Make ready 1 lb Large Shrimp peeled and deveined
4.  Make ready 1/3 cup Parsley chopped
5.  Get 1/2 each Lemon zest (no white part)
6.  Take 1/4 cup Lemon juiced
7.  Prepare 1/2 each Lemon sliced thinly rounds
8.  Make ready 1/4 tsp Red pepper flakes
9.  Get Salt and pepper
10.  Make ready 1 lb Linguini pasta
11.  Get 1/4 cup Salt for water

##### Steps to make Shrimp Scampi:

1.  Start pasta water over heat. Salt the water.
2.  Sauté pan add the butter and olive oil to medium to low heat. Sauté garlic for just a minute. Toss in shrimp to just pink. Salt and pepper. Add pepper Flakes. This only takes 2 minutes. Immediately take of the heat. Toss in parsley and lemon jest, juice and slices. Taste it.
