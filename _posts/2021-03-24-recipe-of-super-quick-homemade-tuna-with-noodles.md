##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna with noodles:

1.  Take 2 cans tuna drained, 3 if you really want to taste the tuna
2.  Take 1 package noodles of your choice, i used gluten free elbow
3.  Take 2 cups frozen peas
4.  Get 1 medium carrot shredded
5.  Make ready 1/2 cup diced onion, i used sweet
6.  Take 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
7.  Get 1 1/2 cups mayonnaise, you can use less or more
8.  Make ready 1 tsp white pepper
9.  Get 1 tsp celery seed
10.  Take 2 tablespoons chopped garlic
11.  Get 1/2 tsp paprika
12.  Prepare 1 tbsp lime juice (or lemon)
13.  Get Approximately 1 cup chopped fresh spinach
14.  Get 1 cup shredded cheese, i used colby jack
15.  Get 1 tbsp yellow mustard
