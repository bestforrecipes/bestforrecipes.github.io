##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp & white clam sauce linguini:

1.  Get 1 lb medium shrimp, deveined and tails removed
2.  Take 2 can white clam sauce
3.  Prepare 1 lb linguini noodles
4.  Take 1/4 cup butter
5.  Take 1 tsp olive oil
6.  Make ready 1 tsp fresh ground pepper
7.  Prepare 1 tsp salt
8.  Make ready 3 clove minced garlic
9.  Take 1 sweet yellow pepper diced
10.  Take 1 sweet red pepper diced

##### Instructions to make Shrimp & white clam sauce linguini:

1.  Boil pasta in water till tender. (I always put some oil, salt, and fresh herbs in with my pasta when I boil it.) Drain and set aside when done.
2.  While pasta is boiling, wash shrimp. Melt butter over medium heat. Add shrimp. Let cook till a little pink and starting to curl. Then add garlic. Cook 5-7 min., add oil. Continue to stir till shrimp is pink and curled.
3.  Add pasta to skillet along with all your seasonings. Add both cans of white clam sauce, also add minced garlic and both yellow and red peppers. Keep tossing all ingredients around in skillet till all is incorporated and hot.
