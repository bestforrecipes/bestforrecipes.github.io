##### The ingredients needed to make Bacon Double Cheeseburger Mac n Cheese:

1.  Prepare 1 box Shell Macaroni and cheese
2.  Take 1 packages Extra cheese sauce
3.  Prepare 1 lb Ground Beef
4.  Make ready 1 tbsp Dried minced onion
5.  Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
6.  Get 1 tsp prepared mustard
7.  Get 1/2 packages Bacon Bits

##### Instructions to make Bacon Double Cheeseburger Mac n Cheese:

1.  Cook gound beef in skillet. While cooking add minced onion, garlic, and mustard. Cook until browned.
2.  Drain beef mixture.
3.  While draining, cook macaroni according to package. Drain.
4.  Once drained add in both packets of cheese, bacon bits and beef mixture. Stir to combine.
5.  Serve hot.
