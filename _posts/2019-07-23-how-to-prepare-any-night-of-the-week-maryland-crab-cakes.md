##### The ingredients needed to make Maryland Crab Cakes:

1.  Prepare Sleeves of Ritz Crackers, crushed
2.  Prepare Spicy brown mustard
3.  Get Parsley, or to taste
4.  Take Lemon juice
5.  Make ready Raw egg
6.  Prepare Mayonnaise
7.  Take Worcestershire sauce
8.  Prepare Seafood seasoning (Phillips or Old Bay)
9.  Get Jumbo lump crabmeat

##### Steps to make Maryland Crab Cakes:

1.  Combine all ingredients, except crabmeat, until pasty.
2.  Slowly fold in crabmeat without breaking it up. Form into patties and fry in a combination of olive oil and butter until golden, about 2-3 minutes each side.
