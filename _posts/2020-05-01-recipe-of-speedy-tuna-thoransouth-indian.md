##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Thoran(South Indian):

1.  Take 2 tins Tuna -
2.  Make ready 1 Onion , sliced long
3.  Prepare 1 Tomato (peeled and sliced long)
4.  Make ready 1 sprig Corriander leaves -
5.  Get 1 tbsp Lemon -
6.  Take Masalas
7.  Get 1/2 tsp Turmeric -
8.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Red Chilli Powder or Cayene Powder -
9.  Get 1/4 tsp Garam Masala -
10.  Get 1/2 tsp Salt - or to taste
11.  Make ready 1 tbsp Oil -
12.  Get 1/4 cup Water -

##### Instructions to make Tuna Thoran(South Indian):

1.  Heat the oil in a pan and saute the onions till brown
2.  Add the tomatoes and saute for 5mins
3.  Add the masalas and salt and saute for 1 min.
4.  Add the tuna, mix well to make all the big lumps smaller as well.
5.  Add water, bring to boil. Return to low flame. Cover the pan with a plate so it cooks for another 15mins.
6.  Remove the cover, the water must have dried by now. Keep sauting the tuna mixture till pretty dry and slightly crispy.
7.  Garnish with corriander leaves. That adds a real taste to the tuna thoran. Serve hot with rice or any bread item.
