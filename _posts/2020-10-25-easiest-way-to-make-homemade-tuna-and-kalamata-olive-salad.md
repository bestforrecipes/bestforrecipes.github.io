##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna and kalamata olive salad:

1.  Make ready salad
2.  Make ready 5 oz canned tuna in spring water
3.  Prepare 1/3 cup Kalamata olives sliced
4.  Get 1/3 cup grape tomatoes sliced
5.  Prepare 1/2 tsp dill weed
6.  Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp blue cheese crumbled
7.  Get 2 tbsp cilantro
8.  Prepare 2 1/2 tbsp mayonnaise
9.  Get topping and garnish
10.  Take 1 tsp red crushed pepper flakes
11.  Take 1/4 tsp blue cheese, crumbled
12.  Prepare 1/4 tsp cilantro
13.  Prepare 4 medium pepperoncini, Greek peppers
14.  Get 1 1/2 tbsp sliced Kalamata olives
