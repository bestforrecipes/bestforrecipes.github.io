##### The ingredients needed to make Egg Roll wrapped salmon salad:

1.  Prepare 5 ounces pink salmon
2.  Make ready 1 teaspoon rosemary infused oil, see my recipe or add a little rosemary to taste
3.  Prepare 1 teaspoon dill weed
4.  Make ready 1-1/2 tablespoons mayonaise
5.  Take 2 large egg roll wrappers
6.  Make ready as needed oil to fry with

##### Instructions to make Egg Roll wrapped salmon salad:

1.  Heat oil
2.  Add the ingredients for the salad and mix
3.  Turn the egg roll wrappers to point straight up and down
4.  Add the salmon salad to center of wrapper
5.  Fold sides in
6.  Fold bottom up and roll it upwards
7.  Add flap side down in hot oil fry till crispy
8.  Move to paper towel to absorb excessive oils
9.  Serve I hope you enjoy!
