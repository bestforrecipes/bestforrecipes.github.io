##### The ingredients needed to make Lettuce Salad with Crab Stick and Egg Stir-Fry:

1.  Take 2 long sticks Imitation crab sticks
2.  Get 1 Egg
3.  Take 1/2 a head Lettuce
4.  Prepare 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
5.  Get 1 Japanese mustard
6.  Make ready 1 dash Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Lettuce Salad with Crab Stick and Egg Stir-Fry:

1.  Rip up the lettuce with your hands, and soak in water to crisp up. Drain and dry well. Cut the crab sticks into 4 cm and shred. Scramble the egg finely.
2.  Put the mayonnaise and Japanese mustard in a bowl and mix. Add the ingredients from Step 1 to the bowl and mix well. Taste, and add salt if needed.
3.  Transfer to serving plates and it's done. It's also delicious with pepper as an accent.
