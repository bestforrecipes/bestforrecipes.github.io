##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon, Leek, and Green Onion Fried Rice:

1.  Make ready Cooked salmon (salmon flakes are OK)
2.  Get Japanese leek
3.  Make ready to 5 stalks Green onion or scallion
4.  Prepare uncooked Plain cooked rice
5.  Prepare Eggs
6.  Get Dashi stock granules
7.  Take Vegetable oil

##### Steps to make Salmon, Leek, and Green Onion Fried Rice:

1.  Finely break up the salmon. Chop up the leek and green onion.
2.  Add the eggs to the rice. Microwave the rice first if it's cold.
3.  Mix the rice and egg together well with a ride paddle.
4.  Heat oil in a pan, and quickly stir fry the egg-rice over high heat.
5.  When the rice grains have separated, add the salmon and leek and continue stir frying. Season with dashi stock granules. Taste, and adjust the seasoning.
6.  Transfer to serving plates, scatter with some chopped green onion, and it's done.
