##### The ingredients needed to make Cabbage and Potato Stir Fried With Bonito Flakes:

1.  Take 2 Potatoes (small)
2.  Make ready 2 to 3 leaves Cabbage
3.  Take 1 small packet Bonito flakes
4.  Get 3 tsp ☆ Soy sauce
5.  Get 1 tsp ☆ Mirin
6.  Take 1 tsp ☆ Sake
7.  Prepare 1 tbsp Olive oil (or vegetable oil)

##### Steps to make Cabbage and Potato Stir Fried With Bonito Flakes:

1.  Julienne the potato about 5 mm thick. Heat a frying pan and add the olive oil. Add the potato and stir-fry over medium heat.
2.  Add the cabbage which should be cut up into bite-sized pieces and continue stir-frying.
3.  Once the cabbage has wilted, add the ☆ flavoring ingredients and bonito flakes. Stir-fry quickly and it's done!
