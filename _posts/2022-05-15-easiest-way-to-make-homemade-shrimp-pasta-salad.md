##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp pasta salad:

1.  Get 3 cup Medium pasta shells
2.  Get 8 oz Cooked, shelled shrimp
3.  Prepare 1 cup Chopped celery
4.  Prepare 1 small onion, chopped
5.  Make ready 2 tsp Chopped fresh dill weed
6.  Get 1/4 cup Plain yogurt
7.  Take 1 tbsp Dijon-style mustard
8.  Prepare 1 tbsp Lemon juice
9.  Get 2 tsp Honey
