##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Meat imitation salad:

1.  Make ready 8 oz. imitation crab meat
2.  Prepare 4 large eggs
3.  Take 1 can corn
4.  Prepare to taste Mayo
5.  Get 1/5 tsp Salt

##### Steps to make Crab Meat imitation salad:

1.  Boil 4 large eggs for 15 min
2.  Dip eggs in a water with ice for 2 min to make the shells easier to peel off.
3.  Defrost imitation crab meat, if you bought it frozen
4.  Chop it as in the picture.
5.  Add corn without liquid
6.  Add chopped eggs, 1/5 teaspoon of salt
7.  Add mayo. Mix. Enjoy!
