##### The ingredients needed to make Squid Indonesian fried rice:

1.  Make ready 2 cups cold rice
2.  Make ready 1 cup squid cut into 2 cm finger size
3.  Prepare 1 handful finely chopped coriander
4.  Get 1 stalk spring onions chopped
5.  Make ready 2 eggs whisked
6.  Make ready 10 fresh chili finely chopped (you can skip)
7.  Get 4 finely chopped garlic
8.  Get 6 finely chopped shallots
9.  Take 1/4 whole onion finely chopped
10.  Make ready 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce (I love sweet, you can decrease the amount)
11.  Make ready 1/2 tsp ground white pepper
12.  Prepare 1 tsp chicken powder
13.  Get to taste Salt
14.  Take 1 tbs vegetable oil

2.
