##### The ingredients needed to make Crunchy Tuna Sandwich:

1.  Take 2 can White albacore tuna
2.  Make ready 1/2 cup Onion, finely chopped
3.  Take 1/2 cup Cucumber, finely chopped
4.  Make ready 1 cup Celery, finely chopped
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp Sweet pickle relish
6.  Take 1 tbsp Lemon juice
7.  Get 1/2 tsp Dried parsley
8.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Dried dill
9.  Prepare 1/4 tsp Garlic salt
10.  Get 1/4 tsp Ground black pepper
11.  Prepare 1/2 cup Miracle whip or mayo
12.  Prepare 12 slice Bread
13.  Make ready 2 oz Potato chips

##### Steps to make Crunchy Tuna Sandwich:

1.  Open and drain the cans of tuna.
2.  Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl and mix with a fork taking care to break apart the tuna chunks.
3.  Spread over your favorite toasted bread. I like something with a bit of crunch like 5- seed or oat nut bread.
4.  Add a layer of chips.
5.  Place another slice of bread on top, press down with your hand and cut in half.
6.  Serve with more chips or fruit. Enjoy!
