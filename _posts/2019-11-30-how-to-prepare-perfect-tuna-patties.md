##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Patties:

1.  Take 200 grams of canned tuna
2.  Get 2 piece spring onions, chopped
3.  Get 2 garlic cloves, minced
4.  Take 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
5.  Get 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
6.  Make ready 2 tbsp cornmeal
7.  Make ready 6 tbsp ap flour
8.  Prepare 1 egg
9.  Get 1 tbsp mayonnaise
10.  Prepare 1 salt and pepper to taste, i use 1 teaspoon for each
11.  Get frying oil, i used corn oil

##### Steps to make Tuna Patties:

1.  Before using the tuna, leave it for 5 mins in a strainer. Then in a bowl mix the tuna with the onion, garlic, lemon juice, parsley, salt and pepper.
2.  Next add the cornmeal, flour, egg and mayo and mix well.
3.  Heat the oil and drop spoonfuls of the tuna mix. Let the patties cook until golden brown. Enjoy!
