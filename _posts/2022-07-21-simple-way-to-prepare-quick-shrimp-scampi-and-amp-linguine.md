##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp scampi & linguine:

1.  Prepare 1 lb linguine pasta, uncooked
2.  Take 1 lb shrimp, shelled, deveined
3.  Make ready 5 tbsp olive oil
4.  Make ready 3 tbsp unsalted butter
5.  Make ready 3 clove garlic, minced
6.  Make ready 1 large shallot, chopped
7.  Take 1 cup dry white wine
8.  Get 1 tsp lemon zest
9.  Make ready 1 tbsp lemon juice
10.  Get 2 tbsp capers, rinsed
11.  Take 1 small tomato, diced
12.  Take 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
13.  Get 2 tbsp parsley, chopped
14.  Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
15.  Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper

##### Instructions to make Shrimp scampi & linguine:

1.  For pasta, cook until al dente in boiling salted water with two tablespoons of the oil. Then drain and season with a dash of salt and pepper and an additional tablespoon of oil.
2.  In hot pan, add remaining oil and shrimp. Cook a minute or two per side or until just about done.
3.  Add butter, garlic and shallots. Cook another minute or two.
4.  Add wine, zest, juice and cheese. Reduce by half.
5.  Add the capers and tomatoes and parsley and season with salt and pepper.
6.  Add pasta to pan and mix well.
7.  Serve!
