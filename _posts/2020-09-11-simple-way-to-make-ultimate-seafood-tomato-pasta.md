##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood Tomato Pasta:

1.  Make ready 3/4 box penne pasta
2.  Prepare 1 can tuna in oil
3.  Get 1 can crab meat
4.  Get 1 can diced tomatoes
5.  Get 3 tbsp Goya sofrito
6.  Get 2 tbsp minced garlic
7.  Make ready 3 tbsp Chopped green onion
8.  Take red pepper flakes, Italian seasoning, salt, black pepper
9.  Make ready 1 cheese

##### Steps to make Seafood Tomato Pasta:

1.  Bring pot of waterr, oil and salt to a boil
2.  Add pasta to water
3.  In pan, cook tuna, crab meat, garlic, green onion and spices on high for 2 minutes
4.  Add diced tomatoes to pan and cook on medium for 5 minutes
5.  Add tomato-seafood mixture to cooked pasta
6.  Add cheese \*recommended : shredded mozzerella and parmesan
7.  Serve hot
