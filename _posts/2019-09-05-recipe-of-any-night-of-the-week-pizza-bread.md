##### The ingredients needed to make Pizza Bread:

1.  Take 1/4 Of the bread dough from my Basic White bread recipe (2 lb loaf)
2.  Prepare 1 1/2 cup Home made marinara sauce
3.  Get 2 cup Mozzarella
4.  Make ready 1 Optional ingredients I've used - anchovies, black olives, fried onion, fresh garlic, & I've even used Italian Tuna packed in olive oil
5.  Get 2 tsp Red pepper flakes
6.  Get 3/4 cup Grated Parmesan cheese

##### Steps to make Pizza Bread:

1.  Take the dough & press into a pre-oiled 8X11' pan
2.  Pre-heat oven to 400°F
3.  Sprinkle top of loaf with EVOO. Bake until light golden brown
4.  Remove from oven & let sit for a couple of minutes until the top cools slightly - (this is just a precaution I take for myself as I have a tendency to burn myself
5.  Layer your toppings over bread
6.  Pop in oven & bake
7.  Remove when cheese is melted
8.  Slice & serve with salad & a nice wine
