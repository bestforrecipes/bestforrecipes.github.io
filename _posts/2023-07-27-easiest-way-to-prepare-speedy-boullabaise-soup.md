##### The ingredients needed to make Boullabaise Soup:

1.  Get 6 Servings
2.  Make ready 1 piece Shrimp Cube
3.  Get 2 piece Medium size crabs-cut into four
4.  Prepare 1/4 kg Shrimp
5.  Get 1/4 kg Tahong
6.  Get 1/4 kg Halaan
7.  Make ready 1 can evaporated milk
8.  Prepare 2 tbsp garlic -minced
9.  Prepare 1 can Cream of Mushroom
10.  Get 4 cup water
11.  Prepare 2 tbsp white wine
12.  Make ready 2 tbsp minced Ginger
13.  Make ready 3 tbsp minced Onions
14.  Make ready 1 salt
15.  Get 1 pepper

##### Steps to make Boullabaise Soup:

1.  Sautee Garlic , onion and ginger. No oil. Cover until tender.
2.  Add water and Campbell Cream of mushroom. Dissolve and bring to boil.
3.  Add all the remaining ingredients except the milk. Bring to boil. Let boil for 5 mins.
4.  Turn off the stove, then pour the evaporated milk.
