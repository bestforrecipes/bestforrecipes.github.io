##### The ingredients needed to make Crispy Crab Rangoon's:

1.  Take 8 oz Softened Cream Cheese
2.  Make ready 1 clove Minced Garlic
3.  Prepare 2 Green Onions diced
4.  Get 1/4 tsp worcestershire sauce
5.  Prepare 1/2 cup Shredded Crab Meat
6.  Prepare 1 packages Wonton Wrappers
7.  Take 1 Egg
8.  Prepare 2 tsp water
9.  Prepare 1 Oil for deep frying

##### Instructions to make Crispy Crab Rangoon's:

1.  Mix softened cream cheese, garlic and Worcestershire sauce until smooth.
2.  Stir in crab and green onions.
3.  Beat the egg and water together in small bowl.
4.  Place a Tablespoon of the crab mixture in center of wonton wrapper.
5.  Brush edges of wrapper with egg wash mixture. Fold over corners to form a triangle and press to seal.
6.  Deep fry in small batches in 375°F oil until golden brown.
7.  These fry up really quick. I always sprinkle with a pinch of salt as soon as they come out of fryer.
8.  Serve warm with your favorite dipping sauces. Enjoy!!
