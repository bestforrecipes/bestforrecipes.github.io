##### The ingredients needed to make Potato Breaded Salmon:

1.  Take 1 each Salmon
2.  Prepare Malbec asparagus
3.  Make ready 1/4 cup Asparagus
4.  Make ready 1 Salt
5.  Make ready 2 tbsp Malbec (red wine)
6.  Take 1 each Radish (sliced)
7.  Get 1 tsp Onions powder
8.  Prepare potato bread
9.  Take 1 cup Instant mix mash potato
10.  Prepare 2 tbsp Dry parsley
11.  Make ready 1 tsp Orange zest
12.  Make ready sautée zucchini
13.  Make ready 1 each Sliced zucchini
14.  Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Chopped small garlic

##### Steps to make Potato Breaded Salmon:

1.  For the breaded: put mix,parsley and orange in a bowl. Take the salmon and breaded from the belly and put it on a pan for 7 min a side
2.  For the asparagus: sautée asparagus and radishes in a pan NOTE(cook first asparagus in a boiling water aprox 30-60 seconds) then Malbec and others ingredients. Aprox 7 minutes
3.  Put olive oil in a pan let be hot. Put zucchini and toss then put the rest of the ingredients
