##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Salmon with Sesame Mayonnaise Sauce:

1.  Prepare 2 filets, about 220 grams Salmon fillets with skins
2.  Prepare 1 dash Butter
3.  Make ready Sauce
4.  Take 1 tbsp White sesame seeds
5.  Prepare 1 tsp Mirin
6.  Make ready 1 tbsp Sugar
7.  Take 1 tbsp Miso
8.  Get 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
9.  Prepare To garnish
10.  Make ready 1 dash Fresh parsley

##### Steps to make Grilled Salmon with Sesame Mayonnaise Sauce:

1.  Mix the sugar, miso, mayonnaise, mirin, and white sesame seeds in a bowl. This amount is enough for 2 fillets.
2.  Spread parchment paper or foil on a baking tray, and lay the salmon with the sauce. Put the butter on both sides.
3.  Grill in an toaster oven or fish grill.
4.  Serve on a plate sprinkled with parsley to garnish.
