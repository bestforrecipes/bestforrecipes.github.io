##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Tangy Salmon:

1.  Make ready Sauce
2.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp honey
3.  Prepare 2 tbsp brown sugar
4.  Prepare 2 tbsp butter
5.  Make ready 1/4 cup dijon mustard
6.  Make ready 1 tbsp soy sauce
7.  Get 1 tbsp grated ginger
8.  Take 1 Salmon
9.  Make ready 1 salt and pepper (dash of each)

##### Steps to make Sweet & Tangy Salmon:

1.  For sauce: Melt butter, brown sugar, honey, soy sauce and grated ginger.
2.  Once melted, whisk in dijon mustard. Cook 2 min then set aside. (Makes 6 servings)
3.  Heat 1 tsp olive oil in pan.
4.  Lightly salt and pepper salmon and place in pan. Cook 3 min
5.  Flip salmon (should be lightly golden) and top with some sauce. Cook 3 min.
6.  Flip salmon again and top with some sauce on this side. Cook 2-3 more min or until salmon cooked through
7.  YUMMY!! Enjoy!
