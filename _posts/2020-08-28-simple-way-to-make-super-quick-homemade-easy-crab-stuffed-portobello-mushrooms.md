##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Crab Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms:

1.  Make ready 8-12 portobello or button mushrooms
2.  Prepare Filling:
3.  Get 6 oz cream cheese softened
4.  Get 1 cup stuffing mix + 1/2 cup hot water
5.  Make ready 1/2 cup imitation crab meat
6.  Make ready 1/4 cup finely chopped green onions
7.  Take 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
8.  Make ready 2 teaspoons parsley
9.  Take 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
10.  Prepare 1/2 teaspoon worstichire sauce
11.  Take to taste Pepper

##### Steps to make Easy Crab Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms:

1.  Preheat oven to 375. Wash mushrooms, remove and discard stems.
2.  Mix water and stuffing mix and set aside for 5 minutes to soften.
3.  Mix all filling ingredients and stir in stuffing mix. Lay mushrooms cap side down on a foil lined baking sheet. Scoop about a tablespoon of filling into Each one. Bake uncovered 18-20 minutes. You can place under broiler after baking for 30 seconds to one minute to brown tops if desired and garnish with green onions, parmesan and pepper.
