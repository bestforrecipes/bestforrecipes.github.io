##### The ingredients needed to make lobster Cantonese:

1.  Take 2 1/2 lb lobster meat
2.  Prepare 2 cup chopped celery
3.  Make ready 8 oz water cheastnuts
4.  Make ready 1 large onion chopped
5.  Get 1 stick butter
6.  Prepare 1 quart pasta water
7.  Get 1/4 cup water
8.  Take 1/4 cup cornstarch
9.  Get 1 inch ginger sliced thinly
10.  Get 1 tsp salt
11.  Prepare 1 tsp white pepper
12.  Make ready 1 1/2 tsp granulated garlic powder

##### Instructions to make lobster Cantonese:

1.  chop onions
2.  chop celery
3.  melt butter
4.  add lobster and juice to butter add garlic let cook not totally done remove let cool
5.  chop ginger
6.  chop water cheastnuts
7.  add spices add vegetables to liquid stir fry for 3 minutes
8.  add pasta water simmer add lobster
9.  mix cornstarch and water add to lobster and vegetables
10.  stir let thicken serve over noodles
