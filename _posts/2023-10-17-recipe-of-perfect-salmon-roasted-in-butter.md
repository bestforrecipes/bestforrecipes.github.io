##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Roasted in Butter:

1.  Make ready butter
2.  Make ready minced chervil, parsley or dill
3.  Get salmon fillet, 1 1/2 to 2 pounds
4.  Get Salt and freshly ground black pepper
5.  Make ready Lemon wedges

##### Instructions to make Salmon Roasted in Butter:

1.  Preheat the oven to 475 degrees.
2.  Place the butter and half the herb in a roasting pan just large enough to fit the salmon and place it in the oven. Heat about 5 minutes, until the butter melts and the herb begins to sizzle.
3.  Add the salmon to the pan, skin side up. Roast 4 minutes. Remove from the oven, then peel the skin off (optional) - (If the skin does not lift right off, cook 2 minutes longer.) Sprinkle with salt and pepper and turn the fillet over. Sprinkle with salt and pepper again.
4.  Roast 3 to 5 minutes more, depending on the thickness of the fillet and the degree of doneness you prefer. Cut into serving portions, spoon a little of the butter over each and garnish with the remaining herb. Serve with lemon wedges.
