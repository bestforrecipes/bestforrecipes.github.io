##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn Sambal:

1.  Make ready Prawn (skinned & cleaned) 500grm
2.  Make ready Potato 2 - diced & fried
3.  Take Garlic Ginger paste
4.  Make ready Onion 2 - sliced
5.  Make ready Tomato 1 - sliced
6.  Prepare Dry chilli - blended
7.  Prepare 3 tbspn Oil
8.  Get to taste Salt
9.  Take to taste Sugar

##### Steps to make Prawn Sambal:

1.  Add oil in a wok/pan. Add onion. Fry until it turn glassy. Add garlic ginger paste. Cook until oil breaks. Add dry chilli paste. Cook until oil breaks.
2.  Add prawns. Mix well. Cook for 2 minutes. Add salt, sugar tomato and fried potato. Stir and mix well. Cook until the gravy thickens (5 to 15min)
3.  This dish can be served with rice.
