##### The ingredients needed to make Okonomiyaki:

1.  Prepare About 2/3 cup (100g) of flour
2.  Prepare 1 (8 g) packet of Hondashi
3.  Get 1 medium or large egg
4.  Make ready 120 ml luke warm water
5.  Prepare 1/4 head (200 g) cabbage
6.  Make ready 100 g pork (or other desired ingredients)
7.  Prepare 2 Tbsp Tenkasu tempura (optional)
8.  Get to taste Okonomiyaki sauce
9.  Make ready to taste Mayonnaise
10.  Get to taste Katsuoboshi (thin dried flakes of tuna)
11.  Take Cooking oil for frying
12.  Take to taste Aonori (seaweed flakes)

##### Steps to make Okonomiyaki:

1.  Mix hondashi into water, then combine with flour and egg in a bowl. Mix until smooth.
2.  Cut cabbage into thin bite sized pieces. Cut pork into easy to eat pieces.\* Add the cabbage, pork\*, and tempura balls into the mixture from step one and mix so everything is coated in batter.
3.  Add oil to fry pan and pour the mixture in, making a circular shaped patty. Cook on low to medium heat until the bottom is well cooked (dark brown but not burnt), then flip it carefully and continue cooking, reducing the heat.
4.  Cook until cooked through (use a toothpick to test that the batter in the centre is cooked)
5.  Serve on a plate and top with okonomiyaki sauce, mayonnaise, katsuoboshi, and aonori to taste.
6.  Other common fillings are octopus, shrimp, potato + cheese + mochi, or plain. Also commonly added is pickled ginger.
