4.  Get 1 tbsp hot sauce
5.  Make ready 1/2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
6.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
7.  Prepare 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
8.  Take 1/8 cup minced onions
9.  Take 1/2 tsp Adobo seasoning
10.  Take 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
