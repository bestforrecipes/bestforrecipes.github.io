##### The ingredients needed to make Chilli Squid rings:

1.  Prepare 250 gm Squid
2.  Prepare 2 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
3.  Prepare 1 tsp Turmeric powder
4.  Take as needed Salt
5.  Make ready 1/2 cup Tamarind water
6.  Make ready 1 cup Chopped shallots
7.  Take 3 Green chillies chopped
8.  Get 1 tsp Curry leaves
9.  Make ready 1 tbsp Ginger garlic crushed
10.  Get 1 Tomato chopped

##### Steps to make Chilli Squid rings:

1.  Wash the squid clean and cut it into round slices.
2.  Marinate it with turmeric powder, chilli powder and salt 10 min.then add all the remaining ingredients.
3.  Mix it well. Cook it in a earthern pot on medium heat.add hot water if necessary for cooking purpose.when it is cooked and gravy becomes thick.add 1 tbsp coconut oil.stir it and remove from heat.
4.  Serve hot.
