##### The ingredients needed to make Mediterranian Monkfish:

1.  Take 500 Grams Monkfish
2.  Get 500 Grams Cherry Tomatoes
3.  Take 1 White Onion
4.  Get 100 Grams Basil
5.  Get 1/2 Red Pepper
6.  Take 1/2 Green Pepper
7.  Take 1 Large Lemon
8.  Take 100 ml Olive Oil
9.  Get 1 Small Ciabatta
