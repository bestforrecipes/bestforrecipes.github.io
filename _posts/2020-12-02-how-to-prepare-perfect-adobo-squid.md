##### The ingredients needed to make Adobo Squid:

1.  Take 1/2 kg Squid (sliced, innards removed)
2.  Get 1 pc Onion (sliced)
3.  Take 2 cloves Garlic (chopped)
4.  Make ready 1 thumb Ginger (stalk slice)
5.  Take 5 tbsp Soy Sauce (Toyo)
6.  Take 1 tbsp Vinegar
7.  Get 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
8.  Make ready Post-cook:
9.  Get Spring Onion (Med Stalks)
10.  Take Chilli

##### Steps to make Adobo Squid:

1.  Apply cooking oil, just enough to cover cooking pan
2.  Saute garlic, then Onion, then Ginger
3.  Include Squid, then mix
4.  Just as water rise, apply Soy Sauce, then mix
5.  Apply vinegar, then Brown Sugar. Mix
6.  Cover cooking pan. Wait till water evaps and cooking becomes saucy (about 10 mins)
7.  Apply Spring Onion & Chilli. Mix.
