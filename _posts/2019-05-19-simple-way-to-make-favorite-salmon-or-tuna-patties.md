##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon or Tuna Patties:

1.  Make ready Salmon or Tuna
2.  Prepare Chopped Onions
3.  Get Seasoned Bread Crumbs
4.  Prepare Egg
5.  Make ready Olive Oil
6.  Get Salt and Pepper to your liking

##### Instructions to make Salmon or Tuna Patties:

1.  Shred the salmon or tuna (canned would work fine just be sure to drain first) then incorporate remaining ingredients in a medium bowl
2.  Hand create the patties to your liking.
3.  Lightly coat pan with olive oil and place over medium-low heat.
4.  Cook both sides of the party until browned.
