5.  Prepare 1/2 cup stock(shrimp stock)
6.  Prepare 5 grams unsalted butter
7.  Prepare 1 juice from half of lemon(big)
8.  Get 1 dash salt nd black pepper to taste
9.  Take 1 chopped parsley
