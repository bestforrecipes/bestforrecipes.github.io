##### The ingredients needed to make Sausage & Cheese Stuffed Shells:

1.  Get 1 box 12oz. Jumbo Pasta Shells
2.  Get 1 lb Ground Italian Hot Sausage
3.  Make ready 1 16 oz. Container Ricotta Cheese
4.  Get 3 cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese divided
5.  Prepare 1 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese divided
6.  Take 1 Salt & Pepper to taste
7.  Get 1 Garlic Powder to taste
8.  Make ready 1 Onion Powder to taste
9.  Prepare 3 cup Pasta Sauce
10.  Make ready 1 tbsp Parsley Flakes
11.  Prepare 2 tsp Olive Oil
12.  Take 1 Italian Seasoning for garnish

##### Instructions to make Sausage & Cheese Stuffed Shells:

1.  Pre heat oven to 350
2.  In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil. Enough to cover shells.
3.  Cook shells for 9 minutes.
4.  Remove shells, drain water, and place on a wax paper covered cookie sheet to cool.
5.  In a skillet, brown sausage meat, seasoned with salt &pepper, garlic & onion powder, and set aside to cool.
6.  In a large bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, 1 and 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, and 1/2 cup parmesan cheese.
7.  Mix until well blended, add parsley and sausage.
8.  Mix together until well blended.
9.  Lightly grease a baking pan with olive oil. Add thin layer of pasta sauce.
10.  Take shells and stuff each one with sausage and cheese mixture.
11.  Place shells in pan, cover with pasta sauce, remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
12.  Garnish with Italian Seasoning and bake for 30 minutes.
13.  Enjoy!
