##### The ingredients needed to make Korean-style Negitoro:

1.  Make ready 200 grams Sashimi quality tuna (tuna offcuts) or toro for negitoro
2.  Get 2 pieces worth A… Grated ginger
3.  Prepare 2 tbsp Soy sauce
4.  Prepare 1 tbsp Mirin
5.  Prepare 1 tsp Sesame oil, rice vinegar, gochujang
6.  Take 1 stalk Green onions (sliced very thinly)
7.  Take 4 leaves worth Shredded shiso leaves
8.  Prepare 1 tsp Ground sesame seeds

##### Steps to make Korean-style Negitoro:

1.  Mix the A ingredients together in a bowl.
2.  Mince the tuna (use the offcuts near the spine, or fatty tuna offcuts for negitoro).
3.  Combine the seasoning ingredients from Step 1 and the chopped tuna. Top with shiso leaves and sesame seeds.
