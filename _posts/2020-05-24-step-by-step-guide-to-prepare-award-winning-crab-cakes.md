##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Cakes:

1.  Take 8 oz Crab Meat
2.  Make ready 2 each Green Onions, minced
3.  Get 1/2 cup Crackers, crushed
4.  Get 1 each Egg, lightly beaten
5.  Get 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
6.  Make ready 1 tsp Dijon Mustard
7.  Prepare 1/2 tsp Old Bay Seasoning
8.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Granulated Garlic
9.  Make ready 1 Salt & Pepper to taste
10.  Get 1 Oil, as needed

##### Instructions to make Crab Cakes:

1.  Combine egg, mayonnaise, mustard, and spices and mix thoroughly
2.  Place crab meat, minced green onions, and crackers in bowl with liquid mixture.
3.  Mix together just until the liquid is absorbed.
4.  Divide mixture into 4 portions, and form into patties approximately 1/2 inch thick.
5.  Put enough oil in a skillet to come 1/4 to 1/3 of the way up the crab cakes, place over medium heat.
6.  When oil is hot, place patties into skillet and cook approximately 5 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and heated completely through.
7.  Remove patties from skillet, and allow to drain on a rack or paper towels to remove excess oil.
