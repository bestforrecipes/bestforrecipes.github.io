##### The ingredients needed to make Lemon garlic shrimp:

1.  Get 2 bags raw shrimp shells on
2.  Get 2 stick salted butter
3.  Take 1 chopped basil and parsley
4.  Take 4 tbsp lemon juice
5.  Make ready 4 tsp sweet red pepper paste. ( pimento paste)
6.  Get 4 tsp chopped garlic
7.  Take 1 salt and pepper
8.  Make ready 1 french loaf
9.  Take 1 tbsp chili ginger paste

##### Instructions to make Lemon garlic shrimp:

1.  Make compound butter. Add basil., parsley, lemon juice, garlic, pepper paste and salt and pepper
2.  Put shrimp in casserole dish. Salt and pepper shrimp. Drop butter on top . place in oven till shrimp is cooked. Stirring occasionally.
3.  When done let sit for 5 main. Use French loaf for dipping in sauce
