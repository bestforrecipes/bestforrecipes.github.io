##### The ingredients needed to make Papaya Stir-Fry:

1.  Prepare 1/2 Papaya
2.  Make ready 1/2 Carrot
3.  Prepare 1 can Canned tuna
4.  Make ready 100 ml ◎Dashi stock
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp ◎Soy sauce
6.  Get 1 tbsp ◎Sake
7.  Make ready 1 tbsp ※Sesame oil (for stir frying)

##### Steps to make Papaya Stir-Fry:

1.  Cut the papaya in half and peel the skin. Julienne the papaya with a slicer or something similar and soak in water to remove the acerbic taste.
2.  Cut the carrot into the same sized thin strips.
3.  Heat the sesame oil in a frying pan. Add the papaya and carrot and stir-fry so that they are coated in the oil. Once they become tender, add the tuna.
4.  Add the ◎ ingredients and simmer until the liquid evaporates.
5.  Once the liquid has evaporated, it's done.
