##### The ingredients needed to make Pickled Plum and Shirasu Cheese Toast - Tart and Delicious:

1.  Make ready 1 slice Sliced bread
2.  Get 1 Butter
3.  Make ready 1 Pickled plum (remove the pit and chopped and smashed)
4.  Make ready 10 grams Shirasu (boiled and dried baby sardines)
5.  Take 20 grams Pizza cheese (or melting slice cheese)
6.  Prepare 1 small amount Aonori

##### Steps to make Pickled Plum and Shirasu Cheese Toast - Tart and Delicious:

1.  Spread the butter and pickled plum on a bread slice and sprinkle with shirasu.
2.  Sprinkle the pizza cheese (or melting slice cheese) and toast in the oven for 2-3 minutes.
3.  Sprinkle with aonori, and it's done.
