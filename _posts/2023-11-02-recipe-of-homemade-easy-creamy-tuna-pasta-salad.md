##### The ingredients needed to make EASY Creamy Tuna Pasta Salad:

1.  Take 500 grams orecchiette
2.  Get 425 grams canned tuna, drained
3.  Prepare 3 celery sticks, diced
4.  Make ready 3 spring onion sprigs,diced
5.  Prepare 1/2 cup mayonnaise
6.  Make ready 1 teaspoon lemon juice for extra zest (optional)
7.  Prepare 1 pinch salt and pepper

##### Steps to make EASY Creamy Tuna Pasta Salad:

1.  Boil water in a medium size sauce pan, add orecchiette and cook until soft.
2.  Drain the pasta whilst running cold water through it. Set aside in a medium to large bowl.
3.  Finely chop spring onion and celery then add to the pasta.
4.  Combine the mayonnaise to the ingredients.
5.  Season with salt and pepper and add a splash of lemon juice if you want a more zesty taste.
6.  Mix well and enjoy! Feel free to refrigerate for 20 minutes if you want it chilled.
