##### The ingredients needed to make 🎄Christmas Healthy Menu❄️:

1.  Get 2 tomatoes
2.  Prepare 1 vegetable
3.  Make ready 1/4 tsp black pepper
4.  Prepare 1 bowl rice
5.  Take 50 g Salmon
6.  Get 1/2 cup Mixed vegetables

##### Instructions to make 🎄Christmas Healthy Menu❄️:

1.  Prepare all the ingredients and cut the salmon and tomatoes into small pieces
2.  Put the tomatoes and mixed vegetables into the pot with the semi cooked rice and mixed with the rice, and cutting the vegetables into a large piece
3.  After the rice is well cooked and add them into the frying pan, adding the salmon on the top of the mixed rice
4.  Fried the rice with the salmon and mixed them well quickly
5.  Adding the vegetables at the bottom of the dish as a base and adding the mixed rice at the top of the vegetables
6.  Add the black pepper finally and the Christmas Health Dish is ready to served! Enjoy :)
