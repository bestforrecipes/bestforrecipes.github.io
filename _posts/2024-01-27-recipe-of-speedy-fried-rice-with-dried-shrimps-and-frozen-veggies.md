##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Rice with Dried Shrimps and Frozen Veggies:

1.  Take 2 cup steamed rice
2.  Prepare 1 tsp garlic paste
3.  Take 1/2 shallot paste (ground or slice a couple of shallots thinly)
4.  Make ready 1/4 cup frozen vegetables
5.  Prepare 3 tbsp dried shrimps, soaked in water for about 15 minutes (discard the water or use it as broth later)
6.  Prepare 2 tsp honey
7.  Make ready 1 tsp oil

##### Steps to make Fried Rice with Dried Shrimps and Frozen Veggies:

1.  Heat the oil in the wok. Add garlic paste, shallot paste, and stir until fragrant.
2.  Add dried shrimps, then frozen veggies. Lower the heat.
3.  When the veggies are partially thawed, add honey.
4.  Add the rice, and stir until well-combined.
