##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken & chorizo jambalaya:

1.  Take 1 tbsp olive oil
2.  Get 2 chicken breast, chopped Or King prawns
3.  Take 1 onion, diced
4.  Get 2 pepper, thinly sliced
5.  Make ready 2 garlic cloves, crushed
6.  Take 75 g chorizo, sliced
7.  Take 1 tbsp Cajun seasoning
8.  Prepare 250 g long grain rice
9.  Take 1 can plum tomato
10.  Get 600 ml chicken stock

##### Steps to make Chicken & chorizo jambalaya:

1.  Heat the oil in a large frying pan with a lid and brown the chicken for 5-8 mins until golden. Remove and set aside.
2.  Tip in the onion and cook for 3-4 mins until soft.
3.  Then add the pepper, garlic, chorizo and Cajun seasoning, and cook for 5 mins more.
4.  Stir the chicken back in with the rice, add the tomatoes and stock
5.  Cover and simmer for 20-25 mins until the rice is tender.
