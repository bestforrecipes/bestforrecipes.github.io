##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Salad with Roasted Peppers:

1.  Prepare 3 cans tuna/ drained
2.  Make ready Roasted red and green pepperS/ chopped
3.  Take 1 cup scallions / chopped
4.  Make ready 1 tsp paprika
5.  Take Salt (choice of yours)
6.  Prepare 2-3 tbs Mayo

##### Instructions to make Tuna Salad with Roasted Peppers:

1.  Roast peppers in broiler peel, dry and chop them, chop the scallions
2.  Mix them with sandwich spread and veggies, add salt and paprika,
3.  Serve with leaf of lettuce
