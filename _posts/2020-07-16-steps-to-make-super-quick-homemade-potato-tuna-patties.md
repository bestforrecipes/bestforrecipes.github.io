##### The ingredients needed to make Potato Tuna Patties:

1.  Get 500 gr mashed potatoes (peel and halve potatoes, boil until tender, mash with 1 tbsp of butter or margarine)
2.  Take 1 can tuna, drain the excess oil
3.  Make ready 1 tbsp breadcrumbs
4.  Take 1 tsp grated parmesan (or cheddar)
5.  Get 1 tsp maizena
6.  Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic powder
7.  Get Oil for molding and frying

##### Steps to make Potato Tuna Patties:

1.  Combine mashed potatoes with tuna, bread crumbs, parmesan, maizena, and garlic powder. Mix until smooth. You might want to add a bit of breadcrumbs if it's too watery, or a bit of milk if the mixture is too solid.
2.  Wash and dry your hands. Add a bit of oil to your hand, and form patties.
3.  Heat about 1 tbsp of oil in a pan. When it is hot, put patties inside. Cook using low heat. Flip the patties carefully. They will be golden brown when done, but still a bit soft.
