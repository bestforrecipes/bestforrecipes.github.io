##### The ingredients needed to make 5 Ingredients Dinner

Crab&Corn soup:

1.  Take 1 can(200g) creamed corn
2.  Prepare Fresh crab meat (or in brine) as desired
3.  Make ready 1 cup spring onion, thinly sliced
4.  Make ready 300 ml chicken stock
5.  Make ready 1 tablespoon ginger, finely chopped
6.  Make ready 1 bit of black pepper to taste(optional)

##### Instructions to make 5 Ingredients Dinner

Crab&Corn soup:

1.  The 5 ingredients
2.  Add ginger in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir until aromatic.
3.  Add stock and creamed corn. Bring to the boil and reduce heat to medium.
4.  Add crab meat and 1/3 of spring onion to stock mixture, stir to combine, add black pepper.
5.  Ladle soup evenly among serving bowl. Top with the remaining spring onionand serve immediately.
