##### The ingredients needed to make Argentine Red Shrimp With Tomatoes & Feta Cheese:

1.  Prepare 8-10 oz Argentine red shrimp raw peeled and deveined
2.  Get Olive oil for coating the shrimp
3.  Get Freeze-dried oregano or fresh
4.  Make ready Salt and Pepper
5.  Take 2 medium tomatoes peeled, cored, and sliced roughly chopped
6.  Get 1-2 Tbs olive oil
7.  Prepare 1/3 cup diced shallots
8.  Get 1-2 garlic cloves minced
9.  Get Salt and pepper
10.  Take Dash or so of white wine
11.  Take Red pepper flakes
12.  Prepare 1-2 oz. Feta cheese crumbled
13.  Make ready Freeze-dried mint leave flakes or fresh
14.  Make ready Cooking, assembly, and baking
15.  Get Preheat the oven to 400f
16.  Make ready In a bowl, add a little olive oil, salt, pepper, a few dashes of oregano, and toss the shrimp in the seasoned oil to coat and set aside
17.  Take Heat a medium saute pan to medium and add 1-2 Tbs Olive Oil
18.  Make ready Add shallots, garlic, and salt and pepper to taste, saute until soft, about five minutes
19.  Prepare Add the tomatoes, and red pepper flakes. Simmer, mashing the tomatoes a bit. Cover and cook down until thickened. Remove the lid and cook to a sauce
20.  Take Add a splash of white wine and simmer for a few minutes more
21.  Take Transfer the tomatoes, shallots, and garlic onto individual ovenproof serving dishes and spread across the bottom
22.  Take Layer the shrimp across the tomatoes and crumble the feta over the shrimp
23.  Take Put into the oven and cook about 10-15 minutes until bubbly
24.  Get Remove and sprinkle with Mint and serve along with crusty bread and White Wine
