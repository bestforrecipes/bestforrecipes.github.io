##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Salmon in Black Pepper:

1.  Make ready 2 salmon fillets
2.  Prepare 1 spoon grounded black pepper
3.  Take 1 teaspoon salt
4.  Make ready 2 spoons olive oil

##### Steps to make Grilled Salmon in Black Pepper:

1.  Mix black pepper and salt on a big plate
2.  Cover the salmon in the mix, until it is all covered and "black"
3.  Roast the broad side, not the skin, for 4 minutes, on a grill with olive oil, mid flame. Press lightly. Then for one minute plus on each side. Serve
