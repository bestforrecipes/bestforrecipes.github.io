##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Tater Tot Casserole:

1.  Make ready Tater Tots
2.  Get Tuna
3.  Get Peas
4.  Get can of cream of mushroom soup

##### Steps to make Tuna Tater Tot Casserole:

1.  Preheat oven to 375° F.
2.  Mix cream of mushroom soup, tuna, and peas in 8"x8" baking dish.
3.  Place tater tots in a single layer over the top of the mixture.
4.  Add salt, cheese, and/or paprika for flavor (optional).
5.  Bake in oven uncovered for 35 min, and serve.
