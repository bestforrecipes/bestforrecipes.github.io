##### The ingredients needed to make Tiff's Tuna casserole:

1.  Prepare 1/2 green bell pepper - diced
2.  Take 1/2 red bell pepper - diced
3.  Get 1/2 yellow onion - diced
4.  Take 2 carrots - diced
5.  Make ready 2 stalks of celery- diced
6.  Get 2 tbsp butter
7.  Take 1/3 cup white cooking wine
8.  Make ready 2 10 oz. cans of cream of mushroom soup
9.  Get 1 soup can full of milk
10.  Prepare 3/4 cup of sour cream
11.  Take 2 12 oz. cans of solid white tuna- undrained
12.  Make ready 1/2 bag of frozen peas
13.  Make ready 1 tsp fresh cracked black pepper
14.  Take 2 tsp seasoning salt- I use Johnny's
15.  Take 1 tsp garlic powder
16.  Get 1 16 oz. bag of rotini noodles- cooked according to directions of pkg
17.  Take 1/2 cup bread crumbs or crushed ritz crackers

##### Instructions to make Tiff's Tuna casserole:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°F
2.  Sauté diced peppers, carrot, onion and celery in 2 Tbls butter until tender.
3.  Add cooking wine and continue cooking for an additional 2 mins.
4.  Remove from heat and set aside.
5.  In a large bowl mix both cans of mushroom soup,milk, sour cream, season salt, garlic powder and pepper. Mix well.
6.  Now add both cans of undrained tuna, frozen peas and sautéd vegetables. Mix well and add cooked rotini noodles.
7.  Add to greased 13x9 baking dish and bake for 40 minutes. Add bread crumbs and bake an additional 5 minutes.
8.  Enjoy!
