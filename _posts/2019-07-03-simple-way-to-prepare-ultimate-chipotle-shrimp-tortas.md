##### The ingredients needed to make Chipotle Shrimp Tortas:

1.  Take 1 lb cleaned and deveined shrimp
2.  Get 1 chili DE arbol powder seasoning
3.  Make ready 1 garlic salt
4.  Get 1 oregano
5.  Make ready pinch ground cumin
6.  Prepare 1/2 tsp minced garlic
7.  Get 2 tbsp butter
8.  Make ready 1 tsp mayonnaise
9.  Make ready 1/2 sliced onion
10.  Prepare toppings
11.  Get 1 torta bread
12.  Prepare 1 sliced avocado
13.  Prepare 1 Napa cabbage
14.  Get dressing
15.  Make ready 2 Chipotle from can n some of juice
16.  Get 1/4 chopped onion
17.  Take 1/2 bunch cilantro
18.  Make ready 1 garlic salt to taste
19.  Make ready 1 cup sour cream
20.  Get 1 tsp mayonnaise

##### Instructions to make Chipotle Shrimp Tortas:

1.  Start by cleaning and butter flying your shrimp. Sprinkle with desired amount of seasonings listed. Warm a pan add butter and garlic, now add in, onion cook about 3 minutes and in in shrimp cook shrimp till pink about 6 minutes or so set aside.
2.  Add all ingredients for Chipotle sauce into blender cream well taste for seasoning.refridgerate till use.
3.  Cut open your torta bread add mayo heat a small skillet and lightly toast your bread mayo side down till golden.
4.  Now assemble, add Chipotle dressing to bottom, shrimp, cabbage, avocado, more dressing etc.. enjoy!
