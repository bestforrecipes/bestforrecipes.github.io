##### The ingredients needed to make Zesty Jumbo Prawns:

1.  Prepare 8 Jumbo Prawns
2.  Make ready 1 tsp degi mirch
3.  Take 1/2 tbsp tap lemon zest
4.  Make ready 2 pinches chilli flakes
5.  Prepare 1 tsp crushed black pepper
6.  Make ready 1 tsp olive oil
7.  Take 2 tsp butter
8.  Make ready 6 crushed garlic cloves
9.  Make ready 1 tbs lemon juice
10.  Prepare to taste Salt
11.  Make ready as needed Coriander leaves to garnish

##### Steps to make Zesty Jumbo Prawns:

1.  Remove the heads and devein the prawns leaving the shells and tails intact
2.  After washing them well pat them dry, add salt, degi mirch and lemon zest. Drizzle olive oil and mix well
3.  Marinate it for atleast half an hour
4.  In a pan heat up 1 tsp butter, add marinated prawns cook on high flame for 2 minutes
5.  Lower the flame, cover and cook for 5 minutes.
6.  Now open the lid, cook till most of the liquid evaporates but not dry.
7.  Add crushed garlic, pepper and remaining butter. Stir well.
8.  Add lemon juice, cook for another minute
9.  Garnish with coriander leaves and a pinch of chilli flakes. Serve hot or warm.
