##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Bake:

1.  Prepare crab bake
2.  Make ready 16 oz crab
3.  Make ready 1/2 cup sour cream
4.  Get 1 tsp garlic powder
5.  Get 8 oz cream cheese
6.  Prepare 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
7.  Take 1 tbsp milk
8.  Get 1 tbsp sugar
9.  Make ready 1 1/2 cup cheddar cheese

##### Instructions to make Crab Bake:

1.  Preheat oven to 375°
2.  Mix all ingredients (except 1 cup cheese) together
3.  Spread in pan
4.  Top with remaining cheese
5.  Bake for 20-30 minutes or until cheese is bubbly
