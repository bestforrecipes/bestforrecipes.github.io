##### The ingredients needed to make Seaweed Hummus:

1.  Take 1 cup boiled chickpeas (Kabuli Chana)
2.  Get 15-20 roasted seaweed sheets
3.  Prepare 2 garlic cloves
4.  Get 1-2 green chilies
5.  Prepare 1/4 cup coriander roots
6.  Take 1/4 cup sesame seeds
7.  Get 1 tbsp. lime juice
8.  Prepare 2-3 tbsp. cup olive oil
9.  Get 1/2 tsp. cumin powder
10.  Get 2 pinches pepper powder
11.  Prepare to taste salt
12.  Prepare extra boiled chickpeas, seaweed and olive oil to garnish

##### Instructions to make Seaweed Hummus:

1.  Blend together all the above mentioned ingredients to a smooth paste. Add some water if required.
2.  Transfer to a serving bowl. Garnish with some boiled chickpeas, seaweed and olive oil.
3.  Serve as a dip along with some veggie crudites, chips, tacos, croutons, tortillas or as a spread on sandwiches / parathas.
