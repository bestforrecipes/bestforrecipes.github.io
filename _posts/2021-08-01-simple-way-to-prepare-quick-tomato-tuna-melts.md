##### The ingredients needed to make Tomato Tuna Melts:

1.  Take 1 can Light Tuna in Water (5 oz)
2.  Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Your favorite Italian Dressing
3.  Prepare 1 Tomato
4.  Get 1 slice Provolone Cheese

##### Instructions to make Tomato Tuna Melts:

1.  Preheat broiler to 500°.
2.  Use a paring knife to hull out the center of the tomato, then scoop out flesh with a spoon.
3.  Drain tuna. Combine well with Italian Dressing.
4.  Stuff the tomato with the tuna salad, top with a slice of cheese, and broil until cheese is golden.
5.  Eat it up!
