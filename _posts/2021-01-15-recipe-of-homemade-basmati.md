##### Instructions to make Basmati:

1.  Wash and per boil your basmati for 20mins and drain.
2.  In a pan add in sesame oil, onions, fennel n cumin, prawns, add in your peppers, garlic n scotch bonnet and stir fry
3.  Add in your seasonings and sauces
4.  Add in your drained rice and seasoning to taste..cook for 10mins… and serve hot. U can eat with salad by d side
