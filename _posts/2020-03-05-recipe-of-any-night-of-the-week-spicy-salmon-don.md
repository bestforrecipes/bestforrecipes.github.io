##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Salmon Don:

1.  Take 100 g raw sushi grade salmon
2.  Get Sauce
3.  Take 1 tsp minced ginger
4.  Prepare 1 tsp minced garlic
5.  Prepare 1 tsp toban djan
6.  Take 2 tsp sesame oil
7.  Take 2 tsp sugar
8.  Prepare 1 tsp soy sauce

##### Instructions to make Spicy Salmon Don:

1.  Chop up salmon in cubes with knife pointing in opposite direction of skin and flesh
2.  Marinate in soy sauce for five minutes
3.  Mix together sauce ingredients
4.  Pour sauce over salmon
5.  Put salmon over white rice or greens for a salad. Add scallions as topping.
