##### The ingredients needed to make Whosayna’s Prawn Tempura:

1.  Prepare Marinade:
2.  Make ready 1 kg Prawns (with tails)
3.  Prepare 1 tsp Salt
4.  Get 1 tsp Paprika
5.  Prepare 1 tbsp Garlic paste
6.  Get 1/2 Lemon juice
7.  Take Coating:
8.  Make ready 1 cup All Purpose flour
9.  Get 1/4 cup Cornflour
10.  Get 1 Egg
11.  Prepare 1 tsp Baking powder
12.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Blackpepper powder
13.  Take Salt (little)
14.  Take Cold Water

##### Instructions to make Whosayna’s Prawn Tempura:

1.  Marinate the prawns and keep in fridge for three hours
2.  Keep wok filled with oil on flame
3.  In a bowl beat egg add the rest add little cold water at a time and mix till you get pouring consistency
4.  Dip each prawn in the batter and put in hot oil, put atleast six at a time
5.  Deep fry till crispy golden color remove on strainer then lay on platter with kitchen towel on it
