##### The ingredients needed to make Crab salad:

1.  Get 1 lb crab meat-chopped
2.  Get 1 lb any noodle you like. I used large shells
3.  Make ready 1 celery-diced
4.  Make ready 1 Onion-diced
5.  Take 1 mayonnaise
6.  Take 1 mustard
7.  Make ready 1 paprika-garnish

##### Steps to make Crab salad:

1.  Boil noodles until done and drain.
2.  Add noodles to crab meat, celery and onions. Amount of onion and celery up to you!
3.  Add mayo and small amount of mustard. Stir until creamy. Top with paprika
4.  Place in frig until time to eat
