##### The ingredients needed to make Umami-Rich Fluffy Dashimaki Tamago With Crabsticks and Mentsuyu Sauce:

1.  Make ready 2 Eggs
2.  Get 2 Crabsticks
3.  Get 1 tbsp Mentsuyu
4.  Prepare 1 tbsp Water
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp Vegetable oil

##### Steps to make Umami-Rich Fluffy Dashimaki Tamago With Crabsticks and Mentsuyu Sauce:

1.  Chop up the crabsticks roughly.
2.  Put the eggs, chopped up crabsticks, mentsuyu sauce and water into a bowl and mix well to make the egg mixture.
3.  Heat the oil in a frying pan over low-medium heat. Add the egg mixture from Step 2 and cook.
4.  Neaten up the omelette, lighty brown both sides and turn off the heat. Cut into easy-to-eat pieces, transfer to serving plates and it's done!
