##### The ingredients needed to make Garlic Butter Sautéed Oysters:

1.  Get 1 pack Oysters
2.  Make ready 4 tbsp Flour
3.  Take 1 tbsp Oil
4.  Get 1 Butter
5.  Take 2 1/2 tsp Grated garlic
6.  Take 1 dash Salt and pepper
7.  Make ready 1/2 tbsp Salt (to rinse the oysters)

##### Instructions to make Garlic Butter Sautéed Oysters:
