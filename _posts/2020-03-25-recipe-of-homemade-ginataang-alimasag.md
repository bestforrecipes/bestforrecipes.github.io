##### Steps to make Ginataang Alimasag:

1.  In a large pot, sauté the garlic, onion, and ginger.
2.  Add the ground black pepper and coconut milk then bring to a boil. Add the Thai chilli and simmer for 5 minutes. Add fish sauce.
