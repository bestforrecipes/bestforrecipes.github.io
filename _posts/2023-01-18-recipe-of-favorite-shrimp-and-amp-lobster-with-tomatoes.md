##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp & Lobster with tomatoes:

1.  Get 1 small onion, diced
2.  Prepare 1 red, yellow & green peppers, sliced
3.  Take 1/2 lb mushrooms, sliced
4.  Take 3 garlic cloves, minced
5.  Make ready 4 tomatoes, diced
6.  Get 1 olive oil, to coat the saute pan
7.  Make ready 1 salt & pepper to taste
8.  Take 1 lb large shrimp, peeled & deveined
9.  Get 3 small lobster tails
10.  Get 1 fresh basil, rolled up & sliced
