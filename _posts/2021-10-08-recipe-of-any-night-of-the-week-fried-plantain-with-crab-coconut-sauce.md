##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Plantain with Crab Coconut Sauce:

1.  Make ready 1 stick plantain
2.  Make ready 1 cksp vegetable oil
3.  Make ready pinch salt
4.  Prepare 2 cksp Crab coconut sauce

##### Steps to make Fried Plantain with Crab Coconut Sauce:

1.  Wash plantain, peel off the back and slice to your desired shape sprinkle salt
2.  Heat up frying pan, once its hot add vegetable wait for oil to heat up,
3.  Place into into the frying and fry; flip over when the side di same till you done frying your plantain
4.  Serve with crab coconut sauce if ypu have one.
