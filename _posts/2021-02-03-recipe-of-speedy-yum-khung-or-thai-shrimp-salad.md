##### The ingredients needed to make Yum khung or thai shrimp salad:

1.  Get 300 grams of shrimp
2.  Get 1 cup of chopped celery and coriander together
3.  Get 1 lettuce cut into 2 cm long
4.  Prepare 1/2 cup of chopped green onions
5.  Get 1/4 cup of lime juice
6.  Prepare 1/4 cup of pickles garlic chopped with juice
7.  Get 2 tsp of fish sauce
8.  Make ready 2 tsp of sugar
9.  Take 1 tsp of chicken stock powder
10.  Prepare 1 onion slices
11.  Get 3 thai red chilies finally chopped
12.  Prepare 1 tomatoes slices
13.  Take 1 pack of glass noodles
