2.  Get 2.5 lbs salad shrimp
3.  Make ready 4 large tomatoes, diced
4.  Prepare lime juice (about 10)
5.  Make ready 1 extra large sweet onion
6.  Get 2 large bunches of cilantro
7.  Get 1/2 can orange soda
8.  Get to taste tapatio
9.  Get 1/2 can beer
10.  Prepare to taste ketchup
11.  Take salt
12.  Get granulated garlic (powder)
13.  Prepare white wine vinegar, ~3tbsp
14.  Prepare to taste oregano
15.  Prepare 4 cucumbers diced
