##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Lime Shrimp:

1.  Make ready 1/2 kilo shrimps, deveined and peeled except for the heads and tails
2.  Take 1 Red bell pepper (any color of your choice), squared for skewering
3.  Prepare 1/3 cup Olive oil
4.  Take 1/4 cup lime juice
5.  Take 4 tbsps Worcestershire sauce
6.  Make ready 6 tbsps honey
7.  Make ready 4 big garlic cloves, chopped
8.  Get to taste Salt and pepper
9.  Take Barbecue sticks / skewers

It tastes like you ordered it straight from a restaurant, or spent hours in the kitchen, all while being made in minutes! In a large nonstick skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and season it with salt and pepper to taste. Delicious Garlic Honey Lime Shrimp from Six Sisters' Stuff.

##### Instructions to make Honey Lime Shrimp:

1.  Make a marinade mixing the olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, honey, lime juice, and garlic. After shrimps have been deveined and shelled, put them in the marinade and leave to marinade for 10 minutes.
2.  After 10 minutes, skewer red bell pepper and shrimps together, alternating pepper and shrimp.
3.  Take a grill pan and add olive oil (or butter) on it and grill the shrimp until they turn pink.
4.  Serve and enjoy! ☺️
