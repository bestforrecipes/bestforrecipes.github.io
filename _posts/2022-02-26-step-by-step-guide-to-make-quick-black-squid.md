##### The ingredients needed to make Black Squid:

1.  Get 1/2 kg Fresh squid with the ink in it
2.  Prepare 2 eggs
3.  Take 1 sweet soy sauce ( according taste )
4.  Prepare 100 ml of water
5.  Get 1 tbsp oyster sauce
6.  Prepare 1 chili (according taste)
7.  Get 5 onion
8.  Prepare 4 garlic
9.  Prepare 1 slice lengkuas / galangal (indonesian spice)
10.  Take 2 daun jeruk purut/ kaffir lime leaves (indonesian spice)
11.  Get 2 daun salam/ bay leaves (indonesian spice)
12.  Prepare 1 tbsp tomato sauce
13.  Take 1 tbsp chili sauce
14.  Take 1 salt , sugar and pepper powder (according taste)
15.  Make ready 1 pinch of beef flavor bouillon powder
16.  Prepare 3 tbsp oil to sauteing

##### Instructions to make Black Squid:

1.  wash the squid, reserving the ink in it and cut shaped like a ring
2.  slice all ingredients except kaffir lime leaves, bay leaves and galangal ( just mashed to get the flavor)
3.  make scrambled eggs and place on a plate
4.  saute all the spices and enter the squid
5.  put all the sauce and season and add the water
6.  enter the scrambled eggs and cook until the squid is cooked
