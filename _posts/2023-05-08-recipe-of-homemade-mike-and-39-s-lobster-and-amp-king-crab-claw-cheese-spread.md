##### The ingredients needed to make Mike's Lobster & King Crab Claw Cheese Spread:

1.  Get 1 Pound Rock Lobster Tails \[meat weight - fine chopped\]
2.  Take 1 Pound Pre-steamed King Crab Claws \[meat weight -fine shread\]
3.  Take as needed Quality Dry White Wine
4.  Take 4 (8 oz) Packages Philadelphia Cream Cheese \[room temp\]
5.  Prepare 1/2 Cup Chives \[fine chopped\]
6.  Take 1/2 tsp Lemon Pepper
7.  Make ready 1 Dash Lemon Juice
8.  Take 1/2 tsp Old Bay
9.  Take ● Options
10.  Make ready Toasted Bagles
11.  Prepare Chilled Vegetables
12.  Take Lemon Wedges
13.  Prepare Crackers

##### Instructions to make Mike's Lobster & King Crab Claw Cheese Spread:

1.  Place shelled seafood on a cookie sheet with white wine. Bake seafood at 350° for 20 minutes. Pull from oven and crack shells once cooled. Rough chop meat or shread. Mix everything together and chill.
2.  Serve spread on toasted Bagles, crackers or dip vegetables in. Also, with lemon wedges.
3.  This spread makes for great gifts!
4.  Enjoy!
