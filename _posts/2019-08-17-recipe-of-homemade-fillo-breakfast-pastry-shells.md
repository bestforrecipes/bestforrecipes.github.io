##### The ingredients needed to make Fillo Breakfast Pastry Shells:

1.  Get 2 large eggs
2.  Take 1 cup shredded cheese
3.  Take 2 Sausage patties
4.  Get 1 cup chopped onion
5.  Take 1 box fillo pastry shells
6.  Take tbsp canola oil

##### Steps to make Fillo Breakfast Pastry Shells:

1.  Heat oven to 350°| Spritz your cookie sheet with canola oil|
2.  Whisk together your eggs, crumbled sausage, cheese and onions.| Fill your shells with egg mixture|
3.  Line baking sheet with filled fillo shells| Put them in the oven for 25 minutes

5.  Serve and enjoy!!
