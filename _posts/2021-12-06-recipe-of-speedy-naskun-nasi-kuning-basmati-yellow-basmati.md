##### The ingredients needed to make Naskun–Nasi Kuning–Basmati (Yellow Basmati):

1.  Take 1 cup basmati, wash well and then rinse
2.  Prepare 3 cup water (you can use less if you want denser rice. We prefer softer rice)
3.  Prepare 1 tbsp coconut oil
4.  Prepare 1 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
5.  Get 1 tsp salt
6.  Take 1 agar-agar sachet for rice (you can skip this, I add this to add more fiber into this rice)

##### Instructions to make Naskun–Nasi Kuning–Basmati (Yellow Basmati):

1.  Combine all in rice cooker. Stir well.
2.  Press cook and wait until it's ready.
3.  Fluff the rice before serving.
