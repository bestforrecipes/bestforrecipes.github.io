##### The ingredients needed to make My Crab Cakes:

1.  Get 1 lb jumbo lump crab
2.  Prepare 1/4 cup red bell pepper, chopped
3.  Make ready 1/4 cup scallions, chopped
4.  Prepare 1 egg
5.  Get 1/2 tsp old bay seasoning
6.  Make ready 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
7.  Prepare 1 tbsp dijon mustard
8.  Get 1/4 tsp worcestershire sauce
9.  Prepare 1/2 cup mayonnaise
10.  Take 3/4 cup plain breadcrumbs
11.  Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin

##### Instructions to make My Crab Cakes:

1.  Mix everything together in a big bowl (add crab last), form into patties. You can make 4 large or 8 average size patties.
2.  Pan fry cakes in oil, turning once.
