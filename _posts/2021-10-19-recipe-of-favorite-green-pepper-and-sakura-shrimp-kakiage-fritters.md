##### The ingredients needed to make Green Pepper and Sakura Shrimp Kakiage Fritters:

1.  Prepare 4 small Green bell peppers
2.  Prepare 10 grams Sakura shrimp (dried)
3.  Make ready 1 tbsp Flour
4.  Get \[A\] Or use tempura flour
5.  Get 3 tbsp Flour
6.  Take 1 tbsp Katakuriko
7.  Prepare 2 tsp Mayonnaise
8.  Prepare 50 ml Ice cold water

##### Instructions to make Green Pepper and Sakura Shrimp Kakiage Fritters:

1.  Remove the stem end and seeds from the peppers, cut in half lengthwise, and cut into 2 mm slices.
2.  Put the peppers and sakura shrimp in a bowl. Add 1 tablespoon flour and mix well.
3.  Add the combined \[A\] ingredients and mix together roughly.
4.  Fry spoonfuls in 360°F/180°C oil until crispy.
5.  Serve with ponzu or salt.
