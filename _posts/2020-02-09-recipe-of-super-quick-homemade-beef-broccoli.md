##### The ingredients needed to make Beef Broccoli:

1.  Prepare 2 Bunches Broccoli
2.  Take 450 gr Beef thinly sliced, cubed
3.  Prepare 2 tbsp Oyster Sauce
4.  Prepare 1 tsp Corn Starch, mix with water
5.  Make ready as needed water
6.  Make ready 1/2 Yellow Onion,thinly sliced
7.  Take 2 tsp Sesame Oil
8.  Prepare as needed Extra Virgin Olive oil to stiry fry
9.  Make ready for Marinade :
10.  Prepare 2 tbsp Fish Sauce
11.  Prepare 1 tbsp Kecap Manis(sweet soy sauce)
12.  Prepare 1/4 tsp Pepper

##### Instructions to make Beef Broccoli:

1.  Marinade the beef with Marinade ingredients and leave it for about 30minutes.
2.  To boil broccoli, start by cutting off the stem and separating the top into individual florets. Then, add the florets to a pot of boiling water and cook them for 3-4minutes
3.  Heat the extra virgin olive oil with medium level then Saute The beef until soft,then put aside
4.  Saute the onion until you can smell the aroma, then add the cooked beef, oyster sauce, sesame oil, mixed corn starch-water,water and the cooked broccoli. Cook them for about 2-3 minutes
5.  For the appearance, form circular Broccoli as shown in the picture and add the beef on the top. Now it's done! Enjoy!♥️
