##### The ingredients needed to make Sour Eggplant Hot Sauce (SAMBAL TERONG ASAM):

1.  Get shrimp, peel and slice back
2.  Prepare (± 150 gr) sour eggplant
3.  Take cayenne peppers (to taste)
4.  Take small, red onion
5.  Get Grilled shrimp paste
6.  Get Sugar and salt

##### Steps to make Sour Eggplant Hot Sauce (SAMBAL TERONG ASAM):

1.  Fried shrimp until cooked, drain. Peel sour eggplant and wash, then cut into small pieces.
2.  Puree chili then add eggplant slices and sour shrimp paste. Add the sugar and salt, stirring until well blended.
3.  Serve sambal/hot sauce with shrimp.
4.  NOTE : Sour Eggplant ((Solaum ferox Linn)
