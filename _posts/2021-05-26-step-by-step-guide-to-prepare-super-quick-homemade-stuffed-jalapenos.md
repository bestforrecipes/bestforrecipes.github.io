##### The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Jalapenos:

1.  Take 12 Jalapenos
2.  Make ready 16 oz cream cheese
3.  Make ready 1 packages Crab meat
4.  Make ready 1/2 cup Cheddar Cheese
5.  Get 1/4 cup Light Cream
6.  Make ready 2 tbsp old bay
7.  Prepare 1 tbsp garlic powder
8.  Make ready 1 tbsp chipotle powder

##### Steps to make Stuffed Jalapenos:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°F
2.  Cut your jalapenos in half, making 24
3.  Clean out seeds and rinse in cold water. Set to the side.
4.  In a bowl, mix all other ingredients together until blends uniform.
5.  With a spoon, or fork, stuff your jalapenos and place on a baking sheet.
6.  Once you stuff the peppers place in the oven for 6 to 9 min or until heated through. There will be some filling left.
