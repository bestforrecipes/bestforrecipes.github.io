6.  Make ready 3 garlic cloves
7.  Make ready 2 tsp lemon juice
8.  Prepare 1/4 tbsp salt
9.  Take 400 grams canned tuna
10.  Take 1 onion, cut into thin slices
11.  Prepare 2 tbsp fresh parsley
