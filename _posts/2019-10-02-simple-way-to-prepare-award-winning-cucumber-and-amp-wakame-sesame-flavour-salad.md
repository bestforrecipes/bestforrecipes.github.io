##### The ingredients needed to make Cucumber & Wakame Sesame Flavour Salad:

1.  Take 1 Continental Cucumber OR 2 smaller Cucumbers \*about 300g
2.  Get 1/4 teaspoon Salt
3.  Get 2-3 tablespoons Dry Cut Wakame
4.  Take 1/4 Onion \*thinly sliced
5.  Take 1 tablespoon Toasted Sesame Seeds
6.  Take 1 tablespoon Sugar
7.  Get 1 tablespoon Rice Vinegar
8.  Take 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
9.  Take 1/2 tablespoon Sesame Oil

##### Instructions to make Cucumber & Wakame Sesame Flavour Salad:

1.  Thinly slice Cucumber into a bowl. Add Salt and massage, then set aside.
2.  Soak Dry Cut Wakame in cold water until soft, drain well.
3.  Combine Sugar, Rice Vinegar, Soy Sauce and Sesame Oil in a large bowl. Squeeze the sliced Cucumber to remove excess salty water, and add to the bowl. Add all other ingredients and mix well to combine.
