##### The ingredients needed to make Tasty Salmon Croquettes:

1.  Make ready 2 cans Pink Salmon
2.  Take 1/3 cup finely chopped onion
3.  Make ready 1/3 cup finely chopped bell pepper
4.  Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
5.  Take 2 tsp hot sauce
6.  Make ready 2 tsp mustard
7.  Take 2/3 cup plain breadcrumbs
8.  Get 2 eggs
9.  Prepare to taste Salt and pepper

##### Steps to make Tasty Salmon Croquettes:

1.  Drain excess liquid fron salmon
2.  Dump Salmon into medium-sized bowl (I try to remove bones and fish skin)
3.  Add remaining ingredients and stir well.
4.  Form into patties. (I like them just smaller than 1/2 inch thick, and about 2.5 inches round).
5.  Preheat canola oil in skillet. It should be enough to cover about half of a patty. Then cook patties on medium heat on each side until each croquette is firm. Then place croquettes on top of a paper towel in a pan to remove excess oil.
6.  Enjoy!
