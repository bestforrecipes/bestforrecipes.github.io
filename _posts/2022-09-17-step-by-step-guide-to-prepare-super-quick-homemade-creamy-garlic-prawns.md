##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Garlic Prawns:

1.  Get 500 g Uncooked Prawns
2.  Get 1 Onion \*finely chopped
3.  Get 2 cloves Garlic \*finely chopped
4.  Prepare 30 g Butter
5.  Get 3 tablespoons Plain Flour
6.  Prepare 1/4 cup White Wine
7.  Make ready 1 cup Chicken Stock OR Seafood Stock
8.  Prepare 1/8 teaspoon Ground Chilli \*OR as required
9.  Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Salt \*alter the amount to suit your taste
10.  Get White Pepper \*OR Black Pepper
11.  Take 1/2 cup Cream
12.  Get 2 tablespoons chopped Parsley
13.  Get Freshly Cooked Rice OR Crusty Bread to serve

##### Instructions to make Creamy Garlic Prawns:

1.  Prepare Prawns. If you use Frozen Prawns, thaw them thoroughly.
2.  Heat Butter in a large frying pan or pot over medium heat, cook Onion and Garlic until soft. Add Plain Flour and stir to mix. Add white Wine and Stock, and bring to the boil, stirring constantly.
3.  Add Ground Chilli, and season with Salt and Pepper to taste.
4.  Add Prawns. When Prawns are just cooked, add Cream and Parsley, and bring back to the boil. Check the flavour and add extra seasonings if required.
5.  Serve with freshly cooked rice OR nice crusty bread.
