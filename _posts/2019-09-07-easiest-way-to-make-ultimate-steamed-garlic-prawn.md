##### The ingredients needed to make Steamed Garlic Prawn:

1.  Prepare 300 gr prawn, split the back and take out the dirt, keep the head and the skin
2.  Take 1 Tbsp fresh lime juice
3.  Take 1 tsp salt
4.  Get 6 stalks green onions, thinly sliced
5.  Take 1/2 red chili pepper, thinly sliced diagonally
6.  Make ready 2 Tbsp sesame oil
7.  Take For the sauce:
8.  Prepare 6 cloves garlic, minced
9.  Get 1 inch ginger, minced
10.  Prepare 1 Tbsp seafood soy sauce
11.  Take 1 tsp Chinese cooking wine
12.  Take to taste white pepper powder

##### Instructions to make Steamed Garlic Prawn:

1.  Mix the prawn with salt and lime juice. Set aside for 15 minutes.
2.  In a small bowl, mix the sauce ingredients together.
3.  Arrange prawn on a heat resistant plate.
4.  Pour sauce ingredients on top of the prawn.
5.  Steam prawn for 8 minutes only in a big preheated pot. Remove from the heat. (Please DO NOT steam the prawn too long. It will make them chewy and not sweet anymore)
6.  Place sliced green onions and sliced red chili pepper evenly on the prawn.
7.  Heat sesame oil in a small pan until very hot. Pour sesame oil on the prawn evenly.
8.  Serve over warm plain porridge or rice. Yum! ðŸ˜‹.
