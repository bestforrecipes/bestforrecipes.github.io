##### The ingredients needed to make Thai Prawns w coconut milk n vegetables:

1.  Make ready Shrimps or Prawns
2.  Prepare 2 can coconut milk
3.  Get 3 cloves garlic soaked in olive oil chopped
4.  Make ready Ginger chopped
5.  Make ready Okra tops and bottoms cut
6.  Get cubes Squash or kalabasa peeled and cut into
7.  Get Ampalaya optional
8.  Make ready Eggplant optional
9.  Take String beans cut into 2 inches cut
10.  Prepare Chilies
11.  Prepare 1 onion sliced and softened

##### Steps to make Thai Prawns w coconut milk n vegetables:

1.  In a hot pot with oil sauté garlic until fragrant then onions until soft then ginger until aroma starts
2.  Add in the string beans and okra then season then add the coconut milk then add others vegetables
3.  Simmer until squash is tender then add shrimps or Prawns
4.  Then serve
