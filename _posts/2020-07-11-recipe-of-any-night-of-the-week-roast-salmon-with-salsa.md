##### The ingredients needed to make Roast Salmon with Salsa:

1.  Get 2 medium plum tomatoes, chopped
2.  Make ready 1 small onion, chopped
3.  Get 1 clove garlic, peeled
4.  Take 1 jalapeno, seeded and chopped
5.  Make ready 2 tsp cider vinegar
6.  Take 1 tsp chili powder
7.  Get 1/2 tsp ground cumin
8.  Take 1/2 tsp salt
9.  Make ready 2-4 dashes hot sauce
10.  Get 1-1/2 lbs salmon, skinned and cut into 6 portions

##### Instructions to make Roast Salmon with Salsa:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F.
2.  Place all ingredients except salmon into food processor, chop until finely diced and uniform.
3.  Place salmon in large roasting pan and spoon salsa on top.
4.  Roast salmon until flaky on outside but still pink inside, about 15 min.
