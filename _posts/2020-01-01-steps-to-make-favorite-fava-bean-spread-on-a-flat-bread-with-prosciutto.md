##### The ingredients needed to make Fava Bean Spread on a Flat Bread with Prosciutto:

1.  Make ready fresh or frozen fava beans (I used frozen)
2.  Prepare red or green apple core taken out
3.  Take lemon (get 2 tsp zest & 1 tblsp juice from it)
4.  Take olive oil
5.  Make ready prepared mustard
6.  Take of flatbread
7.  Make ready prosciutto (thin slices of ham, roast beef, pastrami, or smoked salmon can be used for variation)

##### Instructions to make Fava Bean Spread on a Flat Bread with Prosciutto:

1.  boil beans in water for 3 minutes until tender but still firm
2.  rinse in cold water
3.  in a blender pulse beans, oil, lemon zest & juice, half apple and mustard till mixture is creamy but chunky
4.  spread fava bean mixture onto flatbread, top with slice of prosciutto or topping of choice
