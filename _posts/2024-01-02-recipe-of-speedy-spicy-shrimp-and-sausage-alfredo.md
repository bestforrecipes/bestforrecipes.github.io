##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Shrimp and Sausage Alfredo:

1.  Take 1 lb Shrimp (Frozen peeled devained)
2.  Take 1 lb Sausage links
3.  Get 1 packages Fettuccine Noodles
4.  Take 1/2 White Onion
5.  Get 1 Jalapeño
6.  Prepare 2 (32 oz.) Newman's Own Alfredo Sauce
7.  Prepare 1 Tony Chachere's Creole Seasoning (to taste)
8.  Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil

##### Steps to make Spicy Shrimp and Sausage Alfredo:

1.  Start boiling Noodles with 1 tablespoon of Olive oil
2.  Chop onion and Jalapeño and cook with Sausage and 1 tablespoon of Olive Oil
3.  Remove cooked Sausage, Jalapeño and onions
4.  Cook Shrimp in remaining oil adding Creole Seasoning
5.  Once Noodles are complete drain noodles and shrimp and add Alfredo Sauce. Mix well and ENJOY!
