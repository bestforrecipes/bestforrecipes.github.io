3.  Prepare 150 gr edamame
4.  Get 1 big carrot, diced
5.  Take 1/4 onion, minced
6.  Take 2 cloves garlic, minced
7.  Take 2 Tbsp soy sauce salt (if necessary) and white pepper powder (to taste)

10.  Prepare For scrambled eggs:
