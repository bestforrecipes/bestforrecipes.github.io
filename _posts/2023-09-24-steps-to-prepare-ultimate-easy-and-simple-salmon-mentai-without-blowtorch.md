##### The ingredients needed to make Easy and Simple Salmon Mentai (without blowtorch):

1.  Make ready Salmon
2.  Prepare salmon fillet
3.  Prepare salt and pepper
4.  Take butter
5.  Prepare Sauce
6.  Get mayonaise
7.  Prepare tomato sauce
8.  Prepare salt and sugar
9.  Get Rice
10.  Get serving of cooked rice / vegetable fried rice / furikake rice
11.  Take Optional
12.  Take Seaweed or furikake for topping

##### Instructions to make Easy and Simple Salmon Mentai (without blowtorch):

1.  Cut salmon thinly (to make cooking time faster) and add a little bit of salt and pepper.
2.  Add butter to you heated pan and add your salmon
3.  Makes sure they cooked evenly. And let them rest.
4.  Pre heat oven to 180°c. While waiting for the oven to heat up, make your sauce. Mix all sauce ingredients in a glass.
5.  Put your rice / fried vegetable rice furikake rice in an oven-proof dish, top with salmon, then the sauce, then seaweed (I don't have seaweed so I'm using furikake)
6.  Put inside the heated oven for 10-18 minutes. And let it rest outside for a little while
7.  Enjoy
