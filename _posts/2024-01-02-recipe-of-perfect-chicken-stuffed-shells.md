##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Stuffed Shells:

1.  Get 1 packages Large Shells
2.  Get 1 H.E.B Italian Chicken(whole precooked chicken)
3.  Make ready 1 large onion
4.  Prepare 8 oz Sliced Mushrooms
5.  Make ready 4 clove Garlic
6.  Take 2 cup Mozzarella and Monterrey jack (grated)
7.  Make ready 1 cup Parmesan (grated)
8.  Take 2 Eggs
9.  Take 2 can Alfredo Sauce
10.  Get 15 oz Ricotta
11.  Prepare 16 oz Cottage Cheese
12.  Prepare 1 tbsp Garlic powder, Onion powder, italain seasoning, Parsley

##### Instructions to make Chicken Stuffed Shells:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°F
2.  Boil shells to al-dente then drain and let cool till easy to handle
3.  Peel chicken into small pieces
4.  Dice onion, mushrooms, garlic ( recommend putting garlic in a garlic smasher )
5.  Saute onions, mushrooms, garlic and seasonings together
6.  Mix ricotta, cottage cheese, saute mix, peeled chicken 1/2 cup of mozzarella/Monterrey jack/parmesan and 2 beaten eggs in a large bowl
7.  Stuff shells with mixture(one spoon full works great in each shell)
8.  Add a layer of alfredo sauce to cover bottom of pan, layer shells next to eachother and add remaining alfredo sauce and cheese on top
9.  Bake for 30min or when its slightly brown on top
