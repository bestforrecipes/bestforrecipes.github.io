2.  Get pasta, angel hair
3.  Take 3 clove garlic, minced
4.  Take 2 tbsp oil
5.  Get pepper flakes
6.  Prepare 1 1/2 cup tomatoes, deseed chopped
7.  Make ready 2 tbsp butter
8.  Make ready 1 cup white wine
9.  Prepare 1 large lemon juice
10.  Get 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
11.  Get basil

##### Steps to make Quick shrimp scampi:

3.  Add shrimp. Cook about 4 minutes. Add white wine, lemon juice and tomatoes. Cook 2 minutes. Add butter and melt down.
