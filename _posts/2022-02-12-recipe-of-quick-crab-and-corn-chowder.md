##### The ingredients needed to make Crab and corn chowder:

1.  Take 1 small onion diced
2.  Take 2 cloves garlic chopped
3.  Make ready 1/2 jalapeno diced small
4.  Make ready 3 tbsp unsalted butter
5.  Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
6.  Take 1/4 cup white wine
7.  Make ready 1 tbsp capers
8.  Get 6 fresh basil leaves chopped small
9.  Take 2 small red potatoes 1/2" diced
10.  Prepare 1 can corn 15 oz
11.  Make ready 3/4 cup grape tomatoes sliced in half
12.  Make ready 1/2 cup crab claw meat
13.  Get 1/2 cup shrimp cleaned and chopped
14.  Make ready 3 tbsp flour
15.  Get 1 cup heavy cream
16.  Make ready 2 cups milk
17.  Make ready to taste Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Crab and corn chowder:

1.  Melt butter in pan add olive oil onions and garlic saute oh let's not forget the jalapeno
2.  Make a roux with the flour. don't burn your roux. Now add wine
3.  Add heavy cream and milk diced potatoes capers and crab and shrimp
4.  Add tomatoes and basil corn salt and pepper to taste. A little zatarains seasoning always helps
5.  Enjoy a cold beer with this
