##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet Salmon:

1.  Make ready Salmon fillet
2.  Get Sea Salt
3.  Take Black Pepper
4.  Get Lemon Juice
5.  Take Honey BBQ

##### Steps to make Sweet Salmon:

1.  Season salmon evenly with salt and pepper on both sides
2.  Squeeze lemon juice over salmon
3.  Cook salmon, skin down, over indirect heat 300° for approximately 30mins
4.  Brush with bbq sauce. (I make my own sauce, but I cannot share that recipe with you)
5.  If skin sticks to grill, it's ok. I take the skin off before serving anyway. ENJOY!
