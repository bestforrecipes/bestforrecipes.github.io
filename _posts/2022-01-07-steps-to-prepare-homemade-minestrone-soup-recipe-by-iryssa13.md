##### The ingredients needed to make Minestrone Soup

recipe by iryssa13:

1.  Make ready 1 large onion (cubed)
2.  Prepare 2 medium potato (cubed)
3.  Prepare 2 medium carrots (cubed)
4.  Make ready 3 stick celery (sliced)
5.  Get 1 can whole peeled tomatos
6.  Take 1 cup pasta (macaroni/shell/spiral)
7.  Get 1 tbsp tomato paste
8.  Make ready 3 beef stock cubes
9.  Take 6 cup water

##### Steps to make Minestrone Soup

recipe by iryssa13:

1.  cube all veges into 2 cm cubes
2.  place the stock cubes and can of tomato into a large pot with the water
3.  place the pasta into pot and boil for 5 mins
4.  add the remaining veges starting with the hardest
5.  let the veges boil for another 10mins
6.  add the tomato paste then salt and pepper to taste
7.  let boil till the veges are soft
8.  you may add more liquid (water) if needed
