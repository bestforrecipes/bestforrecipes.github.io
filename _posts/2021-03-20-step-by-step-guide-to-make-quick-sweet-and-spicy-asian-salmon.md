##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Spicy Asian Salmon:

1.  Make ready Marinade
2.  Make ready orange juice
3.  Make ready soy sauce
4.  Get minced ginger
5.  Make ready brown sugar
6.  Take (pea size) sriracha sauce
7.  Take minced garlic
8.  Make ready rice wine vinegar
9.  Take salmon
10.  Prepare salt

##### Steps to make Sweet and Spicy Asian Salmon:

1.  Mix all ingredients for about 3mins so the brown sugar is well incorporated.
2.  LIGHTLY salt the salmon.
3.  In a shallow pan or Zip loc, pour marinade on salmon and let sit for about an hour.Turn after 30min
4.  Preheat oven at 450°F
5.  Transfer Salmon into a pan (lined with foil for easy clean up!). Do not discard marinade
6.  Cook Salmon skin side down for about 8-10min(varies depending on the thickness of your salmon). Brush on marinade
7.  If you have marinade left over you can: Boil it down and top the salmon or add to some rice.Enjoy!!
