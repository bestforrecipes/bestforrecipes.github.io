##### The ingredients needed to make Pasta in Tuna-Tomato-Cream Sauce:

1.  Make ready 250 g Pasta (Tortellini prefered)
2.  Take 200 ml Cream
3.  Prepare 1 can Tuna
4.  Prepare 1 Tbsp Tomato Paste
5.  Make ready 1 Tsp Salt
6.  Get 1/4 Tsp Pepper
7.  Make ready 1/2 Tsp Garlic Dried
8.  Make ready 1-2 Tbsp Dried Basil

##### Instructions to make Pasta in Tuna-Tomato-Cream Sauce:

1.  Cook the Pasta
2.  Heat small Pot with 1 Tbsp Oil and fry the Tuna for 2 mins
3.  Pour in Cream
4.  Add the rest of the Ingridients (maybe thicken the Sauce for your liking with Cornstarch)
5.  Serve
