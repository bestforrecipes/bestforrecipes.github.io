##### The ingredients needed to make Upgraded Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Prepare medium macaroni shells
2.  Make ready light chunk tuna
3.  Get white mushrooms
4.  Get minced fresh parsley
5.  Prepare onion, chopped
6.  Make ready minced fresh garlic
7.  Prepare butter
8.  Take condensed cream of celery soup
9.  Prepare breadcrumbs
10.  Make ready shredded Parmesan
11.  Take frozen peas and carrots
12.  Prepare shredded mozzarella

##### Instructions to make Upgraded Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Boil macaroni shells and drain. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2.  Combine all ingredients except Parmesan, mozzarella, butter, and bread crumbs in a large baking dish.
3.  Cover mixture with bread crumbs and event space thinly sliced butter. Cover with aluminum foil.
4.  Bake in preheated oven for 30 min. Uncover and add shredded Parmesan and mozzarella. Bake for an additional 15 min.
