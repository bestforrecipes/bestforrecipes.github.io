##### The ingredients needed to make Crispy Pork and Shrimp Balls:

1.  Get Ground Pork
2.  Prepare Ground Shrimp
3.  Make ready minced garlic
4.  Make ready pepper
5.  Make ready sugar
6.  Take soy sauce
7.  Prepare fish sauce
8.  Get sesame oil
9.  Get eggs
10.  Make ready tapioca starch
11.  Make ready baking powder

##### Steps to make Crispy Pork and Shrimp Balls:

1.  Mixed all ingredients in a bowl ( put the shrimp in food processor or blender together with garlic… so the taste of garlic mix well with the rest of ingredients)
2.  Use your hands to mix all the ingredients and feel it… add some more tapioca starch as needed until it's less wet
3.  Use a spoon to grab make a balls with a size of golf ball…
4.  Heat up cooking oil and put the balls in while the oil still cold… and put on medium heat
5.  Cook for about 15 minutes (flip it as needed) until the color turned into golden brown… and Done!!!.. woohooo… eat it with some hot sauce or mayonaisse… kids will loves it
