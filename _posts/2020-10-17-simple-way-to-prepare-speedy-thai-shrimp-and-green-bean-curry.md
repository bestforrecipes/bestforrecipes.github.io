##### The ingredients needed to make Thai Shrimp and Green Bean Curry:

1.  Prepare 1/2 lb raw shrimp,peeled and deveined
2.  Make ready 1 cup carrot, julienne (or shredded)
3.  Get 2 cup Green beans, halved
4.  Take 2 tsp Coconut oil
5.  Take 2 garlic cloves, minced
6.  Prepare 2 1/4 tbsp thai red curry paste
7.  Get 3/4 tbsp light brown sugar (or full tbs if u still find it too spicy)
8.  Make ready 2 tsp fish sauce
9.  Get 1/2 cup water
10.  Prepare Green onions (for garnish)

##### Steps to make Thai Shrimp and Green Bean Curry:

1.  In a saucepan, heat oil and sauté garlic until fragrant. (About 2 min)
2.  Add the curry paste, fish sauce and brown sugar. Cook together for 1 min.
3.  Pour in water, stir until the curry paste has dissolved (should be sauce consistency now)
4.  Add your green beans and shredded carrots and cook 3-4 min.
5.  Add your shrimp, stir together and allow to cook for about 5 min until shrimp is pink and cooked through.
6.  Serve over brown or white rice, Garnish with sliced green onion.
