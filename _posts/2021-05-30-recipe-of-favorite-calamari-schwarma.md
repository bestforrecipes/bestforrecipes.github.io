##### The ingredients needed to make Calamari Schwarma:

1.  Prepare 10 pita breads
2.  Get 1 head lettuce, shredded
3.  Prepare 4 tomatoes, diced
4.  Get 470 ml Burger and Salad Dressing
5.  Make ready 2 kg crumbed calamari rings

##### Instructions to make Calamari Schwarma:

1.  Fry calamari in a deep fryer at 180°C for 4 minutes
2.  Lightly toast pita breads on flat grill or in nonstick pan
3.  Cut pita breads open
4.  Spread Seafood dressing in the pitta breads - - [https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/344494-burger-and-salad-dressing](https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/344494-burger-and-salad-dressing)
5.  Add lettuce and tomato
6.  Finish with calamari rings
