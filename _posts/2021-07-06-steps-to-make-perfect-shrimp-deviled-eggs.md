##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Deviled Eggs:

1.  Take small fried shrimp (no tails)
2.  Make ready hard boiled eggs
3.  Prepare mayonnaise or miracle whip
4.  Prepare mustard
5.  Make ready Seasons
6.  Make ready relish
7.  Take Paprika (for looks)

##### Instructions to make Shrimp Deviled Eggs:

1.  Cut boiled eggs in half long ways, emptying the yoke in a separate bowl. Rince off the egg white pieces then pat dry with paper towel.
2.  Add mayo or miracle whip, mustard and seasonings into yoke bowl, mixing well. Make sure there are no yoke chunks.
3.  Add relish in yoke mix. Pour yoke mix in a zipblock bag, snip corner and pipe into egg white pieces. (You can also spoon the mixture into egg white pieces)
4.  Sprinkle paprika on the eggs then place a piece of fried shrimp onto the deviled egg. Place into fridge to chill. Serve when ready to eat.
