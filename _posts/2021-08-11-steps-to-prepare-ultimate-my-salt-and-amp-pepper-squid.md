##### The ingredients needed to make My Salt & Pepper Squid:

1.  Get 1 tbls butter
2.  Take 1 tsp red chilli diced
3.  Get 1 tsp salt and pepper seasoning
4.  Get Cut about 24 strips of squid

##### Instructions to make My Salt & Pepper Squid:

1.  Ingredients
2.  Salt and pepper seasoning
3.  Melt the butter and add the chilli and fry for one minute then add the salt n pepper seasoning 3
4.  Stir then add the squid
5.  Fry for 5 - 10 minutes stirring.
6.  Add a lid for a further 5 minutes
7.  Add it to a serving dish and have it on iys own or with some other side dish
