##### The ingredients needed to make Chickpea Chuna:

1.  Make ready 1 can Chickpeas, drained
2.  Prepare 1 gherkin, finely diced (2 Tbs)
3.  Prepare 1 Tbs Spring onion
4.  Get 1 Nori sheet, finely cut
5.  Prepare 1/4 Red onion, finely diced
6.  Get 2 Tbs Vinaigrette
7.  Get TT Seasoning
8.  Make ready TT Hot sauce (optional)
