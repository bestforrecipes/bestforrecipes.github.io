##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Dijon Salmon:

1.  Take salmon
2.  Take olive oil
3.  Prepare lemon juice
4.  Prepare dijon mustard
5.  Get honey
6.  Make ready salt
7.  Prepare black pepper
8.  Take 2-3 cloves garlic (optional)

##### Steps to make Honey Dijon Salmon:

1.  Preheat oven to 190°C
2.  Lightly brush a baking tray or dish with olive oil or butter.
3.  Place salmon in the tray.
4.  Mix the other ingredients in a small bowl to make your marinade.
5.  Pour the marinade over the salmon and place in oven for about 15 minutes.
