##### The ingredients needed to make Brad's lobster roll:

1.  Take 1 (3 lb) lobster, deshell, and Chop into bite sized pieces
2.  Take 1 shallot, minced
3.  Get 2 celery stalks, cut down middle lengthwise then slice thin
4.  Make ready Juice of half a lemon
5.  Make ready 1 tsp minced garlic
6.  Get 3-4 tbs mayonnaise
7.  Make ready 1/2 tsp white pepper and old bay seasoning
8.  Get 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
9.  Prepare Garnish
10.  Prepare Belillo rolls
11.  Make ready Shredded merlot belvitano cheese
12.  Get Lemon wedge
13.  Take Creme friach

##### Steps to make Brad's lobster roll:

1.  Mix all of the lobster ingredients in a mixing bowl. Chill for 1 hour.
2.  Split the belillo roll. Toast if desired. Spread creme friach on roll. Top with lobster and shredded cheese. Squeeze lemon wedge over top. Serve immediately. Enjoy.
