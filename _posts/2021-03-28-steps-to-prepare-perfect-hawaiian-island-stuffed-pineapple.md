##### The ingredients needed to make Hawaiian Island stuffed Pineapple:

1.  Make ready 10 medium shrimp
2.  Take 3 oz honey ham
3.  Get 2 cup medium grain rice
4.  Prepare 1 pineapple
5.  Take 1 can coconut milk
6.  Prepare 1 tsp paprika
7.  Make ready 1 tsp Lemon pepper seasoning
8.  Take 1 tsp salt
9.  Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
10.  Get 1/4 cup strawberries
11.  Make ready 1/4 cup pineapples

##### Instructions to make Hawaiian Island stuffed Pineapple:

1.  Mix peeled shrimp, oil and all seasoning in bowl. Place in skillet and cook til done.
2.  Slice pineapple in half vertically. ..then gut the inside. Place chunks to the side.
3.  Cook the rice while substituting coconut milk for 1 cup of water.
4.  Place ham in bottom of pineapple. Take one scoop of cooked rice place in pineapple. Place strawberries and pineapples inside. Place cooked shrimp in pineapple then cover with ham.
5.  Place in oven 375 for 10 min.
