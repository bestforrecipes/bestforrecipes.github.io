##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Salmon Bake:

1.  Take 1 salmon fillet (wild caught)
2.  Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
3.  Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
4.  Get 1 tsp mustard

##### Instructions to make Easy Salmon Bake:

1.  Preheat oven to 375°F.
2.  If frozen, thaw out the salmon fillet by placing it in the refrigerator the day before. Or, use the defrost option on the microwave, which takes about 15 minutes.
3.  In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, mustard, and garlic with a basting brush.
4.  On a greased cookie sheet, place the thawed out or fresh salmon.
5.  Spread the bowl mixture onto the salmon using the basting brush.
6.  Place salmon in oven for 30 minutes or until the fillet reaches an internal temperature of 145°F. That is if you want the salmon well done.
