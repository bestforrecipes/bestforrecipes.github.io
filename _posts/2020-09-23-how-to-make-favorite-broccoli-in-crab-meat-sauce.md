1.  Get 1 head crab
2.  Take 1 tbsp corn flour
3.  Prepare 1 head broccoli
4.  Get 2 clove garlic
5.  Get 1/2 cup chicken stock
6.  Prepare 1 tsp soya sauce
7.  Make ready 1 dash salt

##### Steps to make Broccoli in crab meat sauce:

4.  when crab is cooked remove the meat n put in bowl

8.  add crab meat to stock
9.  stir n add seasoning then pour on broccoli
