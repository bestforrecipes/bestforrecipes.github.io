##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Mentai Rice:

1.  Make ready 260 g salmon skin-off
2.  Prepare 1 cup cooked rice
3.  Get Seaweed
4.  Prepare 2 tbs soy sauce
5.  Prepare 1 tsp sesame oil
6.  Take Mentai Sauce
7.  Make ready 5 tbs kewpie mayonnaise
8.  Take 2.5 tbs sweet chilli sauce
9.  Prepare 2 tbs tobiko

##### Steps to make Salmon Mentai Rice:

1.  Preheat oven to 180°
2.  Marinate salmon in soy sauce and sesame oil for 10 mins and pan fry
3.  Mix mayonnaise, chilli sauce, and tobiko together and set aside
4.  Mix cooked rice with seaweed in pyrex
5.  Place salmon on top of rice and pour mentai sauce over it
6.  Add seaweed on too of the sauce
7.  Bake rice in 180° oven for 10-18 mins
