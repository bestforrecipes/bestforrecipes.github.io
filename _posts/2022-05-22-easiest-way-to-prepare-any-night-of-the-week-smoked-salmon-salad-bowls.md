##### The ingredients needed to make Smoked Salmon Salad Bowls:

1.  Prepare 2 potatoes cut into small cubes
2.  Take 1 tbsp olive oil
3.  Take 1 garlic clove minced
4.  Prepare 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
5.  Take 100 g smoked salmon
6.  Get 1 avocado diced
7.  Prepare 10 cherry tomatoes halved
8.  Prepare 1/2 small cucumber
9.  Get 1 tbsp lemon juice
10.  Take 2 eggs
11.  Get 2 handfuls rocket or spinach
12.  Prepare salt and pepper for seasoning

##### Steps to make Smoked Salmon Salad Bowls:

1.  Preheat the oven to 200 oC - Mix together the potatoes, olive oil, garlic and paprika - Bake in the oven until crisp, about 20 minutes
2.  Boil the eggs for about 5 minutes, peel and cut in half - Toss the rocket/spinach and cucumber in the lemon juice and divide between two bowls
3.  Top with the crispy potatoes, smoked salmon, avocado, cherry tomatoes and egg - Season to taste
