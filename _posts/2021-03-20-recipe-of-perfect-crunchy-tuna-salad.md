##### The ingredients needed to make Crunchy Tuna Salad:

1.  Make ready 1 can tuna fish
2.  Get 1/2 cup miracle whip
3.  Make ready 1 tablespoon pickle relish
4.  Prepare 1 teaspoon Mustard
5.  Get 1 small green onion
6.  Make ready 1 small yellow onion
7.  Take 6-8 green olives
8.  Prepare 1 small celery rib
9.  Get 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
10.  Make ready 1 teaspoon celery seed
11.  Take 1 teaspoon salt
12.  Get 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
13.  Get 1/2 teaspoon pepper
