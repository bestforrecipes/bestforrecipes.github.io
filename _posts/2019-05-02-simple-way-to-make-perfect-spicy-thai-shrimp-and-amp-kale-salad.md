##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Thai Shrimp & Kale Salad:

1.  Take 1 bunch Kale
2.  Take 1 cup Snow peas
3.  Prepare 1 cup Fresh corn
4.  Get 3 Shredded carrots
5.  Get 3 tbsp Creamy peanut butter
6.  Take 1 1/2 tsp Siracha
7.  Prepare 4 tbsp Coconut milk
8.  Get 1/2 tsp Sesame oil
9.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Olive oil
10.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Apple cider vinegar
11.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp Maple syrup
12.  Prepare 1/2 tsp Grated ginger
13.  Prepare 1 pinch Salt
14.  Get 8 Shrimp
15.  Get 2 clove Garlic
16.  Prepare 1 tbsp Chopped fresh basil
17.  Prepare 2 Limes

##### Instructions to make Spicy Thai Shrimp & Kale Salad:

1.  Mix kale, corn, snow peas & carrots
2.  Whisk ingredients 5-13. Toss with veggies.
3.  Mix shrimp with garlic, sesame oil, lime juice, siracha, basil, salt, pepper, & sesame seeds
4.  Sautee shrimp in sesame oil 1-2 minutes each side on med-high heat.
5.  Top salad with shrimp and squeeze fresh lime.
