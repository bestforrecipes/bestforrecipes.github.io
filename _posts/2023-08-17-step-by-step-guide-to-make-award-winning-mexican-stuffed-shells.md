##### The ingredients needed to make Mexican Stuffed Shells:

1.  Take 10 Shells
2.  Take 1 lb Ground Beef (I used turkey)
3.  Prepare 1 cup Salsa
4.  Take 1 can Enchilada Sauce (10 oz)
5.  Make ready 1 envelope Taco Seasoning
6.  Get 1 cup Cheddar Cheese
7.  Get 1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese
8.  Make ready 1/2 Onion
9.  Make ready 2 oz Cream Cheese

##### Steps to make Mexican Stuffed Shells:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°F.
2.  Boil shells until al dente.
3.  Brown ground beef and onions.
4.  Add taco seasoning when it is browned.
5.  In a separate bowl mix together enchilada sauce and salsa.
6.  Add cream cheese to the ground beef.
7.  Put salsa mixture at the bottom of a baking dish.
8.  Assemble shells.
9.  Put the rest of the salsa mixture over top.
10.  Bake in oven for 30 mins.
11.  Sprinkle with cheeses then put back in the oven uncovered for 15 minutes.
12.  Serve and enjoy!
