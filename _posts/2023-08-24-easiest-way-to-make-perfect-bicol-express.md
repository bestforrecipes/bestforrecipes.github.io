##### The ingredients needed to make Bicol Express:

1.  Make ready 1 lb pork, cut into small pieces
2.  Make ready Garlic, minced
3.  Prepare Onions, sliced
4.  Make ready 2 roma tomatoes, sliced
5.  Take 1 cup coconut milk
6.  Make ready Red chili, sliced
7.  Take Green chili, sliced
8.  Get 2 tbsps shrimp paste
9.  Prepare Ground black pepper

##### Instructions to make Bicol Express:

1.  In a pan, fry pork until golden brown, remove from pan. Use the remaining oil to saute garlic and onions
2.  Add tomatoes, cook until tomatoes softened.
3.  Add the fried pork, combine well
4.  Add coconut milk, stir and cook until the pork absorbs the coconut milk
5.  Add red chili, shrimp paste and sprinkle some ground black pepper, mix well
6.  Add green chili and simmer for 2 minutes.
7.  Serve
