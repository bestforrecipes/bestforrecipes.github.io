##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Croquette:

1.  Take 2 cans tuna in water, drained
2.  Make ready 1 shallot, minced
3.  Get 2 garlic cloves, minced
4.  Prepare 1 egg
5.  Get 1 tbsp spicy mustard
6.  Take 1 tbsp mayo
7.  Make ready juice of one lemon
8.  Get squirt of sriarcha sauce
9.  Prepare 1 cup breadcrumbs
10.  Take dash basil and seasoning salt
11.  Take oil for frying

##### Steps to make Tuna Croquette:

1.  Mix all ingredients except oil in a bowl.
2.  In a large skillet heat oil. About 1/2 inch of oil. Medium high heat
3.  Form 4 patties. Pat each side with a bit more breadcrumbs to help hold together
4.  Fry in skillet, about 5 mins per side.
5.  Drain on paper towel before serving.
