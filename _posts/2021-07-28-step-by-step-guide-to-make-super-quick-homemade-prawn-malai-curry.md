##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn malai curry:

1.  Get 400 gm prawn
2.  Make ready 1 cup desiccated coconut
3.  Prepare 1 cup coconut milk
4.  Make ready 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
5.  Prepare 2 onions chopped and grinded
6.  Get 1 tsp red chilli Powder
7.  Prepare 1 tsp garam masala powder
8.  Make ready 1 tsp turmeric powder
9.  Take 1 tsp cumin seed
10.  Make ready 1 bay leaf
11.  Make ready 1 handful whole garam masalas
12.  Prepare To taste salt and sugar
13.  Take As needed oil and ghee

##### Steps to make Prawn malai curry:

1.  Marinate the prawn with salt and turmeric powder
2.  Heat oil and temper cumin seed bay leaf and whole garam masalas
3.  Add prawns shallow fry and add onion paste
4.  Now add ginger garlic paste and saute well
5.  Add salt, turmeric powder and red chilli Powder and mix well
6.  Keep the heat low and let it soft
7.  Add coconut and coconut milk and mix
8.  Finally add sugar and garam masala powder,sprinkle ghee and serve
