##### The ingredients needed to make Healthy Tasty Fried Rice:

1.  Prepare 1 tsp oil
2.  Get 1 egg
3.  Take 6 prawns
4.  Get 4 mussels
5.  Get 1 1/4 bowl rice

##### Steps to make Healthy Tasty Fried Rice:

1.  Prepare all the ingredients and add the oil and the chicken to the pan, add the rice accordingly
2.  Add the mussels and prawns to the rice and add the egg finally
3.  Mixed them well and do not over cooked after adding the egg. Mixed the egg with the rice quickly and all done! Very easy. Enjoy!
