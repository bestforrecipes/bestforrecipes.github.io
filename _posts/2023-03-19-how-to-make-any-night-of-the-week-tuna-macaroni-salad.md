##### The ingredients needed to make tuna macaroni salad:

1.  Get 2 cup elbow noodles
2.  Get 2 hard boiled eggs (chopped)
3.  Take 2 tbsp chopped celery
4.  Get 2 tbsp chopped green peppers
5.  Take 2 chopped scallions
6.  Prepare 6 slice of cucumber chopped
7.  Take 1 tbsp fresh chopped parsley
8.  Make ready 1 can flaked tuna in water drained
9.  Take 1 tbsp shredded carrot
10.  Make ready 1/2 tsp garlic powder
11.  Prepare 1/4 cup ranch dressing
12.  Make ready 1/2 tsp onion powder
13.  Take 3/4 cup hellmans mayo
14.  Make ready 1/2 tsp vinegar

16.  Get 1/4 tsp lawrys season salt

##### Instructions to make tuna macaroni salad:

1.  Boil noodles in salted water with oil and also boil the eggs and put aside
2.  Chop up the eggs..celery..green pepper..scallion..cucumber..parsley and add the shredded carrots and tuna along with the noodles and mix all together
3.  Add the rest of the ingredients and mix together …chill for a bit and serve !
