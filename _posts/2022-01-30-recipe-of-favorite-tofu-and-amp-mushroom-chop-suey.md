##### The ingredients needed to make Tofu & Mushroom Chop Suey:

1.  Get 1 can button mushroom sliced
2.  Get 3 blocks tofu cut into cubes
3.  Make ready Carrot
4.  Take Green beans
5.  Make ready 1 tsp salt
6.  Get 1 tsp sugar
7.  Make ready 1 tsp black ground pepper
8.  Prepare 1/4 cup oyster sauce
9.  Take 1 tablespoon cornstarch
10.  Make ready 2 tablespoon soy sauce
11.  Take 2 cloves garlic chopped
12.  Get 1 medium onion chopped
13.  Prepare 1/4 cup water

##### Steps to make Tofu & Mushroom Chop Suey:

1.  Sauté garlic, onions together with carrots and string beans for 2-3 minutes in medium high fire (it makes veggies crunchy in my experience)
2.  Add the mushroom and tofu stir gently then add the salt, pepper, sugar, soy sauce and oyster sauce.
3.  In a bowl mix the cornstarch with water and then pour it in the veggies. Let it simmer until the sauce thickens
