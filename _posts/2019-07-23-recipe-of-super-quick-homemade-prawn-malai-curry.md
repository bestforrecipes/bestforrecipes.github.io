##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn Malai Curry:

1.  Prepare 500 gram Prawns
2.  Get 200 gram coconut milk
3.  Take 50 gram coconut paste
4.  Get 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
5.  Get 1 onion cut into thin slices
6.  Take 1-2 bay leaves
7.  Prepare 8-10 cloves Garlic cut into thin slices
8.  Prepare 1 tablespoon Ginger
9.  Prepare 1 teaspoon chilli powder
10.  Prepare 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder
11.  Prepare 2-3 green chillies
12.  Get To taste salt
13.  Prepare To taste sugar
14.  Get As needed oil

##### Instructions to make Prawn Malai Curry:

1.  Wash and clean the prawns. Add turmeric powder and salt and keep it aside
2.  Heat a pan and add some oil into it. When hot add the prawns and fry it lightly and remove
3.  Into the same oil add bay leaf, sliced onion, garlic, green chillies and saute till light brown
4.  Add ginger paste, coconut pate, turmeric powder, salt and mix well. Let it cook for 2 mins and then add the prawns and saute again for 2-3 mins
5.  Add water and let it cook on low flame for 5-7 mins. Once prawn is well cooked and soft, add the coconut milk and some sugar
6.  Add garam masala and remove from pan. Prawn malai curry is ready to be served with rice
