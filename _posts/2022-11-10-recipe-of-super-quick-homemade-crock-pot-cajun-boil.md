##### The ingredients needed to make Crock-Pot Cajun Boil:

1.  Get 4 red potatoes (halved)
2.  Get 1 med. onion (quartered)
3.  Get 1 lemon
4.  Get 1 stalk celery (cut in thirds)
5.  Prepare 3-4 tbs Old Bay seasoning
6.  Make ready 1/2-1 Lb Keilbasa (1 in rounds)
7.  Get 3-4 frozen corn on the cob
8.  Make ready 1 lb shrimp in shell
9.  Prepare water

##### Steps to make Crock-Pot Cajun Boil:

1.  Add potatoes, onion, celery, and seasoning to Crock-Pot. Squeeze lemon juice all over, add lemon halves. Top with water. High: 3 hours.
2.  Add Keilbasa and corn. High: another 2-3 hours. (Til potatoes are tender)
3.  Add shrimp. Cook til shrimp are pink and done. About 10-15 mins.
4.  Drain off water & serve.
