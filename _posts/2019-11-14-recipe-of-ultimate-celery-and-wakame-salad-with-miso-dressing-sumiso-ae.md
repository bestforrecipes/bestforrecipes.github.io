2.  Prepare 9 g Wakame seaweed (dried)
3.  Make ready 6 sticks Imitation crab meat (surimi)
4.  Take 3 tsp Miso
5.  Make ready 1.5 tsp sugar
6.  Take 2 tsp rice vinegar
7.  Take 1 tsp sesame paste (if any)
