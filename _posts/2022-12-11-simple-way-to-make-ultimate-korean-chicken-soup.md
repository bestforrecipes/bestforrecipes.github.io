2.  Get Spring onion
3.  Get 2 pieces ginger
4.  Prepare 2 pieces garlic
5.  Prepare kelp, dried mushroom, some leftover vegetables
6.  Take 1-2 packs Korean Ginseng Herb Tea
7.  Get 1 chicken leg
8.  Take 2 dates
9.  Prepare 2 tablespoon sticky rice

##### Instructions to make Korean Chicken Soup:
