##### The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Beef Alfredo Casserole:

1.  Prepare 2 lb ground beef
2.  Make ready 1 can whole kernel corn
3.  Get 1 medium shell noodles
4.  Take 3 cup shredded cheese
5.  Make ready 1 small container of French's Fried Onions
6.  Prepare 2 jars of Alfredo sauce
7.  Prepare 1 McCormick's Smokehouse Maple Seasoning

##### Instructions to make Cheesy Beef Alfredo Casserole:

1.  Preheat oven to 375°F. Brown the ground beef and season with the seasoning. Drain the meat and set aside.
2.  Cook noodles according to package. Drain and set aside.
3.  In a large bowl combine all ingredients except for fried onions and 1 1/2 cups of cheese. Mix together well.
4.  Pour into a 9X13 pan. Top with fried onions and the cheese. Bake for 10 minutes or until bubbly.
