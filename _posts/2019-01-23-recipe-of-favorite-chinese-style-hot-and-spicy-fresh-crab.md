##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese Style Hot and Spicy Fresh Crab:

1.  Prepare 2 Fresh Crabs
2.  Take 1 Tablespoon sugar
3.  Prepare 1 bowl of warm water
4.  Get 7 cloves garlic
5.  Make ready 6 pieces ginger
6.  Make ready 2 tablespoons seasoned soy sauce for seafood
7.  Prepare 2 tablespoons light soy sauce
8.  Get 3 tablespoons Chilli Bean Sauce (Toban Djan)
9.  Prepare 6 Finger chillis
10.  Get 1 tablespoon corn starch
11.  Make ready 1 bunch spring onion

##### Instructions to make Chinese Style Hot and Spicy Fresh Crab:

1.  Wash the crabs under cold water
2.  Put the crab upside down on a chopping board and cut in half
3.  Chop up spring onion, chillis, garlic and prepare other ingredients
4.  Fry chillis, chilli sauce, ginger, garlic and spring onion in oil until you can smell the fragrance
5.  Put a tablespoon of potato starch and mix with the warm water. Mix the soy sauce and sugar into the water. Mix well. Stand to the side
6.  Add the crabs to the wok with the chilli mixture and fry together for one minute
7.  Add the bowl of water to the wok. The water should be just up to the top of the crabs
8.  Simmer for 10 minutes with a lid on the wok
9.  Add salt if you need it
10.  Serve in a bowl and enjoy
