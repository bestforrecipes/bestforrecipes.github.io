##### The ingredients needed to make Spiced monkfish:

1.  Take 1 monkfish tail fillet per person (scale up the other ingredients if cooking for more than 2)
2.  Get 1 tsp Korean chili powder
3.  Make ready 1/2 tsp turmeric (or 2 tsp curry powder instead of the two spices)
4.  Make ready 1 tsp paprika
5.  Make ready 1/2 tsp fine salt
6.  Take 1 shallot, finely diced
7.  Get 2 tbsp. butter
8.  Make ready 1 pinch saffron
9.  Make ready about 50ml veg stock made with a stock cube or fresh
10.  Take 2 tbsp. crème fraiche
11.  Take 1 little chopped parsley
