##### The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Alfredo Chicken Shells:

1.  Take 2-3 Cups Shredded Chicken
2.  Get 1 Cup shredded Parmesan Cheese
3.  Prepare 1 Head Broccoli Chopped
4.  Get Sliced Portobello Mushrooms
5.  Get 1 Box Large Shells
6.  Take Alfredo Sauce (I use Bertolli)

##### Steps to make Stuffed Alfredo Chicken Shells:

1.  Make large shells as directed on box
2.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees
3.  Mix chicken, broccoli, mushrooms, 1/2 cup of parmesan, and 3/4 of Alfredo sauce
4.  Put some of the Alfredo Sauce at the bottom of a large baking dish
5.  Fill cooked shells with chicken mixture and place shells into baking dish
6.  Top shells with leftover 1/2 cup parmesan
7.  Bake in oven at 375 degrees for 30 minutes
