##### The ingredients needed to make Fish Burger:

1.  Make ready fish burger
2.  Make ready 1 dash salt and pepper
3.  Get 1 tbsp each lemon juice , sweet cili sauce, wasabi and dill
4.  Make ready 3 tbsp fine chop cilantro and pickle ginger ( optional )
5.  Prepare 1/2 egg optional
6.  Get 1 tsp dried Italian herbs (optional )
7.  Make ready 1 cup cook tuna or mackerel fish or any fish
8.  Take peanut butter and cili sauce
9.  Make ready 1 tbsp mix fine dice garlic and shallot
10.  Make ready 2 tbsp thai sweet chili and creamy peanut butter
11.  Take garnish
12.  Prepare 1 tsp peanut chunk and fresh onion ring

##### Steps to make Fish Burger:

1.  FISH OPTIONAL
2.  baked mckeral fish 200 C for 20 minute then flake it or used tuna in brine but drain it
3.  SHAPE INTO SMALL BURGER
4.  mix fish burger ingredient and shape into burger and pan grill it or broil it till lightly brown then turn over gently and reshape it again
5.  DRESSING
6.  mix chili and peanut butter with garlic and shallot and serve on fish burger
