##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp and Veggies Pancake / Haemul Pajeon:

1.  Make ready 1 medium cucumber cut into small strips
2.  Make ready 1 medium carrot cut into small strips
3.  Make ready 1/2 cup milk
4.  Take to taste Pepper and salt
5.  Take Spanish Paprika
6.  Prepare 1 onion chopped
7.  Make ready 1 large egg
8.  Get Shrimp
9.  Make ready 1/4 cup flour for binding

##### Steps to make Shrimp and Veggies Pancake / Haemul Pajeon:

1.  Cut the cucumber and carrots into small strips
2.  In a bow mix the egg, milk, shrimp, salt and pepper, carrots, cucumber flour, onions and paprika.
3.  Form each into a patty like shape and fry in vegetable oil for 3 minutes for Both sides. Serve with hot rice!
