##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Tuna pasta:

1.  Get 1 kg spaghetti noodle
2.  Take 2 medium onions- chopped
3.  Prepare 2 can tuna flakes
4.  Prepare 2 clove garlic, chopped
5.  Make ready 1 can small mushroom sliced
6.  Prepare dash salt and pepper
7.  Make ready dash italian herbs- mccormick
8.  Take 2 packages 500ml italian sauce
9.  Prepare 1 piece parsley

##### Instructions to make Easy Tuna pasta:

1.  Follow pack of noodles until draining
2.  Open cans of tuna, and use as oil for sauteeing onions, garlic, sliced mushroom and tuna flakes
3.  Add italian sauce and let it simmer until its bubbly
4.  Put the drained noodles and stir consistently in low fire until boiling
5.  Put off fire and let it cool, serve with your parsley. Enjoy this simple tuna pasta :)
