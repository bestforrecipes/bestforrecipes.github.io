2.  Take 5 oz canned tuna in spring water
3.  Take 1/4 cup pickled jalapeños plus juices
4.  Take 4 tbsp mayonnaise
5.  Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp chopped chives
6.  Prepare 1 tbsp lemon juice
7.  Prepare salt
8.  Prepare 1/4 tsp granulated garlic powder
