2.  Prepare 2 medium onions chopped
3.  Take 4 cloves garlic chopped

5.  Make ready Okra tips and points cuts

7.  Take 1 eggplant sliced into half moon shaped
8.  Take Ampalaya or bitter gourd seeds spooned out and sliced
9.  Prepare Shrimp paste
10.  Make ready 2 finger green chilies
11.  Make ready 4 tomatoes chopped
