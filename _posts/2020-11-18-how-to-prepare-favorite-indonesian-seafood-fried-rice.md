##### The ingredients needed to make Indonesian Seafood Fried Rice:

1.  Make ready 100 grams white rice
2.  Prepare 3 onion
3.  Get 8 red hot chilli
4.  Make ready 30 grams brocolli
5.  Get 3 calamari squid
6.  Make ready 3 meatball fish
7.  Make ready 10 grams crab meat
8.  Prepare 1 salt
9.  Prepare 1 soy sauce
10.  Take 1 margarine

##### Steps to make Indonesian Seafood Fried Rice:

1.  chop onion, chilli, squid, meatball and brocolli
2.  melt butter in a sucepan
3.  put onion,chilli,squid,meatball,crab meat, and brocolli in saucepan. waitl 2 or 3 minutes, dont forget to stir
4.  put rice in the saucepan, stir vigorously till everything mix together
5.  add salt, soy sauce, pepper to seasoning.
6.  stir vigorously
7.  fried rice done, and serve it
