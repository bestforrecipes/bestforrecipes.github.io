##### The ingredients needed to make simple homemade vegetable soup:

1.  Prepare 1/4 kg chayote
2.  Take 1/2 slice of big cabbage
3.  Take 1 piece cauliflower
4.  Take 1 piece carrot(sliced)
5.  Get 1 cup chicharo peas(or 1/4 kilo)
6.  Take 1/4 kg cloud ear mushroom(bok nee)
7.  Make ready 1/2 kg quail eggs(20-30 pieces boiled & peeled shell)
8.  Take 1/4 cup corn starch mixture(3 tbsp cornstarch mixed in 1/4 cup water)
9.  Make ready 1 piece egg
10.  Prepare 1/2 clove garlic minced
11.  Take 1/2 clove onion minced
12.  Take salt
13.  Make ready 1/4 kg shrimp cleaned and peeled of from its shell
14.  Prepare 10 piece tempura
15.  Take 1 piece tomato minced
16.  Prepare 1 tbsp oyster sauce (to taste)
17.  Get 1 liter chicken stock

##### Steps to make simple homemade vegetable soup:
