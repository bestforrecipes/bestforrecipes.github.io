##### The ingredients needed to make Broiled Lobster:

1.  Take 2 whole lobsters
2.  Take Mozzarella
3.  Make ready Oregano
4.  Take Paprika
5.  Get Complete seasoning
6.  Prepare Salt
7.  Get Pepper
8.  Prepare 2 scoop Butter
9.  Take Garlic
10.  Get 2 limes

##### Instructions to make Broiled Lobster:

1.  Cut Lobster’s in half length wise by using a sharp knife place between eyes and hit top of knife with a meat tenderizer or hammer to split
2.  Wash lobsters thoroughly discarding fat and digestive tract, Trim lobsters with kitchen scissors to make presentable
3.  Sprinkle seasoning on lobsters cut limes and squeeze juice on lobsters
4.  Smash 3 pieces of garlic and throw them in a sauce pan with butter and let simmer to absorb garlic, set aside.
5.  Preheat oven to 350. Drizzle olive oil on large oven pan or cookie sheet and place lobsters meat side up and bake for 25 min, baste with garlic butter twice before pulling out of oven and again after. Top with mozzarella. Enjoy with steamed veggies or simply by itself
