##### The ingredients needed to make Quick shrimp scampi:

1.  Make ready 1 lb shrimp
2.  Take pasta, angel hair
3.  Take 3 clove garlic, minced
4.  Take 2 tbsp oil
5.  Get pepper flakes
6.  Take 1 1/2 cup tomatoes, deseed chopped
7.  Make ready 2 tbsp butter
8.  Get 1 cup white wine
9.  Make ready 1 large lemon juice
10.  Take 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
11.  Make ready basil

##### Instructions to make Quick shrimp scampi:
