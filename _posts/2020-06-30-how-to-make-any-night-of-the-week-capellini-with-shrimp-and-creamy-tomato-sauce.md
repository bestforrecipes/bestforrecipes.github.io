##### The ingredients needed to make Capellini with Shrimp and Creamy Tomato Sauce:

1.  Get 3 tbsp olive oil
2.  Make ready 1 lb peeled large shrimp
3.  Make ready 3 clove garlic, forced through a garlic press
4.  Prepare 1/4 tsp dried oregano
5.  Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
6.  Take 1/4 tsp pepper
7.  Take 1/2 cup sweet (red) vermouth
8.  Get 15 oz canned diced tomatoes, drained
9.  Prepare 3/4 cup heavy cream
10.  Get 1/2 tsp fresh lemon juice
11.  Take 1/2 lb capellini

##### Steps to make Capellini with Shrimp and Creamy Tomato Sauce:

1.  Cook capellini in a pasta pot of boiling salted water.
2.  Heat oil in a 12" heavy skillet over medium-high heat until it shimmers.
3.  Cook shrimp and garlic with oregano, salt, and pepper, turning once until golden, about 2 minutes total.
4.  Stir in vermouth and tomatoes, scraping up any brown bits from bottom of skillet.
5.  Add cream and briskly simmer until sauce has thickened sightly, about 1 minute.
6.  Stir in lemon juice.
7.  Serve hot and enjoy!
