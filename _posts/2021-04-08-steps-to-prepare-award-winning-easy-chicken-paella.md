##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Chicken Paella:

1.  Take paella
2.  Prepare salt and pepper
3.  Take olive oil
4.  Take boneless chicken breasts
5.  Prepare plain flour ( for dusting )
6.  Get paella rice (can use risotto rice)
7.  Get pancetta (or smokey bacon)
8.  Take chorizo
9.  Make ready onion - finely chopped
10.  Get garlic cloves chopped finely
11.  Prepare hot chicken stock
12.  Make ready smoked paprika
13.  Get pinches of saffron
14.  Prepare handfulls of fresh or frozen peas
15.  Take king prawns (optional, can use any seafood)

##### Instructions to make Easy Chicken Paella:

1.  preheat oven to 190c or gas mark 5
2.  season chicken breasts, lightly dust with flour
3.  heat a large wok or paella pan with olive oil
4.  brown the chicken breats in the pan
5.  once all chicken breasts have been browned,place the chicken on a baking tray and place in the centre of the oven for 30 mins
6.  whilst chicken is cooking in the oven, fry the chorizo and panchetta in the wok or pan until brown and crispy.
7.  then add the garlic and onion, cook until soft
8.  in the meantime, infuse half the stock with the saffron.
9.  then add smoked paprika, the rice and the infused chicken stock to the pan. leave to cook for 20 mins on a medium heat. stir from time to time
10.  after 20 minutes the rice should be nearly cooked. add the rest of the stock, along with peas, prawns and any seafood you are adding.
11.  cook for a further 10 minutes.
12.  take the chicken out of the oven, cut in to large chunks.
13.  add the chicken to the pan and cook for another 5 minutes.
14.  check the rice is fully cooked before serving, enjoy!
