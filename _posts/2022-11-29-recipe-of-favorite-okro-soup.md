##### Steps to make Okro soup:

1.  Bleach palm oil till it's clear
2.  Add your grounded Ata rodo, allow to fry
3.  Add your pomo, meat and crab allow to boil together and add in your meat stock
4.  Allow to boil together and add seasoning if needed
5.  Add your grated okro
6.  Allow to boil, add you fish and cray fish…. Allow to simmer.
7.  Serve with eba or semo or pounced yam.
