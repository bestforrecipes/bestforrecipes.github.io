##### The ingredients needed to make My Favorite Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Take egg noodles
2.  Make ready tuna in water
3.  Prepare cream of mushroom soup
4.  Take mayonnaise
5.  Make ready chopped celery and onion (mixed)
6.  Prepare garlic chopped
7.  Prepare grated parmesan cheese
8.  Get shredded Muenster cheese
9.  Take Ritz crackers
10.  Take butter
11.  Take whole milk
12.  Prepare of salt and pepper to taste, I added a dash of celery salt too

##### Instructions to make My Favorite Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Boil water and cook noodles as directed, drain about 2 minutes before they're finished cooking. They'll cook the rest of the way in the oven. No one wants soggy noodles.. :)
2.  Preheat oven to 375°F.
3.  In a small skillet, melt butter, add onion, celery and garlic. Cook about 7 minutes.
4.  Once noodles are done, in a greased casserole dish add all ingredients and mix well. Make sure your tuna is drained. Leave Ritz crackers and a handful of cheese out of the mixture, once all mixed, top with rest of the cheese and crushed crackers.
5.  Cook at 375 for 30 minutes.
