##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Pad Thai:

1.  Get big rice noodles
2.  Make ready Some bean sprouts (soya)
3.  Prepare fresh shrimps
4.  Take eggs
5.  Make ready tofu
6.  Take small red onion
7.  Get garlic
8.  Get onion
9.  Get fish sauce
10.  Make ready oil
11.  Make ready Chili powder (optional)
12.  Prepare lime
13.  Take Some pounded peanuts

##### Steps to make Simple Pad Thai:

1.  Clean the shrimps and set aside
2.  Heat the pan, add oil then add chopped garlic and onion. Once they smell good, add tofu, shrimps and eggs. Scramble them together.
3.  Add noodles, stir nicely. Add fish sauce, mix well. Add bean sprouts and spring onions, mix nicely. Taste it before serving it. You can serve with slice of lime, pounded peanuts and chili powder.
