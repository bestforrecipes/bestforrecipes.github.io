##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Potato salad:

1.  Take 2 potato
2.  Make ready 70 g canned tuna
3.  Get 1/2 onion
4.  Make ready 2 tbsp mayonnaise
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp soy sauce
6.  Get 1 tbsp vinegar
7.  Prepare pinch black pepper
