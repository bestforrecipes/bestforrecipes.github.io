##### The ingredients needed to make Uzbekistan Potato Salad Ball:

1.  Make ready 2 small potato (1 large potato)
2.  Make ready 1 carrot(small)
3.  Prepare 2 boiled egg
4.  Prepare 80 g cheese
5.  Take 30 g green spring onion
6.  Prepare 100 g smoked salmon
7.  Take 1.5 teaspoon soy sauce
8.  Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
9.  Get 4 tablespoon yogurt (Greece yogurt)
10.  Take Sesame

##### Steps to make Uzbekistan Potato Salad Ball:

1.  Boil or steam potatoes and carrot
2.  Shred potato with shredder (large)
3.  Shred egg, carrot and cheese with shredder (small)
4.  Chop green onion and salmon finely and mix all together with seasoning
5.  Make small balls
6.  Put sesame on balls
