##### The ingredients needed to make Mofongo con Sopa de Mariscos:

1.  Make ready soup
2.  Make ready 1 lb shrimp
3.  Take 1 lb crab meat
4.  Take 1 lb lobster meat
5.  Take 1 lb scallions
6.  Prepare 3 small pottatoes
7.  Take 1 pints chicken broth
8.  Prepare 1 pints watter
9.  Make ready 4 tbsp sofrito Goya
10.  Take 1 tsp salt
11.  Prepare 1 tbsp minced onnion
12.  Make ready 1 tbsp minced garlic
13.  Prepare 1 tsp crushed pepper
14.  Prepare 1/2 cup tomato paste
15.  Prepare 1 tbsp corn starch
16.  Take mofongo
17.  Take 7 large green planters
18.  Make ready 4 tbsp bacon bits
19.  Take 2 tbsp minced garlic
20.  Take 1/4 cup olive oil
21.  Take 1 gallon canola oil

##### Steps to make Mofongo con Sopa de Mariscos:

1.  cook soup
2.  dice potatoes in small cubes
3.  add all ingredients (except corn starch)in a large pot
4.  bring to a boil
5.  add corn starch to thicken broth
6.  cook mofongo
7.  peel and cut plantains in 1 inch pieces
8.  deep fry plantains till golden brown
9.  mash plantains while adding olive oil to keep moist
10.  add bacon bits and minced garlic
11.  form mashed plantains in small balls
12.  serve meal!!!!
13.  place plantain balls inside bowl and cover with soup
