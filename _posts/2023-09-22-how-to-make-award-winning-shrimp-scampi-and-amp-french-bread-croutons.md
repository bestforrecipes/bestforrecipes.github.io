##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Scampi & French bread croutons:

1.  Prepare Croutons:
2.  Take French bread cut into one inch cubes
3.  Take 2 tbsp butter
4.  Take to taste parsley
5.  Prepare to taste Italian blend seasoning
6.  Make ready to taste Tony Chachere's seasoning
7.  Get Scampi
8.  Make ready 2 lbs shrimp, best from the Gulf of Mexico
9.  Take half stick butter
10.  Get to taste garlic powder
11.  Get to taste parsley
12.  Get to taste celery seed
13.  Take to taste Italian blend seasoning
14.  Make ready to taste Tony Chachere's seasoning
15.  Get cornstarch slurry
16.  Take Garnish:
17.  Make ready parsley
18.  Make ready fresh grated parmesan cheese

##### Steps to make Shrimp Scampi & French bread croutons:

1.  For croutons, melt butter and add seasonings into a non stick skillet, toss French bread cubes in and saute until croutons absorb butter and toast lightly, set aside
2.  In a saute pan, melt butter and seasonings, add shrimp and cook until shrimp turn pink, between 5-10mins, add cornstarch slurry to tighten liquid, do not overcook, shrimp will turn rubbery
3.  Place croutons on individual plates, pile shrimp on top, garnish with parmesan and parsley
4.  Enjoy
