##### The ingredients needed to make Bake Garlic shrimps:

1.  Get 1 lb shrimps
2.  Take ginger
3.  Make ready green onion
4.  Take 6 clove garlic
5.  Prepare 1/2 stick butter
6.  Prepare soy sauce
7.  Get rice wine

##### Steps to make Bake Garlic shrimps:

1.  Clean shrimps, cut it open on the top with shell off in the middle part of the shrimps
2.  Cut garlic, ginger, and green onion in dice size, then mix it well with butter
3.  Per heat the oven with 350°F, then place step 2. On top of the shrimp(line up the shrimp head to tail on the plate) then put in the oven for 10 to 15 minutes
