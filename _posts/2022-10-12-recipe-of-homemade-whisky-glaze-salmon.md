##### The ingredients needed to make Whisky Glaze Salmon:

1.  Take 4 salmon head or salmon filllet optional
2.  Get whisky glaze sauce
3.  Take 1 tbsp sea salt
4.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp black pepper
5.  Prepare 3/4 cup whisky
6.  Make ready 2/3 cup brown sugar
7.  Prepare 1 clove mince garlic
8.  Get 1 tbsp honey
9.  Prepare 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
10.  Get 1 tsp apple cider vineger
11.  Get 1/2 tsp ground mustard powder

##### Steps to make Whisky Glaze Salmon:

1.  Put all ingredient in a pan and bring to a boil then simmer till sauce started thicken about 3 minute then let it cool
2.  The glaze can be keep up to a week at this point
3.  Apply a layer of glazed on the salmon and broil for 20 minute on both side
