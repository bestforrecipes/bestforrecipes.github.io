##### The ingredients needed to make Quick Spicy Seafood Noodles :):

1.  Get milk
2.  Make ready fish stock,chicken stock,any stock you have :)
3.  Take kaffir lime leaves
4.  Get stalks of lemongrass ,chopped,crush
5.  Prepare sliced of galangal
6.  Make ready shallots / crushed
7.  Get each corianderand spring onion
8.  Get ground roasted peanuts
9.  Make ready iceberg lettuce
10.  Prepare vermicelli,glassnoodles ,or any noodle you like ,soak in watr until soft and ready to use
11.  Make ready roasted chili paste
12.  Make ready optional as needed chopped fresh hot chili incase need spicyness
13.  Get seasoning
14.  Prepare lime juice
15.  Take sugar
16.  Make ready seafood,shrimps ,squids,mussels,fish fillet ,enough for 2 bowls
17.  Make ready fishsauce substitute by salt or soya sauce

##### Instructions to make Quick Spicy Seafood Noodles :):

1.  Add stock to soup pot,add everything including seasoning ,except seafood,roasted peanuts ,coriander,spring onion,let it boil,after 3 mins taste ..add more salty or sour taste to suite your taste in this step
2.  Add seafood ,cook more 2mins ,until seafood almost cooked ,add noodles ,stir ,cook 2 more mins ,its done
3.  On serving bowl base with iceberg lettuce or bean sprout
4.  Pour hot noodle soup into bowls,top with roasted ground peanuts,coriander,spring onion ,serve hot :)
