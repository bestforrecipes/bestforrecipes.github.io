##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn Gravy:

1.  Make ready 4 Tomatoes
2.  Take 100 gm small onions
3.  Get 1/4 spoon Turmeric powder
4.  Make ready 2 spoon Chilli powder
5.  Take 1 spoon Garam masala
6.  Take 1 spoon Garlic paste
7.  Make ready 1/2 spoon Jeera powder / cumin
8.  Get as required Curry leaves, Mustard and Oil
9.  Prepare 1 small cup Coconut milk
10.  Prepare 750 gm Prawns
11.  Take as required Salt
12.  Prepare 1 cup water

##### Steps to make Prawn Gravy:

1.  In a pan add oil and then mustard seeds, curry leaves, chopped small onions and salt.
2.  After onion turns light brown then add garlic paste, turmeric powder, chilli powder and jeera powder. Mix well cook for 2 mins
3.  Add tomatoes and then cook for 10 mins
4.  Add cleaned and washed Prawns. Stir well for 2 mins then add salt and 1 cup of water. Mix well close the lid and cook for 15 mins in medium flame.
5.  Then add coconut milk and Garam masala, cook for another 10 mins in low flame.
