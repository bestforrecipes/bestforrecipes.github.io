##### The ingredients needed to make Genovese with Shrimp and Mozzarella Cheese:

1.  Prepare 400 grams Peeled shrimp
2.  Take 100 grams Mozzarella cheese (1 cm cubes)
3.  Prepare 150 grams Shimeji mushrooms
4.  Make ready 2 tbsp Olive oil
5.  Take 1 Salt and pepper
6.  Make ready 4 tbsp White wine
7.  Get 6 tbsp Genovese pesto
8.  Prepare 200 grams Pasta

##### Steps to make Genovese with Shrimp and Mozzarella Cheese:

1.  Heat olive oil in a pan and add the shrimp, shimeji mushrooms and white wine and season with salt and pepper.
2.  Add the cooked pasta, mozzarella cheese and genovese pesto and quickly toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper.
