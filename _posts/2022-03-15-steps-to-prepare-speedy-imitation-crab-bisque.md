##### The ingredients needed to make Imitation Crab Bisque:

1.  Prepare 20 oz Imitation Crab (or real crab/losbter-chunk style)
2.  Take 1 pints Heavy Whipping Cream
3.  Make ready 4 tbsp Unsalted Butter
4.  Make ready 1 cup Milk
5.  Make ready 1 cup Chicken Stock
6.  Get 1/4 cup White Wine
7.  Make ready 2 small Onions (chopped)
8.  Make ready 2 Bay Leaves
9.  Take 1/4 tbsp Cayenne Pepper
10.  Make ready 1/2 tbsp Chili Powder
11.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp Ground Cinnamon
12.  Take 1 tbsp Dried Parsley
13.  Take 2 tbsp Garlic Salt
14.  Prepare 1 Salt & Pepper (to taste)

##### Steps to make Imitation Crab Bisque:

1.  Melt butter in a deep saute pan.
2.  Add onions. Saute until slightly brown.
3.  Add all other ingredients and simmer on low for 30 minutes. Stir occasionally.
