##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Shrimp Scampi:

1.  Take 1 lb large devained & cleaned shrimp
2.  Make ready 1/3 cup butter
3.  Get 1 tbsp minced garlic
4.  Prepare 1 tbsp parsley
5.  Get 1/4 cup dry white wine
6.  Prepare 1 tsp salt
7.  Make ready 1 tsp pepper
8.  Make ready 1 tsp red jalapeno in jar
9.  Prepare 1 tsp dried onion flakes
10.  Prepare 1/2 tsp italian seasoning
11.  Get 1 lemons for side
12.  Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
13.  Make ready 1 pinch red pepper flaked

##### Instructions to make Spicy Shrimp Scampi:

1.  add butter, garlic & olive oil to pan cook about 3-4 minutes on med heat. Add in wine, and jalapeno paste cook another 3 minutes let wine cook out. Add in shrimp and all seasonings cook till shrimp turn pink about 4-5 minutes.
2.  serve with whatever sides and lemons.
