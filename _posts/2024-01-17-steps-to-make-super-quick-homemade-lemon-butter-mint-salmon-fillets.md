##### The ingredients needed to make Lemon Butter Mint Salmon Fillets:

1.  Get salmon fillets
2.  Get lemon
3.  Prepare fresh mint
4.  Get butter

##### Steps to make Lemon Butter Mint Salmon Fillets:

1.  Preheat oven to 350° or heat skillet to medium high.
2.  Place salmon fillets on tin foil. Garnish with mint, 1tbsp butter, salt and pepper. Close foil.
3.  Cook in oven or on skillet until done. Remove from foil and serve with fresh sprigs of mint and a dash of lemon juice.
