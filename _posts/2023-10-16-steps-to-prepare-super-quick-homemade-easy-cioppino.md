##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Cioppino:

1.  Get 1st step:
2.  Make ready 1/4 cup olive oil
3.  Take 1/4 cup butter
4.  Get 3 rib celery
5.  Get 1 diced onion
6.  Take 1 minced garlic clove
7.  Make ready 1 bay leaf
8.  Make ready 1/2 tsp chopped red jalapenos (in a jar)
9.  Get 2nd step:
10.  Get 1 large can italian crush tomatoes
11.  Prepare 1 can fish stock
12.  Prepare 1 box low sodium chicken stock
13.  Take 1/2 cup dry white wine
14.  Get seasonings:
15.  Prepare 1 tsp garlic salt
16.  Make ready 1 tsp old bay seasoning
17.  Prepare 2 tsp oregano
18.  Make ready 1 tsp red chili flakes
19.  Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
20.  Prepare 1 handful of clams
21.  Prepare seafood:
22.  Take 12 cleaned & devained prawns tail on
23.  Prepare 1 packages calamari (optional)
24.  Take 1 fish fillet of choice cut into 6-8 pieces
25.  Prepare 12 mussels ( i used box ones were just as good)
26.  Prepare 1 lemon juiced
27.  Get 1 bunch italian parsley

##### Steps to make Easy Cioppino:

1.  start by add all ingredients from step 1 into pot. cook down till onions start to sweat about 3-4 min on med high heat
2.  now add all your ingredients from step 2 & seasonings. bring to a simmer and taste for seasoning at this time.
3.  turn down heat to med-low simmer 30-35 min.
4.  now add your seafood it only needs about 7minutes to cook till mussels open. NOTE:if your using box mussels and any are already open discard they wont be good.) TURN OFF add the lemon juice & chopped italian parsely
5.  serve with a baguette. .Enjoy!
