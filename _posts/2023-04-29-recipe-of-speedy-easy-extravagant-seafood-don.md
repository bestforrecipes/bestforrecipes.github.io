##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Extravagant Seafood Don:

1.  Prepare 1 pack Tuna, salmon, squid, any sashimi
2.  Prepare 1 large bowl's worth Hot cooked rice
3.  Take 1 bit less than 1 teaspoon Sushi vinegar
4.  Prepare 1 Sudachi (or lemon)
5.  Prepare 1 dash Cucumber, shiso leaves, etc.
6.  Take 1 slice Tamagoyaki (optional)
7.  Take 1 Wasabi
8.  Prepare 1 Sushi ginger (optional)
9.  Make ready 1 White sesame seeds
10.  Prepare 1 Shredded nori seaweed

##### Instructions to make Easy Extravagant Seafood Don:

1.  Add the sushi vinegar and sudachi juice to one large rice bowl's worth of rice. Mix to make the sushi rice.
2.  Put the sushi rice in a bowl. Add the sesame seeds and finely shredded nori seaweed on top (optional).
3.  Put tuna, salmon, squid or other sashimi slices on top of the rice.
