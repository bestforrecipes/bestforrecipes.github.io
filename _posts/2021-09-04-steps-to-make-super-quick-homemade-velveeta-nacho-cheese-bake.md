##### The ingredients needed to make Velveeta Nacho Cheese Bake:

1.  Get 1 box Velveeta shells & cheese
2.  Make ready 1 lb ground beef
3.  Take 1 envelope taco seasoning mix
4.  Prepare 3/4 cup sour cream
5.  Make ready 3/4 cup shredded cheddar, divided
6.  Get 3/4 cup thick & chunky salsa or picante sauce
7.  Make ready 1/2 cup coarsely crushed fritos or tortilla chips

##### Steps to make Velveeta Nacho Cheese Bake:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F.
2.  Prepare mac & cheese as directed on package.
3.  While mac & cheese is cooking, cook beef and taco seasoning as directed on package. Keep warm.
4.  Mix sour cream with mac & cheese.
5.  Spoon half of mac & cheese mixture in a 8-inch square baking dish. Then add ground beef, half of the shredded cheese and remaining mac & cheese.
6.  Cover and bake for 15 minutes.
7.  Top with salsa, remaining shredded cheddar and chips. Bake 5 minutes uncovered.
8.  Serve with your favorite nacho or taco toppings.
