##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Salmon Sashimi Don:

1.  Prepare 200 g Sashimi-grade Fresh Salmon \*bones removed, sliced
2.  Get 1/4 Red Onion \*finely sliced
3.  Prepare 1-2 handfuls Vegetables \*Today I used ‘Shungiku’
4.  Take \*e.g. Salad Leafy Vegetables, Mizuna, Endive, Spinach, Carrot, etc
5.  Make ready 2 servings Freshly Cooked Rice
6.  Prepare 1/2 sheet Nori (Seaweed Sheet) \*cut into thin strips OR torn into small pieces
7.  Get <Sauce>
8.  Prepare 1 clove Garlic \*grated
9.  Get 2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
10.  Make ready 1 tablespoon Sugar
11.  Prepare 1 tablespoon Rice Vinegar
12.  Get 1/2 tablespoon Toban Djan (Chilli Bean Sauce)
13.  Get 1/2 tablespoon Sesame Oil
14.  Make ready 1/2 tablespoon Toasted Sesame Seeds

##### Instructions to make Spicy Salmon Sashimi Don:

1.  Combine all the sauce ingredients in a large bowl. Add Salmon slices and vegetables, and mix to combine.
2.  Half fill a bowl with freshly cooked rice and cover it with the Salmon & Vegetable mixture. Place plenty of Nori on top and enjoy.
