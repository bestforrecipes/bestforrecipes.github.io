##### The ingredients needed to make Hennessy Honey Salmon:

1.  Make ready salmon cut into portion sizes
2.  Prepare lemon
3.  Take minced garlic
4.  Take Hennessy
5.  Get honey
6.  Take onion powder
7.  Make ready paprika
8.  Make ready Cajun seasoning
9.  Make ready Fresh ground black pepper and fresh ground pink himalayan salt
10.  Prepare olive oil
11.  Make ready Parsley flakes for garnish

##### Instructions to make Hennessy Honey Salmon:

1.  Combine your dry ingredients.
2.  Heat honey to a boil in a small sauce pan. Once honey bubbles add Hennessy, minced garlic and dry ingredients and simmer for 5 minutes
3.  Cool sauce for 10 to 15 minutes or until it thickens
4.  Brush olive oil on salmon and season portions with black pepper and pink salt
5.  Turn oven on broil and cook salmon for 6 to 8 minutes depending on thickness of portions
6.  Take salmon out of oven and coat with the Hennessy honey sauce then broil for another 4 minutes or until light brown on top
7.  Garnish salmon with lemon and parsley flakes
