##### The ingredients needed to make Miami Shrimps in Tangy Minty sauce:

1.  Prepare 10 piece Jumbo Shrimps
2.  Get 2 large Tomatos
3.  Prepare 20 mint
4.  Get 1 medium onion, chopped
5.  Take 20 clove Garlic
6.  Get 4 piece Green chillies
7.  Get 4 tbsp Olive Oil
8.  Make ready 4 cup water

##### Instructions to make Miami Shrimps in Tangy Minty sauce:

1.  De-Shell the middle portion of the shrimps keeping the head and tail intact. Do not throw the shells.
2.  Boil the shells in water till reduced to two cups. Run through strainer and preserve the stock.
