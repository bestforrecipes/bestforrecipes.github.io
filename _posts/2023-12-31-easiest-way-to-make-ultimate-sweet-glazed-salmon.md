##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet Glazed Salmon:

1.  Take Salmon filet
2.  Get Packed Brown Sugar
3.  Prepare Melted Butter
4.  Take Lemon Juice
5.  Prepare Dill Weed
6.  Prepare Chili Powder
7.  Take Lemon pepper seasoning

##### Steps to make Sweet Glazed Salmon:

1.  Rub salmon with lemon pepper seasoning & set aside.
2.  Mix all the other ingredients (except salmon & lemon pepper) in a small bowl.
3.  Pour 1/2 a cup of the mixture into a sauce pan and warm over low heat stirring occasionally.
4.  Grill salmon for 5 minutes on medium then brush with the remaining mixture.
5.  Continue cooking 10-15 minutes with the cover down, basting occasionally.
6.  Serve with the warmed sauce.
