##### The ingredients needed to make Oven Baked Soy Salmon:

1.  Take Salmon Fillets-skin removed
2.  Make ready Dark Soy Sauce
3.  Prepare Spring Onions
4.  Prepare Some parsley
5.  Prepare Paprika
6.  Make ready Black pepper
7.  Make ready Kitchen Foil

##### Instructions to make Oven Baked Soy Salmon:

1.  Add Soy sauce, spring onion and parsley in container to make Marinade, add the salmon and leave to marinade in refrigerator for minimum of 1 hour
2.  Take salmon out and put on kitchen foil. Doinkle eith paprkia and black pepper pour over 2 tea spoons of the marinade and erap in Foil Paper. Put in oven gas mark 5 and Cook for spproximately 20 minutes.
3.  Serve with sweet potato mash and broccoli
