##### The ingredients needed to make spanish paella:

1.  Prepare 1 onion
2.  Get 1 1/2 can tinned tomatoes
3.  Prepare 1 chicken stock
4.  Prepare 250 grams paella rice
5.  Get 1 tsp paprika
6.  Make ready 1 1/2 medium chorizo
7.  Take 1 1/4 clove dried red chilli
8.  Make ready 1 chicken fillet
9.  Take 1/4 red dried chilli
10.  Get 1 dozen raw or cooked kind prawns
11.  Make ready 1 dozen mussels
12.  Make ready 1 dozen squid rings or octopus
13.  Get 1 salt and pepper
14.  Get 1 tbsp olive oil
