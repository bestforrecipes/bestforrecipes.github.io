3.  Make ready 1 1/2 cup water
4.  Take 1 tbsp butter, divided
5.  Get 1 (12 ounce) package frozen peas
6.  Get 1 tbsp soy sauce
7.  Get 1 tbsp chopped fresh rosemary
8.  Make ready 1 salt and pepper
