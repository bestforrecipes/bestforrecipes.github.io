##### The ingredients needed to make Papaya in Coconut Milk:

1.  Get 1 medium raw papaya
2.  Get 1 small garlic, minced
3.  Prepare 1 small onion, chopped
4.  Take 1 piece red chilli
5.  Take 1 bunch horse radish (moringa) leaves
6.  Make ready 2 cup coconut milk
7.  Make ready 20 grams dried shrimp (hibe)

##### Steps to make Papaya in Coconut Milk:

1.  Saute the garlic and onions for 5 minutes, or until the onion is semi transparent.
2.  Add the papaya and saute for 10 minutes.
3.  Add the coconut milk, dried shrimp and a dash of fish sauce. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes in low heat.
4.  Add the moringa leaves and chilli. Simmer for 2 more minutes.
5.  Serve hot over rice or as a soup.
