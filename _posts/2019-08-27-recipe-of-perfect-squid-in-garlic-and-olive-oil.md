##### The ingredients needed to make Squid in Garlic and Olive Oil:

1.  Make ready 250 g Squid
2.  Get 3 Tbsp Olive Oil
3.  Take 8 cloves Garlic
4.  Take 2 pcs. Bay leaf
5.  Get 1/4 Tsp pepper
6.  Take 1 pc. Chili finger, sliced
7.  Make ready 1 Tbsp Liquid Seasoning
8.  Get 1 Tbsp Calamansi
