##### The ingredients needed to make Ground Burger Cream Cheese Stuffed Shells:

1.  Get 1 stick Cream cheese
2.  Make ready 1 lb Ground meat
3.  Get 1 box Lasagna noodles
4.  Take 2 can Tomatoes crushed
5.  Prepare 1 cup Parm cheese

##### Instructions to make Ground Burger Cream Cheese Stuffed Shells:

1.  Mix stuffing mix cream cheese and sautéed ground meat and parm
2.  Boil lasagna noodles add salt
3.  Add stuffing to shell add stuffed shells in a square pan then add tomatoes crushed on top then sprinkle parm cheese on top then bake at 350 for 20 mins till parm is golden brown
4.  Easy, delicious,golden
