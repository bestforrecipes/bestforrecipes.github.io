##### The ingredients needed to make Smoked Salmon Appetizer:

1.  Prepare 3/4 cup finely chopped white onion
2.  Make ready 3 tbsp drained capers
3.  Get 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
4.  Get 4 tsp dijon mustard
5.  Take 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
6.  Take 8 oz cream cheese (room temperature)
7.  Prepare 24 thin slices of sourdough baguette
8.  Take 6 oz thinly sliced smoked salmon, cut into 24 pieces

##### Instructions to make Smoked Salmon Appetizer:

1.  stir together first 5 ingredients in a bowl to make relish, season with salt and pepper
2.  spread cream cheese onto bread, add 1 piece of salmon, top with relish
3.  serve on serving platter and enjoy
