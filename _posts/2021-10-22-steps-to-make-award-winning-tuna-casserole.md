##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Casserole:

1.  Make ready cooked elbow macaroni
2.  Get family size cream of mushroom
3.  Prepare tuna
4.  Prepare crushed frenchs fried onions
5.  Take pepper
6.  Make ready garlic salt

##### Steps to make Tuna Casserole:

1.  grease 13 by 9 inch pan with cooking spray
2.  add 3 cups of cook macaroni. add a dash of pepper and garlic salt
3.  stir in family size cream of mushroom and 3 cans of tuna. also you can use crushed French's fried onions and stir together
4.  bake at 350°F for 40 minutes
5.  take out and add remaining French's fried onions and continue baking for 15 more minutes until top is golden brown
