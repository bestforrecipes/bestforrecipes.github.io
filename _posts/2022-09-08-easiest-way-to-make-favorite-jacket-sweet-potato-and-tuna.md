##### The ingredients needed to make Jacket Sweet Potato and Tuna:

1.  Make ready Jacket Sweet Potato
2.  Get 5 medium sized sweet potatoes
3.  Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
4.  Get 50 ml cooking oil
5.  Prepare 5 tsp butter
6.  Make ready Tuna Mayo
7.  Make ready 5 tins tuna
8.  Take 1/4 cup mayonnaise
9.  Prepare 1/4 cup salad cream
10.  Get 1/2 chopped onion
11.  Take Handful chives
12.  Take 4 choppedchillie
13.  Make ready 1 tsp pepper
14.  Prepare 1 tsp paprika
15.  Prepare Serving
16.  Prepare 2 cups greated cheddar cheese

##### Instructions to make Jacket Sweet Potato and Tuna:

1.  Preheat oven to 180° C.
2.  Pierce sweet potatoes with a knife or a fork. Rub the potatoes with oil and salt and pepper. Bake for 1 hour for medium size or 1.5 hours for large size.
3.  In a bowl, combine all ingredients for tuna and refrigerate till serving.
4.  To serve. Cut the sweet potatoes in half or a cross. Add a tspn of butter on each potato. Top with tuna and cheese.
5.  Serve with roasted tomatoes and cucumber.
6.  Enjoy…
