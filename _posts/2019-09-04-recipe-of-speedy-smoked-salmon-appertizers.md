##### The ingredients needed to make Smoked Salmon Appertizers:

1.  Make ready cooked potatoe slices
2.  Make ready 1 packages cream cheese
3.  Make ready 1 packages smoked salmon
4.  Prepare 3 potatoes your choice
5.  Get 1 fresh dill
6.  Get 1 spray of cooking spray
7.  Get 1 drizzel of olive oil
8.  Take 1 salt & pepper to taste

##### Instructions to make Smoked Salmon Appertizers:

1.  Preheat oven to 150 (gas fan forced) spray baking tray with cooking oil. . Slice potatoes to round disks about 1mm thick ( I left the skins on) place on tray season with salt and pepper..drizzel all potatoes with olive oil.. bake in oven for 20-25 mins
2.  After 20-25 check potatoes–prick with a knife. If soft they are done
3.  While potatoes r cooling a little. Slice in thick strips the smoked salmon.
4.  After potatoes have cooled spread generously with cream cheese. Add the cut smoked salmon & arrange creativly…top with a sprig of dill and enjoy
