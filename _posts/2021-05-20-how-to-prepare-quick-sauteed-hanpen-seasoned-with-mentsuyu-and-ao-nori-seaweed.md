##### The ingredients needed to make Sautéed Hanpen Seasoned with Mentsuyu and Ao-nori Seaweed:

1.  Take 1 Hanpen
2.  Prepare 1 small Onion
3.  Prepare 1 tbsp Aonori
4.  Make ready 1 splash Mentsuyu (3x concentrate)
5.  Get 1 tbsp Butter or margarine

##### Steps to make Sautéed Hanpen Seasoned with Mentsuyu and Ao-nori Seaweed:

1.  Thinly slice the onion into wedges. Cut the hanpen into bite-sized pieces.
2.  Melt butter in a pan, and sauté the onion until tender.
3.  Add the hanpen to the onions from Step 2. Pour in a splash of mentsuyu, then sprinkle on aonori.
4.  Of course, this recipe also works with chikuwa!
