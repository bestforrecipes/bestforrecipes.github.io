##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood Pasta:

1.  Make ready 155 g cooked pasta of your choice
2.  Take 75 g Garlic & Herb Butter
3.  Prepare 1 Tbsp olive oil
4.  Make ready 225 g Hake cut into bite sized pieces
5.  Make ready 150 g Prawns shelled and deveined
6.  Make ready 120 g smoked mussels
7.  Prepare to taste Salt and black pepper
8.  Make ready 1 tsp paprika
9.  Get 100 g asparagus
10.  Get 60 g cherry tomatoes sliced in half
11.  Make ready 250 ml fresh cream
12.  Take 1 heaped teaspoon of basil pesto
13.  Take 1 Jalapeno chopped
14.  Prepare Parmesan as desired

##### Steps to make Seafood Pasta:

1.  Melt butter in a pan then add in fish. Season with salt, pepper and paprika. Stir and when just about done, add in prawns and continue to cook until prawns are done. Remove from pan.
2.  Add olive oil to pan and toss in the asparagus. Season with salt and black pepper. When partially cooked, add in your cherry tomatoes. Stir to mix in.
3.  Stir in fresh cream and add in chopped jalapeno.
4.  Add in basil pesto and stir. Allow to cook for a few minutes. You will notice cream will start to thicken.
5.  Add to sauce the cooked hake, prawns and smoked mussels. Mix in.
6.  Lastly add in the cooked pasta and stir to combine.
7.  Serve with grating of parmesan. Enjoy!
