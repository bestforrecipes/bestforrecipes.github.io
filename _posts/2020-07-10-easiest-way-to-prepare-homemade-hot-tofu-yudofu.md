##### The ingredients needed to make Hot Tofu (Yudōfu):

1.  Get ‘Momen’ Tofu \*medium soft type, cut into large pieces
2.  Prepare Water
3.  Make ready Kombu (Kelp) \*optional
4.  Get <Sauce>
5.  Get Soy Sauce
6.  Get Mirin
7.  Make ready Sake (Rice Wine)
8.  Prepare Katsuobushi (Bonito Flakes)
9.  Get Spring Onion \*finely chopped

##### Steps to make Hot Tofu (Yudōfu):

1.  Place Water and Kombu in a pot, gently place Tofu, and add more Water just enough to cover. Slowly heat over medium heat. When started boiling, it’s ready to serve. \*Note: Do not boil Tofu for too long.
2.  Meanwhile, make Sauce. Heat Soy Sauce, Mirin and Sake (Rice Wine) in a saucepan over medium heat, and bring to the boil. After gently boiling for 1 minute, add Katsuobushi (Bonito Flakes) and remove from the heat. Add Spring Onion.
3.  Pick up Hot Tofu using a slotted spoon or small skimmer, and enjoy with the Sauce. \*Note: You may wish to add Shichimi (Japanese Chilli Spice Mix) and grated Ginger.
