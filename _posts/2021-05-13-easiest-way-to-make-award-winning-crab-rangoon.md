##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Rangoon:

1.  Take Appetizer
2.  Make ready Green Onions
3.  Make ready Fresh Ginger
4.  Prepare Fresh Minded Garlic
5.  Take Fresh Crab
6.  Take Fresh Cilantro
7.  Take Vegetable Oil
8.  Prepare Wonton Wraps
9.  Make ready water to close wonton wraps
10.  Make ready Cream Cheese

##### Steps to make Crab Rangoon:

1.  Preheat Oil to 350
2.  Mix cream cheese until smooth
3.  add garlic, ginger, green onions, crab and mix gently.
4.  Place a teaspoon of the mixture on wonton wrap and seal one side with water and close.
5.  Do not stack on each other place separately on a flour dusted pan.
6.  Place about 4-5 in hot oil til lightly brown, drain on paper towels.
7.  A great sauce for dipping is sweet n sour sauce.
