3.  Prepare 2-3 tbsp Crème fraîche
4.  Take 1 tsp butter
5.  Take 1 tsp Cayenne pepper
6.  Get 1 red chilli (finely chopped)
7.  Get Salt and pepper
8.  Take Spring onions (finely chopped)
