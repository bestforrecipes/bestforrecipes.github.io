##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Bake Salmon With Vegetables:

1.  Take 2 salmon fillets
2.  Get 1 bunch thin asparagus
3.  Take 1 cup Cherry Tomatoes
4.  Prepare 1 Tablespoon butter
5.  Make ready 2 Tablespoons honey mustard dressing
6.  Take 2 Tablespoons cucumber yogurt or favorite garnish

##### Steps to make Easy Bake Salmon With Vegetables:

1.  Gather your ingredients
2.  Pre-heat your Oven to 400°F.
3.  Layer the bottom of the pan with the asparagus , drizzle with butter.
4.  Place the salmon fillets on top, brush honey mustard dressing on top.
5.  Add your cherry tomatoes
6.  Bake in your oven for 17-20 mins, until salmon is fully cooked, you can check the thickest part.
7.  Top with favorite sauce (Greek Yogurt with cucumbers) and serve.
