##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Masala:

1.  Make ready Crab – 4 medium size cleaned
2.  Prepare 1 tsp Fennel Seeds –
3.  Get Onion – 1 large chopped finely
4.  Prepare Green Chilli – 2 slit
5.  Make ready Leaves Curry
6.  Make ready Ginger Garlic Paste – 1 tblspn
7.  Get Coriander Powder – 1 tblspn
8.  Make ready 1 Tomato
9.  Make ready 2 tsp Chilli Powder –
10.  Take 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder –
11.  Prepare 2 tsp Garam Masala Powder –
12.  Get to taste Salt
13.  Take 4 tbls Oil
14.  Get Water – 1/2 cup to 1 cup

##### Instructions to make Crab Masala:

1.  Heat oil in a pan, crackle in cumin and fennel seeds. - - Add in onions, green chilli and curry leaves and saute this till golden brown.
2.  Add in ginger garlic paste and cook for 1 min. - Add in tomato and cook till mushy.
3.  Add the spice powders and salt along with water and mix well. Cook that for 5 to 8.
4.  Add crab and stir well in the gravy. Add the remaining water and cook covered for 5 to 6 mins. Or until the gravy thickens/ dry. Garnish with coriander leaves.
