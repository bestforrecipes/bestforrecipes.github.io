##### The ingredients needed to make Bicol Express:

1.  Make ready 400 Grams pork
2.  Get 3 cloves garlic chopped
3.  Take 1 onion chopped
4.  Make ready 2 Green Finger chilies sliced thinly then chopped
5.  Make ready 3 Thai chilies chopped
6.  Take 1 can Coconut milk
7.  Prepare Shrimp paste
8.  Take Water

##### Steps to make Bicol Express:

1.  Heat oil and saute garlic and onion until fragrant then cook Pork until light brown.
2.  Sprinkle black pepper and stir add water reduce until little liquid is left add 1tbsp shrimp paste and chilies stir.
3.  Add Coconut milk simmer until reduced and thick then turn off heat serve with hot Rice.
