##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Seafood Salad:

1.  Get 3 1/4 head romain lettuce
2.  Make ready 2 oz shaved parm
3.  Make ready 3 each shrimp
4.  Get 3 each sea scallops
5.  Make ready 1 each red pepper

##### Instructions to make Grilled Seafood Salad:

1.  prep grill heat (525) oil
2.  preheat oven 450
3.  oil & roast red pepper( skin should be charred)
4.  remove skin from red pepper (be sure to get all skin off & discard) cut flesh into thin strips about 1/4 inch.
5.  lightly oil and sear lettuce (just looking for grill marks)
6.  grill shrimps & scallops salt & pepper to taste
7.  assemble salad drizzle with dressing add shaved parm and serve (Caesar dressing recommended)
