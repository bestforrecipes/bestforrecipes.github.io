##### The ingredients needed to make Celery w/ Squid:

1.  Make ready 3 stalks celery
2.  Get 1 carrot
3.  Prepare 3 pcs medium size squid
4.  Prepare Slices garlic and ginger
5.  Make ready 1 tsp cooking wine
6.  Get Dash salt

##### Instructions to make Celery w/ Squid:
