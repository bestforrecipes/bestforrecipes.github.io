##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Salmon Filet:

1.  Make ready salmon
2.  Get large Onions sliced
3.  Take garlic cut lenghtwise
4.  Take lemon juice
5.  Make ready Olive oil
6.  Make ready cinnamon
7.  Take Bay leaves
8.  Take Salt and pepper

##### Steps to make Grilled Salmon Filet:

1.  Put Salmon filets in a large bowl.
2.  Add all remaining ingredients over Salmon.
3.  Let stand in the fridge for 3 hours
4.  Grill in large pan on high heat 2 slices at a time for 4 min each side
5.  Enjoy
