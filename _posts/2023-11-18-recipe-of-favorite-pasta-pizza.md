##### The ingredients needed to make Pasta Pizza:

1.  Take 1 packet pasta
2.  Take 2 cans tuna
3.  Take Tomato juice
4.  Prepare 1 tbsp tomato paste
5.  Get 1 cube chicken soup
6.  Make ready Salt
7.  Get Black pepper
8.  Make ready 1 Sliced onion
9.  Prepare Mozzarella
10.  Make ready 1 Sliced green pepper
11.  Take 1 1/2 tbsp mayonnaise
12.  Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp squeeze cheese
13.  Make ready Sliced olives
14.  Make ready Oil

##### Steps to make Pasta Pizza:

1.  Boil the pasta in water and salt till it's medium rare, then wash it with cold water
2.  Mix the pasta with the cheese abd the mayonnaise. Put it aside.
3.  Heat a pan and put the tuna in it. Stir a little while.
4.  Put the oil and stir. Now the onions and keep up stirring.
5.  Now put the tomato juice, the tomato paste, the cube of soup, some salt and black pepper. Stir, lighten the heat and cover the pan for a while.
6.  In the oven pan, put the pasta first, the the tuna batter, the sliced green pepper, the sliced olives and at last the mozarella.
7.  Put in the oven for about 15 minutes then serve.
