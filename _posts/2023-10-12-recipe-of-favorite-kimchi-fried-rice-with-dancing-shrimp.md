##### The ingredients needed to make Kimchi Fried Rice with Dancing Shrimp:

1.  Get kimchi fried rice
2.  Get 4 cup day old rice
3.  Take 2 tbsp sesame oil
4.  Make ready 1 quart kimchi from a locally made from an Asian store
5.  Prepare dancing shrimp
6.  Prepare 2 tbsp vegetable oil
7.  Get 2 tbsp Thai red peper flakes
8.  Take 2 tbsp minced garlic
9.  Take 4 dash red pepper sauce
10.  Take 1 lb shrimp
11.  Make ready 1 tbsp soy sauce
12.  Get 1 tbsp raw brown sugar
13.  Make ready 1 cup cilantro, chopped

##### Steps to make Kimchi Fried Rice with Dancing Shrimp:

1.  Drain kimchi and rough chop. Heat oil and add rice, followed by kimchi. Keep warm until shrimp is cooked.
2.  Heat oil in a pan. Add the garlic, pepper flakes, peppper sauce, soy sauce, sugar and shrimp. Cook until pink. Toss with the cilantro at the end.
3.  Place a bed of kimchi fried rice in a bowl or on a plate. Top with the dancing shrimp.
