##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna, Mayonnaise, Kimchi and Natto Rice Bowl:

1.  Make ready 1 bowl Plain cooked rice
2.  Make ready 1/2 bag ＊ Bean sprouts
3.  Get 1/2 tbsp ＊ Sesame oil
4.  Prepare 1 ＊ Salt and pepper
5.  Make ready 50 grams Kimchi (from Korea)
6.  Get 2 tbsp ● Canned tuna
7.  Take 2 tbsp ● Mayonnaise
8.  Prepare 1 pack ● Natto (fermented soy beans)
9.  Get 1 Finely chopped green onion

##### Instructions to make Tuna, Mayonnaise, Kimchi and Natto Rice Bowl:

1.  Combine the natto, the sauce and Japanese mustard that comes with it, as well as the tuna and the mayonnaise.
2.  Heat a frying pan and add the ● sesame oil and bean sprouts and stir fry. Season with salt and pepper.
3.  Put the rice in a large rice bowl.
4.  Add the bean sprouts.
5.  Put the kimchi on top, plus the natto-tuna-mayo mix, garnish with the chopped green onions and it's done.
