##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Pork Bicol Express:

1.  Prepare cubes Pork cut into
2.  Prepare 4 garlic cloves chopped
3.  Make ready 2 Onions chopped
4.  Get 1 Tbsps shrimp paste or bagoong
5.  Get 2 green chilies chopped
6.  Get 2 Thai red chilies chopped
7.  Make ready 1 cup coconut milk

##### Instructions to make Spicy Pork Bicol Express:

1.  In a hot pan with oil over medium hi heat sauté garlic and onions until fragrant and translucent. Then add bagoong or shrimp paste stir until incorporated.
2.  Then add pork until not pink anymore add coconut milk then chilies simmer for 10 min.
3.  Serve with hot rice
