##### The ingredients needed to make Maple Mustard Glazed Salmon:

1.  Prepare 1 1/2 lb salmon fillet. (cut into 6 pieces)
2.  Get 1/4 cup butter. (melted)
3.  Prepare 1/4 cup maple syrup
4.  Make ready 2 TBS grainy mustard (can use dijion wholegrain)
5.  Make ready 2 TBS soy sauce
6.  Make ready pepper. (to taste)

##### Instructions to make Maple Mustard Glazed Salmon:

1.  Preheat oven to 425
2.  Arrange salmon in baking dish
3.  In small bowl whisk together butter, maple syrup, mustard, and soy sauce
4.  Pour mixture over salmon
5.  Add pepper on top to taste
6.  Bake approx 15-20 mins or until salmon flakes with fork
