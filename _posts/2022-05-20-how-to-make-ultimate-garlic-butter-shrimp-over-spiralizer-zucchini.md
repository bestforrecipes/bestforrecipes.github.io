##### The ingredients needed to make Garlic Butter Shrimp over Spiralizer Zucchini:

1.  Make ready large zucchinis (1-1/2 lbs)
2.  Prepare carrots, peeled
3.  Get coconut oil
4.  Prepare medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
5.  Make ready garlic cloves, minced
6.  Make ready butter
7.  Get chopped fresh flat parsley

##### Steps to make Garlic Butter Shrimp over Spiralizer Zucchini:

1.  Shred or spiralize zucchinis and carrots, set aside.
2.  In large skillet, heat oil. Add shrimp and cook 2-3 min. until pink. Add garlic and cook 1 min. until fragrant.
3.  Add zucchini and carrot noodles, reduce heat and toss well. Add butter and cook 1 min. until melted.
4.  Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
