##### The ingredients needed to make Japanese Home Style Fried Rice;):

1.  Make ready 200 g white rice
2.  Get 40 g thinly sliced pork
3.  Make ready 3 crab sticks
4.  Get 2 chinese cabbage leaves
5.  Make ready 1 spring onion
6.  Prepare 1 egg
7.  Take 1 tsp sesame
8.  Take 1 pinch sea salt
9.  Make ready 1 pinch black pepper
10.  Make ready as needed sesame oil

##### Steps to make Japanese Home Style Fried Rice;):

1.  Cut Chinese cabbage leaves into appropriately sized pieces
2.  Put some sesame oil into a pan
3.  Continue stir frying until chinese cabbage becomes wilted, after that you will put thinly sliced pork into a pan
4.  Put white Rice into a pan and then mix it up
5.  Break an egg into a pan and then mix it up
6.  Put chopped spring onion and crab stick into a pan, then mix it
7.  Serve and you're done:)!
