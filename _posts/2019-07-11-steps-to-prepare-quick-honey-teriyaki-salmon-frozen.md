##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Teriyaki Salmon (frozen):

1.  Get 2 salmon portions (frozen)
2.  Get 1 tbsp butter or margarine
3.  Get 1/3 cup Teriyaki sauce/marinade
4.  Make ready 1 tsp minced garlic
5.  Make ready 1 lemon juice
6.  Make ready 1 Italian seasoning

##### Instructions to make Honey Teriyaki Salmon (frozen):

1.  Run salmon under cook water to remove glaze then pat dry.
2.  Melt butter. Mix butter, garlic and Teriyaki marinade then drizzle over frozen salmon.
3.  Sprinkling Italian seasoning
4.  Broil for 7 minutes. add lemon juice as desired.
5.  Serve hot.
