##### The ingredients needed to make Tom Ka. Soup my version:

1.  Get 15 med shrimp peeled @ deveined
2.  Take 1 1/2 tbsp Thai red curry paste
3.  Get 1 can baby corn 15 Oz can
4.  Take 1 can unsweetened coconut milk 14 oz
5.  Prepare 1 carrot med. sliced thin
6.  Make ready 1 box Swanson Thai ginger broth 32 oz
7.  Prepare 1 packages mixed mushrooms, use half, add more as needed
8.  Get 1 tbsp sugar add more to taste
9.  Make ready 1/3 cup chopped cilantro
10.  Get 2 limes zested. and juiced
11.  Get 1 salt to taste
12.  Prepare 1 tbsp coconut oil

##### Steps to make Tom Ka. Soup my version:

1.  Saute carrot with oil 5 minutes
2.  Add mushrooms saute for 3 minutes, add baby corn.
3.  Add broth, red curry paste, sugar,lime juice and zest. Bring to a boil. Turn heat down, simmer for 20 minutes.
4.  Add coconut milk, shrimp, bring back to a boil, add cilantro, stir and turn off heat, put a lid on it. Wait 5 minutes. The shrimp will be perfect. Enjoy!
