##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Lime Bites:

1.  Take crab meat (6 oz)
2.  Prepare green onions, chopped
3.  Make ready capers, chopped
4.  Prepare dill
5.  Get fresh thyme leaves
6.  Take lemon pepper
7.  Get lime juice
8.  Get greek yogurt
9.  Take real mayonnaise
10.  Take spinach leaves
11.  Make ready vegetable ritz crackers
12.  Make ready fresh thyme leaves for garnish

##### Steps to make Crab Lime Bites:

1.  Mix all ingredients in a medium sized bowl. Chill until ready to serve.
2.  Top crackers with spinach leaves and then crab mixture. Garnish with fresh thyme leaves and serve.
