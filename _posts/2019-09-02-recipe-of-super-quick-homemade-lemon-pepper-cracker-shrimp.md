##### The ingredients needed to make Lemon Pepper Cracker Shrimp:

1.  Take 1 lb shrimp
2.  Take 2 eggs beaten
3.  Prepare 1 cup flour
4.  Make ready 1 cup salteen crackers/bread crumbs
5.  Prepare 1 cup lemon pepper seasoning
6.  Take 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
7.  Make ready 1 oil

##### Instructions to make Lemon Pepper Cracker Shrimp:

1.  Heat your pan on medium with an inch of oil, or enough to cover a shrimp
2.  Defrost your shrimp and peel of the skin with tail and all. Also give it a quick rinse under water
3.  Get some crackers in a zip lock bag and crush them till they are itty bitty crumbs. Add some parmesan cheese and shake it up well. Pour in a flat bowl
4.  Pour your beaten eggs into a seperate bowl and now prepare your flour. Mix your cup of flour with at least 4 tbs of lemon pepper seasoning or to your liking, and put that in a seperate bowl.
5.  Now you have your flour, eggs, and crumbs all in their own bowl. Now, grab a shrimp and cover it with flour. Then into the beaten eggs and after that coat it with your crackers crumbs. Finally, place it to fry in your inch of oil. Cook till gooden brown
6.  Now serve to your liking with pasta and such and Enjoy!!
