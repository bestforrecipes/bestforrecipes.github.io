##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Rangoon Cheeseball:

1.  Prepare cream cheese - softened
2.  Make ready mayonnaise
3.  Prepare garlic powder
4.  Get large green onions - finely chopped
5.  Take can white crab meat - drained well

##### Instructions to make Crab Rangoon Cheeseball:

1.  In a medium sized bowl mix together cream cheese, mayonnaise, and garlic powder until well combined.
2.  Gently fold in green onions and crab meat. Form mixture into a ball shape and transfer to a serving dish. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hrs before serving (overnight is best).
3.  Serve with "wonton chips". Cut raw wonton wrappers into fourths. Deep fry in batches until golden brown. Drain on paper towel lined plate. Salt lightly while hot if desired. Spread cheeseball onto chips. Enjoy!
