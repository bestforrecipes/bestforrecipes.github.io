##### Instructions to make Pork Binagoongan:

1.  Combine pork cubes, water, peppercorn and vinegar in a pot and bring to a boil for 30 mins or until meat is tender. Then set aside.
2.  Saute garlic, onion and tomatoes in a pan.
3.  Add pork and shrimp paste. Add 1/3 cup pork stock and let simmer for 30 mins or until pork becomes brown.
4.  Add chili and sugar according to taste.
