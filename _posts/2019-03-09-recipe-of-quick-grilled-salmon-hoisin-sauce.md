##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Salmon Hoisin sauce:

1.  Take 140 gm salmon slice
2.  Get 1 pinch black pepper
3.  Prepare 1 pinch dried dragon herb
4.  Get 1 pinch piri piri chili powder
5.  Prepare 1 pinch salt
6.  Get Sesame seeds
7.  Get Olive oil
8.  Take Hoisin sauce recipe
9.  Make ready 1 dl oyster sauce
10.  Make ready 0.5 dl light soya sauce
11.  Make ready 1 pinch cinnamon
12.  Prepare 1 pinch white pepper
13.  Get 50 gm brown sugar
14.  Prepare 1 pinch salt
15.  Make ready 2 tablespoon tapioca powder (mixed with3 tablespoon water)
16.  Take 300 ml water

##### Instructions to make Grilled Salmon Hoisin sauce:

1.  Lay the salmon on the baking tray. Add olive oil the homemade Hoisin sauce ontop the salmon then sprinkle the rest of the ingredients on the salmon. Grilled in 200° for 7min.
2.  For the Hoisin sauce recipe, add all the ingredients in the pot except the tapioca water. Let it boiled around 10min then add in the tapioca water, continue to stir until the sauce become thicker then is ready to glace ontop the salmon.
3.  Tips: Good for meat too.
