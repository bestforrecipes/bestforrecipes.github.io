##### The ingredients needed to make Keto Friendly Spinach Tuna Salad:

1.  Get 1/2 avocado
2.  Make ready 1/4 lbs tuna (I used canned)
3.  Take 2 tbs mayo
4.  Take 1/4 cup spinach
5.  Make ready 1/2 cucumber
6.  Prepare 1 tbs Dijon mustard
7.  Make ready 1/2 lemon juice
8.  Take to taste salt and pepper

##### Steps to make Keto Friendly Spinach Tuna Salad:

1.  Cut avocado and cucumber,squeeze the lemon over them
2.  In a bowl,mix the tuna,mayo,the mustard
3.  Combine with veggies,add baby spinach,salt and pepper
