##### The ingredients needed to make Hot Honey Roasted Salmon:

1.  Make ready 2 skinless salmon fillets
2.  Take 1/2 cup honey
3.  Take 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and diced
4.  Get slices Lemon
5.  Make ready Add-ins: Salt, pepper, garlic powder

##### Steps to make Hot Honey Roasted Salmon:

1.  Preheat oven to 425. Line a baking sheet with foil.
2.  Season salmon with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Place on baking sheet.
3.  In a small saucepan, heat honey and jalapeño over medium low heat until heated through.
4.  Remove from heat and spoon about 1 tbsp of honey over each salmon fillet. Top with a lemon slice and jalapeños if desired.
5.  Bake for 15-20 mins until salmon is flaky.
