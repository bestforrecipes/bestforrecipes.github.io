##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp and Creamy Queso Grits:

1.  Take shrimps, peeled and devained
2.  Make ready water
3.  Make ready salt
4.  Make ready heavy cream
5.  Get quick grits
6.  Get Quesadilla cheese
7.  Make ready butter
8.  Make ready saffron
9.  Prepare Adobe Con Sazon
10.  Prepare Creole sesoning
11.  Prepare cayenne pepper
12.  Take green onions, chopped

##### Instructions to make Shrimp and Creamy Queso Grits:

1.  In a medium pot, add heavy cream, water and salt on medium-high heat.
2.  Once the cream mix starts to steam, add in the grits and cook for 3 minutes.
3.  Once the grits start to thicken add in cheese and cook for another 2-3 minutes until cheese has melted and grits are at desired thickness. Be sure to turn the heat down to medium-low and constantly stir the grits until the cheese has melted completly.
4.  While the grits are cooking, in a seperate pan add butter and melt on medium-low heat.
5.  In a bowl season shrimp with below seasonings.
6.  Once butter has melted add in saffron and stir for 30 seconds before adding in the shrimp. Cook on medium-high heat for 5 minutes, or until shrimp has cooked through and is pink. Then toss in green onions, and remove from heat.
7.  Serve grits topped with shrimp and a sprinkle of cayenne pepper!
