##### The ingredients needed to make Cucumber and Wasabi Brown Sushi Rolls:

1.  Prepare 1 cup brown rice
2.  Make ready 2 cups water
3.  Take 1 small cucumber - julienned
4.  Make ready 4 sheets Nori
5.  Prepare 1 splash sushi vinegar
6.  Take 1 splash Mirin
7.  Take Pinch Wasabi
8.  Make ready Sushi mat

##### Steps to make Cucumber and Wasabi Brown Sushi Rolls:

1.  Wash the sushi rice. Add the rice and water to the pan and bring to the boil. Cover with a lid and simmer for 35 minutes or until the water has absorbed. Turn off the heat and leave to stand for a further 10 minutes.
2.  Using a cutting motion add the mirin and vinegar to the rice.
3.  Using the sushi mat, place a sheet of nori and cover with the rice (leaving a 1cm edge at the bottom)
4.  Arrange your cucumber strips along the middle of the rice and add a pinch of wasabi. Pick up the end of the sushi mat nearest to you, roll and cut into equal portions with a sharp, wet knife.
5.  Serve with pickled ginger or your favourite sushi condiments
