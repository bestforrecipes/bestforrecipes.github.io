##### The ingredients needed to make Naples-style Octopus Salad:

1.  Take 200 grams Steamed octopus
2.  Prepare 100 grams Raw scallops
3.  Make ready 1/2 Cucumber
4.  Take 1/2 Carrot
5.  Prepare 1/2 Tomato
6.  Get 1/2 Lemon
7.  Make ready 3 tbsp Extra virgin olive oil

##### Instructions to make Naples-style Octopus Salad:

1.  Chop up the cucumber, carrot (peeled), and tomato (seeds removed) into bite sized pieces.
2.  Chop the octopus and scallops into bite sizes.
3.  Add olive oil and mix everything in a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and chill in the fridge for 10 minutes.
4.  Serve with lemon to squeeze right before eating.
