##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Quick and Easy:

1.  Get Salmon Filets
2.  Get Lemon Juice
3.  Make ready Salt
4.  Make ready Pepper
5.  Get Garlic Powder
6.  Prepare dried Basil

##### Instructions to make Salmon Quick and Easy:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F
2.  Place salmon filets on a cooking sheet and dribble lemon juice evenly on boths sides of the filets.
3.  Combine salt, pepper, garlic powder, and basil in a bowl then sprinkle on both sides of the salmon filets.
4.  When the oven reaches temperature, place the salmon filets in the oven for 10 - 12 minutes or until fully cooked.
5.  Eat and enjoy
