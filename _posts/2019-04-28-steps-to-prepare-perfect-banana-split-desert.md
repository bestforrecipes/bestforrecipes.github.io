##### The ingredients needed to make Banana Split Desert:

1.  Take 1 packages Graham crackers crushed
2.  Prepare 6 oz package vanilla pudding
3.  Make ready 3 cup milk
4.  Make ready 1 can crushed Pineapple drained
5.  Get 2/3 lb fresh strawberries sliced
6.  Make ready 2 large bananas sliced
7.  Make ready 12 oz Cool Whip
8.  Get 1 Magic shell
9.  Prepare 1 hot fudge
10.  Get 1 cherries
11.  Make ready 1 stick unsalted butter

##### Steps to make Banana Split Desert:

1.  Crush a package of Graham crackers. It will make 2 cups of crushed graham cracker crumbs.
2.  Melt stick of butter
3.  Mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter and press into a baking dish. You can also use crushed pretzels for the crust.
4.  Mix package of pudding with 3 cups of milk and beat.
5.  Spread pudding over graham cracker crumbs.
6.  Slice fresh strawberries and place in the dish over the pudding.
7.  Slice bananas and place over pudding.
8.  Drain crushed pineapples and spread over mixture.
9.  Spread cool whip over the ingredients.
10.  Squeeze magic shell over cool whip.
11.  Squeeze hot fudge over cool whip.
12.  Place cherries over the cool whip.
13.  Place in the fridge for a few hours to allow pudding ti set.
