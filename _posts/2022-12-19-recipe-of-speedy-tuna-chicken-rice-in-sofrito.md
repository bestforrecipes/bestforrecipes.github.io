##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna chicken rice in sofrito:

1.  Take 2 can tuna fish
2.  Make ready 15 oz can full of broken jasmine rice
3.  Make ready 1 cup sofrito , I used Goya brand
4.  Make ready 15 oz can of chick peas
5.  Take 1 quart chicken broth
6.  Take 4 large chicken breast, boneless and skinless
7.  Prepare 5 bay leaves
8.  Prepare 1 tsp celery salt
9.  Get 1 tsp ground black pepper
10.  Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
11.  Make ready 1 cup frozen sweet green peas
12.  Prepare 1 pints water
13.  Get 1/4 cup Italian dressing vinergette

##### Steps to make Tuna chicken rice in sofrito:

4.  Add chickpeas and sofrito stir occasionally add bay leaves, let cook 3 minutes add sweet peas
