##### The ingredients needed to make Tuscan Shrimp in creamy garlic sauce:

1.  Get 24 oz large shrimp, tails on
2.  Take 6 garlic cloves, minced
3.  Get 4 Tbsp butter, I use unsalted
4.  Take 3 C fresh spinach, trim stems
5.  Take 2 1/2 tsp dried Italian herbs, or 1 tsp each: dried basil,
6.  Get oregano and parsley
7.  Take 1 small vidalia onion, diced
8.  Take 5 oz sun dried tomato strips, drain off oil
9.  Get 3/4 C white wine
10.  Prepare 1 3/4 C half and half
11.  Take 1 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp pepper
12.  Take 1 C fresh parmesan cheese, grated
13.  Take 1 tsp cornstarch, 1 Tbsp cold water to thicken sauce
14.  Take 1 1/2 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

##### Instructions to make Tuscan Shrimp in creamy garlic sauce:

1.  Heat a large pan over medium heat. Melt 2 Tbsp butter and saute garlic for 2 minutes. Add shrimp and cook for 2 minutes on each side (until pink). Put in a bowl and set aside.
2.  Melt 2 Tbsp butter and cook onion for 2-3 minutes until softened. Pour in white wine and let this reduce a little. Add the sundried tomatoes and cook for about 2 minutes.
3.  Reduce heat down to medium-low and add the half and half. Bring to a simmer stirring frequently. Now add S&P.
4.  Add the spinach leaves and let them wilt into the sauce. Add the parmesan cheese and simmer for another minute. Add the water/cornstarch mixture and keep simmering and stirring until sauce thickens.
5.  Put shrimp back in the pan; sprinkle in the herbs and chopped parsley. Stir together and simmer for a few minutes to heat everything together.
6.  Serve over pasta.
