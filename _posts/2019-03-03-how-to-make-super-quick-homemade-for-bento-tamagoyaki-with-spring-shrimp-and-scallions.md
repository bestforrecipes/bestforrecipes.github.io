##### The ingredients needed to make For Bento: Tamagoyaki with Spring Shrimp and Scallions:

1.  Make ready 2 Eggs
2.  Prepare 1 to 2/3 tablespoons Sugar
3.  Make ready 1 tbsp Sakura shrimp - tiny dried shrimp
4.  Prepare 1 tbsp Green onions or scallions
5.  Make ready 1 Vegetable oil
6.  Make ready 1 tsp Soy sauce

##### Steps to make For Bento: Tamagoyaki with Spring Shrimp and Scallions:

1.  Beat the eggs.
2.  Add sugar, soy sauce, sakura shrimp, and minced scallions. Mix together.
3.  Grease the tamagoyaki pan and make the rolled omelets.
4.  When slightly cooled, cut into your preferred sizes and enjoy.
