##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp and Pine Nut Spaghetti:

1.  Prepare uncooked spaghetti
2.  Prepare peeled and deveined medium shrimp
3.  Prepare pine nuts
4.  Prepare 1% low fat milk, divided
5.  Take all-purpose flour
6.  Make ready dijon mustard
7.  Prepare ground nutmeg
8.  Prepare salt
9.  Take ground black pepper
10.  Take grated parmesan cheese
11.  Get finely chopped fresh basil
12.  Get torn fresh basil leaves
13.  Make ready pesto (optional) instead of 1/3 cup chopped basil

##### Steps to make Shrimp and Pine Nut Spaghetti:

1.  Bring 4 qts water to a boil in a large saucepan. Add pasta and cook 7 minutes. Add shrimp to pan, cook 3 minutes or until shrimp are done and pasta is al dente. Drain, keep warm.
2.  While pasta cooks, heat a small nonstick skillet on medium heat. Add nuts to pan and cook 2 minutes or til golden brown.
3.  Combine 1/2 cup milk and flour in a saucepan, whisking until well blended. Place pan on medium heat and gradually stir in remaining milk. Stir in mustard and nutmeg. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to medium- low, and cook 5 minutes or until mixture begins to thicken; stirring constantly. Stir in S&P; cook 1 minute. Add pasta and shrimp, cheese and chopped basil, tossing gently to combine. Sprinkle with nuts and torn basil.
