##### The ingredients needed to make Coconut Shrimp Pasta:

1.  Make ready 250 grams Pasta
2.  Prepare 1 cup Tomatoes sauce
3.  Get 1/2 cup Grounded onions
4.  Take 2 tbsp Coconut oil
5.  Make ready 250 grams Medium shrimp
6.  Take Pepper
7.  Get Salt
8.  Make ready 3 tbsp Cream milk

##### Instructions to make Coconut Shrimp Pasta:

1.  Cook the pasta al dente.
2.  In a pan, put the coconut oil and the onions. Let it cook for 3 minutes or getting clear.
3.  Put the shrimp, and sautè until it stay cooked. About 3 minutes.
4.  Add tomatoes sauce, and let it cook for about 5 minutes or a little bit more. Than add the cream milk!
5.  Add salt, pepper.
6.  Add pasta to the sauce. Mix it. It's ready!
