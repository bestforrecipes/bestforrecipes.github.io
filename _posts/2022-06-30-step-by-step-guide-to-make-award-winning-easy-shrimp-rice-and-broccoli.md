##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Shrimp Rice and Broccoli:

1.  Make ready 12 oz Frozen cooked Shrimp (no tails)
2.  Take Goya Yellow Spanish Style Rice
3.  Get 14 oz Broccoli Florets
4.  Get Salt and Pepper to taste

##### Instructions to make Easy Shrimp Rice and Broccoli:

1.  Make rice according to box instructions except use 1 1/2 cups of water and add the broccoli and shrimp in before you simmer. Add your salt and pepper.
2.  Simmer on medium for 30 minutes or until water is absorbed.
3.  Enjoy!
