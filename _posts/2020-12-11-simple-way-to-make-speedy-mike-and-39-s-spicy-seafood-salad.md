##### The ingredients needed to make Mike's Spicy Seafood Salad:

1.  Prepare ● For The Seafoods \[all fully chilled & rough chopped\]
2.  Take 1 Pound Pre-Steamed Lobster
3.  Prepare 1 Pound Pre-Steamed Shrimp
4.  Get 1 Pound Pre-Steamed Scallops
5.  Get 2 Pounds Pre-Steamed Immatation Crab Meat \[don't use real crab\]
6.  Take ● For The Fruits & Vegetables \[all chilled & rough chopped\]
7.  Get 2 LG EX Firm Beefeater Tomatoes \[de-seeded\]

9.  Make ready 1 EX LG Vidalia Onion
10.  Take 1 LG Red Onion

13.  Take 1/2 Cup Green Onions
14.  Make ready 1 Medium Green Bell Pepper \[de-seeded\]
15.  Take 1 Medium Red Bell Pepper \[de-seeded\]
16.  Prepare 1 Medium Orange Bell Pepper \[de-seeded\]
17.  Make ready 1 Medium Yellow Bell Pepper \[de-seeded\]
18.  Make ready 3 LG Celery Stalks
19.  Make ready 1 Bunch Quartered Radishes
20.  Make ready 7 Cloves Minced Garlic
21.  Prepare 1 Bunch Fresh Cilantro
22.  Get 1/4 Bunch Fresh Parsley
23.  Take 2 EX LG Jalapeño Peppers \[de-seeded\]
24.  Prepare 2 LG Habenero Peppers \[de-seeded\]
25.  Take ● For The Seasonings
26.  Make ready 1 tsp Crushed Mexican Oregano
27.  Make ready 1 tsp Fresh Ground Black Pepper
28.  Get 1 tbsp Crushed Saffron
29.  Make ready to taste Tabasco Sauce
30.  Get ● For The Fluids
31.  Take as needed Chilled Clamato Juice \[i used 4 cups\]

##### Instructions to make Mike's Spicy Seafood Salad:

1.  Rough chop vegetables and mix everything together. Chill for 6 hours, if not longer. Stir occasionally.
2.  Serve with additional Tabasco, crackers, tortilla chips and/or French bread. Enjoy!
