##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Italian Pasta Salad:

1.  Take 6 oz package of pepperoni
2.  Take 1 lb tri color spiral pasta is best or pasta shells
3.  Prepare 15 oz can of sliced black olives
4.  Prepare 8 oz package of fresh mozzarella
5.  Take 6 oz crumbled feta cheese
6.  Make ready 3 med-large tomatoes diced or 1 package of grape tomatoes halved
7.  Get 1/2 cup small diced red onion
8.  Make ready 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
9.  Prepare 1 cup Italian dressing or to taste
10.  Make ready 1 1/2 tsp of Italian seasoning or to taste

##### Steps to make Easy Italian Pasta Salad:

1.  Cook pasta for about 7 min, drain and rinse with cold water and chill in fridge.
2.  While pasta is cooking dice your tomatoes, onion and basil
3.  Meanwhile Dice fresh mozzarella into small cubes. And cut pepperoni slices into quarters
4.  Combine pasta and ingredients into a large bowl.
5.  Add seasoning and dressing till that the pasta is coated nicely. Add more if desired.
6.  ENJOY!
