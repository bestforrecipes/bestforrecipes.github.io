##### The ingredients needed to make Pork Steamed Fried Rice:

1.  Get Topping
2.  Make ready 1/4 cup Fresh Green Peas
3.  Prepare 1 medium sized Onion, chopped
4.  Take 3 clove Garlic, crushed
5.  Take 4 tbsp Soy Sauce
6.  Take 2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
7.  Make ready 2 tbsp Cornstarch
8.  Get 1 cup Pork Broth
9.  Prepare 10 pcs Shrimp
10.  Get 1/2 kg Pork Tenderloin, cubed
11.  Take Steamed Fried Rice
12.  Take 2 cup Steamed Rice
13.  Get 1 tbsp Hoisin Sauce
14.  Take 1 tbsp olive oil

##### Steps to make Pork Steamed Fried Rice:

1.  Pour oil in a hot pan and saute onions and garlic for about 1 - 2 minutes.
2.  Add pork and mix until pork's fat starts to ooze out. Add shrimp and stir for 3-4 minutes then add soy sauce.
3.  Adjust heat to medium then add a cup of broth and let mixture boil for 5 minutes. Add Worcestershire sauce.
4.  Dissolve cornstarch in a half cup of cold water, add to the pan, just enough to thicken the sauce. Stir continuously then pour fresh green peas.
5.  Let it boil for another 5 minutes then set aside.
6.  In another pan, pour 1 tbsp olive oil then add steamed rice.
7.  Add hoisin sauce, mixing thoroughly with rice.
8.  In a bowl, place fried rice and top with the topping sauce.
