##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Stove Top Mac and Cheese:

1.  Make ready 1 lb pasta of choice, I like spirals for this
2.  Make ready 1 creamy cheese packet from velvet shells and cheese. Save pasta from box for something else
3.  Make ready 1/2 stick butter
4.  Get Salt
5.  Take 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese
6.  Prepare 1 1/2 cups heavy cream

##### Steps to make Creamy Stove Top Mac and Cheese:

1.  Boil the noodles according to package directions.
2.  Drain noodles and add back to the pot. Squeeze the cheese packet, 2 cups of cheese, butter and cream and salt. Mix and put the lid on the pot. Give it a few minutes to melt and stir it again. I leave it on the burner I cooked it on for the residual heat. You’re ready to eat!
