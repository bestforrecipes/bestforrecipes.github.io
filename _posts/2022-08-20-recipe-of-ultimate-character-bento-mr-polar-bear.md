##### The ingredients needed to make Character Bento Mr. Polar Bear:

1.  Make ready 1 small bowl Plain cooked rice
2.  Get 1/4 sheet Toasted nori seaweed
3.  Make ready 1 Vegetables and side dishes of your choice
4.  Take 1 Imitation crab sticks
5.  Get 1 Ham & cheese

##### Instructions to make Character Bento Mr. Polar Bear:

1.  Leave 2 tablespoons of cooked rice and pack the rest into the bento. Sprinkle with your preferred topping and cover in toasted nori seaweed.
2.  Shape the rest of the rice into the polar bear's face. Cut out the eyes, nose and mouth from a sheet of roasted nori seaweed and make his cheeks with ham.
3.  Slice off the red outer layer of the imitation crab stick and cut to a width of 5-6 mm to create the scarf. Wrap it around the bear's neck and decorate with sliced cheese.
