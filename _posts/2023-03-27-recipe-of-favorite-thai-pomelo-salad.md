##### The ingredients needed to make Thai Pomelo Salad:

1.  Take 2 kinds of lettuce
2.  Get 2 slices pomelo
3.  Prepare 4 pcs steamed shrimp
4.  Make ready 1 shallot (sliced)
5.  Get leaves Corriander
6.  Make ready 1 bird eye chili
7.  Prepare 2 tbsp crushed peanut
8.  Get 2 tbsp honey glazed almond
9.  Take 1 tbsp almond
10.  Get 2 tbsp lemon juice
11.  Take 2 tbsp palm sugar (diluted)
12.  Take 1 tsp fish sauce
13.  Take 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
14.  Prepare Olive oil

##### Instructions to make Thai Pomelo Salad:

1.  Prepare all ingredients
2.  Mix palm sugar, lemon juice, balsamic vinegar, and fish sauce.
3.  Drizzle the sauce onto the salad
