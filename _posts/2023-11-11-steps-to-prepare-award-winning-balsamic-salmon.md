##### The ingredients needed to make Balsamic Salmon:

1.  Make ready Salmon Filet
2.  Prepare Balsamic Vinegar
3.  Prepare Olive Oil
4.  Make ready Garlic
5.  Make ready Honey
6.  Take Dijon Mustard

##### Instructions to make Balsamic Salmon:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F.
2.  Brown garlic in olive oil.
3.  Then add balsamic vinegar.
4.  Then add honey and mustard.
5.  Brush glaze over the salmon.
6.  Bake for 5 minutes.
7.  Take it out and add more glaze then bake for another 10 minutes.
8.  Garnish with parsley and serve.
