2.  Make ready 50 grams Spaghetti pasta
3.  Make ready 1 1/2 liter water
4.  Take 1/2 pints salt
5.  Make ready The sauce
6.  Prepare 10 grams fresh tomatto
7.  Prepare 25 ml olive oil
8.  Take 4 tbsp chili sauce
9.  Prepare 1 tsp salt and pepper
10.  Get 50 ml lemon juice
11.  Prepare 10 grams squid
12.  Prepare 10 grams shrimp
13.  Prepare 1 tbsp minched garlic
