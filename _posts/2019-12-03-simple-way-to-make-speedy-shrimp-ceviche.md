##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Ceviche:

1.  Take 1 cup Onion
2.  Prepare 1 cup Tomatoes
3.  Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Cilantro
4.  Get 1 tsp Lime
5.  Get 1 pinch Salt
6.  Make ready 1 cup Large shrimp, cut in halves

If you've never made ceviche before, this is the recipe you need. Traditional ceviche recipes consists of raw seafood tossed with an acidic marinade (think: citrus juice or vinegar) that "cooks" the fish. In this shrimp ceviche recipe, we cook the shrimp before marinating it in lemon, lime and orange juices, plus chiles for some heat. Avocado adds creaminess to help the dish come together.

##### Steps to make Shrimp Ceviche:

1.  Rinse vegetables
2.  Dice onions
3.  Dice tomatoes
4.  Cut large shrimp into halves or thirds (however you prefer)
5.  Soak shrimp in lime juice (fresh or bottled) for 30 minutes
6.  Put tomatoes and onions into a tumbler or bowl for mixing, then add a dash of salt and cilantro
7.  Pour lime soaked shrimp into mixture and mix or stir well
8.  Chill dish for an hour then serve cold
