##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Goulash:

1.  Take 1 can tuna
2.  Take 1 pack ramen noodles
3.  Get 1/2 can pinto or black beans
4.  Take Sriracha sauce

##### Instructions to make Tuna Goulash:

1.  Cook ramen
2.  Heat beans
3.  Open can of tuna
4.  Dump ramen noodles, beans, and tuna all in a bowl.
5.  Add however much Sriracha sauce you feel is appropriate, but not too much.
6.  Mix all together and enjoy!
