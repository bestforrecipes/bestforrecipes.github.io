##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna with noodles:

4.  Get 1 medium carrot shredded

7.  Get 1 1/2 cups mayonnaise, you can use less or more
8.  Take 1 tsp white pepper

10.  Take 2 tablespoons chopped garlic
11.  Get 1/2 tsp paprika
12.  Prepare 1 tbsp lime juice (or lemon)
13.  Prepare Approximately 1 cup chopped fresh spinach
14.  Take 1 cup shredded cheese, i used colby jack
