##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Crab Legs:

1.  Make ready 2 lb frozen crab legs
2.  Make ready 1/4 cup Canola or Olive Oil
3.  Prepare 2 tbsp Lemon Pepper

##### Instructions to make Grilled Crab Legs:

1.  Dethaw 2 to 4 lbs of crab legs by putting in pot and letting cold water run over them in sink.
2.  Preheat grill.
3.  While grill is preheating brush oil over crab legs.
4.  Sprinkle lemon pepper over oiled crab legs. The more you use the better. :-)
5.  Grill crab legs high heat 3 to 3 1/2 minutes. Then flip for another 3 to 3 1/2 minutes.
