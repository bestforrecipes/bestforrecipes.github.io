##### The ingredients needed to make Butter Sautéed Carrot Ribbons:

1.  Get 1 Carrot
2.  Prepare 1 tbsp Butter
3.  Take 1 Salt and pepper
4.  Make ready 1 Shredded nori seaweed

##### Instructions to make Butter Sautéed Carrot Ribbons:

1.  Peel the carrot, then cut thin long slices from it with a vegetable peeler.
2.  Heat butter in a pan and add the carrot slices. Season with salt and pepper.
3.  Transfer to a serving plate. Scatter shredded nori seaweed and it's done.
