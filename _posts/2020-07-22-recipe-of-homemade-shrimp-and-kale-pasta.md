##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp and Kale Pasta:

1.  Prepare Shrimp and Kale
2.  Make ready 12 ounces frozen chopped kale
3.  Take 1/2 teaspoon salt
4.  Take 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper powder
5.  Get 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
6.  Prepare 1/4 cup capers
7.  Prepare 1/2 cup chopped kalamata olives

9.  Prepare 1/3 cup red wine

14.  Make ready 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
15.  Get As needed shredded Parmesan cheese for topping

##### Instructions to make Shrimp and Kale Pasta:

1.  Have the kale thawed. Slice and fry the bacon when crisp set it aside. Add the kale to the bacon drippings. Add the spices, capers and olives. Sauté for 20minutes.
2.  Boil your pasta as per instructions on package.
3.  Add 2 tablespoons of butter. Sauté for 3 minutes and add a cup of the pasta water to the kale. Simmer 7 minutes. Add half the cheese to the kale along with the pasta.
4.  Fortify with the wines allow to reduce for 12 minutes add the other half of the cheese. Stir in. Now add the cream and stir coating all the ingredients in the pan. Allow to thicken but not to boil. If it boils the sauce will break.
5.  Serve with parmesan cheese to taste on top. I hope you enjoy!!!
