##### The ingredients needed to make Pork Binagoongan:

1.  Make ready Pork (cut to medium sized cubes)
2.  Get Shrimp paste
3.  Make ready Garlic (minced)
4.  Make ready Onion (minced)
5.  Get Tomatoes (quartered)
6.  Prepare Eggplant (optional)
7.  Make ready Siling haba / green long chilis
8.  Take Scallions
9.  Prepare Salt
10.  Make ready Pepper
11.  Make ready Sugar

##### Steps to make Pork Binagoongan:

1.  First, boil pork in a pot of water with salt & pepper until tender. Then set aside.
2.  In a pan, saute garlic, onion, tomatoes and chilli.
3.  Toss in the pork. Fry until brown.
4.  Add 1/4 cup of water used for boiling the porkp. Simmer for 5mins. (you can also add the eggplants at this point)
5.  Add the shrimp paste.
6.  Season with salt, pepper and sugar.
7.  Garnish with scallions. Serve.
