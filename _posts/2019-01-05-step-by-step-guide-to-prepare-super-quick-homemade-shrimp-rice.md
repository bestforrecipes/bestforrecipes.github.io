##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Rice:

1.  Get White Rice
2.  Prepare 2 cloves garlic
3.  Make ready 1 red pepper
4.  Make ready 1 large onion
5.  Get 2 asparagus
6.  Get 1 habanero pepper
7.  Get 2 large potatoes
8.  Prepare 2 carrots
9.  Get Scallions
10.  Make ready Azafrán
11.  Get Smoked paprika
12.  Get Salt
13.  Prepare Pepper
14.  Take Shrimps
15.  Take Vegetable broth

##### Steps to make Shrimp Rice:

1.  Chop your garlic, red pepper, habanero pepper, onion and scallions in little pieces
2.  Dice your carrots, potatoes and asparagus
3.  Stir fry all your vegetables and your rice with olive oil, salt,pepper,azafrán and smoke paprika then add the vegetable broth until start to cook the rice till starting to boiling and add the carrots potatoes, asparagus and shrimp till cook
