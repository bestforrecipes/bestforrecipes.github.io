##### The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Salmon w/ Crab and Shrimp:

1.  Get 4 salmon filets
2.  Get 8 oz crab meat
3.  Get 8 oz bay shrimp or salad shrimp
4.  Get 1 cup shredded brie cheese
5.  Get 1 tbsp old bay seasoning
6.  Get 2 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
7.  Prepare 2 tbsp butter (optional)

##### Instructions to make Stuffed Salmon w/ Crab and Shrimp:

1.  Mix together crab meat, brie cheese, bay shrimp, and old bay in a bowl.
2.  Slice salmon filets down the center and rub a teaspoon of olive oil on each filet.
3.  Stuff each filet with a generous amount of the crab meat mixture. (You will probably have some leftover)
4.  (Optional) Cut 1/2 tablespoon of butter and put it on top of each filet.
5.  Bake at 350° for about 20-25 mins. Depending on how thick your filets are.
6.  Broil for another 5 mins. or until golden brown.
7.  Pair with your favorite sides and ENJOY!!!
