##### The ingredients needed to make Thai-style Seafood Spicy Soup:

1.  Make ready 5 Shallots
2.  Get 3 Lemongrass
3.  Prepare 1000 ml Water
4.  Prepare 6 Shrimps
5.  Get 1 Squid
6.  Get 10 tbsp Lime juice
7.  Prepare 15 tbsp Fish sauce
8.  Get 1 packages Angel mushroom

##### Instructions to make Thai-style Seafood Spicy Soup:

1.  Boil shallots and lemongrass in a boil water.
2.  Clean shrimps and chop squids.
3.  Add shrimps and squids into the boiling water.
4.  Add washed angel mushroom.
5.  Season with lime juice and fish sauce.
6.  Pour in a bowl and serve with rice.
