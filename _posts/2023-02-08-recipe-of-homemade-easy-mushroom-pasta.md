##### The ingredients needed to make Easy mushroom pasta:

1.  Take 10 oz Mushroom pasta sauce
2.  Get 1 packages shell noodles
3.  Prepare 1 bunch Uncooked spinich (washed)
4.  Get 1 tbsp sea salt
5.  Take 1 tbsp black pepper
6.  Take 1 tbsp granulated sugar
7.  Take 1 tsp chopped garlic
