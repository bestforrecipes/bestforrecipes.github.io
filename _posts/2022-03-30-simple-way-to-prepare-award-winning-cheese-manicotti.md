##### The ingredients needed to make Cheese Manicotti:

1.  Get 18 Jumbo Shells
2.  Prepare 1 lb Ricotta Cheese (or cottage cheese)
3.  Take 1 1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese
4.  Take 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
5.  Make ready 1 Egg
6.  Prepare 1 1/2 tsp Parsley
7.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
8.  Prepare 1 jar Spaghetti Sauce
