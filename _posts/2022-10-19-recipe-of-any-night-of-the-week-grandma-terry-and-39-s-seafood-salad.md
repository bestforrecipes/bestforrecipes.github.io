##### The ingredients needed to make Grandma Terry's Seafood Salad:

1.  Make ready 4 pounds calamari cleaned and cut into rings
2.  Get 4 pounds shrimp cleaned and cut in thirds
3.  Make ready 1 1/2 pounds octopus cut into bite sized pieces
4.  Make ready 1 whole head celery chopped
5.  Get 1 large jar sliced green olives
6.  Make ready 1 whole bulb garlic minced (about 8 gloves)
7.  Get 4 cup Wesson vegetable oils
8.  Take Juice of 12 lemons

##### Steps to make Grandma Terry's Seafood Salad:

1.  Cook each type of seafood separately in boiling water. Transfer to bowl of ice to stop cooking process.
2.  Combine all ingredients in large bowl AMD refrigerate. Serve cold.
