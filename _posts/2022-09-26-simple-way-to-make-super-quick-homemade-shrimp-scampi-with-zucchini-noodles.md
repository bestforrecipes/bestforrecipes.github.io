##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Scampi with Zucchini Noodles:

1.  Make ready SHRIMP AND ZUCCHINI
2.  Prepare 1 lb medium raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
3.  Make ready 3 medium zucchini, ends removed and cut into thin strips to resemble spaghetti
4.  Get 1 1/2 tbsp cajun seasoning
5.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp olive oil
6.  Make ready 1/2 tbsp butter, salted or unsalted
7.  Take SAUCE
8.  Get 1 Shallot, sliced
9.  Make ready 3 garlic cloves, minced
10.  Make ready 2 tbsp dry white wine
11.  Take 1/2 cup low sodium chicken broth
12.  Get 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
13.  Get 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
14.  Prepare 1 tsp hot sauce, such as Franks brand
15.  Take 1/4 tsp black pepper and salt to taste
16.  Get 1 tbsp heavy cream
17.  Make ready GARNISH
18.  Prepare 2 tbsp fresh grayed romano cheese, plus more for serving
19.  Make ready 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

##### Steps to make Shrimp Scampi with Zucchini Noodles:

1.  Season shrimp with cajun seasoning
2.  Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet. Add shrimp and cook until.pink and cooked through, about 3 minutes on each side. Remove to a plate and cover to keep warm
3.  Add shallot and garlic to the same skillet the shrimp was cooked in and cook 1 minute just to soften. Add wine and cook until almost gone
4.  Add broth, lemon juice, red pepper flakes, hot sauce, cream, black pepper and salt to taste. Bring to a simmer
5.  Add zucchini, tossing to coat with sauce and cook until just tender about 2 minutes
6.  Turn out onto serving platter, sprinkle with romano cheese, top with shrimp and parsley. Add more romano cheese when serving
7.  Note, The zucchini can be cut by knife. I use a simple tool that when you put the zucchini in and twist the noodles come out. I got it at the grocery store. I picture it below
