##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Cocktail:

1.  Take 1 medium tomato
2.  Make ready 2 medium jalapeños
3.  Make ready 1/2 cup onion
4.  Make ready 1 bunch cilantro
5.  Prepare 20 oz sunkist orange soda
6.  Get 15 oz ketchup
7.  Make ready 20 oz minute maid orange juice
8.  Get 20 oz salsa valentina
9.  Prepare 50 medium shrimp
10.  Take 1 medium container or pot

##### Steps to make Shrimp Cocktail:

1.  Wash & take the tale off the shrimp
2.  Place shrimp into medium size pot
3.  Add ketchup, salsa valentina, orange soda, orange juice to pot
4.  While shrimp is sitting in the juices. Dice the tomatoe, onion, jalapeños & chop the cilantro.
5.  Add these to the mix
6.  Enjoy!
