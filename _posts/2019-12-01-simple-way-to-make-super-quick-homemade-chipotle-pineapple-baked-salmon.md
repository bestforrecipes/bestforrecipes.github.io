##### The ingredients needed to make Chipotle-Pineapple Baked Salmon:

1.  Get 4 salmon filets
2.  Make ready 1 cup crushed pineapple
3.  Take 1 cup chipotle salsa
4.  Take 1 tbsp roasted, ground cumin
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp roasted, ground coriander
6.  Get 2 tsp smoked paprika
7.  Prepare 2 tsp onion powder
8.  Get 2 tsp garlic powder
9.  Make ready 1 salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Chipotle-Pineapple Baked Salmon:

1.  Marinate salmon in salsa for 4-48 hours.
2.  Put salmon on a baking sheet with non-stick cooking spray. Pour marinade over filets. Season. Top with crushed pineapple. Bake at 400°for approximately 15 minutes or until thermometer reaches 145°
