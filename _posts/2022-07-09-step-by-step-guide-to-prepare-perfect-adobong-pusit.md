##### The ingredients needed to make Adobong Pusit:

1.  Prepare squid
2.  Prepare 3 pcs garlic cloves
3.  Prepare 1 onion
4.  Take 1 tomato
5.  Make ready 2 part soy sauce
6.  Take 1 part vinegar
7.  Make ready 2 part oyster sauce
8.  Get salt
9.  Make ready pepper
10.  Take vegetable oil
11.  Make ready water

##### Instructions to make Adobong Pusit:

1.  Heat vegetable oil in pan then saute garlic, onions and tomato.
2.  Add in squid and cook for a minute.
3.  Put in vinegar, don't stir. Cook for a minute, add in soy sauce and oyster sauce. Add water as needed.
4.  Season with salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!
