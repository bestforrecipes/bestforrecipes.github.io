2.  Get 200 grams string beans
3.  Get 200 grams canned white beans
4.  Prepare 150 ml natural yogurt
5.  Get 3 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
6.  Prepare 3 garlic cloves
7.  Get 2 tsp lemon juice
8.  Make ready 1/4 tbsp salt
9.  Make ready 400 grams canned tuna
10.  Prepare 1 onion, cut into thin slices
11.  Get 2 tbsp fresh parsley
