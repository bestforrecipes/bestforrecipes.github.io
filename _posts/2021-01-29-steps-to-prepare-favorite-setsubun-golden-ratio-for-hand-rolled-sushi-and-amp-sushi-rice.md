##### The ingredients needed to make Setsubun: Golden Ratio for Hand-Rolled Sushi & Sushi Rice:

1.  Prepare 540 ml White rice
2.  Make ready Vinegar Mix
3.  Make ready 4 tbsp Vinegar
4.  Take 3 tbsp Sugar
5.  Make ready 2 tsp Salt
6.  Take Fillings
7.  Prepare 1 Tuna (sashimi quality)
8.  Make ready 1 Amberjack (buri)
9.  Make ready 1 Salmon
10.  Prepare 1 Shrimp
11.  Prepare 1 Negitoro (minced fatty tuna with scallions)
12.  Make ready 1 Avocado
13.  Get 1/2 Cucumber
14.  Take 3 Imitation crab sticks
15.  Take 1/2 packet Daikon radish sprouts
16.  Take 1/2 can Canned tuna mixed with mayonnaise
17.  Prepare 1 tbsp Umeboshi paste
18.  Prepare 1 Mentaiko spicy salted pollack or cod roe
19.  Prepare 1 pack Natto (fermented soy beans)
20.  Take 10 leaves Shiso leaves
21.  Make ready 10 leaves Leafy lettuce
22.  Make ready 1 all Accompaniments with the sashimi packs
23.  Get 1 Mayonnaise・wasabi・soy sauce
24.  Make ready 30 sheets Nori seaweed (large sheet cut into 4)

##### Steps to make Setsubun: Golden Ratio for Hand-Rolled Sushi & Sushi Rice:

1.  \[Vinegar mix\] Put everything in a pan and turn on the heat to medium. Stir until the sugar and salt has dissolved and the mix is transparent.
2.  Cook the rice with a little less water than usual. I don't have a sushi rice tub, so I just transfer the cooked rice to a bowl. Add the vinegar mix and mix.
3.  Place the fillings on dishes, and then just roll and eat.
4.  \[Fillings\] Roll whatever you like, such as shrimp & avocado; tuna mayo & daikon radish sprouts; shiso & umeboshi & cucumber; fresh tuna & avocado
5.  The accompaniments that come in the sashimi packs can also be used as fillings, or just pour over dressing and eat like a salad.
