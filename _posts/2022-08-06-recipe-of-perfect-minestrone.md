##### Instructions to make MINESTRONE:

1.  Using a LARGE crockpot, place every thing BUT the uncooked pasta in the crockpot in the order of listed ingredients.
2.  After everything but the pasta is in the crockpot, cook for 4 hours on crockpot "high" setting.
3.  Prepare water in a sauce pan for boiling pasta. Add a tablespoon of olive oil and a pinch os salt to the water.
4.  When water comes to a boil, drop pasta in and boil for 1 minute less than the "al dente" instructions on the package.
5.  When pasta is done, rinse thoroughly through a strainer with COLD water and set it aside.
6.  When soup has cooked for the 4 hours, add the cooked pasta quickly, and cook on crockpot high setting for another 1/2 hour.
7.  At the end of cooking time, keep soup on crockpot "warm" setting until ready to serve. (Serve immediately when possible.)
8.  Serve with garlic chessy bread.
