##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood Cheese Ball:

1.  Make ready 1 packages imitation crab meat (flake style)
2.  Take 2 blocks cream cheese
3.  Prepare 1 Old Bay seasoning to taste
4.  Make ready 1 garlic powder to taste

##### Instructions to make Seafood Cheese Ball:

1.  Set cream cheese out to soften
2.  Add Old Bay, and garlic to cream cheese and mix well
3.  Flake imitation crab meat and fold into cream cheese
4.  Form into a ball and refrigerate for at least a hr.
5.  Serve with your favorite crackers or chips.
