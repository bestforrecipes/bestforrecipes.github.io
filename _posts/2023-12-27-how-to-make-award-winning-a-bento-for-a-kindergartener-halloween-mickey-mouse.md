##### The ingredients needed to make A Bento for a Kindergartener (Halloween Mickey Mouse):

1.  Make ready 1 Hot cooked rice
2.  Prepare 1 stick Imitation crab stick
3.  Get 1 Nori (dried seaweed sheet)

##### Instructions to make A Bento for a Kindergartener (Halloween Mickey Mouse):

1.  Mold the rice into a ghost shape. Wrap two small rice balls in nori seaweed.
2.  Place the shapes from step 1 into the middle of the bento, and pack in the side dishes.
3.  Make the hat by cutting a piece of the crab stick. To make the brim, use a smaller part of the crab stick.
4.  Cut out the eyes, nose, and mouth from the nori seaweed, and stick on the face.
5.  Cut off a bit of the imitation crab stick, and use it for Mickey's tongue! Done!
