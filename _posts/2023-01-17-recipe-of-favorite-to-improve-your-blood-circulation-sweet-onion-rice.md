##### The ingredients needed to make To Improve Your Blood Circulation: Sweet Onion Rice:

1.  Get 160 grams Sweet new harvest onion
2.  Make ready 360 ml Uncooked white rice
3.  Get 3 tbsp Sakura shrimp
4.  Prepare 1 Aburaage
5.  Prepare 1 tbsp ★Cooking sake
6.  Make ready 1 tsp ★Sesame oil
7.  Prepare 1 tbsp ★Mirin
8.  Make ready 1/2 tsp each ★Salt, soy sauce

##### Steps to make To Improve Your Blood Circulation: Sweet Onion Rice:

1.  Rinse the rice and soak in water for about 30 minutes.
2.  Slice the onion to 5 mm width. Pour hot water over the aburaage to drain excess oil, and cut into 5 mm wide strips.
3.  Put the rice from Step 1 in a rice cooker. Add water up to the 2 cup mark, add the ingredients from Step 2, and the ★ ingredients. Stir well and set the rice cooker to cook.
