##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Fried Rice:

1.  Make ready 300 gr rice
2.  Take 1 egg
3.  Make ready 3 tbsp Vegetables oil
4.  Make ready 1 tbsp garlic oil or sesame oil
5.  Make ready 1 tsp seasoning
6.  Make ready 1 tbsp tomatoes sauce
7.  Make ready 1 tbsp sweet sauce
8.  Get 1 tbsp rice wine
9.  Prepare to taste Salt
10.  Take Spices
11.  Take 3 cloves garlic
12.  Get 5 shallots
13.  Make ready 2 stalks spring onion
14.  Make ready 1 tsp dried shrimps (ebi)
15.  Take 2 small chilies

##### Instructions to make Simple Fried Rice:

1.  Prepare the spices and ingredients
2.  Chopping spices
3.  Grinding until become paste
4.  Saute spices with oil till fragrant. Add rice and all ingredients. Fried till dried and cook. Serve.
