##### The ingredients needed to make Cocktail Bean Salad:

1.  Prepare 80 to 100 grams Mixed beans
2.  Make ready 40 grams Onion
3.  Make ready 50 grams Chicken tenders (or shrimp, scallops, salmon, etc.)
4.  Make ready 30 to 50 grams Yogurt Mayonnaise (See
5.  Make ready 1/4 tsp ✿ Krazy Salt (seasoned salt)
6.  Get 5 grams ✿ Honey

##### Instructions to make Cocktail Bean Salad:

1.  Put the chicken tender in a heatproof container, pour over a little wine (not listed in the ingredients) and steam-cook in the microwave. When it's cooked, let it cool down and shred it.
2.  Slice the onion thinly. Sprinkle with a little salt and massage with your hands. Rinse in water, then squeeze out well.
3.  Put the mixed beans, onion and chicken tender in a bowl, and mix well with the ✿ ingredients. Done!
4.  The version in the photo has 4 black tiger shrimp that were steamed and cut into 8 mm or so piece instead of the chicken.
5.  To make the yogurt mayonnaise: 50 g of drained yogurt + 10 g of mayonnaise, mixed together. It's refreshing and healthy.
