##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Avocado Tuna Salad:

1.  Get 2 avocado
2.  Make ready 1 can tuna flakes in water
3.  Take 3 tomatoes
4.  Make ready 1 large onion
5.  Take 1 black pepper to taste
6.  Get 1 juice from 1 small lemon

##### Instructions to make Creamy Avocado Tuna Salad:

1.  Chop onions and tomatoes.
2.  Drain water from tuna and mix with onion and tomatoes
3.  Sprinkle with black pepper
4.  Chop avocado and add to tuna mixture. Don't worry about stirring too much
5.  Lightly drizzle lemon juice over the salad and top with more black pepper
6.  Enjoy with warm wholewheat bread or alone!
