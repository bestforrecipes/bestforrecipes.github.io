2.  Take 4 boiled eggs (chopped)
3.  Take salt
4.  Prepare pepper
5.  Prepare 1 cup mayo
6.  Take 2 tbs. yellow mustard
7.  Get paprika
8.  Make ready 2 tbs. white sugar

##### Steps to make Tasty Tuna:
