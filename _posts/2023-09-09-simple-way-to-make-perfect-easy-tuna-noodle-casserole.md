##### The ingredients needed to make Easy tuna noodle casserole:

1.  Take cream of mushroom soup (10 3/4oz)
2.  Prepare condensed or regular milk
3.  Get baby sweet peas
4.  Take tuna,drained
5.  Take medium egg noodles (about 6oz)
6.  Get seasoned bread crumbs
7.  Take olive oil (or other oil)
8.  Prepare onion powder
9.  Get garlic powder
10.  Take salt
11.  Get melted butter

##### Instructions to make Easy tuna noodle casserole:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°
2.  In a large pot add the olive oil, onion powder, garlic powder,and salt.
3.  Fill the pot half way with water and bring to a boil.
4.  Add noodles and boil for 6 minutes. Drain noodles in a colander.
5.  In a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish, mix mushroom soup,milk,peas, drained tuna, and cooked noodles.
6.  Bake for 20 minutes.
7.  While the casserole is baking, mix bread crumbs and butter together in a small bowl.
8.  Take out casserole and stir. Sprinkle bread crumb mixture evenly over casserole and bake for 6 more minutes or until crumbs are lightly browned.
