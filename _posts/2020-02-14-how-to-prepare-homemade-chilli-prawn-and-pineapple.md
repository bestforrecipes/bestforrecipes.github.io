##### The ingredients needed to make Chilli Prawn and Pineapple:

1.  Prepare 1 medium onion chopped
2.  Get 4 red chilies chopped
3.  Make ready 2 tblsp massaman curry paste
4.  Take 1 cup pineapple juice
5.  Prepare 1 1/2 cup fish stock/ prawn heads
6.  Make ready 2 cups green beans halved
7.  Get 1 red pepper chunky chopped
8.  Prepare 200 g baby corn halved lengthwise
9.  Take 1 small pineapple chopped
10.  Take 400 g prawns
11.  Take 2 tblsp coriander chopped

##### Steps to make Chilli Prawn and Pineapple:

1.  On medium heat fry onion and chilli. Once soft add curry paste and fry till fragrant. Add pineapple juice and water and prawn heads (or stock) bring to boil and simmer for 8-10 minutes.
2.  Add red pepper, green beans, baby corn and stir. Remove prawn heads. Add pineapple and simmer for 5 minutes. Add prawns and cover. Cook for 90 seconds.
