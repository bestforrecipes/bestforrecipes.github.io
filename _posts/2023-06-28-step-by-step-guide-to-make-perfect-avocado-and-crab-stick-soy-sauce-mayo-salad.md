##### The ingredients needed to make Avocado and Crab Stick Soy Sauce Mayo Salad:

1.  Prepare 1 packet Imitation crab sticks
2.  Make ready 1 Avocado
3.  Prepare 2 tbsp ● Mayonnaise
4.  Prepare 1 tsp ● Soy sauce
5.  Make ready 2 cm in tube ●Wasabi
6.  Make ready 1 Lettuce for decoration (optional)

##### Instructions to make Avocado and Crab Stick Soy Sauce Mayo Salad:

1.  Mix the ● seasonings in a bowl. Cut the avocado into bite sizes. Add the avocado and crab sticks into the mixed seasonings and toss. Transfer to a serving dish.
