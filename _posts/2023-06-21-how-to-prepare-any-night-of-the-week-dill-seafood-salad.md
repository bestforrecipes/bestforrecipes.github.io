##### The ingredients needed to make Dill Seafood Salad:

1.  Get 3 stalks celery, finely diced
2.  Take 1 small yellow onion, finely diced
3.  Get 1 small red onion, finely diced
4.  Make ready 2 tbsp Old Bay Seasoning
5.  Prepare juice from two lemons
6.  Take 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
7.  Prepare 2 tbsp butter
8.  Prepare 1 tbsp cajun seasoning
9.  Make ready 20 oz shrimp, cooked, deveined, tail off, cut into 1/4" chunks
10.  Make ready 10 oz bay scallops, halved
11.  Take 1/2 cup mayonaisse
12.  Prepare 1/4 cup sour cream
13.  Prepare 12 Buns

##### Steps to make Dill Seafood Salad:

1.  In a cast iron skillet melt 1/2 tbsp butter over medium-high heat. Work your protein in small batches and pat it dry prior to cooking to minimize temperature drop on the pan. Cook all the seafood and set aside.
2.  Combine all the other ingredients in a large bowl.
3.  Mix everything together and refrigerate for about 30 minutes. You can serve this on bread or alone. Throw some toppings on; lettuce and tomato, bacon, avocado, onion straws. I like to use Hawaiian sweet rolls with onions straws for some crunch.
