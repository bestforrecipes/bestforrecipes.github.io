##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Shala:

1.  Prepare 12 large blue crabs
2.  Make ready 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
3.  Prepare 31 oz can of tomato sauce
4.  Make ready 2 large stalks of celery chopped
5.  Make ready 1 large onion, chopped
6.  Get 1/2 cup sweet peppers chopped
7.  Make ready 1 tbsp minced garlic
8.  Get 1 tsp sweet basil
9.  Take 1 tsp whole leaf oregano
10.  Get 2 tbsp olive oil
11.  Take 4 bay leaves

##### Steps to make Crab Shala:

1.  Add the following ingredients into a pan. Celery, peppers, onions, 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Sautee till tender
2.  In a pot add theses ingredients. Basil, oregano, tomato sauce, crushed tomatos, bay leaves, garlic, and crabs. Bring to boil add salt to taste.
3.  Mix the onions and peppers with shala let it simmer covered for 30 minutes. Stirring occasionally.
4.  Serve over rice or noodles. Serve hope you enjoy!
