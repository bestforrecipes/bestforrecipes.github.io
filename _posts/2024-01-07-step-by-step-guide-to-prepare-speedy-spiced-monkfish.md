5.  Prepare 1/2 tsp fine salt

7.  Take 2 tbsp. butter
8.  Take 1 pinch saffron
9.  Get about 50ml veg stock made with a stock cube or fresh
10.  Make ready 2 tbsp. crème fraiche
11.  Take 1 little chopped parsley
