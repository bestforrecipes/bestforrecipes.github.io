##### The ingredients needed to make Open Faces Crab Sandwiches:

1.  Make ready 1 loaf sourdough bread
2.  Make ready 2 dungeness crab, picked
3.  Make ready 2 tbsp butter
4.  Make ready 1 clove garlic
5.  Get 1 packages cream cheese
6.  Get 8 slice cheese (swiss, cheddar, or another favorite)
7.  Take 1 salt and pepper to taste

##### Instructions to make Open Faces Crab Sandwiches:

1.  Melt butter in large skillet. Add crab, garlic, salt and pepper. Cook until just warm.
2.  Toast bread in a toaster or broiler.
3.  Immediately spread cream cheese on each piece of bread.
4.  Pile as much crab on each piece as it can hold.
5.  Place a slice or two of cheese over the crab and put on a cookie sheet.
6.  Put the sandwiches in the broiler for 3-5 minutes or until the cheese is melted. Enjoy!
