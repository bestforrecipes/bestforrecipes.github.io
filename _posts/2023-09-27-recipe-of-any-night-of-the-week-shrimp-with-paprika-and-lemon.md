##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp With Paprika and Lemon:

1.  Get 1 1/2 lb Shrimp
2.  Make ready 2 Lemon
3.  Prepare 5 tbsp Paprika
4.  Make ready 1 clove Garlic
5.  Take 1/3 cup Olive oil
6.  Make ready 3 tbsp Butter
7.  Prepare 1 Parsley

##### Steps to make Shrimp With Paprika and Lemon:

1.  Add the butter, olive oil and garlic to a fry pan and let it heat for two minutes.
2.  Add the shrimp when the garlic is almost frying. Close with pan cover and turn shrimps around after 5 minutes and wait for 5 more minutes.
3.  Add the paprika over shrimps. Close pan and wait for one minute.
4.  Spread fresh parsley on the top.
5.  Mix shrimp around and serve with lemon on the side.
