##### The ingredients needed to make Corny Crab Chowder:

1.  Get 8 oz fresh crab meat or refrigerated canned crab meat
2.  Take 2 T olive oil
3.  Prepare 5-6 russet potatoes, peeled and chopped (about 13 oz)
4.  Get 1 small onion, chopped
5.  Take 48 oz low-sodium chicken broth
6.  Prepare 1-1/2 c frozen corn
7.  Get 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
8.  Make ready 1 T dried basil
9.  Prepare 1/2 c half and half
10.  Get 1/2 tsp salt
11.  Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper
12.  Make ready chopped fresh parsley or dried oregano to garnish
