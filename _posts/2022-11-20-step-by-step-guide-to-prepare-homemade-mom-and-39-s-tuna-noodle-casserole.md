##### The ingredients needed to make Mom's Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Get 1 canned tuna
2.  Get 1 cream of mushroom soup
3.  Take 1 pickle relish
4.  Make ready 1 red bell pepper; julienne
5.  Make ready 1 green bell pepper; julienne
6.  Get 1 celery; small dice
7.  Make ready 1 egg noodles
8.  Take 1 mozzarella; shredded
9.  Make ready 1 cheddar; shredded
