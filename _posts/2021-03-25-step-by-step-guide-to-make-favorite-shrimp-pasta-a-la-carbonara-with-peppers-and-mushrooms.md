##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Pasta a la Carbonara with Peppers and Mushrooms:

1.  Prepare part 1
2.  Get 2 quart boiling water
3.  Take 1 tbsp olive oil
4.  Get 1 tsp salt
5.  Prepare 12 oz pasta
6.  Prepare part 2
7.  Make ready 4 each eggs
8.  Take 1/4 cup dry white wine
9.  Get 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
10.  Get part 3
11.  Prepare 8 oz bacon
12.  Prepare 2 tbsp minced garlic
13.  Make ready 2 chopped colored peppers
14.  Make ready 1 lb shrimp
15.  Get 1 tbsp chopped parsley
16.  Take 1 tbsp salt
17.  Take 1/2 tsp black pepper
18.  Make ready 8 oz mushrooms, chopped
19.  Prepare 1 cup chopped olives(optional)

##### Instructions to make Shrimp Pasta a la Carbonara with Peppers and Mushrooms:

1.  Place the pasta, oil and salt in the water. Cook until a la dente.
2.  Mix eggs, wine and cheese together in a small bowl.
3.  Dice bacon and cook until crispy. Drain off most of the grease. Add garlic, seasonings and shrimp and saute in bacon over medium heat until cooked. Add the peppers and mushrooms towards the end.
4.  Drain pasta and put back in the pot with the egg mixture. Stir with the hot pasta until the eggs are cooked by the heat of the pasta.
5.  In a large pasta dish toss pasta with bacon, shrimp and vegetables.
