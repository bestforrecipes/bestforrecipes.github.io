##### The ingredients needed to make Upgraded Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Take 16 oz medium macaroni shells
2.  Get 12 oz light chunk tuna
3.  Make ready 6 medium white mushrooms
4.  Get 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
5.  Prepare 1 small onion, chopped
6.  Make ready 4 clove minced fresh garlic
7.  Take 4 tbsp butter
8.  Make ready 1 can condensed cream of celery soup
9.  Get 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
10.  Take 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan
11.  Prepare 12 oz frozen peas and carrots
12.  Prepare 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella
