##### The ingredients needed to make Buffalo Shrimp:

1.  Get deviened shrimp w/tail on.
2.  Make ready eggs
3.  Make ready milk
4.  Take flour
5.  Prepare favorite hot sauce (mine Texas Pete)
6.  Prepare unsalted butter
7.  Make ready paprika
8.  Prepare of garlic powder
9.  Prepare of black pepper
10.  Get Oil for frying

##### Steps to make Buffalo Shrimp:

1.  Prep shrimp; removing the shell, leaving tail on.
2.  Mix 2 eggs and 1 cup milk together.
3.  Prep a plastic bag with 1 1/2 cup of flour.
4.  Turn on stove and heat oil on medium high heat. About 375°F.
5.  Put shrimp in fridge for about 5 minutes while the oil heats up.
6.  In a small sauce pan mix together the hot sauce, butter, paprika, garlic powder and pepper. Set aside and keep warm on low.
7.  Drip the shrimp in the egg mixture and then in the flour. Placing the shrimp in the flour individually, coating each one before frying.
8.  Add shrimp to the pan and fry in batches of six or more but don't over crowed them.
9.  Drain shrimp and toss is the sauce that you set aside. Enjoy
