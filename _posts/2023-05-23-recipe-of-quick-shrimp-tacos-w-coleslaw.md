##### Instructions to make Shrimp Tacos w/ Coleslaw:

2.  Pre-heat oven on 400°F
3.  Season with Adobo, Mild taco seasoning, mustard, parsley, olive garden salad etc
4.  Place in a pan, making sure shrimp are flat so it can cook
