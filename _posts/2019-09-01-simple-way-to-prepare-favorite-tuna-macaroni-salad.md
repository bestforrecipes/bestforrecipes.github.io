##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Take 4-6 servings of your favorite shaped pasta noodles cooked
2.  Make ready 1 can tuna
3.  Get 1 can sweet green peas
4.  Make ready 1 cup miracle whip
5.  Take 1/2 onion finely chopped
6.  Make ready 1 teaspoon fresh chopped parsley
7.  Take 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella or grated parmesan
8.  Get Desired Amounts of the following;
9.  Get Dash Mrs
10.  Get Black or white garlic
11.  Take Salt
12.  Take Pepper
13.  Prepare Celery seed

##### Steps to make Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Boil noodles to soften. Strain and pour into a large mixing bowl. Fold and gently mix in with all other ingredients.
2.  Add seasonings and spices to taste. Then cool in the fridge.
