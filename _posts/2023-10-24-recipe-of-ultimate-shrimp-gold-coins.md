##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Gold Coins:

1.  Make ready 8-10 king size or jumbo shrimps
2.  Take 1 egg
3.  Get as needed Cornstarch
4.  Prepare Little water
5.  Prepare 1/4 th tsp baking powder
6.  Take 1/4 tsp garlic powder or paste
7.  Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
8.  Take as needed Breadcrumbs
9.  Take Refined oil for deep frying
10.  Make ready To taste salt

##### Steps to make Shrimp Gold Coins:

1.  Make a batter with little water, cornstarch,egg, baking powder,1 tsp warm oil,little salt,black pepper powder and garlic powder.Beat well.
2.  Dip shrimps in the batter.The shrimps should be well coated. Roll in breadcrumbs.Heat oil and deep fry shrimps till they are golden brown.Serve hot with green chilli sauce.
