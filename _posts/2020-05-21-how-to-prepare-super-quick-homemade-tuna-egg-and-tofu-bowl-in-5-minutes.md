##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna, Egg and Tofu Bowl in 5 Minutes:

1.  Prepare 1 can Canned tuna (light)
2.  Make ready 1/2 block Tofu
3.  Get 1 Egg
4.  Take 1 bowl worth Cooked rice
5.  Make ready 1 tsp Dashi stock granules
6.  Prepare 1 tsp Oyster sauce
7.  Prepare 1 tsp Soy sauce

##### Instructions to make Tuna, Egg and Tofu Bowl in 5 Minutes:

1.  Heat a skillet and add the lightly drained tuna and tofu. Sauté while crumbling the tofu.
2.  Add the seasonings and beaten egg and cook until the egg is half way done. Turn off the heat and cook it through in residual heat.
3.  Serve it on a bowl of rice and it is done!
