##### The ingredients needed to make Homemade LOBSTER mac and cheese:

1.  Take 3 medium cooked lobsters
2.  Take 4 cup cheese (your preferences)
3.  Get 2 cup milk
4.  Make ready 2 1/2 cup cooked pasta
5.  Take 2 1/3 tbsp all-purpose flour
6.  Make ready 2 stick butter
7.  Prepare 2 1/2 tsp rosemary
8.  Make ready salt

It's a mixture of fat (usually butter) and flour that is used to create thick sauces. Serve this homemade mac and cheese recipe all by itself for an easy lunch or dinner! My kids love it with chopped up hot dogs. Any pasta shape works here, but corkscrews are our favorite for holding the warm, velvety sauce inside.

##### Steps to make Homemade LOBSTER mac and cheese:

1.  Remove all meat from lobsters (be careful not to cut yourself, maybe use gloves) cut meat into chunks and set aside.
2.  In a medium saucepan combine the flour butter and milk to make a roux. 1 1/2 stick of Butter first(saving the remaining butter to top), then flour. Cook a little making sure not to scorch. Then add milk. It will get thick but you may need to add more milk.
3.  To the roux add rosemary salt and cheese
4.  Finally add lobster meat and pasta. Add all ingredients to baking pan. Sprinkle the top with bread crumbs and add butter on top.
5.  Bake in oven set to 400° for 15 mins or until top is browned and butter is melted.
6.  All measurements are approximate may have to adjust accordingly. .
7.  I made hush puppies and crab cakes to go along with this but it can easily be the main dish!! Enjoy!
