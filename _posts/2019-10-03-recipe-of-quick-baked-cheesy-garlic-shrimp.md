##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Cheesy Garlic Shrimp:

1.  Get 1 kg large SHRIMP cleaned, trimmed
2.  Take 1 cup grated cheddar CHEESE
3.  Prepare 1 cup grated quick melt CHEESE
4.  Get 1/2 cup BREAD CRUMBS
5.  Make ready 1/4 cup salted BUTTER
6.  Make ready 5 gloves minced GARLIC
7.  Make ready 1 pc minced red BELL PEPPER
8.  Prepare 1 pc minced green BELL PEPPER

##### Steps to make Baked Cheesy Garlic Shrimp:

1.  Slice the back of each shrimp from head to tail. Remove the black vein and open shrimp like butterfly. Set aside.
2.  In a sauce pan, melt butter. When the butter is melted, remove from heat.
3.  Combine garlic and bell pepper with the melted butter.
4.  In a separate bowl, mix all the cheese and bread crumbs.
5.  Prepare baking tray lined with parchment (or foil). Arrange shrimps side by side, meat facing up. Season with salt and pepper.
6.  Place butter mixture on each shrimp followed by the cheese mixture.
7.  Place in a pre-heated oven and bake at 180 degrees Celsius for 8 to 10 minutes.
