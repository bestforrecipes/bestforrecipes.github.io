##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Salmon:

1.  Take 2 clove garlic
2.  Make ready 6 tbsp light olive oil
3.  Make ready 1 tsp dried basil
4.  Get 1 tsp salt/pepper
5.  Get 1 tbsp lemon juice
6.  Take 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
7.  Take 2 salmon fillets (6 oz each)

Baked salmon seasoned with garlic, roasted lemons, and fresh herbs. A healthy family dinner ready in a snap. Cooking in the oven at a moderate temperature and then broiling at the end ensures moist. The key to good salmon is not overthinking it.

##### Steps to make Baked Salmon:

1.  In medium glass bowl, prepare marinade by mixing: garlic,olive oil, basil, salt/pepper, lemon juice, and parsley.
2.  Place salmon fillets in medium baking dish, and cover with marinade. Marinated in the fridge for 1 hour, turning occasionally.
3.  Preheat oven to 375°F
4.  Place fillets in aluminum foil, cover with marinade, and seal. Place salmon in glass dish, and bake 35-45 minutes, until easily flakes with fork.

Today, all we're doing is rubbing the fillets with a little oil and sprinkling them with salt and pepper before popping them in the.
