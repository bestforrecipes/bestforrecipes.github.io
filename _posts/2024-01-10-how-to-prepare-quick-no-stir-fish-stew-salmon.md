##### The ingredients needed to make No Stir Fish Stew (salmon):

1.  Get Fish Seasoning
2.  Prepare 3 teaspoons cumin
3.  Take 3/4 teaspoons salt
4.  Make ready 1 lime juiced
5.  Make ready 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
6.  Prepare 5 gloves of garlics minced
7.  Get 1 splash water
8.  Get 500 g Salmon fillet chopped into big peices(half your palm)
9.  Prepare Other ingredients
10.  Make ready Olive oil
11.  Get 1 onion sliced
12.  Make ready 1 zucchini cubed or bell peppers
13.  Take 1 carrot cubed (optional)
14.  Take 1/2 cane of crushed tomatoes or 1 sliced tomato
15.  Make ready 220 ml almond milk or any milk or cream of your choice
16.  Make ready 1 handful parsley or coriander
17.  Prepare Salt and pepper

##### Steps to make No Stir Fish Stew (salmon):

1.  Marinade fish chunks for at least 1 hr
2.  In a pot, add some oil, stack your in ingredients in the order of: onion, vegetables, tomatoes, marinaded fish, milk, parsley, salt and pepper
3.  Bring mixture to boil and simmer in low heat for 20 minutes. Serve with anything that could soak up graveys
