##### The ingredients needed to make Peppered Shrimp & Lobster:

1.  Make ready precooked chopped lobster tail
2.  Take chili paste
3.  Get McCormick hot shot black & red seasoning
4.  Take course ground black pepper
5.  Get garlic salt
6.  Take finely diced serrano chili
7.  Prepare olive oil
8.  Prepare butter
9.  Prepare minced garlic
10.  Prepare cleaned & devained shrimp

##### Steps to make Peppered Shrimp & Lobster:

1.  start by heating a large skillet with butter, garlic & olive oil cook on med heat about 1 minute add in diced serrano chili cook another minute. add in shrimp & chopped lobster and all seasonings along with chili paste. Cook 6-7 minutes till shrimp are nice & pink. This is a spicy dish Enjoy!
