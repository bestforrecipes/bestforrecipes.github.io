##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Mustard Salmon:

1.  Get 1/4 Green pepper
2.  Take 1/4 Red pepper
3.  Prepare 4 shrimps
4.  Make ready 1/4 onion
5.  Get 1 salmon fillet
6.  Make ready 1 tsp honey mustard
7.  Get 1 pinch salt & pepper

##### Steps to make Honey Mustard Salmon:

1.  Chop red and green pepper and the onion and cook them until onion browns. Add salt and pepper too
2.  Preheat oven to 180C and spread the honey mustard over the salmon
3.  When the oven gets warm enough put the shrimps and the rest of the ingredients into it. Cook it all for about 8-10 minutes
