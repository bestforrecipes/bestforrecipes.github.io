##### The ingredients needed to make Alaskan Smoked Salmon Spread:

1.  Take 18 oz smoked salmon, skinless, chopped fine
2.  Get 16 oz cream cheese
3.  Prepare 4 Tbls green onions, chopped fine
4.  Take 2 tsp worshershire
5.  Make ready 2 Tbls fresh dill, chopped fine
6.  Take 1 Tbls capers, chopped
7.  Take 1 tsp lemon juice
8.  Get 1 tsp hot pepper sauce
9.  Get 1/4 cup half n half

##### Instructions to make Alaskan Smoked Salmon Spread:

1.  Mix all ingredients.
2.  Serve with crackers
3.  Allowing the salmon spread to chill in the refrigerator overnight will meld the flavors. However, it is delicious enough that you can enjoy it now!
