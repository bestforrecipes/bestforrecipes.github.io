##### The ingredients needed to make Coconut Milk Fish Curry:

1.  Get 6-7 pieces Rohu fish ( you can use prawn in the place of Rohu
2.  Take 1 cup fresh coconut milk
3.  Make ready 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
4.  Get To taste Salt
5.  Prepare To tsste Garam masala
6.  Take 1 chopped tomato
7.  Get 1 chopped onion
8.  Get 1 Tbsp chopped ginger and garlic
9.  Take as needed curry leaves
10.  Get 2-3 green chilli slices
11.  Get as needed Chopped coriander
12.  Get 1tsp Coriander & Cumin Powder
13.  Make ready As needed Refined oil
14.  Take As needed Water

##### Instructions to make Coconut Milk Fish Curry:

1.  Fry the fishes first after marinating with turmeric & salt
2.  Again hit a pan. Fry onion & chopped ginger-garlic till golden brown.
3.  After that add tomatoes & coriander, cumin powder. Add 2tbsp water. Cook till the tomatoes became soft. Add curry leaves.
4.  Add coconut milk, salt & turmeric. Bring it a boil & add fish fry.
5.  Add garam masala & reduce the gravy. Add chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
