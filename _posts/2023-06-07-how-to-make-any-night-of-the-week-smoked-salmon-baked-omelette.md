##### The ingredients needed to make Smoked Salmon Baked Omelette:

1.  Get olive oil
2.  Make ready small onion diced
3.  Take garlic clove minced
4.  Prepare large handful of spinach
5.  Get smoked salmon
6.  Take eggs
7.  Make ready cherry tomatoes halved
8.  Get dairy free milk
9.  Make ready chives
10.  Get dairy free cheese grated

##### Steps to make Smoked Salmon Baked Omelette:

1.  Preheat the oven to 180 oC - Add the olive oil to a shallow pan over a medium heat - Saute the onion, tomatoes, spinach and garlic for a couple of minutes
2.  Whisk together the eggs, milk, chives, cheese and season with salt and pepper - Stir in the cooked vegetables and flaked salmon
3.  Divide between two individual casserole dishes and place in the oven - Bake for 25 minutes or until the eggs are set
