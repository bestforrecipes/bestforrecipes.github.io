##### The ingredients needed to make Tinklee's Lemon, Garlic and Pepper Salmon:

1.  Prepare 2 salmon fillets
2.  Make ready 2 tbsp butter
3.  Prepare 1 zest of 1 lemon
4.  Make ready 1 clove garlic (minced)
5.  Take 1/4 tsp ground black pepper

##### Instructions to make Tinklee's Lemon, Garlic and Pepper Salmon:

1.  Mix softened butter, lemon zest, garlic, and pepper
2.  prepare a shallow baking dish, place your 2 salmon fillets, spread with butter spread, half on each fillet
3.  Broil about 7" from heat, Broil for 8-12mins, then turn off heat and rest for 10mins in oven, enjoy
