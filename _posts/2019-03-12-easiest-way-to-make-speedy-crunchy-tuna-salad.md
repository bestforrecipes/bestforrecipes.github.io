##### The ingredients needed to make Crunchy Tuna Salad:

1.  Make ready 1 can tuna fish
2.  Make ready 1/2 cup miracle whip
3.  Get 1 tablespoon pickle relish
4.  Get 1 teaspoon Mustard
5.  Prepare 1 small green onion
6.  Get 1 small yellow onion
7.  Get 6-8 green olives
8.  Take 1 small celery rib
9.  Prepare 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
10.  Get 1 teaspoon celery seed
11.  Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
12.  Prepare 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
13.  Make ready 1/2 teaspoon pepper

##### Steps to make Crunchy Tuna Salad:

1.  Chop All Veggies to desired sized pieces. Mix together in a bowl with pickle relish, mustard, miracle whip, and parmesan cheese. \*(the green olives I used are feta cheese stuffed, my favorite! But any or none will do as well!)
2.  Open and drain tuna fish. Mix well into other ingredients.
3.  Add seasonings and desired spices if any. Mix Well.
4.  My Family enjoys this as "Toasted Tuna Sandwich" Night! We put it on Our favorite toast with crispy lettuce and it is Delicious! However I have also put this on Salads and Enjoyed it that way as Well! Yummo! Enjoy!
