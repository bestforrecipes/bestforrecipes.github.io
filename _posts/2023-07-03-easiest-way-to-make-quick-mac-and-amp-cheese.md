##### The ingredients needed to make Mac & Cheese:

1.  Get 16 oz Shell macaroni
2.  Prepare 8 oz cream cheese
3.  Make ready 8 oz mild cheddar cheese
4.  Prepare 8 oz sharp cheddar cheese
5.  Prepare 4 oz white cheddar cheese
6.  Get 8 oz Colby jack cheese
7.  Make ready 6 slices velveta cheese
8.  Make ready 3 cups buttermilk
9.  Get 3 tbs sour cream
10.  Get 2 tbs Morton nature seasoning
11.  Make ready 2 tsp garlic pepper
12.  Get 2 tsp season salt

##### Instructions to make Mac & Cheese:

1.  Set oven to low broil & start boiling salt water for the noodles.
2.  Start with a large sized sauce pan and add the buttermilk together with the cream cheese & sour cream until they are thoroughly mixed & melted over med/low heat. (Approx 10-20 mins)
3.  After the mixture has thickened up a bit start to add the cheese slowly, leaving the 8oz of Colby Jack cheese for the topping.
4.  Add the seasonings
5.  Sprinkle the leftover Colby Jack cheese on the macaroni and broil for 15 minutes or until the the cheese starts bubbling.
