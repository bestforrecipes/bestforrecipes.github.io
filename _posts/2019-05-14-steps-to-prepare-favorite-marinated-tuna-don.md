##### The ingredients needed to make Marinated Tuna Don:

1.  Prepare 150 grams Tuna (sashimi grade)
2.  Take 1 Green onions
3.  Take 1 Toasted sesame seeds
4.  Make ready 1 Sesame oil
5.  Take 1 Shredded nori seaweed
6.  Make ready 2 bowlfuls Plain steamed rice
7.  Take Seasonings
8.  Take 50 ml Soy sauce
9.  Get 25 ml Sake
10.  Take 25 ml Mirin
11.  Take 1 tsp Sugar
12.  Make ready 1 Wasabi

##### Instructions to make Marinated Tuna Don:

1.  Warm all the seasoning ingredients in a pot. Let cool, then dissolve the wasabi.
2.  Marinade the tuna for a while.
3.  Put the rice, tuna, sesame seeds, green onions, and chopped nori into a rice bowl, sprinkle with sesame oil, and serve.
