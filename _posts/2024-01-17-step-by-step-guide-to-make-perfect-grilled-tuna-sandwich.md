##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Tuna Sandwich:

1.  Prepare 4 Whole wheat Bread slices
2.  Make ready 4 tbsp Tuna (Crumbled)
3.  Prepare 1 small size Onion (Chopped)
4.  Make ready 1 Green Chilli (Chopped)
5.  Get 1 tbsp Green Bell Pepper (Chopped)
6.  Get 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
7.  Prepare 1 tbsp Chilli Garlic Chutney
8.  Prepare As needed Salad Seasoning
9.  Get 1/2 tsp Dry Oregano
10.  Take 1/2 tsp Red Chilli Flakes
11.  Prepare 1/2 tsp Mexican Seasoning
12.  Prepare As needed Butter

##### Instructions to make Grilled Tuna Sandwich:

1.  In a bowl, add tuna, onions, green chillies, green bell pepper, mayonnaise, red chilli garlic chutney, salad seasoning, red chilli flakes, oregano, mexican seasoning. Mix well and set aside.
2.  Take four bread slices and spread some butter on them. I have used whole wheat bread. Add the prepared tuna stuffing only on two slices.
3.  Cover with the remaining two bread slices on which butter has already been spread. Place the tuna sandwiches in a preheated griller and grill till crisp and golden.
4.  Remove and serve Grilled Tuna Sandwich immediately with red chilli garlic chutney or with any dip, chutney or sauce of your choice.
