##### The ingredients needed to make Meatless Kare-Kare (Peanut Sauce):

1.  Make ready firm Tofu, cubed
2.  Take Pechay
3.  Get medium Eggplant, cut 1in. lengthwise
4.  Make ready String beans, cut 1.5 in
5.  Prepare Garlic
6.  Get medium Onion
7.  Take pouch Kare-Kate mix (Mama Sitas), dissolved in 2 cups water
8.  Prepare Peanut butter
9.  Get Cooking oil
10.  Take Bagoong Alamang (Shrimp paste)

##### Steps to make Meatless Kare-Kare (Peanut Sauce):

1.  Fry tofu until golden brown, set aside
2.  Heat oil (1tbsp) in a sauce pan, sauté onion and garlic
3.  Pour in Kare-kare mix, simmer.
4.  Add in string beans, eggplant, tofu.
5.  Put peanut butter, mix until it dissolves
6.  Add pechay last when other vegetables are half cooked, simmer.
7.  Served with shrimp paste.
