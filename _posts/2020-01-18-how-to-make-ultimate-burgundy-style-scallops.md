##### The ingredients needed to make Burgundy-style Scallops:

1.  Take 2 Scallop (large and with shell)
2.  Get 1 stalk Fresh parsley
3.  Prepare 1 clove Garlic
4.  Make ready 15 grams Salted butter
5.  Prepare 2 tbsp Panko

##### Steps to make Burgundy-style Scallops:

1.  Remove the scallops from their shells and cut into 3 or 4 bite-sized pieces. The frill and coral taste delicious too, so I used them as well.
2.  Mince garlic and parsley, and mix with butter returned to room temperature. Place this mixture on top of your scallops, and top with panko powder.
3.  Place the scallops on the top rack of your oven and bake for 7 minutes at 430°F/220°C.
