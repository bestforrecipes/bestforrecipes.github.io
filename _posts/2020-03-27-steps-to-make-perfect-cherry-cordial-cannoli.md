##### The ingredients needed to make Cherry Cordial Cannoli:

1.  Make ready For the filling
2.  Get ricotta
3.  Take mascarpone cheese
4.  Get powdered sugar
5.  Take mini chocolate chips
6.  Make ready maraschino cherries chopped
7.  Take almond extract
8.  Take For shells
9.  Prepare all purpose flour
10.  Get granulated sugar
11.  Take cinnamon
12.  Prepare salt
13.  Prepare unsalted butter cut into tiny pieces
14.  Make ready your favorite red wine
15.  Make ready large egg yolk
16.  Prepare egg white
17.  Prepare Canola oil for frying

##### Instructions to make Cherry Cordial Cannoli:

1.  For Filling: combine everything in a bowl and fold together. Chill in fridge at least 45 min.
2.  For Shells: In a mixing bowl mix together flour sugar cinnamon and salt. Add butter and work it in with your fingers until it feels sandy.
3.  Add wine and egg yolk and stir untill it comes together. Knead until smooth, cut in half, roll out into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate 45 min to an hour.
4.  In a dutch oven, or large pot, bring canola oil up to 360°.
5.  Roll dough out very thin and cut into 3-4 inch circles. Spray cannoli forms with cooking spray and wrap circles around form, brushing the ends with egg white to hold them together.
6.  Fry untill deep golden brown. Pipe filling into shells as they cool. Repeat until you are out of filling and serve.
