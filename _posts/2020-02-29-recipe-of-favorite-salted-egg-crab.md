##### The ingredients needed to make Salted Egg Crab:

1.  Take crabs
2.  Prepare spring onion (slice)
3.  Take ginger (slice)
4.  Prepare SAUCE:
5.  Prepare salted egg yolk (steamed and mashed)
6.  Get butter
7.  Get oil
8.  Take garlic (minced)
9.  Get curry leaves
10.  Prepare eye-bird chilli (optional)
11.  Get evaporated milk (390 g)
12.  Get Salt
13.  Prepare Sugar

##### Steps to make Salted Egg Crab:

1.  Steam the crab with ginger and spring onion 12 mins. Put aside.
2.  Heat oil and butter. Add garlic, curry leaves and chilli until fragrant.
3.  Add evaporated milk, salt, pepper, mashed egg yolk. Stir and cook until the sauce gets thickened.
4.  Add the crab and toss well to coat. Ready to serve.
