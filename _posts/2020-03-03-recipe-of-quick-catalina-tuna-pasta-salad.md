##### The ingredients needed to make Catalina Tuna Pasta Salad:

1.  Get 1 box Elbow or Tri-color Pasta
2.  Get 2 can White Albacore Tuna
3.  Prepare 1 Green Pepper (Diced)
4.  Take 1 Onion (Chopped)
5.  Take 1 cup Classic Catalina Salad Dressing
6.  Get 2 tbsp Mayo
7.  Take 2 tbsp Sweet Relish
8.  Take 1 Salt and Pepper to taste

##### Steps to make Catalina Tuna Pasta Salad:

1.  Cook pasta according to package.
2.  Drain and place pasta and tuna in a large bowl
3.  Add onion, pepper, and relish to pasta
4.  Pour dressing and mayo on top and combine all the ingredient together
5.  Salt and pepper to taste
6.  Chill in fridge before serving
