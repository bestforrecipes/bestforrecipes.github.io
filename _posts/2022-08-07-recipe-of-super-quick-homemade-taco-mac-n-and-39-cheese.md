##### The ingredients needed to make Taco Mac n' Cheese:

1.  Make ready 1 box macaroni shells
2.  Take 1 lb ground beef
3.  Make ready 1/2 onion; diced
4.  Get 2 tsp garlic; minced
5.  Get 1 cup salsa
6.  Make ready 8 oz cream cheese
7.  Get 2 cups milk
8.  Take 1/2 cup sour cream
9.  Take 1 envelope taco seasoning
10.  Get 2 cups Fiesta Blend cheese

##### Instructions to make Taco Mac n' Cheese:

1.  Boil & drain noodles, set aside.
2.  Cook ground beef with onion, drain grease. Add minced garlic & cook for 5 more mins. Then add taco seasoning & salsa, cook for a couple more mins. Set aside.
3.  In seperate sauce pan add milk, cream cheese & sour cream, cook til cream cheese melts & whisk until smooth. Then add fiesta blend shredded cheese, whisk til that melts.
4.  Now combine noodles, hamburger mix, & cheese mixture, stir until everything is mixed together evenly. Sprinkle with some more cheese if you like & place in oven at 350° for 5-10 mins or until cheese melts on top. Serve & enjoy!
