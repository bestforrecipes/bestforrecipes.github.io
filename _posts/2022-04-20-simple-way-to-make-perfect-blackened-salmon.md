##### The ingredients needed to make Blackened Salmon:

1.  Get Salmon
2.  Get Salt
3.  Get ground black pepper
4.  Make ready olive oil, extra virgin
5.  Prepare lemon juice
6.  Get garlic powder
7.  Get onion powder

##### Instructions to make Blackened Salmon:

1.  In a mixing bowl add the following ingredients
2.  Massage into salmon
3.  Heat a skillet med/high sear salmon on both sides for 2 mins on each side
4.  Preheat oven to 400° while searing salmon
5.  Transfer salmon skin side up and juices to a baking dish
6.  Let finish in oven for 15 mins or until skin is crunchy to your liking
