##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn Umai:

1.  Prepare 250 grams Fresh Prawn (de-shelled and de-veined)
2.  Prepare 1 cup Lime Juice (Calamansi)
3.  Get 1 tablespoon Vinegar
4.  Take 1 Tomato (Sliced into 4)
5.  Take 4 cloves Garlic
6.  Prepare 1 Onion (Sliced finely)
7.  Make ready 5 Shallots
8.  Prepare 2 Inches Ginger (Sliced finely)
9.  Prepare Chillies (Sliced finely)
10.  Take Salt and Sugar

##### Instructions to make Prawn Umai:

1.  Clean the prawn thoroughly.
2.  Pour the lime juice and vinegar over the prawn and mixed thoroughly. Add more lime juice if necessary.
3.  Add the rest of the ingredient and season to taste. Put in refrigerator for at least half an hour for the flavour of the seasoning and ginger to be infused in the umai.
