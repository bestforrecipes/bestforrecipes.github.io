##### The ingredients needed to make Vickys Easy Pesto Prawn Skewers, GF DF EF SF NF:

1.  Get 8-10 tbsp Vickys Free-From Pesto recipe
2.  Take 500 g raw king prawns, shelled & de-veined
3.  Get to taste Fresh lemon juice

##### Steps to make Vickys Easy Pesto Prawn Skewers, GF DF EF SF NF:

1.  Marinade the prawns in the pesto for at least 30 minutes but preferrably overnight - make sure they're tossed in the pesto well before covering and setting in the fridge
2.  Heat the grill / broiler to medium-high
3.  Thread the prawns onto wooden skewers - soak these briefly in water so they don't burn under the grill
4.  Grill for 1 and a half minutes on each side. Done!
5.  Serve with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice and a garden salad
