##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Salt Ramen with Homemade Soup:

1.  Make ready 200 grams Chinese-style noodles
2.  Make ready 900 ml Water
3.  Make ready 2 tbsp ☆ Chicken stock granules
4.  Get 1 tsp ☆ Bonito-based soup stock
5.  Make ready 1 pinch ☆ Salt
6.  Make ready 1 dash ☆ Grated garlic

##### Instructions to make Easy Salt Ramen with Homemade Soup:

1.  Boil 900 ml of water in a pot.
2.  When it starts boiling, add ☆ condiments to make the soup.
3.  Cook the Chinese-style noodles according to the instructions on the package.
4.  Dish up the cooked noodles and soup. Top with desired ingredients and serve.
