5.  Get 1 tablespoon tamari soy sauce
6.  Prepare 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
7.  Take 2 tablespoon ketchup
8.  Make ready 1 teaspoon Sriracha hot sauce
9.  Prepare 2 tablespoons minced celery
10.  Get 1 green onion,chopped
