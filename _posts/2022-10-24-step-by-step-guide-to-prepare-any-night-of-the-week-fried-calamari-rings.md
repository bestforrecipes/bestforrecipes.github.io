##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Calamari Rings:

1.  Get Calamari
2.  Make ready White flour
3.  Make ready Egg
4.  Prepare Water
5.  Get Panko
6.  Prepare Oil for deep frying

##### Instructions to make Fried Calamari Rings:

1.  Remove the guts from the calamari and peel its skin off. Cut into ring-shaped slices (chop the tentacles and fins into chunks).
2.  Coat the squid evenly with flour (unlisted; 2-3 tablespoons).
3.  Mix the flour and egg.
4.  Add the water and continue mixing.
5.  Dredge the calamari rings in the egg mixture and then the panko.
6.  You can make this much with a single calamari.
7.  Deep-fry.
8.  Voila! It's done.
