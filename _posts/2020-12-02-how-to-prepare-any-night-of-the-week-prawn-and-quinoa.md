5.  Take 1 cup vegetable broth
6.  Take Prawns peeled

##### Instructions to make Prawn and Quinoa:

1.  On medium heat, fry onion and garlic till soft. Add chilli flakes and and then add quinoa and sauté for a minute.
2.  Add vegetable broth, bring to the boil and then simmer covered for 20-30 minutes or till cooked. Halfway through add the prawns and cover.
3.  Add parsley. Season with Salt and Pepper and serve hot.
