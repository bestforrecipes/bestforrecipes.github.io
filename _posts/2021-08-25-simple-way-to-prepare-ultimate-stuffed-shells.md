##### The ingredients needed to make Stuffed shells:

1.  Make ready 1/2 packages Jumbo shells
2.  Take 1 lb ground beef
3.  Take 1/2 cup chopped onion
4.  Get 1 clove garlic, chopped
5.  Make ready 26 oz jar pasta sauce
6.  Get 15 oz ricotta cheese
7.  Get 2 cup shredded mozzarella
8.  Prepare 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
9.  Get 1 egg

##### Instructions to make Stuffed shells:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°F.
2.  Cook pasta according to package
3.  In large skillet, brown ground beef, onion, and garlic
4.  Stir in pasta sauce
5.  In a large bowl, mix ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella, parmesan cheese, and egg
6.  Stuff shells with cheese mixture
7.  Pour half of pasta sauce in 13x9 baking pan
8.  Arrange shells in sauce
9.  Pour remaining sauce over shells
10.  Cover and bake for 30 minutes
11.  Uncover, top with remaining cheese, and bake for 5 minutes
