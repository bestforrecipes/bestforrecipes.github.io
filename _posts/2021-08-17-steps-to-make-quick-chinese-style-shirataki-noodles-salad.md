##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese-style Shirataki Noodles Salad:

1.  Take 1 bag Shirataki noodles (kon'nyaku thread noodles)
2.  Prepare 1/2 Cucumber
3.  Get 4 stick Imitation crab sticks
4.  Make ready 1 tbsp ☆Sugar
5.  Get 2 tbsp ☆Soy sauce
6.  Prepare 3 tbsp ☆Vinegar
7.  Get 2 tbsp ☆Sesame seeds
8.  Take 1 Sesame oil (optional)

##### Instructions to make Chinese-style Shirataki Noodles Salad:

1.  Chop the shirataki noodles into appropriate lengths. Sprinkle salt. When it's drained of water to a certain extent, boil briskly in boiling water. This makes it easier for the flavors to penetrate.
2.  Julienne the cucumber and rub in salt.
3.  Combine ☆ condiments. Add the shirataki noodles, julienned and lightly drained cucumber, and loosened crab sticks.
4.  Let the ingredients soak up the flavor for 30 minutes to an hour to finish. Use a generous amount of condiments. Drizzle sesame oil if you'd like.
