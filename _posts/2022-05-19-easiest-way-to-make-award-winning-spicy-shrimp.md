##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Shrimp:

1.  Make ready 6 Bacon strips, diced
2.  Get 1 cup Unsalted Butter, cubed
3.  Make ready 2 tbsp Seafood seasoning
4.  Make ready 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
5.  Take 1 1/2 tsp chili powder
6.  Make ready 1 tsp ground black pepper
7.  Make ready 1 tsp Louisiana - style hot sauce
8.  Take 1/4 tsp Dried Basil
9.  Take 1/4 tsp Oregano
10.  Get 1/4 tsp Thyme
11.  Get dash Ground Red pepper
12.  Make ready 2 clove Garlic, minced
13.  Prepare 1 1/2 lb uncooked shell-on medium shrimp

##### Steps to make Spicy Shrimp:

1.  In a large skillet, cook bacon over medium heat until partially cooked but not crisp; drain.
2.  Stir in butter, seafood seasoning, mustard, chili powder, pepper, hot sauce, basil, oregano, and thyme. Cook over low heat for 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute longer.
3.  Place the shrimp in a ungreased 13x9 baking dish. Add sauce and stir to coat. Bake, uncovered for 20-25 minutes or until shrimp turns pink, stirring twice.
