##### Steps to make Stone BBQ Salmon:

1.  Mix honey, Dijon mustard and garlic together in a bowl.
2.  Place salmon skin down on stone cooking plate (Can use Cedar plank or baking pan if using the oven)
3.  Add chopped basil, spring onions, tomatoes ground ginger, salt & pepper.
4.  Smear mustard & honey mix over the top skinless side.
5.  Place on medium heat BBQ for approximately 15 minutes. Ensure heat is even under stone plate to prevent cracking.
