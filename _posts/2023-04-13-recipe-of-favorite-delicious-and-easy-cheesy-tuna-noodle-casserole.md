##### The ingredients needed to make Delicious and easy cheesy tuna noodle casserole!:

2.  Take cream of mushroom soup
3.  Get cheddar cheese (half cup mixed and half on top before the oven. add more if desired)
4.  Make ready of peas and carrots
5.  Get rotini noodles (3 cups uncooked)
6.  Take tuna drained (5-6 oz)
7.  Prepare milk
8.  Make ready .5 cup -plain or italian bread crumbs ( half cup)
9.  Make ready full can of water

##### Steps to make Delicious and easy cheesy tuna noodle casserole!:

1.  preheat oven 350
2.  pour chicken broth and can of water in pan on stove medium high heat bring to boil
3.  when boiling add 3 cups rotini noodles and reduce heat to medium
4.  when noodles are soft add cream of mushroom soup, milk and tuna.
5.  mix well and turn off heat on stovetop. add half cup of cheese and peas and carrots and mix again
6.  transfer to small square casserole dish add remainder of cheese and sprinkle bread crumbs on top
7.  put in oven for 30 to 45 mins until bubbling
8.  let cool for few minutes and enjoy! also great with rolls and or green beans :)
