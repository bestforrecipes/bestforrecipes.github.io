##### The ingredients needed to make Cucumber & French beans salad with egg and tuna:

1.  Make ready 2 Japanese cucumbers
2.  Prepare 1 bunch French beans
3.  Make ready 8 small cherry tomatoes
4.  Get 4 each bird eye chili
5.  Take 1/2 can tuna chunks
6.  Prepare 1 pinch coarse black pepper
7.  Prepare 1 egg

##### Steps to make Cucumber & French beans salad with egg and tuna:

1.  Washed, peeled and cut cucumbers
2.  Washed, cut French bean into roughly 4cm
3.  Bring a pot of water to boil egg (hard boiled), removed shell
4.  Bring another pot of water to boil for branching the French beans approx 5 minutes
5.  Roughly cut bird eye chili in a bowl
6.  Washed cherry tomatoes and put them in the bowl with the roughly cut bird eye chili
7.  once everything done mixed them together
8.  Placed the half can of tuna chunks on top of the salad, drizzled with 2 tblspoon olive oil and 4 tblspoon of balsamic vinegar.( You may add more if you like more dressing)
9.  Sprinkled a pinch of black pepper on top and ready to eat
