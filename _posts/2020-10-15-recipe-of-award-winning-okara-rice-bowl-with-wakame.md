##### The ingredients needed to make Okara Rice Bowl with Wakame:

1.  Prepare 50 grams Fresh okara
2.  Get 50 grams Re-hydrated wakame
3.  Prepare 1 Egg
4.  Prepare 1 Crab sticks (optional)
5.  Get 1/2 tbsp ◎Miso
6.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp ◎Mirin
7.  Make ready 1/2 tbsp ◎Water
8.  Get 1 bowl Cooked white rice
9.  Take 1 Roasted sesame seeds (for the topping)

##### Steps to make Okara Rice Bowl with Wakame:

1.  Combine the ◎ ingredients. Add the egg and beat.
2.  Put the wakame and a small amount of sesame oil (not listed) in a frying pan and lightly sauté. Add the okara.
3.  Add Step 1 to Step 2 and mix. Turn off the heat when the egg is soft-set.
4.  Fill a bowl with rice and top with the wakame egg mixture from Step 3. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and it's complete.
5.  This can also be enjoyed as a snack without rice. Increase the volume by increasing all of the ingredients, except for the egg. Feel free to make modifications.
