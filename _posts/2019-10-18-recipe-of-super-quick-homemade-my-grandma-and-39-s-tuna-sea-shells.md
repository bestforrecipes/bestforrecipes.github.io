##### The ingredients needed to make My Grandma's Tuna Sea Shells:

1.  Get Pasta————
2.  Get 1 tablespoon salt
3.  Get as needed water for cooking the shells
4.  Take 1 pound pasta medium shells
5.  Make ready Tuna Sea Shells——–
6.  Make ready 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
7.  Take 1/2 cup milk
8.  Prepare 1 pound shredded extra sharp cheddar cheese
9.  Take 1 tablespoon grated parmesan cheese
10.  Take 10 ounces canned tuna drained
11.  Get 2 tablespoons butter
12.  Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
13.  Prepare to taste ground black pepper
14.  Take Topping———–
15.  Get 1 pinch salt
16.  Make ready 2 tablespoons melted butter
17.  Prepare 1/4 cup bread crumbs I used panko
18.  Take Garnish———–
19.  Prepare 1/4 teaspoon dill weed
20.  Take 1 teaspoon chopped parsley for garnish

##### Instructions to make My Grandma's Tuna Sea Shells:

1.  Preheat oven 350 degrees Fahrenheit
2.  Cook the pasta according to the instructions on box.
3.  Drain and set aside.
4.  Heat butter, and milk add cheeses. Do NOT let this boil. It will break and become grainy.
5.  When it thickens. Add pasta.
6.  Mix the melted butter with the breadcrumbs and lightly salt.
7.  Mix parsley, shells, and tuna with 2 tablespoons of parsley.
8.  Add the pasta mixture to an oven safe skillet.
9.  Add breadcrumbs. Put the Tuna Sea Shells in oven for 15-20 minutes. Bake longer if breadcrumbs are not browned enough.
10.  Let rest 5 minutes.
11.  Add garnish and serve.
