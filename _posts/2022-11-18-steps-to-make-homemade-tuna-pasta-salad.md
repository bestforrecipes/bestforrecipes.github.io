##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Pasta Salad:

1.  Make ready 300 gram pasta
2.  Make ready 450 tuna
3.  Take 7 tablespoons olive oil
4.  Prepare 2 cloves garlic, crushed
5.  Prepare 1 small onions, finely chopped
6.  Make ready 2 cup diced tomatoes
7.  Get 1 cup fresh basil, torn
8.  Take 3 tablespoon grated parmesan cheese
9.  Get 2 cups crumbled feta cheese
10.  Make ready to taste Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Tuna Pasta Salad:

1.  Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 10 minutes or until al dente; drain. - Combine olive oil, garlic, tuna, onion, tomatoes and basil; let sit at room temperature.
2.  Toss warm pasta with Parmesan and feta. Stir in tomato mixture and sprinkle on salt and pepper. You can add more Parmesan if desired. Serve immediately.
