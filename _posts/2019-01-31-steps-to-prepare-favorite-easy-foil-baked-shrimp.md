##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Foil Baked Shrimp:

1.  Prepare 16/20 prawns
2.  Get medium yukon gold potatoes
3.  Make ready andouille sausage
4.  Prepare old bay
5.  Take crushed black pepper
6.  Make ready salt
7.  Get olive oil
8.  Get chives

##### Instructions to make Easy Foil Baked Shrimp:

1.  Preheat oven to 425F°.
2.  Clean and peel your shrimp.
3.  Dice potatoes to around ½".
4.  Slice your andouille sausage
5.  With the exception of the chives, combine all ingredients in a baking dish. Mix well. Cover with foil.
6.  Bake for around 17-20 minutes. It's ready when the potatoes are cooked through.
7.  Slice your chives neatly and sprinkle them over the top to finish.
