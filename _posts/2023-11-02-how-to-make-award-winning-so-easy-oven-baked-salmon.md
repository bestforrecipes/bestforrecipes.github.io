##### The ingredients needed to make So Easy! Oven Baked Salmon:

1.  Make ready Fresh salmon
2.  Get to 4 tablespoons Mayonnaise
3.  Prepare Panko
4.  Get Powdered cheese
5.  Take Soy sauce
6.  Make ready Parsely Basil etc.
7.  Take Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make So Easy! Oven Baked Salmon:

1.  Season the salmon with salt and pepper.
2.  Put all the ingredients except the salmon into a small bowl and mix. I used blanched and minced spinach in the recipe, but you can use parsley or other green herbs or vegetables for colour. My young child doesn't like parsley.
3.  Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and lay the salmon filets. Spoon the sauce from Step 2 on top of the salmon.
4.  Bake at 340F/170C for 4 minutes Now you are done! Adjust the cooking time to suit your oven. (I always let them finish cooking in residual heat.)
