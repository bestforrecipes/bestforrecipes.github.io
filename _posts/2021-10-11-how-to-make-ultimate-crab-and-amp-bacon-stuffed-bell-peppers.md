##### The ingredients needed to make Crab & Bacon Stuffed Bell Peppers:

1.  Make ready 1/2 packages bacon ( I used turkey) chopped
2.  Prepare 1 lb lump crab meat
3.  Get 1 cup crushed corn flakes
4.  Make ready 2 tbsp mayonnaise
5.  Take 1 egg (room temperature)
6.  Get salt
7.  Prepare cayenne pepper
8.  Take 3 bell peppers halved and stemmed lengthwise
9.  Get 1 chicken broth
10.  Get 1 olive oil to drizzle

##### Instructions to make Crab & Bacon Stuffed Bell Peppers:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°
2.  Cook bacon. Once done place on napkin to drain excess fat.
3.  In bowl, combine crab meat, half of the bacon, mayonnaise, egg, corn flakes, salt, and cayenne pepper.
4.  Spoon into halved peppers and place in roasting pan. Drizzle olive oil on top of peppers. Pour chicken broth around peppers. This will allow the steam to make the peppers tender.
5.  Add remaining bacon to tops of peppers.
6.  Bake for 40 minutes or until peppers are tender. Plate and serve with my black bean couscous.
