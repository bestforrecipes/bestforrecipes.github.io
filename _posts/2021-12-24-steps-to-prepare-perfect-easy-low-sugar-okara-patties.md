##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Low-Sugar Okara Patties:

1.  Make ready 100 grams ★ Fresh okara
2.  Take 1 can ★ Canned tuna
3.  Get 1 tbsp ★ Mayonnaise
4.  Prepare 1 dash Salt and pepper
5.  Make ready 1 tsp Olive oil

##### Instructions to make Easy Low-Sugar Okara Patties:

1.  Add all the ★ ingredients together.
2.  Form the mixture from Step 1 into circular shapes and season with salt and pepper.
3.  Heat some oil in a skillet, place the patties from Step 2 and pan-fry until golden.
4.  It's done (Serve them with mayonnaise if desired and enjoy!).
