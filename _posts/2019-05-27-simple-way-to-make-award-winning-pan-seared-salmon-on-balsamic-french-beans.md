##### The ingredients needed to make Pan Seared Salmon on Balsamic French Beans:

1.  Take centre-cut salmon fillet with skin (approx. 170g each)
2.  Get fresh lemon juice
3.  Make ready olive oil
4.  Prepare Lime juice from half a lime
5.  Take french beans
6.  Take balsamic vinegar
7.  Make ready Salt & Pepper

##### Instructions to make Pan Seared Salmon on Balsamic French Beans:

1.  Place the salmon fillets in a shallow bowl. Add lemon juice, olive oil, a generous pinch of salt & pepper and toss until well marinated. Let rest for 15 minutes.
2.  While the salmon marinates, blanche french beans in lightly-salted boiling water for about 2 minutes tops.
3.  Remove beans from water and place in a bowl. Drizzle vinegar and a pinch of pepper to the beans and toss gently until all beans are well coated.
4.  Blanket beans neatly on plate.
5.  Place salmon onto lightly-oiled pan, skin down over medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes. Shake pan lightly and lift gently with a spatula to loosen from pan to flip to the other side.
6.  Reduce hear to medium and cover pan. Cook for another 4 minutes.
7.  Place salmon on top of blanket of beans. Squeeze half a lime on top of salmon and you can also add a slice of lime on the salmon if you're feeling fancy! ;)
