##### The ingredients needed to make Brad's lobster roll:

1.  Prepare 1 (3 lb) lobster, deshell, and Chop into bite sized pieces
2.  Make ready 1 shallot, minced
3.  Prepare 2 celery stalks, cut down middle lengthwise then slice thin
4.  Take Juice of half a lemon
5.  Take 1 tsp minced garlic
6.  Take 3-4 tbs mayonnaise
7.  Make ready 1/2 tsp white pepper and old bay seasoning
8.  Make ready 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
9.  Take Garnish
10.  Take Belillo rolls
11.  Prepare Shredded merlot belvitano cheese
12.  Make ready Lemon wedge
13.  Make ready Creme friach
