##### The ingredients needed to make Colorful Sweet Potato:

1.  Take 150 g steamed yellow sweet potato
2.  Prepare 50 g steamed purple sweet potato
3.  Take 1-2 teaspoon sugar
4.  Make ready 1 teaspoon butter
5.  Take 1 g agar powder
6.  Get 10 g sugar
7.  Get 50 ml water

##### Instructions to make Colorful Sweet Potato:

1.  Steam sweet potatoes and cut skins
2.  Mash 100g of yellow potato and add butter and sugar
3.  Cut 50g yellow and purple potato and make ball with yellow mashed potato
4.  Make like this shape
5.  Mix agar powder, sugar and water and cook with low heat for 2 minuets after boiling
6.  Brush agar mixture
7.  Garnish like Christmas wreath
