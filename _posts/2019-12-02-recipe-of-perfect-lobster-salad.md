2.  Get 3 Chopped Celery Stalks
3.  Take 1/4 cup Chopped Green Onion
4.  Make ready 1/4 cup Chopped Cilantro
5.  Make ready 1/2 stick Melted Butter
6.  Make ready 4 clove Garlic Crushed
7.  Get 1 tsp Lemon Juice
8.  Prepare 1 tsp Sea Salt
9.  Take 1 tsp Cracked Pepper
10.  Make ready 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
11.  Take 2 tbsp Sour Cream
12.  Get 1 tbsp Softened Cream Cheese
13.  Take dash White Wine

##### Steps to make Lobster Salad:
