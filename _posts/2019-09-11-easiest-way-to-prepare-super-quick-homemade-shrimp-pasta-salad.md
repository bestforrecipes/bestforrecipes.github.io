##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp pasta salad:

1.  Take 3 cup Medium pasta shells
2.  Take 8 oz Cooked, shelled shrimp
3.  Make ready 1 cup Chopped celery
4.  Take 1 small onion, chopped
5.  Prepare 2 tsp Chopped fresh dill weed
6.  Make ready 1/4 cup Plain yogurt
7.  Take 1 tbsp Dijon-style mustard
8.  Make ready 1 tbsp Lemon juice
9.  Get 2 tsp Honey

##### Steps to make Shrimp pasta salad:
