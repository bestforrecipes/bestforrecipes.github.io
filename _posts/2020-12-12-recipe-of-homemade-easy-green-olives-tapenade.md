##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Green Olives Tapenade:

1.  Prepare 1 cup green olives
2.  Get 1 1/2 cup tuna
3.  Take 3 tsp olive oil
4.  Prepare 1 small garlic

##### Steps to make Easy Green Olives Tapenade:

1.  Mix the olives, the minced garlic, the tuna and the olive oil in a bowl.
2.  You can also add some thym or use black olives.
3.  This easy side dish can be used as a spread for your toasts or can be incorporated to your pasta dishes.
