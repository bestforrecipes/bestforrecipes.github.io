##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Ponzu Hot Pot with Gyoza:

1.  Take or more Gyoza dumplings (frozen or homemade)
2.  Take Chinese cabbage
3.  Get Japanese leek
4.  Prepare packet Enoki mushrooms
5.  Get Shimeji mushrooms
6.  Take Carrot
7.  Get Tofu
8.  Take ☆Water
9.  Prepare ☆ Ponzu
10.  Prepare ☆Dashi stock granules (bonito based)
11.  Get ☆ Ra-yu

##### Steps to make Spicy Ponzu Hot Pot with Gyoza:

1.  Cut the vegetables and tofu in suitable sizes.
2.  Pour the ☆ ingredients into a pot and boil. Add the ingredients starting with the ones that take longer to cook (add the gyoza at the end).
3.  Cover with a lid and heat on medium. When all has cooked well, it is done.
