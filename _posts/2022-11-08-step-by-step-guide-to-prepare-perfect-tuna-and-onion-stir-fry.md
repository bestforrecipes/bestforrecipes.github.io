##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna and Onion Stir-Fry:

1.  Get 1 can Canned tuna
2.  Prepare 1/2 Onion
3.  Prepare 2 Eggs
4.  Take 2 tbsp Sugar
5.  Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp Soy sauce

##### Instructions to make Tuna and Onion Stir-Fry:

1.  Heat a frying-pan, and add the canned tuna. Add the sliced onion, and stir-fry.
2.  Add the sugar and soy sauce to the eggs, and beat together. When the onion is soft and wilted, add the egg mixture.
3.  When the eggs are 90% cooked through, turn off the heat and it's done!
