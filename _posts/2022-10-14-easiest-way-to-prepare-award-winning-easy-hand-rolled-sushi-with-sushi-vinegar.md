##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Hand-Rolled Sushi with Sushi Vinegar:

1.  Get 1 Sushi vinegar
2.  Take 1 Nori (dried seaweed sheet)
3.  Prepare 450 grams Hot cooked rice
4.  Take Fillings:
5.  Make ready 1 Negitoro (minced fatty tuna with scallions)
6.  Take 1 Imitation crab sticks
7.  Get 1 Shrimp
8.  Make ready 1 Salmon roe
9.  Take 2 Cucumbers
10.  Make ready 1 Lettuce
11.  Prepare 1 can Canned tuna
12.  Make ready 1 Mayonnaise
13.  Make ready 1 Shiso leaves
14.  Get 1 Tamagoyaki
15.  Take 1 Tuna (sashimi quality)

##### Steps to make Easy Hand-Rolled Sushi with Sushi Vinegar:

1.  Mix the sushi vinegar in with the rice.
2.  Make the tamagoyaki. Remove the oil from the canned tuna and mix it with the mayonnaise. Slice the cucumbers and tear the lettuce.
3.  Lay everything out and you're done! You can eat it with strong soy sauce, mayonnaise, etc.
