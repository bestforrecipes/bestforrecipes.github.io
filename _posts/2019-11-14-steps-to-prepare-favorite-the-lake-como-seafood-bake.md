##### The ingredients needed to make The Lake Como Seafood Bake:

1.  Take Seafood
2.  Make ready 2 dozen Littlenecks Clams
3.  Get 2 lb P.E.I. Mussels, scrubbed, beard removed
4.  Take 1 lb U-8 Sea Scallops
5.  Get 1 lb 15-20 Jumbo Shrimp, peeled, deveined, tails on
6.  Prepare 4 3 - 4 oz. Lobster Tails
7.  Prepare 4 piece Sweet White Corn on the Cob Shucked
8.  Make ready 4 each Italian Sausage (Sweet or Hot)
9.  Get 1 lb Fingerling Potatoes
10.  Get 1 large Onion, Chopped
11.  Make ready 1 cup Pinot Grigio Wine
12.  Make ready 5 Fresh Chopped Basil Leaves
13.  Take 2 stick Butter, cut into 1/4
14.  Get 10 clove Chopped Garlic
15.  Make ready Sea Salt
16.  Get 2 Lemons

##### Steps to make The Lake Como Seafood Bake:

1.  Scrub the Clams & Mussels to remove any Sandy Grit & Rinse off the Potatoes.
2.  Place the Sausage, Potatoes, Corn, Clams & Mussels in a Large Roasting Pan.
3.  Then place Lobster Tails, Shrimp & Scallops on Top of the Clams & Mussels.
4.  Add the Wine, Butter, Lemon Juice
5.  Place Roasting Pan in the Oven or on the Grill & Bake till all of the Clams & Mussels are Open.
