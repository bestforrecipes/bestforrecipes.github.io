##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon With Chinese Wine And Dark Soy Sauce:

1.  Get 1 large salmon fillet or salmon head
2.  Make ready 2 tbsp sesame oil or vegetables oil
3.  Prepare 3 tbsp chinese red wine or any shao xing rice wine
4.  Prepare 5 dried chili pepper (optional add on)
5.  Make ready sauce
6.  Get 2 tbsp dice garlic and 10 slice ginger
7.  Prepare 2 tbsp light soy sauce
8.  Get 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
9.  Take 2 tbsp oyster sauce
10.  Make ready 1/4 cup water
11.  Take 1 tsp white sugar
12.  Take garnish option
13.  Get 1 bunch coriander and tomato

##### Steps to make Salmon With Chinese Wine And Dark Soy Sauce:

1.  WITH OIL SAUTE THE DRIED CHILI,GINGER AND GARLIC TILL ITS FRAGRANCE THEN ADD THE SAUCE,THEN ADD WATER AND JUST BRING SAUCE TO A BOIL
2.  With little oil pan fried the salmon till lts cook then just add the sauce and bring it to a simmer till sauce are thicken add in the scallion and cherry tomato and off heat and serve immediately
