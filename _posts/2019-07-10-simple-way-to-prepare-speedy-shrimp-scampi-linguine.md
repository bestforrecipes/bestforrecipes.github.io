##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Scampi Linguine:

1.  Get 100 g spinach
2.  Get 100 g white prawn deveined
3.  Prepare 1 streaky bacon (cut diced)
4.  Prepare 250 g linguine (boil)
5.  Prepare 2 tbsp butter
6.  Get 4 garlic
7.  Prepare Salt
8.  Prepare Black pepper
9.  Get Water
