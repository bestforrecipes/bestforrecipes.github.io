##### The ingredients needed to make Crispy Salmon Croquettes:

1.  Prepare 15 1/2 ounce can pink salmon
2.  Prepare cooked baked potato, skin removed and mashed
3.  Make ready egg, beaten
4.  Get salt
5.  Take chopped onion
6.  Prepare chopped red, yellow or green pepper
7.  Make ready ground black pepper
8.  Take oil for deep frying
9.  Make ready Panko bread crumbs

##### Instructions to make Crispy Salmon Croquettes:

1.  Heat oil to 350°F while making croquettes.
2.  Separate salmon and remove the hard round bones. The small thin bones can be eaten. They mix up fine.
3.  Add the mashed, cooked potato, egg, salt and pepper. Mix well until all is combined.
4.  Add chopped peppers. Mix until well incorporated.
5.  Shape mixture into egg shaped ovals and roll in panko bread crumbs. If mixture seems dry, just add some of the liquid drained from salmon a little at a time so it will hold together. Usually just 1 to 2 teaspoons at a time if necessary.
6.  I usually shape mine about the size of egg, servings just depends on size you make these.
7.  Deep fry a few at a time to brown and keep from crowding. Flip around if necessary to evenly brown.
8.  This only takes a few minutes. so watch carefully.
9.  Remove from oil and drain on paper towels or cooling rack. Place in 200°F oven to keep warm if necessary.
10.  You can substitute sleeve of saltine crackers to replace the potato. If using saltines, just shape into patties and pan fry. The potato makes these light and fluffy.
