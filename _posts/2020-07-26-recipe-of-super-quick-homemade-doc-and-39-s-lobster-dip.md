5.  Prepare 1/4 cup sour cream
6.  Make ready 2 tbsp lite mayo
7.  Get 1 teaspoon seasoning mix (salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder)
8.  Make ready 1 teaspoon chives
9.  Take 1 dash Worcestershire (very light dash)
