##### The ingredients needed to make Kidney Diet Shrimp Tacos:

1.  Take 1 lb shrimp
2.  Make ready 1/2 cup onions, diced
3.  Take 1/4 cup bell peppers, diced
4.  Take 1 cup cabbage, diced
5.  Take 1/2 tsp cumin
6.  Make ready 1/2 tsp chilli powder
7.  Take Flour tortillas

##### Steps to make Kidney Diet Shrimp Tacos:

1.  Sauteed veggies.
2.  Add shrimp and seasonings. Cook til done
3.  Serve in flour tortillas
4.  I told my tortillas in half and crisp them in the waffle iron without any oil\*
