##### The ingredients needed to make Hipon Pinakbet or Steamed Shrimps w vegetables in Shrimp paste Sauce:

1.  Get shrimps
2.  Get medium onions chopped
3.  Make ready garlic chopped
4.  Get thumb size ginger minced
5.  Take Okra tips and points cuts
6.  Get Sitaw or string beans cut into 2in
7.  Take eggplant sliced into half moon shaped
8.  Prepare Ampalaya or bitter gourd seeds spooned out and sliced
9.  Get Shrimp paste
10.  Make ready finger green chilies
11.  Get tomatoes chopped

##### Steps to make Hipon Pinakbet or Steamed Shrimps w vegetables in Shrimp paste Sauce:

1.  Heat pan and oil in med-hi heat then sauté garlic, onions, tomatoes and ginger until fragrant then add stir fry Green beans
2.  Cook rests of vegetables then add shrimp paste add a some water then simmer cover
3.  After few minutes add arrange finger chilies. Parallel then arrange shrimps with heads and tails stretched out with the head touching the one Chili then the tails touching the other chili then cover so that shrimps steam until shrimps color changes orange.
4.  Turn off heat and serve with hot rice.
