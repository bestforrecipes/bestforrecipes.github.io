##### The ingredients needed to make Kimchi Fried Rice (김치볶음밥):

1.  Make ready Kimchi
2.  Make ready Gochujang sauce (korean chilli paste)
3.  Make ready sesame oil
4.  Get Pork or beef (optional)
5.  Prepare Dried seaweed
6.  Prepare Sesame seeds
