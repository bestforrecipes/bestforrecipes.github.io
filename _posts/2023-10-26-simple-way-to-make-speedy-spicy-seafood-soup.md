##### The ingredients needed to make spicy seafood soup:

1.  Get 2 clove garlic
2.  Take 5 medium chopped up red or yellow potato
3.  Prepare 3 carrots chopped into small pieces
4.  Get 1/2 can chipotle chiles en adobo sauce (like 3Chiles and half the sauce)
5.  Make ready 1 small hand full cilantro
6.  Take 1 small onion sliced
7.  Get 1 salt to taste
8.  Get 1 water
9.  Prepare 2 packages seafood mix a frozen mixture of shrimp, clams, muscles, crab ect.( you can also use 1 pack of shrimp and one pack mix, or add other seafoods to ur liking.)

##### Instructions to make spicy seafood soup:

1.  in soup pan or pot fill half way with water and add the seafood mix and onion, cook about 10 minutes on med-high heat
2.  add potato and carrot
3.  in blender add chiles, adobo sauce, garlic and about a cup of water. blend on high till very smooth, poor into pot.
4.  add cilantro to pot and let simmer about 5 minutes longer
5.  salt to taste
