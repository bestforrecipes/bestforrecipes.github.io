##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Ajillo:

1.  Prepare Ajillo sauce
2.  Get 2 tbsp olive oil
3.  Make ready 1 tbsp chopped garlic
4.  Get 2 cup chicken stock
5.  Take 3 dash hot sauce
6.  Make ready 1/4 cup cold water
7.  Prepare 2 tsp corn starch
8.  Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
9.  Prepare 1 lb jumbo shrimp
10.  Get 1 tbsp carribean jerk seasoning
11.  Make ready 1 Red pepper julienned

##### Steps to make Shrimp Ajillo:

1.  For sauce, heat garlic in oil. Add stock and hot sauce and bring to a boil.
2.  Mix corn starch and water together to make slurry.
3.  Slowly add slurry to sauce. Turn off heat.
4.  Dust shrimp with seasoning.
5.  Saute shrimp in oil. Add peppers.
6.  Add sauce to shrimp.
7.  Serve with crusty bread.
