##### The ingredients needed to make Kanya 's Salt and Peppers Squids:

1.  Get 3 large squids,clean ,cut into half an inch thick
2.  Take 1/3 tsp salt
3.  Prepare 1/3 tsp ground black pepper
4.  Get 1 tsp crushed coriander seeds ,optional
5.  Get 1/2 cup flour
6.  Get 1 egg
7.  Take 1/3 cup cold soda water
8.  Make ready 6 clove garlic
9.  Take 12 hot peppers ,green and red color
10.  Prepare 1 oil enough to fry
11.  Make ready 1/2 tbsp sesame oil
