##### The ingredients needed to make Tasty Quick leftovers Jacket Potato:

1.  Take 1 baking potato
2.  Get Whatever veggies you have in your fridge that need eating!
3.  Make ready In this case:
4.  Make ready Handful pitted black olives
5.  Get 1/2 can leftover Tuna
6.  Get 1 tomato
7.  Get 1/2 red pepper
8.  Prepare Dollop of mayonnaise (or salad cream)
9.  Get 1/2 a sweet onion (or red onion)
10.  Get Grated hard cheese
11.  Take Seasoning
12.  Take Butter for the potato if desired

##### Instructions to make Tasty Quick leftovers Jacket Potato:

1.  Preheat the oven to around 200C. Pierce your washed potato in several places and stick in the microwave on high for about 5 minutes.
2.  Carefully pour about a tablespoon of oil (whichever you prefer but veg/sunflower oils are best for high heats) over the potato making sure it's coated all over & add a sprinkle of salt and pop into the oven on a baking tray.
3.  While the potato is in the oven check out your fridge, if you're like me you always have assorted veggies leftover that will go really well with a baked potato! I like to use raw crunchy veg piled on top of a fluffy potato. Cut the veg into chunks and add the Tuna and seasoning, then mix in a bowl with a dollop of mayo.
4.  Check on the potato, it should be crisping up nicely. Once the skin is golden and a fork goes in easily it's done. If you fancy it add some butter to your jacket then pile on the veggie tuna mix. Grate some cheddar or Parmesan on top.
