##### The ingredients needed to make Heathers Stuffed Shells:

1.  Take 8 oz mozz. cheese, shredded
2.  Make ready 8 oz cheddar cheese, shredded
3.  Take 5 oz container of parmesan & ramano cheese
4.  Prepare 1 lb hamburger
5.  Make ready 1 box barilla jumbo shells
6.  Get 15 oz container of ricotta cheese
7.  Make ready 1 jar of barilla or ragu traditional pasta sauce

##### Instructions to make Heathers Stuffed Shells:

1.  Preheat oven to 400
2.  boil jumbo shells for about 10-11 minutes, set aside to cool off or rinse them off in cool water
3.  brown hamburger in a med skillet, drain grease and set aside
4.  in a large mixing bowl add the mozzarella, cheddar, parmesan, and ramano cheese.
5.  set some cheese aside for the topping. add the burger to the mixing bowl and stir.
6.  spray bottom of baking dish with some pam and pour about a quarter or so of sauce in dish.
7.  with a big tablesoon get a good scoop of shell mixture and stuff those beautiful shells really good and then lay them open side up in the baking dish.
8.  add the remaining sauce on top of the shells and sprinkle with the rest of the cheese.
9.  bake for about 25 minutes, take em out and let cool for like 5 and then stuff your face till your full.
