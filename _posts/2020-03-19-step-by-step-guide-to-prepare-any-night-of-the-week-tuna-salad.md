##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Salad:

1.  Get 12 oz light tuna in water
2.  Take 1 can cannellini beans
3.  Get 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
4.  Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
5.  Get 1 tbsp fresh cilantro, minched
6.  Take 1/8 tsp salt
7.  Make ready 1/8 tsp pepper
8.  Get 1 ripe avocado, peeled and diced

##### Steps to make Tuna Salad:

1.  Combine first seven ingredients. Toss lightly to combine.
2.  Top with avocado.
