##### The ingredients needed to make Nikki's Spicy crab dip:

1.  Get 2 can crab meat
2.  Prepare 16 oz cream cheese
3.  Prepare 1/2 cup mayonnaise
4.  Prepare 2 tbsp cayenne pepper(optional) can add more for spicier dip.
5.  Prepare 4 tsp dill seasoning
6.  Get 1/4 cup lemon juice
7.  Make ready 1 crackers (your choice)

##### Instructions to make Nikki's Spicy crab dip:

1.  Mix everything in a bowl except for the crackers
2.  Serve cold and with crackers
