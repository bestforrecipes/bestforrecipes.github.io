##### The ingredients needed to make Cajun Seafood Sausage Casserole:

1.  Get 2 cups minute brown rice
2.  Prepare 2 bags uncooked medium shrimp (deveined & tail off)
3.  Prepare 5 sausage links, chopped
4.  Take 1 pack imitation crab meat, chopped
5.  Get 2 tbsp cajun seasoning
6.  Make ready to taste salt & pepper
7.  Prepare 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
8.  Take 2 tbsp minced garlic
9.  Take 2 tsp vegetable oil
10.  Get 1 can cream of mushroom
11.  Take 1 can cream of celery
12.  Prepare 1 can diced tomatoes with green chiles

##### Instructions to make Cajun Seafood Sausage Casserole:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°
2.  Sautée in a frying pan with vegetable oil, your chopped onions & minced garlic. Set aside
3.  In large mixing bowl combine shrimp, crab, sausage, seasonings, sauteed onion mix, rice & soups.
4.  Transfer to 9×13 cassarole dish, place in oven for 30 mins. After 30 mins stir, place back in oven for 20 mins. Then top off with cheeses & put back in oven for 10 more mins or until cheeses melt. Enjoy!
