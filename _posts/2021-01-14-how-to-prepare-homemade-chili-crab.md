##### The ingredients needed to make Chili Crab:

1.  Get 1 kg crab
2.  Get 1/2 tsp Chinese 4 spice
3.  Get 1 head Garlic
4.  Get 1/4 cup Oyster Sauce
5.  Take 1/4 cup Banana Catsup
6.  Take 2 tbsp Butter
7.  Take 1/2 cup Soda
8.  Make ready 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
9.  Prepare 5 stick Green Finger Chili
10.  Take 2 stick Onion leeks or Spring Onion
11.  Prepare 1 tbsp Veg. Oil
12.  Take 1 small Ginger (julienne)

##### Instructions to make Chili Crab:

1.  Wash the crab very carefully
2.  Heat the wok, put in Oil and Butter
3.  Add Ginger and Garlic, Saute put the Crab
4.  Add Oyster Sauce, Catsup, Soda, Chinese 4 Spice, Brown Sugar
5.  Simmer for a minute, add half of the Chilies (sliced)
6.  Add pinch of Salt and Pepper
7.  Top with Half of the Chilies and Onion Leeks
