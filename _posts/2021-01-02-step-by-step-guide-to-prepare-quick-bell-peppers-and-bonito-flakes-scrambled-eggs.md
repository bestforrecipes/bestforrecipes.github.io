##### The ingredients needed to make Bell Peppers and Bonito Flakes Scrambled Eggs:

1.  Take 5 small Green bell peppers
2.  Take 1 tbsp Sugar
3.  Make ready 1 tbsp Mirin
4.  Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Miso
5.  Prepare 1 small packet Bonito flakes
6.  Prepare 1 Egg

##### Instructions to make Bell Peppers and Bonito Flakes Scrambled Eggs:

1.  Remove the seeds from the bell peppers and slice thinly.
2.  Heat a pan over medium heat, add the peppers, sugar and mirin and stir-fry.
3.  When the peppers have wilted, add the miso.
4.  Taste. If it's OK, add the bonito flakes.
5.  Stir-fry to evaporate water from the pan, then add the beaten egg.
6.  Stir-fry, scramble, and you're done!
