##### The ingredients needed to make Squid masala:

1.  Prepare 300 gms Squid
2.  Get 1/2 cup Onion paste
3.  Get 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
4.  Get 1 dry red chilli
5.  Prepare 3 tbsp oil
6.  Prepare 1 tsp ginger paste
7.  Get 2 tsp garlic paste
8.  Take 2 tsp green chilli paste
9.  Make ready To taste salt
10.  Prepare 1 tsp turmeric
11.  Get 1 tsp red chilli powder
12.  Get 2 tsp Roasted cumin powder
13.  Prepare 1/3 tsp garam masala powder
