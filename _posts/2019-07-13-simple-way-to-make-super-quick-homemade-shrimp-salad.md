##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Salad:

1.  Get cooked shrimps
2.  Get cherry tomatoes
3.  Make ready onion
4.  Prepare juice squeezed from 1 lemon

##### Steps to make Shrimp Salad:

1.  cooked shrimps cut in small pieces
2.  cut cherry tomatoes in half
3.  thinly slice onion
4.  mix it all together, add chopped cilantro and squeezed juice
