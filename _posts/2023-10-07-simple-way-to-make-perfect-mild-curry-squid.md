##### The ingredients needed to make Mild Curry Squid:

1.  Get Half curry sachet sauce
2.  Prepare 1 small pack coconut milk
3.  Take 2 stalk celery,cut slantinh
4.  Prepare Half onion, cut into cubes
5.  Prepare Half kg squid, cut into rings

##### Steps to make Mild Curry Squid:

1.  Saute onion until translucent, next is the celery. Cook it a while. Then add curry sauce and coconut milk. Simmer it, add in squid and cook fast. Don't overcook the squid. Serve
