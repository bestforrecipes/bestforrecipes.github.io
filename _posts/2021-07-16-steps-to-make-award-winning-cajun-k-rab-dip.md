##### The ingredients needed to make Cajun K-rab Dip:

1.  Take 1/2 lbs. immitation crab meat
2.  Prepare 8 oz cream cheese
3.  Get 1/3 cup sour cream
4.  Take 1 tbsp hot sauce
5.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
6.  Get 1/2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
7.  Make ready 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
8.  Get 1/8 cup minced onions
9.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Adobo seasoning
10.  Prepare 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning

##### Instructions to make Cajun K-rab Dip:

1.  Mix cream cheese, sour cream, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, Cajun seasoning, onions, adobo, and Italian seasoning in a bowl or blender.
2.  Gently fold n cheddar cheese and imitation crab meat.
3.  Chill for at least a half hour before serving.
