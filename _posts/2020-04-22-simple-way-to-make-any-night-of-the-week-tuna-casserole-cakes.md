##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Casserole Cakes:

1.  Prepare 4 cups crushed Potato chips, 10 ounces
2.  Get 12 ounces cream of chicken soup
3.  Prepare 20 ounces canned tuna fish 3 cans drained
4.  Make ready 2 large eggs beaten
5.  Prepare 1-1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
6.  Prepare 1 cup all purpose flour
7.  Make ready As needed oil for frying

##### Instructions to make Tuna Casserole Cakes:

1.  Mix the tuna, potato chips, and soup in a bowl.
2.  Form the cakes
3.  Coat with flour
4.  Then egg
5.  Then breadcrumbs.
6.  Set on parchment paper and put them in the refrigerator for 1-2 hours.
7.  Fry in hot oil till golden brown
8.  Move to a paper towel to absorb excessive oils
9.  Serve I hope you enjoy!
