4.  Get 1 bunch chopped parsley
5.  Take 1 olive oil, extra virgin
6.  Prepare 3 tbsp unsalted butter
7.  Prepare 4 oz white wine
8.  Make ready 1 1/2 tomato heirloom
9.  Make ready salt and pepper
