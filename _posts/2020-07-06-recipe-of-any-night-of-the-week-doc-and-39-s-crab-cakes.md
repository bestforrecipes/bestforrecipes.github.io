##### Steps to make Doc's crab cakes:

1.  Whip mayonnaise, egg, worcestershire, mustard, lemon juice and parsley in separate bowl.
2.  Toss crab meat and crushed crackers in another bowl lightly.
3.  Blend the two bowls together, avoid breaking crabmeat lumps.
4.  Pack together into medium size balls.
5.  Broil on high for 10-12 minutes.
6.  Serve with crackers or on a sandwich roll
