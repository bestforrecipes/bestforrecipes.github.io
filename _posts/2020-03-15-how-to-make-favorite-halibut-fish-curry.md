##### The ingredients needed to make Halibut Fish Curry:

1.  Take hallibut or salmon fish head or any fish fillet
2.  Get water
3.  Take easy and simple diy curry
4.  Take oil
5.  Get large shallot fine dice
6.  Get garlic fine dice
7.  Prepare each of tumeric powder and chili powder
8.  Make ready light soy sauce or fish sauce and pepper
9.  Prepare lemon or lime or kalamansi juice
10.  Prepare fine chop each cilantro stem and leaf
11.  Get italian tomato and tomato juice

##### Steps to make Halibut Fish Curry:

1.  With oil sautee garlic, shallot, cilantro stem, tumeric powder, chili powder, salt and pepper till soft about 15 minute cover lid adding 2 tbsp of water when starting to get dry bit by bit in low heat stir occasionally
2.  Add tomato and juice, cilantro leaf, and lemon juice mix well cover lid let it simmer another 10 minute then add sauce and mash tomato
3.  Add hallibut or salmon or any fish and coat evenly with sauce and add water and cover lid for 10 minute occasionally shake the pan
