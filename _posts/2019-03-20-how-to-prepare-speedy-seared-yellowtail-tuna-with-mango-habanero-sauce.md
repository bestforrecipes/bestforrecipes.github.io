##### The ingredients needed to make Seared Yellowtail tuna With mango habanero sauce:

1.  Take 2 block sushi grade tuna
2.  Take 3 tablespoons bagel mix seeds
3.  Make ready 3 tablespoons black pepper
4.  Prepare 3 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
5.  Make ready 3 tablespoons black sesame seeds
6.  Prepare 1 tablespoon oil
7.  Take 5 tablespoons homemade mango habanero salsa
8.  Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
9.  Get 1/2 cup rice vinegar
10.  Get 1 tablespoon cornstarch

##### Steps to make Seared Yellowtail tuna With mango habanero sauce:

1.  Cover tuna with oil and cover with the mixture of seeds Then seared tuna on all four sides
2.  To make my mango salsa more of an Asian sauce I added 1/4 cup rice vinegar 1/4 cup white sugar 1 tablespoon of starch heated in microwave stir and let cool down
