##### The ingredients needed to make 4 Ingredient Crab Rangoon:

1.  Prepare Egg roll wrappers
2.  Get 8 oz cream cheese
3.  Prepare 3-4 imitation crab sticks
4.  Make ready 3 tbsp granulated sugar
5.  Take 2-3 green onion

##### Steps to make 4 Ingredient Crab Rangoon:

1.  In medium mixing bowl add cream cheese and crab. Mix well.
2.  Add sugar and green onion. Mix again
3.  Spoon about 2 tbsp of mixture into the middle of egg roll wrapper.
4.  Tuck a corner and sides and roll. Dip your finger in water and run along corner before rolling closed.
5.  Warm 3 cups vegetable oil in large to medium skillet.
6.  Brown and serve.
