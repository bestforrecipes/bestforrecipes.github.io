##### The ingredients needed to make Octopus and Lettuce Salad with Rich Mayonnaise Dressing:

1.  Prepare 100 grams Lettuce
2.  Get 100 grams Boiled octopus
3.  Get 1 Shredded nori seaweed, as needed
4.  Take 1 Toasted sesame seeds, as needed
5.  Prepare 1 clove ● Garlic (grated)
6.  Take 1 tbsp ● Mayonnaise
7.  Make ready 2 tsp ● Oyster sauce
8.  Take 1 tsp ● Lemon juice
9.  Take 1 tsp ● Ponzu
10.  Get 1 ● Japanese mustard (tube), 3 cm plus

##### Steps to make Octopus and Lettuce Salad with Rich Mayonnaise Dressing:

1.  Mix the ● ingredients.
2.  Finely hand shred the lettuce. Cut the octopus into bite-sized pieces. Mix with the ingredients from Step 1, add seaweed and sesame seeds and toss.
3.  Transfer to a serving plate and it's done.
