2.  Get 1 1/3 cup crushed Ritz crackers

5.  Get 1/3 cup mayonnaise
6.  Prepare 1 egg
7.  Get 1 tsp dry mustard
8.  Get 1/2 lemon juice
9.  Get 1/4 tsp garlic powder

12.  Prepare vegetable oil
13.  Make ready 1 cup Mayo
