##### The ingredients needed to make Zuppa De Mare:

1.  Take 3 garlic cloves
2.  Get 2 tbsp olive oil
3.  Prepare 1 medium onion chopped
4.  Take 1 can of stewed chopped tomatoes
5.  Make ready 1/2 tsp dry italian herbs
6.  Get 1 salt and pepper
7.  Get 1/2 cup squid, cleaned and sliced
8.  Make ready 1/2 cup shrimps, peeled, deveined with tails on
9.  Take 1 cup mussels or clams (optional)
10.  Prepare 1/3 cup chopped parsley for garnish
11.  Take 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

##### Instructions to make Zuppa De Mare:

1.  Saute chopped onion in medium heat till soft then add garlic and tomatoes, season with salt and pepper.
2.  Add squid, cover and simmer for 2 min, add italian herbs the rest of the seafoods.
3.  Simmer for about 3 more minutes and adjust seasoning. Remove from heat and garnish with grated parmesan cheese and chopped parsley.
4.  Serve while hot with pasta or bread. Enjoy!
