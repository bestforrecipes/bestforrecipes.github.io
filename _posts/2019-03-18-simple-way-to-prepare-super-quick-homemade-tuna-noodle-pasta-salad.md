##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna noodle pasta salad:

1.  Prepare 1 tbsp Lemon juice
2.  Get 1 cup Peas
3.  Get 1/2 Red onion(finely chopped)
4.  Make ready 2 1/2 cup Mayo
5.  Get 1 can White albacore tuna
6.  Get 1 Salt&pepper(to taste)
7.  Get 1 box Pasta(use any fun shape)

2.  Mix in seperate bowl mayo, salt&pepper, lemon, and onion
