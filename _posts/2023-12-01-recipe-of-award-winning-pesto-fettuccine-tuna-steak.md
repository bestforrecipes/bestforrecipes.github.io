##### The ingredients needed to make Pesto Fettuccine Tuna Steak:

1.  Prepare 1 packages Tuna Steaks
2.  Make ready 1 3/4 cup olive oil, extra virgin
3.  Make ready 1 packages Fettuccine noodles
4.  Make ready 1 can basil pesto
5.  Prepare 1 large mushroom
6.  Prepare 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes cut in half

##### Instructions to make Pesto Fettuccine Tuna Steak:

1.  Fry tuna steak on medium heat in the olive oil for about 45 minutes in a large sauce pan
2.  While your tuna steak is cooking, bring some water to a boil in a seprate pan and cook your noodles.
3.  Drain water from noodles and add noodles to the large pan the tuna steaks are in.
4.  And basil pesto. I added the entire 10 oz can.
5.  Add cherry tomatoes that were cut in half. And mushrooms
6.  Alow all ingredients to cook together for 10 additional minutes, on low-medium heat, covered.
7.  Enjoy! Eat with garlic bread & a spinach salad.
