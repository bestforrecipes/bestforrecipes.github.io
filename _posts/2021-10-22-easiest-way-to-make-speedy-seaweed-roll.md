##### The ingredients needed to make Seaweed Roll:

1.  Get FILLINGS
2.  Prepare 1 Japanese seaweed nori
3.  Get 1 tbsp dice cooked roasted pork
4.  Make ready 1 tsp dice onion
5.  Get 1 tsp grated carrot
6.  Take 1 tsp grated cucumber
7.  Take 1 tsp crush peanut
8.  Prepare sauce
9.  Get 1 tbsp each hoisin sauce and peri peri sauce

##### Instructions to make Seaweed Roll:

1.  put all filling on seaweed and sauce
2.  wrap up like burrito
3.  serve as it is or cut halve
