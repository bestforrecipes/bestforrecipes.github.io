##### The ingredients needed to make Mom's Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Make ready 1 packages elbow macaroni
2.  Prepare 1 can tuna
3.  Get 3 hard boiled eggs
4.  Take 1/2 cup mayonnaise or miracle whip
5.  Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
6.  Prepare 1/4 cup diced onion
7.  Take 1/4 cup pickle relish
8.  Take salt and pepper (approximately 1 tsp each)
9.  Make ready 1 tsp paprika
