##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon bake:

1.  Prepare 1 each Salmon fillet
2.  Make ready 3 tbsp minced garlic
3.  Take 1/4 cup fish or soy sauce
4.  Take 1/4 cup honey
5.  Make ready 1 garlic pepper

##### Steps to make Salmon bake:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°F
2.  Spread minced garlic over bottom of baking dish. Add sauce. Pour in honey. Mix together to cover bottom of pan.
3.  Place salmon in pan. Sprinkle with garlic pepper. Turn over. Sprinkle this side. This coats both sides of the fillet.
4.  Cook in oven at 350°F for one hour, depending on the size of the fillet.
