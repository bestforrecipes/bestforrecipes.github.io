##### Instructions to make Sauteèd Shrimp with Steamed Broccoli & Carrots:

1.  Heat a large skillet on the stove to medium-high heat.
2.  Add 1 cup of water.
3.  Add shrimp to water, then lightly season with your choice of spice(s).
4.  Sautee' the shrimp for 20 minutes.
5.  Put the carrots and broccoli in a steam basket if you have one and put the lid on the skillet.
6.  Steam the vegetables for 10 minutes.
7.  Place on a plate or in a bowl and enjoy!!
