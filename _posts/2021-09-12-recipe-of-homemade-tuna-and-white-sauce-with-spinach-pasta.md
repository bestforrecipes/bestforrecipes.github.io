##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna and White Sauce with Spinach Pasta:

1.  Make ready 120 ml Milk
2.  Prepare 3 tbsp ☆Powdered cheese
3.  Prepare 1 pinch ☆Salt
4.  Get 1 can Canned tuna
5.  Make ready 1/2 Onion
6.  Get 1/2 Yellow bell pepper (optional)
7.  Prepare 1 Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Tuna and White Sauce with Spinach Pasta:

1.  Mix together the milk, ☆, cheese, and salt.
2.  Stir-fry the tuna and onion in a frying pan. If you don't drain the tuna, you don't have to add any oil to the pan because the tuna oil is enough.
3.  Add the mixture from Step 1 and the yellow bell pepper to the frying pan and bring to a boil. Check the taste and adjust with salt and pepper if necessary.
4.  Add the boiled fresh spinach pasta to Step 3 and mix for about 1-2 minutes. The fresh pasta recipe is at. - - [https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/144722-raw-spinach-pasta](https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/144722-raw-spinach-pasta)
5.  Transfer to plates, sprinkle as desired with pepper, and enjoy.
6.  If using white pasta, I recommend using red bell peppers and broccoli for a beautiful finish.
