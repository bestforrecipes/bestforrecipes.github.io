##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon with Sudachi Mayonnaise Sauce:

1.  Get 4 slice Raw salmon
2.  Get 1 Salt and pepper
3.  Make ready 1 Plain flour
4.  Take 1 clove Garlic
5.  Take 1 tbsp Oil
6.  Prepare Sudachi Mayonnaise Sauce
7.  Get 3 tbsp Mayonnaise
8.  Prepare 1 squeeze 1 sudachi Sudachi (if not, lemon or other citrus fruits)
9.  Prepare 1 tbsp Sugar
10.  Take 1 Krazy Salt (if not, salt and pepper)

##### Instructions to make Salmon with Sudachi Mayonnaise Sauce:

1.  Rub salt and pepper gently onto the salmon. Coat with flour lightly. Mix the sudachi mayonnaise sauce ingredients.
2.  Heat oil in a pan, add the garlic, and start pan-frying the salmon, skin side first. When the skin becomes crispy, flip, and continue pan-frying until cooked through.
3.  Spread the sudachi mayonnaise sauce onto the salmon from Step 2, and it's done. You can add stir-fried vegetables on top if you want, which adds extra volume.
