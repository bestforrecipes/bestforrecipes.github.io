##### The ingredients needed to make sizzling squid Alfredo:

1.  Prepare this is a squid Alfredo it can be eaten alone, over noodles; over rice, or in a sandwich
2.  Take 8 squid steaks
3.  Take 2 chopped bell peppers
4.  Take 1 chopped onion
5.  Take 1 olive oil
6.  Make ready 1 dash zatar
7.  Take 1 dash salt and pepper
8.  Make ready 1 choppped green onion
9.  Prepare 2 dash hot sause
10.  Prepare 1 chopped chilli pepper
11.  Prepare 2 dash garlic powder
12.  Prepare 2 dash prawns masala seasoning
13.  Take 1 jar of alfredo sauce with roasted peppers

##### Instructions to make sizzling squid Alfredo:

1.  put squid steaks in a pan and cover with water
2.  add the hot sauce and boil till the squid is tender
3.  in a separate pan add olive oil just enough to cover bottom of pan
4.  add the bell pepper onions and cook on low heat will tender
5.  slice the squid steaks into rings and add to sauted vegetables add the seasonings stir
6.  pour the Alfredo sauce into the mixture and simmer for about 10 minutes
