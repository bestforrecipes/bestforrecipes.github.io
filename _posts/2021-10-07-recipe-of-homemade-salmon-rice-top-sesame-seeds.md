##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Rice Top Sesame Seeds:

1.  Make ready fish sauce or light soy sauce
2.  Prepare pepper
3.  Make ready toasted black and white sesame
4.  Get cooked rice
5.  Take samon option
6.  Take grilled or baked salmon fillet

##### Steps to make Salmon Rice Top Sesame Seeds:

1.  Toast black and white sesame seed on a pan for a minute then set aside
2.  Baked or grilled salmon then flake the flesh snd set aside
3.  Cooked white rice as usual then add cooked salmon flake with fish sauce and pepper and mix well then baked it for another 5 minute in a rice cooker
4.  Spoon enough salmon rice unto a serbing bowl and press firmly then turn over and serve salmon rice top toasted sesame seed
5.  Salmon rice top with steamed egg then top spicy sauce
