##### The ingredients needed to make Tomato Pasta with King Crab:

1.  Make ready 1/4 Taraba King crab
2.  Take 1 can Canned tomatoes
3.  Take 60 grams per serving Pasta
4.  Make ready 100 ml Heavy cream
5.  Make ready 3 clove Garlic
6.  Prepare 2 Red chili peppers
7.  Prepare 1 Salt
8.  Make ready 1 Olive oil
9.  Take 6 Cherry tomatoes

##### Instructions to make Tomato Pasta with King Crab:

1.  Slice garlic and heat olive oil over low heat.
2.  Mash the tomato in the can with a potato masher. Add to the pan and simmer over medium heat.
3.  Add crab meat and heavy cream. Season with salt!
4.  When the pasta is cooked, toss everything with the pasta! Incorporate plenty of air as you mix.
