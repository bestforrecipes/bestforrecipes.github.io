##### Steps to make salmon fried rice:

1.  heat oil in fry pan. add whisked egg to fry
2.  toss rice in the frying pan and stir fry with the egg until rice is heated evenly
3.  add salmon in the middle of the rice
4.  stir fry for 3-5 minute until salmon cooked
5.  sprinkle salt,siy sauce and black pepper
6.  stir fry for another minute or two
7.  serve
