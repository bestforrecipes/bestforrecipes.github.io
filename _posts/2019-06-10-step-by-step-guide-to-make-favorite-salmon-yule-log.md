##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Yule Log:

1.  Prepare can Salmon
2.  Make ready package cream cheese softened
3.  Prepare lemon juice
4.  Get grated onion
5.  Make ready horseradish
6.  Take liquid smoke
7.  Get salt
8.  Get chopped pecans

##### Instructions to make Salmon Yule Log:

1.  Drain and flake salmon, take out any bones.
2.  Add all ingredients except pecans, combine well.
3.  Chill for several hours or overnight
4.  Shape into log and chill for 2-3 more hours
5.  Sprinkle pecans on wax paper, roll log in pecans
6.  Chill until ready to serve, serve with crackers of choice. I use Ritz, enjoy!
