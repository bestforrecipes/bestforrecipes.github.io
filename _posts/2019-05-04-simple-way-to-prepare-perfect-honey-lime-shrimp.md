##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Lime Shrimp:

1.  Prepare kilo shrimps, deveined and peeled except for the heads and tails
2.  Take Red bell pepper (any color of your choice), squared for skewering
3.  Make ready Olive oil
4.  Prepare lime juice
5.  Take Worcestershire sauce
6.  Get honey
7.  Get big garlic cloves, chopped
8.  Prepare Salt and pepper
9.  Prepare Barbecue sticks / skewers

##### Instructions to make Honey Lime Shrimp:

1.  Make a marinade mixing the olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, honey, lime juice, and garlic. After shrimps have been deveined and shelled, put them in the marinade and leave to marinade for 10 minutes.
2.  After 10 minutes, skewer red bell pepper and shrimps together, alternating pepper and shrimp.
3.  Take a grill pan and add olive oil (or butter) on it and grill the shrimp until they turn pink.
4.  Serve and enjoy! ☺️
