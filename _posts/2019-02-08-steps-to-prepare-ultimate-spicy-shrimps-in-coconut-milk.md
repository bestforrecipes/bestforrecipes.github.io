##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Shrimps in Coconut Milk:

1.  Take 1/4 cup oil
2.  Make ready 1 chopped medium size onion
3.  Prepare 1 tbsp minced garlic
4.  Prepare 2 tsp minced ginger
5.  Prepare 2 tbsp coriander
6.  Get 1/4 tsp cumin
7.  Make ready 1/4 tsp cinnamon
8.  Prepare 1/8 tsp cayenne
9.  Make ready 1/8 tsp tumeric
10.  Take 1 cup drained canned tomatoes
11.  Get 2 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk
12.  Get 1/2 cup water
13.  Take 1 tsp salt
14.  Take 3/4 cup fresh cilantro
15.  Make ready 1 1/2 Ibs large shrimps, peeled and cleaned

##### Instructions to make Spicy Shrimps in Coconut Milk:

1.  Heat oil in skillet on med-hi heat
2.  Add onions, fry for 3 minutes
3.  Add garlic and ginger, cook for 2 minutes
4.  Add next 5 spice, cook for 1 minute
5.  Add canned tomatoes (or chopped tomatoes) and cook for 3 minutes.
6.  Add coconut milk, water, salt and bring to simmer until thicken, about 5-10 minutes
7.  Add shrimps, simmer and stirring for 5 minutes
8.  Stir in cilantro and serve with lime wedge
