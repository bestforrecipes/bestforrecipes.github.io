##### The ingredients needed to make California Rolls:

1.  Take 450 grams White rice
2.  Make ready 4 tbsp Vinegar mix) vinegar
3.  Make ready 2 level tablespoons Vinegar mix) sugar
4.  Get 2 level teaspoons Vinegar mix) salt
5.  Prepare 4 sheets Nori (sheet of dried seaweed)
6.  Make ready 1 Avocado
7.  Take 12 Imitation processed crab stick
8.  Get 2 eggs worth Thick egg omelette
9.  Get 4 leaves Lettuce
10.  Get 1 Mayonnaise
11.  Take 1 White sesame seeds

##### Instructions to make California Rolls:

1.  Place 1 sheet of nori seaweed on the surface and cover in a layer of sushi rice. Sprinkle sesame seeds over the rice and then cover with a layer of cling film.
2.  Flip everything over and cover 2/3 of the nori seaweed with another thin layer of sushi rice.
3.  Line the lettuce, omelette, avocado and crab sticks along the center of the rice as shown in the photograph and squeeze over some mayonnaise.
4.  Use the cling film to wrap the roll. First wrap one of the edges over the ingredients in the middle and secure in place tightly, then wrap the remaining roll around.
5.  Leave the cling film on as you cut and serve with the wrap still attached. If you do this, the rice won't stick to the plate and can be taken away easily.
