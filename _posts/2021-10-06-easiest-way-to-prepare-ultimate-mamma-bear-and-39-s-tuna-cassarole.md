##### The ingredients needed to make Mamma Bear's Tuna Cassarole:

1.  Take Cassarole
2.  Get 1 lb Egg Noodles
3.  Take 12 oz Chunk Light Tuna
4.  Make ready 1 can Evaporated Milk
5.  Make ready 3 tbsp Salt
6.  Get 2 tbsp Pepper
7.  Prepare 4 quart Water
8.  Take 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
9.  Make ready 10 3/4 oz Cream of Mushroom Soup

##### Instructions to make Mamma Bear's Tuna Cassarole:

1.  375 degree oven
2.  Boil water with salt. Add noodles. Cook 10-11 minutes. Draing noodles and tuna. Mix all remaining items in pot. Add 1 cup cheese ( optional ) and pour in cassarole dish. Add cheese on top. ( optional) Cover with foil. Bake 10 minutes. Uncover, cook 20 minutes more. Remove from oven. Serve.
