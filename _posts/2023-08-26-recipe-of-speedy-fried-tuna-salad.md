##### The ingredients needed to make Fried tuna salad:

1.  Make ready salad
2.  Prepare 10 oz canned tuna
3.  Make ready 2 tbsp mayonnaise
4.  Prepare 1 tsp yellow prepared mustard
5.  Take 1 tbsp sweet salad cubes, relish
6.  Take 2 tbsp diced onion
7.  Get 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
8.  Prepare 1 tbsp baking powder
9.  Make ready sauce
10.  Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp mayonnaise
11.  Make ready 1/2 tbsp sweet salad cubes, relish
12.  Make ready 1/4 tsp dill weed
13.  Take frying
