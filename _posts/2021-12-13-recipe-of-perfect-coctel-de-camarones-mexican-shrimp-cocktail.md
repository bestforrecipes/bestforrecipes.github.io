##### The ingredients needed to make Coctel de Camarones Mexican Shrimp Cocktail:

1.  Take 1/2 large sweet onion, diced
2.  Prepare 2-3 medium tomatoes, diced
3.  Get 1/2 English cucumber, diced
4.  Get 1/2 avocado, diced
5.  Get 2-3 serano peppers, diced
6.  Prepare 2 Tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
7.  Get 1 cup Clamato juice
8.  Get 1 cup tomato juice
9.  Take 2 Tbsp ketchup
10.  Make ready Juice of 1 lime
11.  Take 1 lb shrimp, cooked, cooled and diced
12.  Make ready Dash Worcestershire sauce
13.  Take Dash tobasco sauce
14.  Prepare to taste Salt and black pepper

##### Steps to make Coctel de Camarones Mexican Shrimp Cocktail:

1.  If you are using fresh shrimp, cook these first and let them cool completely.
2.  Chop all vegetables.
3.  Add all ingredients to a container with a lid.
4.  Refrigerate for a couple hours so flavors can blend.
