##### The ingredients needed to make Tamarind Salmon:

1.  Take 4 salmon filets
2.  Prepare 1 bottle tamarind H1 sauce
3.  Prepare 1 tbsp ginger; minced
4.  Get 1 tbsp onion powder
5.  Get 2 tsp lemon peel seasoning
6.  Prepare 2 tsp ground coriander seed
7.  Take 1 tsp garlic powder
8.  Get 1 tsp dried basil
9.  Prepare 1 sea salt
10.  Take 1 EVOO; as needed
11.  Take 1 peppercorn melange

##### Instructions to make Tamarind Salmon:

1.  Marinate fish in enough H1 to cover with minced ginger for 24 hours.
2.  Dry salmon. Cover with just enough oil to cover. Season. Roast at 400° for approximately 10-15 minutes or until thermometer reaches 145°
3.  Variations; Mustard, parsley, Worcestershire, citrus, tarragon, roasted garlic, lemon-pepper seasoning
