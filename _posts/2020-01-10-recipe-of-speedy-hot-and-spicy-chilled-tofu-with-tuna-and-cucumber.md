##### The ingredients needed to make Hot and Spicy Chilled Tofu with Tuna and Cucumber:

1.  Prepare 2 blocks Silken tofu (mini-sized)
2.  Prepare 1/2 can - about 40 grams Canned tuna
3.  Take 1 tsp Ground sesame seeds
4.  Make ready 1 tsp Miso
5.  Get 1/2 tsp Doubanjiang
6.  Prepare 1/4 Cucumber

##### Steps to make Hot and Spicy Chilled Tofu with Tuna and Cucumber:

1.  Cut the cucumber in half lengthwise and slice about 2 mm thick. Drain the oil from the tuna.
2.  Put the cucumber, tuna, sesame seeds, miso and doubanjiang in a bowl and mix together.
3.  Put the mixture from Step 2 on top of the tofu.
