##### Instructions to make Salmon and asparagus:

1.  Place the salmon on tin foil and use half lime to cover them with a splash of oil and some freshly ground pepper.
2.  Wrap into a tight parcel and place in a preheated oven at 180 degrees for 25 minutes
3.  Chop the ends off of the asparagus and place in a warm pan with olive oil and some lime juice, fry on a gentle heat for max of ten minutes. Add a little spring onion if you fancy it.
4.  Serve with side salad and garlic bread.
