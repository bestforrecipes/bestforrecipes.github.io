##### The ingredients needed to make 5 Minute Milk Risotto that Kids Love:

1.  Get 1 Spinach (frozen)
2.  Get 1/3 Canned tuna
3.  Make ready 1 Egg
4.  Take 1 Salt and pepper
5.  Prepare 1 tsp Butter
6.  Take 200 ml Milk
7.  Get 1 rice bowl full Plain cooked rice
8.  Prepare 2 Tbsp Heavy cream (optional)

##### Instructions to make 5 Minute Milk Risotto that Kids Love:

1.  Melt the butter in a small sauce pan, stir-fry the spinach (use frozen spinach as-is), and add the canned tuna (drain the excess oil beforehand).
2.  Beat the egg, pour it into the pan from Step 1, stir-fry with the other ingredients and continue stirring as you would when making scrambled eggs. Season with salt and pepper.
3.  Pour in the milk, once simmered with the other ingredients, add the rice.
4.  Simmer until the rice have plumped up and thickened, (pour in the heavy cream if you have some), and it's done.
