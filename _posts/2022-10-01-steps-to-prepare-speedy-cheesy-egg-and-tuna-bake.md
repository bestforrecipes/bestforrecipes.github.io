##### The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Egg and Tuna Bake:

1.  Make ready 2 (6 oz) canned tuna, drained and fluffed
2.  Take 1/3 cup mozzarella cheese
3.  Make ready 1/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
4.  Get 1/3 diced celery (or vegetable of your choice)
5.  Take 3 tbsp mayonnaise
6.  Prepare 1 tsp sriracha hot sauce
7.  Take 1 egg
8.  Prepare 2 tbsp scallion for garnish (optional)

##### Steps to make Cheesy Egg and Tuna Bake:

1.  Lightly grease a shallow bakeware and set aside.
2.  In a large bowl, mix all the ingredients, except for the egg. Then, transfer the mixture to the bakeware. Lightly press the center of the mixture with a spoon and create a crater.
3.  Crack an egg into the crater and air fry at 380F (190C) for 8-10 minutes until the egg white is cooked through.
4.  Sprinkle some scallion over the tuna bake to serve.
