##### Steps to make Easy Crab Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms:

1.  Preheat oven to 375. Wash mushrooms, remove and discard stems.
2.  Mix water and stuffing mix and set aside for 5 minutes to soften.
3.  Mix all filling ingredients and stir in stuffing mix. Lay mushrooms cap side down on a foil lined baking sheet. Scoop about a tablespoon of filling into Each one. Bake uncovered 18-20 minutes.
