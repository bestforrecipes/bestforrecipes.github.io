2.  Get 4 oz fresh oyster
3.  Get 1/2 pack silk tofu
4.  Get 1 cup summer squash
5.  Prepare 1 tsp pickled chili or kale (optional)
6.  Get 1 garlic and 1 tsp Ginger minced
7.  Prepare 2 Tsp Olive oil
8.  Take Salt and fish sauce
