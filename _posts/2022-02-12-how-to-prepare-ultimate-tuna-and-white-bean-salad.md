##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna and White Bean Salad:

1.  Take 400 g Cannellini Beans
2.  Take 300 g Albacore Tuna
3.  Get 10 Anchovies
4.  Prepare 1 Red Onion
5.  Prepare 450 g Jar Roasted Red & Yellow Peppers
6.  Get 250 g Cherry Tomatoes
7.  Get 100 g Black Olives
8.  Make ready 50 g Parsley
9.  Get 1 tbsp Garlic Puree
10.  Prepare 2 tbsp Olive Oil
11.  Get 1 Juice of Lemon
12.  Make ready 1 tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
13.  Get #MyCookbook

##### Instructions to make Tuna and White Bean Salad:

1.  Chop onion and place in a bowl and add olive oil, balsamic and garlic purée.
2.  Chop peppers and slice tomatoes into quarters and olives into halves and add to mix.
3.  Add cannellini beans together with flaked tuna and finely sliced anchovies.
4.  Finally add juice of lemon and parsley and enjoy.
