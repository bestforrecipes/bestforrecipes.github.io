##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Salmon with Caramelized Onions:

1.  Get Salmon fillets
2.  Prepare Onion
3.  Make ready Black pepper
4.  Get Paprika
5.  Make ready Garlic
6.  Make ready Cayenne pepper
7.  Prepare Onion powder
8.  Prepare Salt
9.  Make ready Dijon mustard
10.  Make ready Brown sugar
11.  Take Olive oil

##### Steps to make Baked Salmon with Caramelized Onions:

1.  Combine pepper, salt, paprika, cayenne, onion powder, garlic, sugar, mustard, and olive oil. This should make a thick paste.
2.  Spread mixture over salmon and let sit for an hour or so
3.  Chop onion. Heat olive oil in a pan and add onion. Cook until golden & caramelized. Set aside.
4.  Preheat oven to 400. Bake salmon for approx 20 mins. I sometimes add a little maple syrup to the bottom of the pan before baking. Make sure to put down non stick foil if you do this for easier cleanup
5.  Spread caramelized onions generously over the top of the salmon. Serve.
