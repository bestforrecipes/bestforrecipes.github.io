##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Fish:

1.  Make ready Fish Filet (Indian Salmon, Sea Bass, or Seer)
2.  Prepare White wine vinegar
3.  Take Gingergarlic paste
4.  Prepare Thyme Dried
5.  Prepare Rosemary Dried
6.  Get Basil Dried
7.  Make ready Salt
8.  Take Olive oil to brush on the grill surface

##### Steps to make Grilled Fish:

1.  Lightly mix all the spices above.
2.  Marinate the fish filet with the mix and let it rest for 1/2 an hour.
3.  Switch the grill ON and let it heat at the default pre-heat setting. (3 mins, approx.)
4.  Grease the grill surface (both base and lid) lightly with a brush dipped in olive oil.
5.  Place the fish on the base and close the lid ensuring that it doesn't touch the top of the filet. Your filet should be grilled in 3-4 mins.
6.  (Optional) If your grill doesn't have this provision in step 5, keep the lid open. Flip the filet after 3-4 mins and grill for 2-3 mins longer. Et, voila! Ready to eat with lightly buttered, mixed-herb rice or pasta!
