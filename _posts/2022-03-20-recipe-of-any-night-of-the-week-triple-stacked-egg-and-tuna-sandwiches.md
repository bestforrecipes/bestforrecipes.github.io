4.  Make ready 3 tablespoon mayonnaise
5.  Make ready 1 teaspoon dill weed
6.  Take 1-1/2 tablespoons chopped parsley
7.  Get 3 tablespoon sweet pickle cubes
8.  Make ready as needed iceberg lettuce
9.  Take as needed sliced Havarti cheese
10.  Get 3 cans tuna drained
