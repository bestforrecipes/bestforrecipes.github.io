##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Tuna Pesto Pasta:

1.  Make ready Pasta
2.  Get Pesto from Gourmet Farms
3.  Prepare Onions
4.  Take Garlic
5.  Take Bell pepper
6.  Get Heavy Cream
7.  Prepare Century Tuna
8.  Prepare Olive Oil
9.  Take Grated cheese

##### Instructions to make Creamy Tuna Pesto Pasta:

1.  Boil pasta until just tender.
2.  Meanwhile, heat olive oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and onion and sauté for 3 – 4 minutes. Add the Century Tuna and continue sautéing until vegetables are soft. Then add the heavy cream
3.  Drain pasta and place back in sauce pan. Mix in vegetable and tuna mixture. Serve with grated cheese on the top.
