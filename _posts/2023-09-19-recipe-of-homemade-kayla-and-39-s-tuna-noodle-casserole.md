2.  Take 12 oz can of tuna
3.  Take 26 oz can cream of chicken soup
4.  Take 1/4 tsp garlic powder
5.  Get 1 salt and pepper to taste
6.  Get 1/3 cup french's cheddar fried onions
7.  Get 1 couple shakes of grated parmesan cheese
8.  Prepare 1/3 cup Panko crumbs
