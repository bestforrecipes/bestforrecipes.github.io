##### The ingredients needed to make Stir-Fried Konnyaku with Bonito Flakes and Kimchi:

1.  Get 1 piece Konnyaku
2.  Get 50 grams Kimchi (finely chopped)
3.  Get 1 handful Bonito flakes
4.  Get 1 tsp Soy sauce
5.  Get 1 tsp Vegetable oil
6.  Prepare 1 dash Sesame oil

##### Steps to make Stir-Fried Konnyaku with Bonito Flakes and Kimchi:

1.  These are the ingredients. Clockwise from the front: Kimchi, bonito flakes, konnyaku (black), oil, soy sauce, sesame oil.
2.  Make lots of holes in the konnyaku with a fork or toothpick. Cut out bite-sized pieces from it with a spoon.
3.  Boil the konnyaku for about 2 minutes, and drain.
4.  Heat the vegetable oil in a frying pan and stir-fry the konnyaku.
5.  Add the chopped kimchi and stir-fry.
6.  Drizzle in the soy sauce, mix and turn off the heat.
7.  Add the bonito flakes and mix.
8.  Drizzle with a little sesame oil to taste, to make it even more fragrant.
