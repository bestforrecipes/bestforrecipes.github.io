##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Fry:

1.  Get As needed Cleaned and wash salmon fish(other fish also)
2.  Take For marinate-
3.  Make ready 3 tbsp lemon juice
4.  Take Pinch turmeric
5.  Prepare 1 tbsp garlic ginger paste
6.  Make ready To taste Salt as taste
7.  Make ready 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
8.  Make ready 1 tsp garam masala
9.  Make ready 1 tsp coriander powder
10.  Take 1 tsp fish masala(any brand)
11.  Get 1 tbsp rice flour
12.  Get 1 tbsp gram flour
13.  Make ready 1 tbsp semolina
14.  Make ready 1 tsp mustard seeds

##### Steps to make Salmon Fry:

1.  Take a bowl and marinate the fish than rest for 10 minutes
2.  After 10 minutes add rice flour, gram flour and semolina mix well with the help of lit bit water and apply on fish as a coating,
3.  Than take pan for frying i made shallow fry you can deep frying also.when oil is hot (medium to high flame)than put fish on pan,cook till golden brown in colour,
4.  After that turn it over and same process repeat on other side
5.  Serve hot with chutney,it's very healthy and nutritional fish.
