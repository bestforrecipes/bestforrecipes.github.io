##### The ingredients needed to make Lemon Herb Salmon:

1.  Take salmon filets
2.  Prepare lemon juice
3.  Get lemon zest
4.  Get olive oil
5.  Get dried majoram, chopped
6.  Take dried thyme, chopped
7.  Get dried basil, chopped
8.  Get sea salt
9.  Prepare pepper
10.  Make ready cooking spray

##### Instructions to make Lemon Herb Salmon:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F
2.  Mix together ingredients in bowl
3.  Marinate the salmon with mixture and store in fridge
4.  Place tin foil on cooking sheets and cover with nonstick cooking spray
5.  Place filets on cooking sheet and cook for 15-20 minutes for larger filets
6.  Serve filets with marinade juices
