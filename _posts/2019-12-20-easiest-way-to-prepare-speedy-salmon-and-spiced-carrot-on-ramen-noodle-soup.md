##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon And Spiced Carrot On Ramen Noodle Soup:

1.  Prepare garnish
2.  Take 1 tbsp fresh dill
3.  Make ready SALMON AND SPICE CARROT
4.  Get 3 tbsp cooked salmon
5.  Make ready 1/2 cup grated carrot
6.  Make ready 1 dash cumin powder
7.  Take 1 dash coriander powder
8.  Get 1/2 tbsp oilive oil
9.  Take NOODLE OPTION
10.  Get 1 fried Hokkien Mee Instant Noodle or any ramen

##### Steps to make Salmon And Spiced Carrot On Ramen Noodle Soup:

1.  cooked ramen/ instant noodles as package ,bring a pot of water to a boil then add in the ramen with its,seasonal package provide and let it simmer for 3 minutes then off heat and serve
2.  SALMON AND SPICE CARROT
3.  Mix carrot with cumin powder and coriander powder then with olive oil and baked it at 200C /400 F for 20 minute then add the cooked salmon flake and mix well for another 5 minutes
4.  TO SERVE JUST TOP SALMON AND SPICE CARROT TOP FRESH DILL ON THE COOKED RAMEN NOODLE SOUP AND ENJOY
