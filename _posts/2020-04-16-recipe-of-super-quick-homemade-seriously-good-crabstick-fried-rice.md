##### The ingredients needed to make Seriously Good ☆ Crabstick Fried Rice:

1.  Make ready 2 long ones Imitation crab sticks
2.  Take 1 Egg
3.  Get 1 leaf Lettuce
4.  Prepare 1 heaping rice bowl full Plain cooked rice
5.  Prepare 1/4 tsp Salt
6.  Prepare 1 dash Pepper
7.  Get 1 dash Soy sauce
8.  Prepare 1 Vegetable oil

##### Steps to make Seriously Good ☆ Crabstick Fried Rice:

1.  Cut the crab sticks into 3cm pieces, and shred finely with your hands. Cut the lettuce into small pieces.
2.  Heat a pan well until it's smoking lightly, and add a generous amount of oil. Add the beaten egg and mix quickly.
3.  Add the rice immediately and stir fry while mixing with the egg. Add the crab sticks and continue stir frying.
4.  Season with salt and pepper, and drizzle the soy sauce on the hot surface of the pan so that it browns a bit. Stir fry everything together to combine.
5.  Add the lettuce at the end and turn off the heat immediately. Mix the lettuce through and it's done. ☆
