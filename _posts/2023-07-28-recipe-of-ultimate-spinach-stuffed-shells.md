##### The ingredients needed to make Spinach Stuffed Shells:

1.  Make ready 1 (16 oz) container of Ricotta cheese
2.  Get 1 packet shredded Mozzarella cheese
3.  Take Basil
4.  Take Salt and pepper
5.  Make ready 5 cloves garlic
6.  Make ready 1 package frozen spinach
7.  Take 1 box jumbo shells
8.  Make ready 1 jar spaghetti sauce
9.  Get Granulated garlic

##### Steps to make Spinach Stuffed Shells:

1.  Coat the bottom of a crock pot with a small amount of the spaghetti sauce and turn on high heat
2.  Mix together Ricotta cheese, half the bag of Mozzarella cheese, basil, salt and pepper, granulated garlic, and chopped garlic and cooled cooked spinach
3.  Stuff the shells and place into crock pot, add the rest of the bag of cheese, and top with remaining jar of sauce!
4.  I added more salt and pepper, granulated garlic, and basil and closed the lid! Cook for 4 hours on high!
