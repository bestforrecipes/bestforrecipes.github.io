##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Salmon Fish Kabobs:

1.  Take 6 oz. boneless pieces of fresh salmon
2.  Make ready 1 TB Chef Luciano's Fish Tikka Masala
3.  Get 1 TB desi ghee

##### Steps to make Spicy Salmon Fish Kabobs:

1.  Rub Fish Masala on fish pieces.
2.  Heat Ghee in a Heavy bottom Pan. Add Fish Pieces and cook 4 minutes on each side.
3.  Serve over boiled Rice. Garnish with Green Chillies and cilantro.
