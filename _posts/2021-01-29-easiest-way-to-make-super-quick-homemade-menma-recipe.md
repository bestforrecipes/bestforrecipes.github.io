##### The ingredients needed to make Menma Recipe:

1.  Get 250 g Bamboo shoots
2.  Take 1 Tbsp sesame oil
3.  Prepare 800 g Water
4.  Make ready 10 g Kombu
5.  Get 15 g Katsuoboshi
6.  Prepare 10 ml Mirin
7.  Make ready 30 ml Dark Shoyu
8.  Prepare 30 ml chashu sauce
9.  Prepare 5 g white sugar

##### Instructions to make Menma Recipe:

1.  Make Dashi from Kombu and Katsuoboshi
2.  Fry the menma in oil on low heat for 3 minutes
3.  Combine all ingredients in pan and boil until liquid is evaporated
