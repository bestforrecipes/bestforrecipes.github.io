##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp, Tomato, Basil Salad:

1.  Take 1 1/2 lb precooked & peeled, large or jumbo shrimp
2.  Make ready 6 cup halved small tomatoes of various sizes & colors.
3.  Get 1 lb. asparagus, trimmed and halved
4.  Prepare 2 small avocados, cubed
5.  Prepare 1 cup fresh basil leaves
6.  Prepare 1/2 cup crumbled natural gorgonzola or blue cheese(optional)
7.  Get 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
8.  Make ready 1 juice of 1 fresh lemon
9.  Prepare 1 tbsp organic dijon mustard
10.  Make ready 1/2 tsp each sea salt & pepper

##### Steps to make Shrimp, Tomato, Basil Salad:

1.  Boil asparagus in sea salted water for 2 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water - Mix all ingredients gently in large bowl, coating with dressing. Serve immediately
