##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Pita:

1.  Get 1 Whole Wheat Pocket Pita, cut in half
2.  Prepare 1 Can Tuna in Water, drained
3.  Prepare 1 Tbs. Onion, diced
4.  Get 1 Tbs. Celery, diced
5.  Get 1 Tbs. Green Bell Pepper, diced
6.  Prepare 2 Tbs. Light Mayonnaise
7.  Prepare Pinch Salt
8.  Get Pinch Pepper
9.  Prepare Pinch Garlic Powder
10.  Prepare 2-3 slices tomato

##### Steps to make Tuna Pita:

1.  In a bowl mix together all the ingredients except the pitas and the tomatoes.
2.  Place the tomatoe slices inside each pita half. Cut in half if needed.
3.  Stuff half of tuna mixture in each pita half.
