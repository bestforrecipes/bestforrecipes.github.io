##### The ingredients needed to make California Rolls For Guests:

1.  Take 350 grams Hot cooked rice
2.  Take 1 enough for the rice Sushi vinegar
3.  Prepare 8 Imitation crab sticks
4.  Prepare 1 Cucumber
5.  Get 2 sheets Nori seaweed
6.  Make ready 1 tbsp Toasted sesame seeds

##### Instructions to make California Rolls For Guests:

1.  To prepare the sushi rice: Stir the appropriate amount of sushi vinegar into the warm cooked rice. Let cool.
2.  Put a sheet of plastic wrap on a sushi mat. Spread on a layer of rice, lay the seaweed on top. On top of the seaweed, arrange the salad leaves, imitation crab meat, and cucumber. Roll it up while pressing the ingredients inside. (The rice should be on the outside of the roll.)
3.  Sprinkle sesame seeds over the rice, cut into desired sizes, and you're all done.
