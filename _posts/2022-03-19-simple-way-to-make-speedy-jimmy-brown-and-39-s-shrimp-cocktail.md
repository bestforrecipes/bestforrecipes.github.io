##### The ingredients needed to make Jimmy Brown's Shrimp Cocktail:

1.  Get Shrimp cocktail
2.  Take 1 kg shrimp
3.  Make ready 1 head iceberg lettuce
4.  Prepare 1 each avocado
5.  Get Seafood Sauce
6.  Take 1 cup whole egg mayo
7.  Prepare 3 tbsp tomato ketchup
8.  Take 2 tbsp worcesteshire sauce
9.  Prepare 2 tbsp lemon juice

##### Steps to make Jimmy Brown's Shrimp Cocktail:

1.  Devein, shell & clean the shrimp
2.  Finally slice the lettuce & avocado
3.  Combine the mayo, ketchup, worcesteshire & lemon juice. Salt & pepper to taste.
4.  Now its about building… Place lettuce at base of bowl followed by shrimp, avocado & sauce.
