##### The ingredients needed to make Avocado Tuna Salad:

1.  Get 1 Ripe avocado
2.  Get 2 can Tuna, drained and squeezed
3.  Prepare 1 Jalapeño
4.  Take 1 as needed Salt

##### Instructions to make Avocado Tuna Salad:

1.  Drain the cans of tuna, squeeze out as much moisture as possible. Place in a mixing bowl.
2.  Half and pit the avocado scoop it out of the skin and add it to the bowl.
3.  Using a cheese grater shred the jalapeño into the bowl. For a less spicy version take out the inner membrane and seeds.
4.  Salt to taste
5.  Combine in the bowl, I used a fork to mash it all together until well combined
6.  Use it anywhere you would normally use tuna salad
