##### The ingredients needed to make Glazed Salmon:

1.  Make ready salmon fillets
2.  Take butter
3.  Get brown sugar
4.  Take clove honey
5.  Prepare soy sauce
6.  Take ground ginger
7.  Make ready dijon mustard
8.  Prepare salt and pepper
9.  Get garlic powder

##### Instructions to make Glazed Salmon:

1.  Prepare glaze by melting butter in a skillet, then adding all other ingredients except salmon on low heat. Takes about 5 minutes for all to combine
2.  Once glaze is finished allow to sit and cool about 10 min.
3.  Place salmon on top of tin foil, add glaze over the top and wrap completely in foil. Let sit in the fridge at least an hour. I prefer 2 hours.
4.  After 1-2 hrs in fridge, keeping salmon wrapped in the foil place on cookie sheet and bake in preheated oven at 450°F for 20 minutes.
5.  Enjoy! I always have a strawberry salad with mine ;)
