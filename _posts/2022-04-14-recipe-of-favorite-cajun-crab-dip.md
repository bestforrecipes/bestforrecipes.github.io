##### The ingredients needed to make Cajun Crab Dip:

1.  Make ready 1 lb Imitation Crab Meat (chopped)
2.  Make ready 2 packages 8oz. Cream Cheese
3.  Make ready 2/3 cup Sour Cream
4.  Make ready 2 tbsp Frank's Hot Sauce
5.  Get 1 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
6.  Make ready 1 tbsp Cajun Spice
7.  Take 1 cup Colby Jack Cheese (shredded)
8.  Prepare 1 tsp Minced Garlic
9.  Prepare 1 tsp Italian Seasoning
10.  Make ready 2 tsp Tabasco Sauce
11.  Make ready 1 tsp Horseradish

##### Steps to make Cajun Crab Dip:

1.  Mix all ingredients, except for the crab and the shredded cheese, in a food processor or mixer.
2.  Fold in the crab and shredded cheese.
3.  Refrigerate 2 hours.
4.  Serve with your favorite crackers. I used Wheat crackers.
