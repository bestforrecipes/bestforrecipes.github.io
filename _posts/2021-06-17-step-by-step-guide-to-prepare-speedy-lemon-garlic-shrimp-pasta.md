##### The ingredients needed to make Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta:

1.  Take Olive oil
2.  Make ready Butter
3.  Make ready 4 cloves Garlic - sliced into tiny bits
4.  Take Ground Red Pepper
5.  Take Large Shrimps
6.  Get Salt
7.  Take Pepper
8.  Take 1 tbsp Dried Oregano
9.  Make ready Parmesan
10.  Prepare Parsley
11.  Prepare Pasta
12.  Take 1 Lemon

##### Instructions to make Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta:

1.  Heal olive oil in a pan and add a cube of butter
2.  Cook shrimp in the heated pan and add the tiny bits of garlic
3.  Add salt and pepper then a tbsp of dried oregano
4.  Add the pasta and another cube of butter
5.  Sprinkle parsley and squeeze the lemon to the mixture
