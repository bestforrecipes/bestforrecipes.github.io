2.  Make ready 1/2 cup chopped onion
3.  Take 1/4 cup lime juice
4.  Get 1/4 cup tequila
5.  Prepare 2 tbs. chile powder
6.  Prepare 2 tbs. garlic powder
7.  Take 2 tbs. salt or less depends on ur liking
8.  Take 1 sliced lemon
9.  Prepare 2 tbs. evoo

##### Instructions to make Mandy's Tequila. Lime SHRiMP Taco's:
