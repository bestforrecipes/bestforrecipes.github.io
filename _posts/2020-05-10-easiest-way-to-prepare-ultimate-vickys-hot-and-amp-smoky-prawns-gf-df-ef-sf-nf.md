##### The ingredients needed to make Vickys Hot & Smoky Prawns, GF DF EF SF NF:

1.  Make ready 200 grams large raw prawns
2.  Prepare 1 tsp olive oil
3.  Make ready 1 tsp worcestershire sauce, gluten-free recipe on my profile (vic20adamant)
4.  Take 1/2 tsp chopped coriander/cilantro
5.  Take 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
6.  Take 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
7.  Make ready 1/4 tsp sugar
8.  Make ready 1 pinch salt
9.  Prepare 2 clove garlic, chopped

##### Steps to make Vickys Hot & Smoky Prawns, GF DF EF SF NF:

1.  Add all ingredients to a bowl and gently toss together
2.  Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes to marinade
3.  Pour into a frying pan and cook off over a medium heat until the prawns turn pink
4.  Serve and enjoy immediately
5.  You could also thread the prawns onto skewers and grill them
