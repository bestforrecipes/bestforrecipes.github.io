##### The ingredients needed to make Fisherman soup:

1.  Make ready 2 medium size cat fish
2.  Get 250 ml(half cup) of periwinkle
3.  Take 500 ml shrimps and prawns
4.  Take Oysters
5.  Make ready 1 little kpomo
6.  Prepare 1 cup ground crayfish
7.  Make ready Half cup of palm oil
8.  Make ready 3 tablespoon all purpose flour as thickener

Finely chop the vegetables and boil. When ready, strain and remove the vegetables. Add the various types of fish, chopped in small pieces; strain the fish pieces liquid into another pot. Strain the fish, removing all bones at the same time.

##### Steps to make Fisherman soup:

1.  Let's start by washing the catfish
2.  Cut up the cat fish into sizable chunks
3.  Boil water and pour on the fish to wash off the dirt.don't leave it in hot water for more than 3-5minutes
4.  Wash fish, place in a pot and cook, make sure to season with enough fresh pepper, onions, salt and seasoning cubes
5.  Allow to cook for 10 minutes
6.  Add washed shrimps and prawns, oysters and periwinkle.
7.  Allow to cook for another 10 minutes
8.  Turn off the heat and turn content into a bowl. Take out three to four chunks of fish and shred
9.  Rinse the pot, place it on the cooker and turn up heat to medium
10.  Fry oil
11.  Add diced onions and stir. Add shredded fish and stir continuously for 5minutee
12.  Sieve out the shrimps, prawns, periwinkle and kpomo and add to the frying content and stir for 5 minutes
13.  Add fish stock. Add the flour(you can always use any other thickener, I prefer flour cos it doesn't leave behind any traces in the tongue or its scent in the nose)
14.  Stir content of pot and allow to cook for 10 mins
15.  Add crayfish
16.  Cook for another 10 mins.
17.  Fisherman soup is best served hot with Fofo.
