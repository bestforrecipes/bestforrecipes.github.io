##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese-style Stir-Fried Squid and Broccoli:

1.  Make ready Squid
2.  Make ready Broccoli
3.  Take Garlic
4.  Get to 2 tablespoons Sesame oil
5.  Make ready ★ Sake
6.  Get ★ Chicken stock powder
7.  Get to 2 teaspoon ★ Oyster sauce
8.  Take ★ Water
9.  Take ★ Salt and pepper
10.  Prepare ◇ Katakuriko
11.  Get ◇ Water

##### Steps to make Chinese-style Stir-Fried Squid and Broccoli:

1.  If you'd like, peel the squid first. Cut it into bite-sized pieces. Divide the broccoli into florets.
2.  Blanch the squid and the broccoli.
3.  Mince the garlic. Heat the sesame oil in a frying pan over low heat, and sauté the garlic.
4.  When fragrant, add the squid and broccoli, and stir-fry briskly.
5.  Add the ★ seasonings. Taste, and adjust the flavor.
6.  Add the ◇ katakuriko dissolved in water to finish.
