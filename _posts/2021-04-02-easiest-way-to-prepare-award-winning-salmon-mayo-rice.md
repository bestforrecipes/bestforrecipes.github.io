##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Mayo Rice:

1.  Make ready 250 g salmon
2.  Get 2 cups white/brown rice
3.  Take 3 tbsp mayonnaise
4.  Make ready 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
5.  Make ready Olive oil extra light
6.  Make ready Furikake seasoning/seaweed flakes
7.  Prepare Cheddar cheese
8.  Take Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Salmon Mayo Rice:

1.  Cook the rice and mix with furikake seasoning after cooked. Set aside
2.  Season salmon with salt and pepper
3.  Fry salmon with olive oil. Set aside
4.  Mix mayonnaise and tomato sauce
5.  Place rice and salmon in a oven-proof dish and cover with the sauce
6.  Bake in the oven 180°C for 10 minutes
7.  Top with cheddar cheese slice, salmon skin or seaweed flakes
