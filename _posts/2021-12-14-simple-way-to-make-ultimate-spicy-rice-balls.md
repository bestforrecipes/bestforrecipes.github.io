##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy rice balls:

1.  Take 1/2 cup sticky rice
2.  Make ready 1/2 lb ground pork
3.  Take 1 egg
4.  Take 2 tbsp soy sauce
5.  Take 1/2 tsp sugar
6.  Prepare 5 green onion finely chopped
7.  Get 1 ginger 1 inch peeled and shredded
8.  Take 1 bird pepper finely chopped
