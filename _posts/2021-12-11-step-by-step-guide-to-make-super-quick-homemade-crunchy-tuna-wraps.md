5.  Make ready 3 tablespoons chopped sweet red pepper
6.  Get 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
7.  Make ready 2 teaspoon prepared mustard
8.  Prepare 2 spinach tortillas 8” room temperature
9.  Make ready 1 cup shredded lettuce
