##### The ingredients needed to make Portuguese Island Shrimp (Spicy):

1.  Make ready onion (or 1 small one)
2.  Get garlic cloves minced
3.  Prepare pimenta (Portuguese pepper,Or sambal OR crushed chills)
4.  Prepare heaping tsp tomato paste
5.  Prepare olive oil
6.  Prepare beer (your fave beer)
7.  Take frozen raw-skin on shrimp
8.  Prepare chopped parsley

##### Instructions to make Portuguese Island Shrimp (Spicy):

1.  Heat olive oil and Sautee onions and garlic.
2.  Add tomato paste and pimenta (or crush chili sauce) cook 2 min
3.  Add whole bottle of beer and let come to rolling boil.
4.  Add frozen shrimp (Add this frozen, while it melts/cooks it will add nice shrimp flavor to broth)
5.  Season with salt to taste. Let everything cook on medium low for another 15-20 min until alcohol from beer cooks out and flavors get delish.
6.  Garnish with parsley an serve with nice crusty bread for dipping in the spicy shrimpy broth :) enjoy.
