##### The ingredients needed to make Tea Marinated Salmon:

1.  Take side of salmon with or without skin; deboned
2.  Make ready packets of ginger-lemon tea
3.  Get olive oil
4.  Make ready ground ginger
5.  Make ready lemon peel or lemon pepper seasoning
6.  Take onion powder
7.  Take garlic powder
8.  Make ready salt and pepper
9.  Take lemon zest for garnish

##### Steps to make Tea Marinated Salmon:

1.  Rub salmon with olive oil. Cover heavily with tea. Marinate for 24 hours
2.  Wash tea off of salmon. Season. Roast at 350° for approximately 30 minutes or until thermometer reaches 145°
3.  Variations; Honey, agave, cajun seasoning, 5 spice seasoning
