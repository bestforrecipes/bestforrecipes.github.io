##### The ingredients needed to make Lemon Salmon with cous cous:

1.  Get salmon
2.  Make ready lemon
3.  Take black pepper
4.  Get thyme
5.  Take coriander
6.  Take chilli pepper - optional
7.  Take portions of cous cous (Mexican flavoured is best)
8.  Get carrots
9.  Make ready cucumber
10.  Take mixed parsley and chives

##### Steps to make Lemon Salmon with cous cous:

1.  Pre heat oven to 180°F
2.  Place both pieces of salmon onto a piece of foil large enough to close into a dome
3.  Squeeze the whole lemon onto the salmon and grate the rine ontop as well
4.  Sprinkle over the coriander, thyme and black pepper.
5.  You can add a small dash of chilli peppers here for extra flavour if you wish
6.  Place into oven for approximately 25 minutes
7.  Whilst the salmon is cooking, cut up your cucumber, carrots, parsley and chive into tiny pieces and mix in with cous cous
8.  Add the sweetcorn at the last minute
9.  Once the salmon is cooked, take out of oven and dish up the cous cous
10.  Place the cooked salmon ontop of the cous cous and then pour the lemon juice from the foil all over the meal - this will give it a fresh taste and calm the flavours down
11.  Sit down and enjoy :)
