##### The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Jalepeno Crab Salad:

1.  Make ready 1 lb imitation crab meat
2.  Get 1 large jalepeno
3.  Prepare 2 stalks of celery
4.  Take 1 cup shredded monterey jack cheese
5.  Take 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
6.  Make ready 1 tbsp garlic powder
7.  Take 1 tsp cayenne pepper
8.  Make ready 1 juice from 1 lemon
9.  Take 1 cup mayonnaise
10.  Prepare 4 small green onions
11.  Get 1 tbsp paprika

##### Steps to make Cheesy Jalepeno Crab Salad:

1.  Dice up jalepeno, celery, and onions. Add everything in bowl and stir up. Refridgerate. Serve with crackers or on toasted bread as a sandwich.
