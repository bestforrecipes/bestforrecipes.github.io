##### The ingredients needed to make Lemon Pepper Salmon:

1.  Make ready Salmon Steaks
2.  Take Real Sweet Cream Butter
3.  Prepare Lemon Pepper Seasoning
4.  Prepare Sea Salt
5.  Take Water
6.  Get Lemon

##### Instructions to make Lemon Pepper Salmon:

1.  Heat butter in large skillet over medium heat.
2.  Add Salmon season with lemon pepper and salt.
3.  Pour water around salmon for juiciness. So it won't be so dry.
4.  Cook flipping half way for 15 minutes or until flaked and done.
5.  Top with lemon slice and enjoy!
