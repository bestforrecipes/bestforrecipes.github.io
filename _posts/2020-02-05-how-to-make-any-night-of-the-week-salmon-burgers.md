##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Burgers:

1.  Take salmon, around 14oz
2.  Prepare jumbo egg
3.  Take breadcrumbs
4.  Take fresh or dried herbs (parsley or dill or chive or etc)
5.  Prepare garlic powder
6.  Take salt
7.  Take onion powder or 1/4 cup chopped onions
8.  Get cayenne pepper
9.  Make ready generous grinds of freshly cracked pepper
10.  Make ready chopped scallion
11.  Take Serving options
12.  Get Lemon wedges
13.  Take Smoked paprika
14.  Take Shredded cabbage
15.  Make ready Cherry tomatoes
16.  Get Avocado
17.  Take Tartar sauce
18.  Prepare Chipotle mayo

##### Instructions to make Salmon Burgers:

1.  Drain the canned salmon, reserving the liquid.
2.  Beat the egg in a bowl. Use a fork to mix in the salmon and all the rest of the ingredients with the egg.
3.  Form patties. I usually do ones about 3-4in wide and 1/2in thick but you can do whatever size you like. If the mix is feeling too dry and won't stick together into patties, add some of the reserved salmon liquid to moisten.
4.  Fry patties in oil over medium to medium-high heat till they are nice and crispy and browned on both sides.
5.  Serve with your choice of dressings.
