##### The ingredients needed to make Super Easy Salmon Patties:

1.  Get fresh salmon packs
2.  Make ready each eggs
3.  Take glutino crackers (one sleeve) or 3/4 sleeve Ritz
4.  Make ready sliced green onion
5.  Prepare minced fresh dill
6.  Make ready shredded parmesan
7.  Prepare minced italian parsely
8.  Get tumeric
9.  Get Kosher salt black pepper
10.  Make ready plain Panko bread crumbs for coating
11.  Take butter 2 tbsp olive oil

##### Steps to make Super Easy Salmon Patties:

1.  Crush crackers finely
2.  Mix all ingredients well, form into patties
3.  Refrigerate one hour
4.  Melt some butter in non stick pan and heat with oil to med high
5.  Coat chilled patties in Panko crumbs
6.  Cook patties about 3 minutes each side until browned flip tenderly
