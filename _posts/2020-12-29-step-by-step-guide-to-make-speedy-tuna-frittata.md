##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Frittata:

1.  Take 2 eggs
2.  Take 2 Tablespoon milk or unsweetened almond milk
3.  Prepare 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
4.  Make ready 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
5.  Make ready 1/4 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese
6.  Make ready 1 can tuna drained and fluffed
7.  Get 2 Tablespoon pickled jalapenos chopped (optional)
8.  Take 2 Tablespoon chopped basil optional
9.  Take 4-6 cherry tomatoes halved

##### Steps to make Tuna Frittata:

1.  In a large bowl, beat the egg, milk, salt and pepper then stir in the rest of the ingredients.
2.  Pour the mixture into a greased shallow bake ware. Air fry at 360F (180C) for about 10 minutes or until the egg is set.
