##### The ingredients needed to make Pork & noodle broth with shrimp:

1.  Make ready Soup
2.  Prepare 3/4 lb pork chop, trimmed, sliced
3.  Prepare 1/2 lb shrimp, raw or cooked
4.  Take 1/4 lb thin egg noodles
5.  Prepare 1 tbsp vegetable oil
6.  Take 2 tsp sesame oil
7.  Make ready 4 shallots, sliced
8.  Prepare 1 tbsp ginger, finely sliced
9.  Make ready 1 clove garlic, crushed
10.  Get 1 tsp sugar
11.  Get 1 1/2 quart stock
12.  Take 2 kaffir lime leaves
13.  Get 3 tbsp fish sauce
14.  Make ready 1/2 lime, juiced
15.  Take Garnish
16.  Take 5 stick fresh cilantro
17.  Get 2 stick scallions, green parts, chopped

##### Steps to make Pork & noodle broth with shrimp:

1.  Boil salted water then cook noodles according to directions. Then drain and refresh in cold water.
2.  Heat wok and add both oils. Once hot, add shallots and stir fry for 3 minutes. Once browned remove from wok and set aside.
3.  Add ginger, garlic, sugar, and stock and bring to a simmer . Add lime leaves, fish sauce and lime juice. Add the pork, then lower the heat, and simmer for 15 more minutes.
4.  Add the shrimp and the noodles and simmer for another 3 minutes or until the shrimp are cooked through.
5.  Serve using the cilantro and scallions as garnish.
