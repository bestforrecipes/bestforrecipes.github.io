3.  Get 1/3 tbsp garlic powder
4.  Take 1 can Tuna
5.  Make ready 1 packages Boil?in?Bag Rice
6.  Get 1/3 cup milk
7.  Make ready 1 dash salt
8.  Take 1 dash marjoram

##### Steps to make Tuna Rice Health Delight:

1.  Complete Boil?in?Bag Rice instructions. I chose go use the natural whole grain brown rice option, its up to you.
2.  Chop up your onions and green peppers as your rice boils.
3.  Drain the rice and move rice to a smaller pot. Add the can of tuna, salt, marjoram, onions, green peppers, garlic powder, and milk.
4.  Stir then reduce heat to low. Cover and let cook for about 10 minutes so that the ingredients can blend.
