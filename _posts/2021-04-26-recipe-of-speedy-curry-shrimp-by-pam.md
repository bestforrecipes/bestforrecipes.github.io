##### The ingredients needed to make Curry Shrimp by Pam…:

1.  Get 20 pcs shrimp clean and devined
2.  Get 1 small onion cut it long
3.  Get 1 big tomato peeled and finely chopped
4.  Take 2 clove minced garlic
5.  Get 1/2 tsp of finely chopped fresh ginger
6.  Make ready 1 cup coconut milk (fresh or in can what you have in hand)
7.  Prepare 2 tbsp cooking oil
8.  Get 1 salt&black pepper to taste
9.  Get 1 tsp chilli flakes
10.  Make ready 1 tsp turmeric powder
11.  Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cummin
12.  Make ready 2 tbsp lemon juice
13.  Make ready 1 chopped cilantro

##### Instructions to make Curry Shrimp by Pam…:

1.  in a saucepan heat oil over medium heat onion in cooked for about 8 min adding garlic and ginger stir add cumin,turmeric,chili flakes cook about a min or so add tomato and coconut milk season with salt and pepper bring to a boil simmer for about 8 min uncovered add shrimp and cilantro cover untill shrimp are cooked like 5 or so stir in lemon.serve over rice!!
2.  enjoy:-)
