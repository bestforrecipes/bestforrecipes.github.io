##### Steps to make Tuna Casserole:

1.  Boil water and cook egg noodles as directed. Preheat oven to 425°.
2.  In a bowl combine drained tuna, cream of chicken soup, cheese (saving 1/2 cup), peas, and seasonings.
3.  Drain noodles and add to mixture. Mix and pour into a greased casserole dish.
4.  Sprinkle crushed potato chips and remaining cheese on top.
5.  Bake for 15-20 minutes.
