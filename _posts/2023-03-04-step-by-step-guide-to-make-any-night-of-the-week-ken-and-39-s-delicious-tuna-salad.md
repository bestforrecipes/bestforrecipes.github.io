##### The ingredients needed to make Ken's Delicious Tuna Salad:

1.  Prepare 8-10 oz Tuna (oil-packed preferred, but not required)
2.  Take 1/2 c mayonnaise
3.  Get 1-2 Tbsp celery, finely chopped
4.  Take 1/2 Tbsp whole-grain Mustard
5.  Prepare 1/2 Tbsp Honey Mustard
6.  Take 1 tsp parsley
7.  Get 2 Tbsp Dill Relish (not sweet)
8.  Get Pepper (to taste)

##### Instructions to make Ken's Delicious Tuna Salad:

1.  Drain oil from tuna, not completely, but mostly. The oil left will give a nice texture and finish to your salad. Set the oil aside, you can add more later if your texture is off.
2.  Mix ingredients as listed, let chill for an hour (or don't, I'm not the boss of you ;).
3.  Serve over toasted bread of your choice (Bagels, muffins, hearty toast are my top choices).
