##### The ingredients needed to make Dhokla Sushi:

1.  Get 3/4 cup idli batter
2.  Prepare 10 capsicum strips
3.  Get 1 tsp oil
4.  Get 1 tbsp curd
5.  Make ready 1/2 tsp fruit salt
6.  Make ready to taste Salt
7.  Prepare Oil for brushing
8.  Make ready 3 tsp cheese spread
9.  Make ready 3 tsp red chilli sauce
10.  Take 2 tsp black sesame seeds (kala til)
11.  Prepare 4 tsp freshly grated coconut

##### Instructions to make Dhokla Sushi:

1.  Combine idli batter,oil, curd,fruit salt and salt in a deep bowl and mix well
2.  Pour 1/3 of prepared batter on greased thali and tap well to spread evenly
3.  Steam it on a steamer for 3-4 minutes or till done.Keep aside
4.  Repeat step 2 and 3 to prepare one more dhokla thali
5.  Cool slightly, loosen the edges and remove with help of flat steel ladle.Keep aside
6.  Place a cling film on clean, dry flat surface and brush it with oil and sprinkle kala til and coconut mixture evenly over it
7.  Place one dhokla sheet and spread half of cheese and sauce mixture over it with help of butter knife
8.  Place capsicum strips overlapping on one side
9.  Roll it very tightly,pressing each time while rolling.Make sure that cling foil is not rolled while rolling
10.  Place the rolled dhokla sushi on a clean dry surface and cut in eight pieces with sharp knife
11.  Now it is ready to serve
