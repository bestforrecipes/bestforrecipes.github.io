##### The ingredients needed to make Quick Jazzy Crab & Mushroom Pizza:

1.  Make ready packaged pizza crust (my favorite is Boboli)
2.  Take jar of your favorite pizza sauce
3.  Get fresh mozzarella cheese ball (cut into 1/4 in thick slices)
4.  Prepare shredded mozzarella (or your favorite cheese)
5.  Make ready fresh crab meat
6.  Prepare sliced any variety fresh mushrooms (as much as you like, I like a lot on mine)
7.  Prepare diced onion (or as much as you like)
8.  Make ready diced bell pepper (or as much as you like)
9.  Make ready diced jalapeño or serrano pepper (depending on how spicy you like it)
10.  Prepare a few squeezes of fresh lime juice
11.  Take Old Bay seasoning or any Creole seasoning
12.  Make ready hot sauce (optional)

##### Steps to make Quick Jazzy Crab & Mushroom Pizza:

1.  Preheat your oven to 375.
2.  Spread as much pizza sauce as you like on your prepared pizza crust.
3.  Lay your mozzarella cheese slices over the sauce.
4.  Add your shredded cheese, then your crab, and your veggies in whatever order you like. (You can also add any other toppings you like, such as pepperoni, fresh basil, tomato, whatever ingredients float your boat.)
5.  Add a few squeezes of fresh lime juice over the top, being sure to get some on the crab meat.
6.  Give it a couple shakes of Old Bay (or any Creole seasoning) over the top. (I also added some black pepper.)
7.  Bake in your preheated 375°F oven for 12-15 min until gooey and slightly browned.
8.  Enjoy!
