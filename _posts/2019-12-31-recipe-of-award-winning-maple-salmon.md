##### The ingredients needed to make Maple Salmon:

1.  Prepare Salmon
2.  Take 1/4 cup maple syrup
3.  Prepare 2 tbsp soy sauce
4.  Take 1 clove Garlic
5.  Make ready 1/4 tsp Garlic salt
6.  Prepare 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
7.  Take 1 lb salmon

##### Steps to make Maple Salmon:

1.  In a small bowl, mix the maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, garlic salt, and pepper.
2.  Place salmon in a shallow glass baking dish, and coat with the maple syrup mixture. Cover the dish, and marinate salmon in the refrigerator 30 minutes, turning once.
3.  Preheat oven to 400°F (200 degrees C).
4.  Place the baking dish in the preheated oven, and bake salmon uncovered 20 minutes, or until easily flaked with a fork.
