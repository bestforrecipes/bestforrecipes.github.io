##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Lobster Tail:

1.  Get Australian Cold Water Lobster Tails
2.  Prepare lemon
3.  Prepare Paprika

##### Steps to make Baked Lobster Tail:

1.  Preheat oven to 400
2.  Take lobster tail out of shell and place on top
3.  Squeeze lemon over top of the lobsters
4.  Sprinkle a small amount of paprika on top
5.  Bake lobster for 18-20 minutes for an 8-10 oz tail
