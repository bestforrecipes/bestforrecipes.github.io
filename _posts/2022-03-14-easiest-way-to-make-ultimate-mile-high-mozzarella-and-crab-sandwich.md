##### The ingredients needed to make Mile-High Mozzarella and crab sandwich:

1.  Prepare 2 slice sandwich thins
2.  Take 1 slice mozzarella ball
3.  Make ready 1 pinch imitation crab meat
4.  Prepare 1 slice tomato

##### Steps to make Mile-High Mozzarella and crab sandwich:

1.  Place bread in toaster
2.  After bread is toasted cut mozzarella ball into a thin slice, place evenly on bread.
3.  Put crab meat on the mozzarella and place in microwave for 10 seconds (or until finely melted)
4.  Add tomato slice if desired
