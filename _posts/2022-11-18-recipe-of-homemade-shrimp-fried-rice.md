##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp fried rice:

1.  Make ready 1 kg peeled shrimps
2.  Prepare Seasoning
3.  Prepare 2 cups extra long grain basmatic rice
4.  Take Meat stock
5.  Prepare 2 cloves blended garlic
6.  Get 3 seeds green scotch bonnet pepper
7.  Get 1 small green bell pepper
8.  Get Dark soy sauce
9.  Make ready 1 small white onion
10.  Get Small cut root onion with stem
11.  Get Butter

##### Instructions to make Shrimp fried rice:

1.  Rinse and cook rice in seasoned meatstock and garlic until tender and fluffy
2.  Dice the vegetables and set aside
3.  Place a clean pan on the fire and add the butter, season shrimp and stir fry in the heated butter
4.  Then add the diced vegetables and some soy sauce
5.  Stir fry and add part of the cooked rice
6.  Continue stir frying and taste for seasoning
7.  Repeat step for remaining portion of rice
