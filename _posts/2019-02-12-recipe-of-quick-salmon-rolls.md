##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Rolls:

1.  Prepare 300 grams smoked salmon
2.  Make ready Fillings
3.  Get 150 grams cream cheese
4.  Get 2 tbsp freshly chopped dill
5.  Make ready 1-2 tsp lemon juice
6.  Take 1/4 tsp mustard powder
7.  Prepare Salt and pepper

##### Steps to make Salmon Rolls:

1.  Mix all the filling ingredients.
2.  Take one slice of salmon, spread with the filling and roll them like you make sushi.
3.  Wrap the log tightly with a cling film and chill in the fridge for an hour or more.
4.  Remove the clingfilm and slice them using a serrated knife. - Serve them on a bed of salad. Enjoy.
