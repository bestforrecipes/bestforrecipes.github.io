##### The ingredients needed to make Yellowfin Ahi Tuna With Asparagus Sauce:

1.  Make ready 3 each Thick cut yellowfin ahi tuna (approximately 1 1/2 lbs) leave out for at least 1/2 hour at room temperature before searing.
2.  Make ready Asparagus sauce
3.  Get 1 bunch Asparagus stems trimmed
4.  Take 2 clove Minced garlic
5.  Take 1/2 each Juice of lemon
6.  Make ready 1 Handful parsley stems removed

Transfer to a large zipper lock bag with the tuna. Seal the bag using the water. Ahi tuna, also known as yellowfin tuna, has a deliciously meaty taste. This hearty fish is an excellent source of protein, is low on fat, and it's extremely simple to prepare.

##### Steps to make Yellowfin Ahi Tuna With Asparagus Sauce:

1.  Lightly steam asparagus until tender. If you don't have a steamer, you can do so stove top by following my step by step guide listed in my recipes.
2.  Combine parsley, asparagus, garlic and lemon juice in blender.
3.  Blend until desired consistency.
4.  In the meantime, sear the ahi tuna approximately 2 minutes on each side. Then drizzle with asparagus sauce. Garnish with lemon twists and parsley.
5.  Your tuna should still be raw at center.
