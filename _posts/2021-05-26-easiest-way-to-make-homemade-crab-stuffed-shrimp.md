##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Stuffed Shrimp:

1.  Prepare Jumbo Shrimp, peeled and a slit cut to hold stuffing
2.  Get jumbo lump crab meat
3.  Make ready celery, finely minced
4.  Get small shallot, finely minced
5.  Get garlic, minced
6.  Prepare mayonnaise
7.  Take sour cream
8.  Prepare lemon juice
9.  Take dijon mustard
10.  Make ready cajun seasoning
11.  Take black pepper and salt to taste
12.  Make ready hot sauce such as franks brand
13.  Take butter, melted
14.  Prepare chicken broth
15.  Make ready bacon, cut into pieces
16.  Get Shredded cheese, I used mix of pepper jack, sharp cheddar and italian four cheese blend

##### Steps to make Crab Stuffed Shrimp:

1.  Preheat oven to 425
2.  Pour butter into baking pan arge enogh to hold shrimp on one layer. Place shrimp pocket side up in dish.
3.  MAKE STUFFING
4.  In a bowl mix mayonnaise, sour cream, celery, shallot, garlic,lemon, mustard, cajun seasoning, pepper and red hot, whisk to combine. Put crab meat in large bowl and add sauce and very gently combine well.Fill shrimp evenly with crab. Top evenly with cheese, pour over chicken broth. Top with bacon.
5.  Bake for 25 to 30 miutes until shrimp is cooked through.
6.  Serve with rolls amd coleslaw or a salad!
