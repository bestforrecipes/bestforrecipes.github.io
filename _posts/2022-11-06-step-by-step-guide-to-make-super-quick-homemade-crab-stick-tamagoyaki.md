##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Stick Tamagoyaki:

1.  Make ready 2 Egg
2.  Prepare 2 stick Imitation crab sticks
3.  Take 1 to garnish Mitsuba
4.  Make ready 1/4 tsp ●Vinegar
5.  Make ready 1/4 tsp ●Chicken soup stock granules
6.  Make ready 1 tsp ●Mirin
7.  Get 1 dash ●Pepper
8.  Take 1 Vegetable oil (or sesame oil)

##### Steps to make Crab Stick Tamagoyaki:

1.  Cut the mitsuba and imitation crab into 3-4 cm pieces. Add in the flavoring ingredients marked ●, crack in the egg, and beat.
2.  Coat a pan with oil, slowly wrap up the omelette over a low heat, and it is done.
3.  Using Japanese leeks in place of the mitsuba is also delicious.
