##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Scramble:

1.  Make ready 5 ounces canned tuna packed in oil
2.  Take 4 large eggs
3.  Make ready 1 cup quickles II see my recipe or just use dill pickle chips
4.  Make ready 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
5.  Get 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
6.  Get 1/2 teaspoon paprika
7.  Prepare 1 teaspoon dill weed
8.  Make ready pinch salt
9.  Take to taste hotsauce, your favorite, optional

##### Steps to make Tuna Scramble:

1.  Heat a pan and add the tuna without draining
2.  Add eggs and cook till eggs are done.
3.  Add to a plate and add the spices to the scramble. Add hotsauce if you desire.
4.  Place quickles on the sides
5.  Serve hope you enjoy!
