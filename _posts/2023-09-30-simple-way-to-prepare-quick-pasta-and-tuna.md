##### Steps to make Pasta and tuna:

1.  Fill a pot with water for the pasta and bring to a rapid boil.
2.  In a different pot fry the tuna in its own oil until heated through.
3.  Add the tomato puree to the tuna with salt and pepper to taste.
4.  Fill the tomato puree tin with water to rinse it out and put it in with the rest.
5.  Leave the sauce to simmer until the pasta is ready.
6.  Cook the pasta until firm or al dente and drain.
7.  Pour the sauce over the pasta.
8.  Serve with parmesan if available.
