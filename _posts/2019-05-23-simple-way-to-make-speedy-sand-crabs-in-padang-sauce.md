##### The ingredients needed to make Sand Crabs in Padang Sauce:

1.  Prepare Couple of sand crab (I use about 7 sand crab)
2.  Get Cooking oil to and yaste
3.  Take 7 cm gingger cut and crushed
4.  Take 3 tomatoes, cut into pieces
5.  Make ready 5 tablespoons oyster sauce
6.  Take 5 tablespoons tomato sauce
7.  Prepare 400 ml water/ any broth
8.  Make ready 3 teaspoon pepper
9.  Get 3 tablespoon sugar
10.  Take 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
11.  Prepare 4-5 tablespoon rice flour, dissolved in a little water
12.  Get Spice (ground)
13.  Prepare 6 spring onions
14.  Make ready 5 cloves garlic
15.  Make ready 7 pieces red chillies (optional)
16.  Take 7 pieces Bird eye chillies
17.  Take 6 cm turmeric (2 tablespoon turmeric powder)
18.  Make ready 5 of candlenuts, roasted

##### Instructions to make Sand Crabs in Padang Sauce:

1.  Clean the crabs, cut into pieces. Crab fried in hot oil until cooked. Or it could be boiled until cooked. Remove and drain.
2.  Heat 4 tablespoons cooking oil, saute ground spices, ginger, and tomatoes until fragrant.
3.  Add oyster sauce, tomato sauce, chili sauce, water / broth, pepper, sugar and rice flour solution. Cook until sauce thickens.
4.  Enter the crabs, mix with seasoning. Lift and serve with warm rice.
