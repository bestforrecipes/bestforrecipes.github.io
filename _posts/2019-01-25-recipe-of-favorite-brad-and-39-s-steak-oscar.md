##### The ingredients needed to make Brad's steak oscar:

1.  Get 2 12 oz rib eye steaks
2.  Prepare 1/2 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
3.  Take 1/4 lb lump crab meat
4.  Get 1/2 cup prepared hollandaise sauce
5.  Prepare 1 salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste
6.  Take marinade
7.  Make ready 1/4 cup each, soy sauce, worchestershire sauce, balsamic vinegar, water, and red wine vinegar

##### Instructions to make Brad's steak oscar:

1.  Mix all marinade ingredients. Marinade steaks for 30-45 minutes
