##### The ingredients needed to make Crab curry:

1.  Prepare 1 kg crab
2.  Get 2 onions paste
3.  Make ready 1 tbsp garlic paste
4.  Take 1 tsp ginger paste
5.  Get 1 tomato
6.  Get 6 green chillies paste
7.  Take 1 tsp Cumin powder
8.  Get 1/2 tsp coriander powder
9.  Prepare 1/3 tsp garam masala powder
10.  Get 4 tbsp oil
11.  Take To taste salt
12.  Take 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
13.  Prepare 2 tsp turmeric powder
14.  Take 2 tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
15.  Prepare 1/2 tsp black cumin seeds
16.  Take 1 dry red chilli
17.  Prepare 2 bay leaves
18.  Get 1 tsp cornflour

##### Steps to make Crab curry:

1.  Add oil in a pan. Fry crabs till golden adding 1 tsp turmeric,1 tsp red chilli powder and salt and keep aside. Add into the oil black cumin seeds,red chilli,bay leaves and saute for few seconds.
2.  Add onion ginger garlic chilli paste. Cook for 5 minutes in low flame
3.  Add tomato pieces. Cook for 4 minutes. Add powderd masalas except garam masala powder. Cook for 4 minutes
4.  Add 2 &1/2 glasses of water. Boil for 2 minutes. Add crabs. Boil mix cornflour with 1 tbsp water and add. Cook for 5 minutes.
5.  Add garam masala. Ready to serve
