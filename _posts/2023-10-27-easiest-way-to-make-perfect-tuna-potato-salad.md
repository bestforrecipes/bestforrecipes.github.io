##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Potato salad:

1.  Prepare 2 potato
2.  Take 70 g canned tuna
3.  Prepare 1/2 onion
4.  Make ready 2 tbsp mayonnaise
5.  Get 1 tbsp soy sauce
6.  Prepare 1 tbsp vinegar
7.  Get pinch black pepper

##### Steps to make Tuna Potato salad:

1.  Boil potato and add vinegar.
2.  Slice onions add 1 tbsp mayonnaise and stir-fry.
3.  When onions wilted, add tuna and soy sauce, fry
4.  Mix \[ 1 \] and \[3 \] and 1 tbsp mayonnaise.
5.  Put black pepper as you like.
