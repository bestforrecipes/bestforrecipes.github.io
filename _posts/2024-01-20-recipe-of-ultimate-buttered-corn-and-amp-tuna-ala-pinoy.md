##### The ingredients needed to make Buttered Corn & Tuna ala Pinoy:

1.  Get Century Tuna 1 can (155g)
2.  Take Jolly Whole Corn 1 can (425g)
3.  Get Cube butter
4.  Get 1/4 cup minced Onion
5.  Take Salt and Pepper

##### Steps to make Buttered Corn & Tuna ala Pinoy:

1.  In medium heat, melt butter in a saucepan and sauté the onion until translucent.
2.  Add tuna and wait until it turns to an appealing brown shade.
3.  Add corn. Wait for it to simmer then sprinkle salt and pepper to taste. Ready to serve a minute after.
