##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Butter Flavored Natto Pasta:

1.  Make ready 100 grams Pasta
2.  Make ready 1 pack Natto (fermented soy beans)
3.  Make ready 1 piece ● Butter
4.  Take 1/2 tsp ● Olive oil
5.  Make ready 1/2 tsp ● Soy sauce
6.  Get 1/2 tsp ● Mentsuyu
7.  Prepare 1 ● Black pepper
8.  Prepare 1 Toasted nori seaweed (if you have it)

##### Steps to make Easy Butter Flavored Natto Pasta:

1.  Boil the pasta. In the meantime…
2.  …Put the ● ingredients in a heatproof bowl, and microwave for about 30 seconds until the butter melts.
3.  You still have time. Mix the natto.
4.  Make the nori seaweed ready.
5.  When the pasta is cooked, drain it and put it into the bowl from Step 2.
6.  Top with the nori seaweed and natto and it's done.
7.  I used a soy sauce with bonito dashi in it. You can use as much soy sauce and/or mentsuyu sauce as you like.
