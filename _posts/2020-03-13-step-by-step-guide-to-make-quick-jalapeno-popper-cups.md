##### The ingredients needed to make Jalapeño Popper Cups:

1.  Prepare 1 packages Frozen mini phyllo tart shells
2.  Prepare 4 oz Cream cheese, softened
3.  Prepare 1/2 cup Shredded cheddar cheese
4.  Make ready 2 Jalapeño peppers, seeded and chopped
5.  Make ready 2 slice Bacon, cooked and chopped

##### Steps to make Jalapeño Popper Cups:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°.
2.  Place shells on a baking sheet.
3.  Stir cream cheese, cheddar cheese, and jalapeños together.
4.  Spoon mixture into cups. Sprinkle with bacon.
5.  Bake about 15-20 minutes. Serve warm.
