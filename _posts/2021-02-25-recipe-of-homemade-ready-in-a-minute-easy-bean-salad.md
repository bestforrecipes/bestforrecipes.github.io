##### The ingredients needed to make Ready in a Minute! Easy Bean Salad:

1.  Take 1 can Canned mixed beans
2.  Prepare 1 can Canned soy beans
3.  Take 1 can Canned tuna
4.  Get 1 can Canned hijiki seaweed
5.  Make ready 3 tbsp Mayonnaise
6.  Prepare 1 dash of each Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Ready in a Minute! Easy Bean Salad:

1.  Put all the ingredients in a bowl, mix, mix, mix and transfer to serving plates. That's it! (If you're using canned tuna in oil, drain the oil beforehand.)
