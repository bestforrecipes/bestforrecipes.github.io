2.  Prepare 1 cup of chopped celery and coriander together
3.  Get 1 lettuce cut into 2 cm long
4.  Take 1/2 cup of chopped green onions
5.  Prepare 1/4 cup of lime juice
6.  Take 1/4 cup of pickles garlic chopped with juice
7.  Get 2 tsp of fish sauce
8.  Get 2 tsp of sugar
9.  Get 1 tsp of chicken stock powder
