##### The ingredients needed to make Pam's Tuna pasta…:

1.  Prepare 250 grams pasta of your choice i used bow tie(any brand)cooked s package direction
2.  Take for the sauce..
3.  Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
4.  Take 1 medium onion, chopped
5.  Take 2 clove garlic minced
6.  Get 1 can tuna flakes in olive oil (150g)
7.  Get 1 can italian pasta sauce(396g)i used fresh mushroom
8.  Make ready 1 tsp dried basil
9.  Get 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

##### Steps to make Pam's Tuna pasta…:

1.  In a sauce pan in medium heat olive oil in add onion and garlic saute until soft adding tuna cooked for about a min or so add pasta sauce combined well. Cheese in mixed until melted salt and pepper to taste and dried basil mixed and let simmer for a min or so.
2.  You can add in your cooked pasta or just pour it over..served hot and enjoy
