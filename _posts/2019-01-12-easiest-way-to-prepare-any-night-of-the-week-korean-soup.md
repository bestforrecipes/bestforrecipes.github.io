##### The ingredients needed to make Korean soup:

1.  Prepare 1 bag Enoki mushroom
2.  Prepare 1/2 bag sprout
3.  Make ready 30 g dry seaweed
4.  Get 1.5 Tsp potato powder
5.  Get 1 Tofu
6.  Make ready 1 egg
7.  Get 4 Tsp chicken soup powder
8.  Prepare 1,8 L water

This is the Korean equivalent of chicken noodle soup - it is a comforting, cure-all soup. It is also spicy and fresh, and easy to make. These soups will keep you warm, healthy and happy thru cold season. Includes Vitamin C packed soybean sprout soup!

##### Steps to make Korean soup:

1.  Pour water into a pot. Heat until water is boiled.
2.  Cut Enoki mushroom in any size, and put them into the pot.
3.  Put chicken soup powder into the pot.
4.  Put Tofu, seaweed into the pot.
5.  Mix potato powder with a small amount of water. Pour the mixture into the pot.
6.  Make beaten egg, pour it in the soup and mix.
