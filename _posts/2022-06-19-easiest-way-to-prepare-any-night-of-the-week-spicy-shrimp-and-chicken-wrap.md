##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Shrimp and Chicken Wrap:

1.  Prepare Handful frozen grilled chicken strips (or cook your own)
2.  Make ready Handful frozen shrimp
3.  Make ready To taste Garlic powder
4.  Get 1/2 teaspoon Seasoned salt
5.  Prepare To taste Green onion
6.  Take To taste Garlic chopped
7.  Prepare 1/4 teaspoon Red onion chopped
8.  Make ready To taste Mae Ploy Sweet Thai Chili sauce
9.  Get Flat Out Italian Herb wrap
10.  Prepare 1/2 tablespoon Honey mustard
11.  Get 1 tablespoon Olive oil
12.  Make ready 1/4 teaspoon Liquid smoke
13.  Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Crispy jalapeños

##### Steps to make Spicy Shrimp and Chicken Wrap:

1.  Halfway unthaw shrimp in cool water, adding a teaspoon or so of olive oil to the pan. Then place shrimp and chicken in the pan.
2.  Add in all your seasonings and stir the mixture around, then a splash of liquid smoke, and chili sauce to your liking. Let it cook.
3.  Make sure chicken and shrimp cook all the way through before adding cheese if you wish.
4.  Spread your wrap with condiment of your choice, (I chose honey mustard.)
5.  Spread mixture out on wrap, then top with crispy jalapeños if you wish. Roll it up and serve with side of your choice.
