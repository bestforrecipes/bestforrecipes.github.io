##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Get 1/2 onion, chopped
2.  Prepare 1/2 Bellpepper - chopped
3.  Get 2 can Tuna (in water)
4.  Get 1 1/2 cup mayonnaise
5.  Make ready 2 cup elbow noodles cooked
6.  Prepare black pepper
7.  Take lemon juice
8.  Prepare garlic and herb season
9.  Take Season salt
10.  Get worcestershire sauce

##### Instructions to make Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Cook elbow noodles
2.  Mix Canned tuna, chopped veggies, mayo, and seasoning
3.  Rinse cooked noodles in cold water
4.  Mix in with Tuna mixture
5.  If need be, add more mayo
6.  Refrigerate for 30 min.
