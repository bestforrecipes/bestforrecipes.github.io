##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Salmon:

1.  Get 500 g salmon
2.  Get 3 Tbsp natural greek yoghurt
3.  Get Juice of half a lemon
4.  Get 1 tsp paprika
5.  Prepare 1 tsp cayenne pepper
6.  Take 2 finely chopped garlic cloves
7.  Make ready Small bunch of chopped dill

##### Instructions to make Baked Salmon:

1.  Mix ingredients and smother over ligtly salted salmon. Leave to marinate for a minimum of 30mins. - This allows the salmon to be flavourful.
2.  Place on lightly greased baking sheet.
3.  Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C for 18-20 mins.
4.  Serve hot with blanched vegetables
5.  Enjoy…
