##### The ingredients needed to make Salsa Marinara:

1.  Get seafood
2.  Take 500 grams shrimps
3.  Get 12 large mussels
4.  Make ready 8 crab sticks
5.  Get 2 large tuna tins
6.  Prepare 150 grams salmon
7.  Get 150 grams squid rings
8.  Get adding with seafood
9.  Get 2 large onions
10.  Prepare 200 ml white wine
11.  Prepare 500 ml tomato puree
12.  Make ready 3 tbsp capers
13.  Take 12 whole green olives
14.  Get 3 bay leaves
15.  Prepare 1 pinch garlic salt
16.  Get 1 pinch pepper
17.  Get Topping
18.  Make ready 1 bunch fresh and chopped parsley

##### Steps to make Salsa Marinara:

1.  Chop onions.
2.  Fry chopped onions till they turn to a light brown.
3.  Add all the seafood.
4.  Add white wine.
5.  Leave on medium heat for 10 mins.
6.  Add tomato puree, olives, capers and bay leaves.
7.  Leave for another 10 mins on low heat.
8.  To decorate add chopped parsley.
9.  Serve with your favourite pasta.
