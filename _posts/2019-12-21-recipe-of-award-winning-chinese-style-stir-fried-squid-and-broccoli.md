##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese-style Stir-Fried Squid and Broccoli:

1.  Get 1 Squid
2.  Prepare 1 head Broccoli
3.  Prepare 1 clove Garlic
4.  Get 1 to 2 tablespoons Sesame oil
5.  Take 1 tbsp ★ Sake
6.  Get 1 tbsp ★ Chicken stock powder
7.  Get 1 to 2 teaspoon ★ Oyster sauce
8.  Prepare 50 ml ★ Water
9.  Get 1 ★ Salt and pepper
10.  Take 1 tbsp ◇ Katakuriko
11.  Take 2 tbsp ◇ Water

Pour mixture of water and katakuriko starch over the ingredients and stir quickly. Add broccoli to the hot pan with the vegetable broth. Cover and reduce the heat to medium-low. Stir until thickened to your desired consistency.

##### Instructions to make Chinese-style Stir-Fried Squid and Broccoli:

1.  If you'd like, peel the squid first. Cut it into bite-sized pieces. Divide the broccoli into florets.
2.  Blanch the squid and the broccoli.
3.  Mince the garlic. Heat the sesame oil in a frying pan over low heat, and sauté the garlic.
4.  When fragrant, add the squid and broccoli, and stir-fry briskly.
5.  Add the ★ seasonings. Taste, and adjust the flavor.
6.  Add the ◇ katakuriko dissolved in water to finish.
