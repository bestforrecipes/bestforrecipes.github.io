##### The ingredients needed to make Japanese-style Pasta with Salmon and Shio-kombu:

1.  Prepare 200 grams Spaghetti
2.  Make ready 3 tbsp ＊ Salmon flakes (store-bought)
3.  Take 2 tbsp ＊ Shio-kombu
4.  Get 1 tbsp ＊ White sesame seeds
5.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp ＊Soy sauce
6.  Make ready 10 grams ＊ Butter or margarine

##### Steps to make Japanese-style Pasta with Salmon and Shio-kombu:

1.  Toss the ＊ ingredients with freshly boiled spaghetti, and it's done. If you work with the pot you used to boiled the spaghetti noodles, you can reduce the amount of dirty dishes.
