##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Po boy sandwich:

1.  Take 1 1/2 cup seafood crumbs
2.  Prepare 1 large egg
3.  Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
4.  Prepare 1 cup vegetable oil
5.  Make ready 1/3 tsp BLACK pepper
6.  Prepare 1/4 tsp chilli powder
7.  Get 1/4 tsp garlic salr
8.  Get 2 cup shrimp
9.  Make ready 1 tarter sauce
10.  Get 1 spicy mustard
11.  Prepare 1 romain lettus
12.  Prepare 1 tomato
13.  Get 1 roll

##### Instructions to make Shrimp Po boy sandwich:

1.  season shrimp with salt pepper garlic salt and chilli powder
2.  coat shrimp in egg
3.  cover seasoned shrimp in seafood crumbs
4.  fry shrimp till golden brown
5.  toast roll and make ur sandwich
