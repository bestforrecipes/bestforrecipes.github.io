##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood Rice Balls:

1.  Take bowlful Cooked rice (warm)
2.  Make ready Chirimen jako (semi-dried salted tiny sardines)
3.  Prepare Salmon flakes (ready made)
4.  Prepare Nori Seaweed Paste
5.  Get Fine chopped leek (optional)

##### Instructions to make Seafood Rice Balls:

1.  Add the ingredients to the warm rice. Add as much shredded leeks as you like. Mix, mix.
2.  Mix all the ingredients together and form into your preferred shape.
