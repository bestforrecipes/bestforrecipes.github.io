2.  Get 1/2 or more (to taste) Spring onion
3.  Get 1/2 Red bell pepper
4.  Prepare 1 slice Chicken ham
5.  Prepare 1 tbsp Soy sauce malt
6.  Get 1 tsp Balsamic vinegar
7.  Get 1 tsp Honey
8.  Make ready 1 tsp Grainy mustard
9.  Take 1 Olive oil
