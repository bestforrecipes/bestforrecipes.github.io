##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet Pepper Baked Salmon:

1.  Take salmon filets
2.  Get Sweet Pepper Sauce
3.  Get sliced sweet peppers
4.  Get Roma tomato
5.  Make ready diced red onion
6.  Take garlic
7.  Get lemon juice
8.  Take Italian seasoning
9.  Take cracked pepper
10.  Prepare oregano
11.  Take olive oil spray
12.  Get sheets aluminum foil

##### Steps to make Sweet Pepper Baked Salmon:

1.  preheat oven to 400°
2.  pull out 2 sheets of foil, enough to hold each filet and wrap closed at top
3.  make each piece of foil into bowl shape to hold salmon filet
4.  lightly spray the inside bottom of each with olive oil cooking spray
5.  place in baking pan & lay filets inside each foil bowl
6.  mold the bowls to form around filet, but leave open at the top
7.  wash & dice peppers & tomato
8.  add peppers, tomato, & remaining sauce ingredients into food processor or blender
9.  puree or blend to texture of your liking, I prefer slightly chunky
10.  pour half of mixture into foil bowls on top of salmon
11.  wrap each foil bowl closed to seal
12.  place in oven, middle rack
13.  bake at 400° for approximately 20 minutes, depending on the size of your filets (you can unwrap the foil to check if it's done, just wrap back closed and place back in the oven if not)
14.  when done, you can scoop the salmon onto a plate or eat it right out of the bowl
15.  serve with steamed broccoli & quinoa/brown rice or sides of your choice!
