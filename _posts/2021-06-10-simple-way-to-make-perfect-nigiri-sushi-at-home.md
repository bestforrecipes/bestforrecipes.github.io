##### The ingredients needed to make Nigiri Sushi At Home:

1.  Make ready 700 grams White rice
2.  Get For sushi vinegar
3.  Take 3 1/2 tbsp Vinegar
4.  Make ready 2 tbsp Sugar
5.  Make ready 1 1/2 tsp Salt
6.  Get Toppings:
7.  Make ready 26 pieces Tuna, yellowtail, salmon, squid, sweet shrimp, scallops
8.  Take 1 Wasabi
9.  Get 1 for 10 eggs Egg carton

##### Steps to make Nigiri Sushi At Home:

1.  Rinse the rice and place in a sieve. When making the rice, use a little less water than you usually do. Once cooked, mix together with the sushi vinegar and let cool.
2.  This is a pack of store-bought sashimi.
3.  Wet the egg carton and put a little more than 1 tablespoon of sushi rice into each of the egg molds and shake it up a bit to shape the rice.
4.  Make 26 small riice balls. Adjust the shapes into ovals.
5.  Top the rice from Step 4 with wasabi, then place on the seafood toppings. Press the topping onto the sushi without being too firm or too gentle.
6.  Transfer to a plate and enjoy!
