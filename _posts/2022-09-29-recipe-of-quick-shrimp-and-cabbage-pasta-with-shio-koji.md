##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp and Cabbage Pasta with Shio-Koji:

1.  Take Spaghetti
2.  Get Shrimp
3.  Make ready leaf Cabbage
4.  Get Corn
5.  Make ready Shio-koji
6.  Get Olive oil
7.  Take Salt
8.  Prepare Garlic
9.  Make ready Soy sauce

##### Steps to make Shrimp and Cabbage Pasta with Shio-Koji:

1.  Cook the spaghetti.
2.  Add the olive oil, and sliced garlic into a frying pan. Sauté the garlic until fragrant.
3.  Add the shrimp, and stir-fry until cooked through. Add the chopped cabbage, and corn, and continue stir-frying. Add the shio-koji as well.
4.  Add the freshly cooked spaghetti, and adjust the flavor with soy sauce.
