##### The ingredients needed to make Mayo'd Crabsticks:

1.  Take 6 Crab sticks, cut lengthways in half
2.  Take 1 1/2 tbsp mayonnaise
3.  Get 1 tbsp White wine vinegar, plus extra if you prefer it stronger
4.  Prepare salt
5.  Get fresh ground black pepper
6.  Take chilli (optional)

##### Steps to make Mayo'd Crabsticks:

1.  In a small bowl add the crab sticks vinegar, salt, chilli and pepper. Mix it all together.
2.  Let it sit in the fridge to absorb the flavours for about 10-15 minutes.
3.  Add the mayonnaise and mix it all together well.
4.  Serve as a light snack, lunch or appetiser and enjoy.
