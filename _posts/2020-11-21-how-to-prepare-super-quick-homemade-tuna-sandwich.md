##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Sandwich:

1.  Prepare 2 slices bread
2.  Prepare 1 can Tuna in Spring water
3.  Take 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
4.  Prepare 1 tablespoon black olives
5.  Get 1 pickle
6.  Get 0.25 apple
7.  Take to taste Ice berg lettuce
8.  Take to taste Salt
9.  Make ready to taste Pepper

##### Instructions to make Tuna Sandwich:

1.  Toast two slices of bread
2.  Open the tuna and take the water out then put it in a bowl
3.  Add the mayonnaise to the tuna and add salt and pepper
4.  Cut the pickle fine squares, cut the olives fine and cut the apple fine add both to the tuna and mix
5.  Spread the tuna mixture on the toast and add some extra salt and pepper
