##### The ingredients needed to make Fishy Pie:

1.  Make ready Salmon
2.  Get Carrot
3.  Take Red onion
4.  Take Celery
5.  Take Leek
6.  Make ready Rosemary
7.  Get Double cream
8.  Get Potatoes (optional)

##### Steps to make Fishy Pie:

1.  Chop up all the veg and fry with a lot of oil. After 10 minutes add the salmon.
2.  Mix in the cream and rosemary. Move to a baking dish. Bake for 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and add skin on sliced potatoes if you’re that way inclined. Return to oven until golden.
