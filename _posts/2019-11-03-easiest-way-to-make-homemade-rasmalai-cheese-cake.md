##### The ingredients needed to make Rasmalai Cheese Cake:

1.  Make ready For Base
2.  Get 200 gm digestive biscuit crumbs
3.  Take 80 gm butter (melted)
4.  Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
5.  Get For mousse
6.  Make ready 500 gm soft cheese
7.  Prepare 500 gm whipped cream
8.  Make ready 100 gm milk
9.  Take 1/8 tsp saffron
10.  Make ready 1/8 tsp Almond and pistachios chopped
11.  Prepare 400 gm rasmalai whole
12.  Prepare 30 gm gelatin or agar agar powder
13.  Take 90 gm water for soaking gelatin

##### Steps to make Rasmalai Cheese Cake:

1.  Mix biscuit crumbs and butter well. Spread in base any shape mould. Refrigerate the mould.
2.  Soak gelatin in 90 ml water and keep aside. - Make mousse mixture by mixing cheese, whipped cream, saffron milk, and vanilla essence.
3.  Remove the refrigerated biscuit crumb mould and arrange the rasmalai layer on top of it.
4.  Melt gelatin in the microwave and add in mousse mixture.
5.  Pour mixture in the mixture and freeze for overnight.
6.  Un-mould the cheese cake and - Keep it in the refrigerator. - Garnish with pistachio flakes and Cardamom seeds. If you like garnish with rasmalai milk. Serve chilled.
