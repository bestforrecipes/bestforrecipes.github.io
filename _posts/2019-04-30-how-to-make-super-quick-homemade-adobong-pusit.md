##### The ingredients needed to make Adobong Pusit:

1.  Prepare 1 kg baby squid
2.  Take tbsp cooking oil
3.  Make ready 1 clove garlic
4.  Make ready 1 small onion, chopped
5.  Take 1 bunch silantro
6.  Make ready 2 tbsp soy sauce
7.  Prepare 2 tbsp vinegar
8.  Make ready 1 tsp sugar
9.  Take 1 pepper

##### Steps to make Adobong Pusit:

1.  Saute garlic onion in pan
2.  Add squid , vinegar and soy sauce let it simmer for 3 -4 mins 🐙🐙🐙
3.  Add Salt , sugar and pepper to taste
4.  Garnish with silantro before serving
