##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Okonomiyaki:

1.  Take 200 gr sliced cabbage
2.  Prepare 3 eggs
3.  Take 1 cup flour
4.  Take Water
5.  Take 1 tsp salt
6.  Get 2 tsp dashi granules
7.  Take Japanese mayonnaise
8.  Make ready Japanese bulldog sauce
9.  Get Aonori
10.  Prepare Oil for frying

##### Instructions to make Simple Okonomiyaki:

1.  Combine sliced cabbage, eggs, flour, salt, dashi in a big bowl. Stir well. Add water bit by bit until the mixture becomes a bit thick and not too runny.
2.  Heat about 1 tbsp oil in a pan. Use medium heat. Pour some batter and try to spread it by patting it with a wood spatula. When you notice the bottom side has become brownish, flip the okonomiyaki and wait until the other side is cooked.
3.  Pour some bulldog sauce on it, then Japanese mayo. Sprinkle aonori, and serve.
