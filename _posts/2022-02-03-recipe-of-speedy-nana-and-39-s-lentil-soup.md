##### The ingredients needed to make Nana's Lentil Soup:

1.  Get 2 cups dried green lentils sorted and washed
2.  Make ready 1 medium red potato cut into small cubes
3.  Take 2 small yellow onions chopped
4.  Get pieces I medium red bell pepper chopped into small
5.  Get 2 sheets roasted seaweed(snack kind) torn into small pieces
6.  Take 2 large cloves of garlic chopped into tiny pieces
7.  Take 2 ramen seasoning pouches beef flavored (or whatever you prefer)
8.  Get to taste Salt if needed and pepper

##### Instructions to make Nana's Lentil Soup:

1.  Cook lentils untill almost done
2.  Add the rest of the ingredients
3.  Cook untill potatoes are tender
4.  Enjoy
