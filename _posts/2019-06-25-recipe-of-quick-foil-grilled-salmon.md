##### Instructions to make Foil Grilled Salmon:

1.  Prepare 2 pieces of aluminium foil large enough to wrap the each salmon piece. Slice the onion into about 1cm rounds and put two of them on each piece foil.
2.  Put a piece of salmon on the onions. Pour in a tsp sake or white wine.
3.  Then put chopped mushrooms and sweet snap on the fish.
4.  Add a tsp of lemon juice, season with salt and pepper.
5.  Wrap the foil around the fish to avoid water coming in when cooking.
6.  Pour water into a pan about 2 cm deep and then put in the wrapped salmon. With the lid on, boil/steam for about 15 min on a medium heat.
