##### The ingredients needed to make Whosayna’s Shrimps Fried Rice:

1.  Make ready 2 cups Rice (boiled in salt and water dein drained)
2.  Get 2 cups Veggies chopped (capsi, french beans, spring onions, carrots)
3.  Get 1 tsp Soy sauce
4.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Blackpepper powder
5.  Get 7 Garlic cloves (chopped)
6.  Make ready 1 tbsp Coriander leaves (chopped)
7.  Take 1 tbsp Vinegar
8.  Get 1" small piece Ginger (chopped)
9.  Take 1 cup Shrimps (marinate with 1 tsp chilli powder, salt, lemon juice and garlic paste)
10.  Prepare 1/2 tsp White pepper

##### Instructions to make Whosayna’s Shrimps Fried Rice:

1.  Take a wok keep on flame add a tbsp of butter or oil let it heat up add prawns and stirfry it till done, remove on a plate, use same wok add oil and then - add garlic and ginger, stir it add veggies in 10 secs add prawns then rice and stir and toss add soy sauce, vinegar, black pepper and coriander leaves toss it well remove in serving bowl. - NB: i added 2 chopped green chillies
