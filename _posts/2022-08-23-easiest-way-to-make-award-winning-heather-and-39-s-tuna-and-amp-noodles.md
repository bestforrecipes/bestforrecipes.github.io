##### The ingredients needed to make Heather's Tuna & Noodles:

1.  Prepare 1 (12 oz) bag wide egg noodles
2.  Take 2 can solid white albacore tuna (drained)
3.  Prepare 2 cans cream of celery soup
4.  Prepare 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
5.  Make ready 1 cup milk
6.  Get to taste Salt
7.  Prepare to taste Pepper

##### Steps to make Heather's Tuna & Noodles:

1.  Cook noodles, drain.
2.  Add all ingredients into pot.
3.  Warm and serve.
