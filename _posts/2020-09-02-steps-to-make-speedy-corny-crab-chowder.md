##### The ingredients needed to make Corny Crab Chowder:

1.  Take 8 oz fresh crab meat or refrigerated canned crab meat
2.  Make ready 2 T olive oil
3.  Take 5-6 russet potatoes, peeled and chopped (about 13 oz)
4.  Get 1 small onion, chopped
5.  Take 48 oz low-sodium chicken broth
6.  Make ready 1-1/2 c frozen corn
7.  Make ready 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
8.  Get 1 T dried basil
9.  Get 1/2 c half and half
10.  Get 1/2 tsp salt
11.  Get 1/4 tsp pepper
12.  Get chopped fresh parsley or dried oregano to garnish

##### Steps to make Corny Crab Chowder:

1.  In large pot, heat oil. Add potatoes and onions. Cook until onion is tender. Add broth and simmer 20 min.
2.  Add remaining ingredients and cook on low 5 min. Serve with garnish if using.
