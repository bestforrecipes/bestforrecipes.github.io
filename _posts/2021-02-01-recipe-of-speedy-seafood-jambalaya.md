##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood Jambalaya:

1.  Get 1/2 cup Butter
2.  Prepare 1 Red Onion
3.  Make ready 6 Green onions
4.  Take 6 Gallic cloves
5.  Make ready 4 Bay leaves
6.  Prepare 2 Jalepenos
7.  Make ready 2 tbsp Creole seasoning
8.  Get 1 tsp Ground Cayenne pepper
9.  Make ready 2 tbsp Tomato paste
10.  Take 5 cup Water
11.  Get 2 Beef Bouillon cubes
12.  Make ready 16 oz Crushed tomatoes
13.  Get 3 cup Short grain brown rice
14.  Take 1/2 lb Jumbo Shrimp
15.  Get 1/2 lb Cleaned & debearded Mussels

##### Steps to make Seafood Jambalaya:

1.  Melt butter in large pot over med heat. Add onions, green onion, bell pepper, garlic, bay leaves, Jalepeno, creole seasoning, cayenne. Cover and cook veg is tender, stirring occasionally, about 15 min.
2.  Mix in tomato paste. add sausage, water, bouillon cubes, tomatoes, rice. Bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to low. Cover and cook until rice is tender. 45-55 min.
3.  Add shrimp and mussels 5 min before rice is done cooking.
