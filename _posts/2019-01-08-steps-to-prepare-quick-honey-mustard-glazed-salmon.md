##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Mustard Glazed Salmon:

1.  Take honey
2.  Prepare grainy Dijon mustard
3.  Prepare salmon fillets
4.  Take Salt and pepper

##### Steps to make Honey Mustard Glazed Salmon:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F.
2.  Season the salmon with salt and pepper.
3.  Combine the honey and mustard in a small bowl and spread on the salmon fillets.
4.  Line baking sheet with aluminum foil, lightly spray with cooking oil and place salmon on the foil.
5.  Roast in oven for 10 minutes or until salmon flakes with a fork.
6.  Serve with your choice of vegetables. Or with rice or couscous. Or a salad. Enjoy!
