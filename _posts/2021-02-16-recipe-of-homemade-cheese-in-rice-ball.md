##### The ingredients needed to make Cheese-in Rice Ball:

1.  Take 1/2 cup Nori seaweed flakes
2.  Take 1 bowl cooked rice
3.  Prepare to taste Salt
4.  Make ready Cheese (hard type)
5.  Take Butter

##### Steps to make Cheese-in Rice Ball:

1.  Ingredients. Mix Nori seaweed with rice and salt to taste.
2.  Make triangle rice ball and put cheese on one side.
3.  Fry with low heat till the surface is crispy
4.  Fry with butter for a little while.
