##### The ingredients needed to make Mini Heart Doilies for Bento:

1.  Prepare 1 Cheese
2.  Make ready 1 dash Imitation crab meat sticks
3.  Get 1 Fish sausage
4.  Prepare 1 Ham

##### Steps to make Mini Heart Doilies for Bento:

1.  Cut out the cheese with a cookie cutter. I used a cutter from a 100-yen-store.
2.  Cut out small circles around the edge with a tiny straw like the one that comes with a juice box.
3.  Cut out small hearts from imitation crab, fish sausage or ham with a cookie cutter. Place them on top of the cheese from Step 2 and it's done.
4.  Place everything on top of nori wrapped rice balls or on another bento item for a Valentine's Day bento box.
