##### The ingredients needed to make Cajun Salmon:

1.  Prepare salmon fillet or steak
2.  Get Seasonings
3.  Get cajun seasoning
4.  Get chili powder
5.  Get paprika
6.  Make ready granulated garlic
7.  Take salt
8.  Get black pepper
9.  Get thyme
10.  Make ready cayenne pepper
11.  Prepare Other ingredients
12.  Make ready melted butter
13.  Get lemon-freshly squeezed

##### Instructions to make Cajun Salmon:

1.  Preheat oven to 400 º.
2.  Rinse and pat dry salmon fillet/steak.
3.  In small bowl, mix melted butter and lemon juice.
4.  In another small bowl, mix all spices until well combined.
5.  Dip salmon in the butter & lemon then sprinkle with spices on top of salmon and pat in.
6.  Spray a medium, oven proof pan with nonstick cooking spray. Sear salmon over medium to high heat for 2-3 minutes each side.
7.  Transfer pan from stove top to oven and cook another 8-10 minutes until salmon flakes easily.
8.  Garnish with lemons and favorite sides.
