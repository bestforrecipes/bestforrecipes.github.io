##### The ingredients needed to make THE BEST Tuna Salad You'll Ever Have:

1.  Get 12 oz Can of albacore tuna
2.  Take 1 stalk of celery
3.  Take 1 tsp Black pepper
4.  Prepare 2 tbsp Sweet relish
5.  Take 1 tbsp Honey mustard
6.  Prepare 2 tbsp Cherchies Lem'N Dill Seasoning
7.  Get 3 tbsp Mayonnaise (more or less, to your liking)

##### Steps to make THE BEST Tuna Salad You'll Ever Have:

1.  Drain and break up tuna chunks into a bowl.
2.  Grind celery in food processor, but not too much. It should be a little chunky. Add to tuna.
3.  Add all other ingredients to the tuna and mix well. I like to use Cherchies Champagne Mustard!
4.  Make a sandwich or put on crackers. Enjoy!
