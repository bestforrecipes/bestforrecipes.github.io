##### The ingredients needed to make Simmering Seafood Delight:

1.  Get Large Ling Cod Filet
2.  Prepare 2 lbs Jumbo Shrimps
3.  Take 1 bag Mixed veggies
4.  Get 3 tblsp Shrimp Bouillon
5.  Make ready 2 qt Fish stock
6.  Make ready to taste Hoisin Sauce

##### Instructions to make Simmering Seafood Delight:

1.  Wash and cube fish filet, devein shrimp
2.  Bring fish stock and shrimp bouillon to a boil
3.  Turn off heat. Mix in veggies, fish, and shrimps.
4.  Allow to slow cook for 30-45 minutes. Adding Hoisin Sauce to soup will add a lil sweet hint to this deliciousness.
