##### The ingredients needed to make Mom's Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Make ready 1 packages elbow macaroni
2.  Get 1 can tuna
3.  Get 3 hard boiled eggs
4.  Take 1/2 cup mayonnaise or miracle whip
5.  Take 2 tbsp olive oil
6.  Get 1/4 cup diced onion
7.  Make ready 1/4 cup pickle relish
8.  Make ready salt and pepper (approximately 1 tsp each)
9.  Take 1 tsp paprika

##### Steps to make Mom's Tuna Macaroni Salad:
