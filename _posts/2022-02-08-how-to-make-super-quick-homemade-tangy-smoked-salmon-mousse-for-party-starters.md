##### The ingredients needed to make Tangy Smoked Salmon mousse for party starters:

1.  Get smoked salmon
2.  Get crème fraîche
3.  Make ready cream cheese
4.  Get fresh dill
5.  Make ready small jar capers
6.  Take large lemon + zest of half
7.  Prepare Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Tangy Smoked Salmon mousse for party starters:

1.  Save a little dill to use for topping. all the ingredients (including juice of the large lemon and zest of half and except the capers) in the food processor and blitz. Add salt and black pepper to taste. Put in an airtight container and in the fridge if you are making the night before.
2.  Servie on small crackers (or volovants) and top with a caper and little leaf of dill. Enjoy!!
