##### The ingredients needed to make Cheddar Pineapple Tuna Loaf:

1.  Prepare 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
2.  Take 5 cans tuna fish drained
3.  Prepare 16 ounces pineapple tidbits
4.  Take 2 large eggs
5.  Get 2 cups butter crackers
6.  Prepare 1 small white onion diced
7.  Take 1 small red onion diced
8.  Get 1 rib celery sliced
9.  Prepare 1 tablespoon butter
10.  Take To taste salt
11.  Make ready To taste ground black pepper
12.  Make ready 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder

##### Steps to make Cheddar Pineapple Tuna Loaf:

1.  Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit dice the onions, and slice the celery. Shred the cheese.
2.  Melt the butter and add the onion and celery. Add the seasonings. Sauté till the onions are translucent.
3.  Crush the crackers. Mix everything together.
4.  Put the mixture in a meatloaf pan. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes.
5.  Let rest, and serve I hope you enjoy. This can be served cold, warm, or hot.
