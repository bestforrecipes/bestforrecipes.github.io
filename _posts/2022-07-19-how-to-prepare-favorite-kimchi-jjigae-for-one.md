##### The ingredients needed to make Kimchi Jjigae for One:

1.  Make ready 1 Chinese cabbage kimchi
2.  Get 1 block Tofu
3.  Prepare 1 dash Green onions or scallions
4.  Get 1 Water
5.  Prepare 1 can Canned tuna
6.  Get 1 rice bowl worth Plain cooked rice

##### Instructions to make Kimchi Jjigae for One:

1.  Put the kimchi with its juice into a pot with tofu and water, then heat. Once it is heated, add the drained tuna and scallions, then serve.
2.  If it's too spicy, add a bit of miso, or if it's too mild, add more of the juice from the kimchi. You can also use ready-made kimchi hot pot stock or kimchi stock.
