##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Casserole:

1.  Make ready Dry macaroni noodles
2.  Take Drained tuna
3.  Get Cream of Mushroom Soup
4.  Take Shredded cheddar cheese

##### Steps to make Tuna Casserole:

1.  Cook macaroni according to package directions. Rinse in cold water.
2.  Put noodles into 1.5 qt dish.
3.  Stir in drained tuna, soup, and cheese (leave enough to sprinkle on top).
4.  Sprinkle leftover cheese on top.
5.  Cover. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
