##### The ingredients needed to make Smoked Salmon and Watercress Pasta:

1.  Get 1 fusilli pasta
2.  Take 1 Grated lemon zest of 1 lemon
3.  Make ready 1 juice of half a lemon
4.  Make ready 150 grams Greek yoghurt
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp cream horseradish sauce
6.  Take 1 handful per person of watercress roughly chopped
7.  Make ready 1 approx 100 gram smoked salmon
8.  Take 1 dash evoo
9.  Get 1 halved cherry tomatos

##### Steps to make Smoked Salmon and Watercress Pasta:

1.  Cook pasta in salted water and drain. Return pasta to the pan.
2.  Add the lemon zest, lemon juice, evoo to the pan of pasta and mix. Add the tomatos and mix. Add the greek yoghurt and horseradish sauce and mix well. Stir in the watercress and smoked salmon. Serve!
