##### The ingredients needed to make Done in 5 Minutes! Super Delicious Baked Crab:

1.  Get 1 as much as you want Crab
2.  Get 1 Mayonnaise
3.  Take 1 Butter, Margarine

##### Steps to make Done in 5 Minutes! Super Delicious Baked Crab:

1.  Preheat the oven to 445F/230C. Meanwhile, cut the boiled crab into desired size. Lay aluminium foil on the baking tray.
2.  Drop a little butter or margarine over the top of the crab - a little at a time. Then squeeze mayonnaise over. If you love the mayonnaise taste, add more.
3.  Put into the oven for 5 minutes. Once the surface browns, it is ready. If you want a darker brown colour, use a hand-held torch.
4.  It is nice as it is, but dipping it into ponzu sauce is even better!
