3.  Take 1/4 cup chopped green onions
4.  Get 1/4 cup sliced water chestnuts chopped
5.  Prepare 3 tablespoons chopped sweet red pepper
6.  Get 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
7.  Take 2 teaspoon prepared mustard
8.  Take 2 spinach tortillas 8” room temperature
9.  Make ready 1 cup shredded lettuce
