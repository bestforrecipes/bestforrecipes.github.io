##### The ingredients needed to make Cucumber Tuna Boats:

1.  Take 1 medium cucumber
2.  Take 1 (5 oz) can tuna, drained
3.  Make ready 2 T Miracle Whip Light
4.  Take 1 T sugar-free sweet pickle relish

##### Instructions to make Cucumber Tuna Boats:

1.  Trim and Peel cucumber. Cut in half lengthwise. Scoop out seeds with a spoon.
2.  In a small bowl, combine tuna, Miracle Whip and relish.
3.  Spoon tuna mixture into cucumber "boats" and serve immediately.
