##### The ingredients needed to make Brad's camarrones a la diablo street tacos:

1.  Make ready 1 1/4 lbs large uncooked prawns, deshelled and deveined
2.  Prepare 1/2 small sweet onion chopped
3.  Get 1 large jalapeño, seeded and diced
4.  Get 1/2 (7 Oz) can Chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
5.  Prepare 1 small can diced green chiles
6.  Take 1 tbs tobasco sauce
7.  Make ready for the toppings
8.  Take Street taco tortillas
9.  Make ready refried beans
10.  Take shredded cabbage
11.  Get shredded mozzarella cheese
12.  Prepare sour cream
13.  Take lime wedges

##### Steps to make Brad's camarrones a la diablo street tacos:

1.  Add a very small amount of oil to a frying pan. Add onion and jalapeño. Saute for 4-5 minutes.
2.  In a blender, blend Chipotle and adobo until smooth. Add to the frying pan with diced green chiles and tobasco.
3.  When sauce gets bubbly. Add prawns. Cook 3-4 minutes until prawns are done.
4.  In another frying pan, heat a small amount of oil. Fry tortillas a few seconds on each side.
5.  Plate tortillas. Spread on beans. Then prawn mix. Top with desired toppings. Serve and enjoy
