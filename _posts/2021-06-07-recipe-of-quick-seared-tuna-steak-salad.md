##### The ingredients needed to make Seared Tuna Steak Salad:

1.  Take 2 Fresh tuna steaks
2.  Take 1 Garlic salt, onion powder, & pepper
3.  Get 1 Organic spring mix
4.  Get 1 Cherub tomatoes
5.  Take 1 Button mushrooms
6.  Get 1 Organic cucumbers
7.  Take 1 Black olives
8.  Make ready 1 Peppercorns
9.  Make ready 1 Olive oil
10.  Take 1 tbsp Butter

##### Steps to make Seared Tuna Steak Salad:

1.  Season both sides of tuna steak with olive oil, garlic salt, onion powder, and pepper.
2.  Heat olive oil, butter and desired amount of peppercorns in a nonstick pan.
3.  Sear on one side until 1/4 of thickness is done, then flip on other side and do the same. Half the thickness in middle should be uncooked.
4.  Let tuna rest a few minutes, then cut slices against the grain and lay on top of salad.
5.  Select dressing of choice and enjoy!
