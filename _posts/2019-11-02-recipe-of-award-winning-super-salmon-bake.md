##### The ingredients needed to make Super Salmon Bake:

1.  Make ready 1 large onion
2.  Take 3 medium sweet potatoes
3.  Get 2 cooked salmon steaks
4.  Make ready 5 medium-sized eggs
5.  Take 1 sprig or 25g of fresh coriander
6.  Take Half a fresh chilli
7.  Prepare 1 yellow pepper
8.  Take 3 cloves fresh garlic
9.  Prepare 1 pinch ground nutmeg
10.  Take to taste Salt n pepper

##### Instructions to make Super Salmon Bake:

1.  Heat the oven to 200°C / Gas Mark 6
2.  Chop the onion small and fry this in a big frying pan, in olive oil with garlic & salt until the onions go clear
3.  Chop the potatoes into 2cm chunks and drop these into the pan to fry for 10 mins
4.  Chop the coriander, the chilli and the peppers and add these to the pan, fry for another 5 mins
5.  Break up the salmon and put this into a big mixing bowl
6.  Drop the contents of your frying pan into the bowl, crack in the eggs, sprinkle in the nutmeg, stir and then put this into an oven proof baking dish
7.  I covered the bake with grated mozzarella for a nice topping
8.  Bake for 25 mins, so that the mozzarella is golden and crispy!
