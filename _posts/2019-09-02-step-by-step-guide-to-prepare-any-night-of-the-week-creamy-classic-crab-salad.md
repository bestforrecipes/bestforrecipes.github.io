##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Classic Crab Salad:

1.  Make ready 4 oz. Chunk-style Crab
2.  Get 2 tbs. finely chopped Celery
3.  Get 1 tbs. finely diced Red Onion
4.  Get 3 tbs. Light Mayo
5.  Take 1/2 tsp. Hot Old Bay Seasoning (or regular)
6.  Get 1/2 tsp. Dried Dill Weed
7.  Take Salt and Pepper (to taste)

##### Instructions to make Creamy Classic Crab Salad:

1.  Chop crab into small chunks. Add all ingredients and mix together well. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour to let flavors meld.
