##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Cooked In Mayonnaise - For Bento:

1.  Get 2 pieces Raw salmon
2.  Make ready 2 tbsp Katakuriko
3.  Take 3 tbsp ＊ Mayonnaise
4.  Get 1 and 1/2 teaspoons ＊ Sugar
5.  Get 1 tsp ＊ Vinegar
6.  Get 1 tsp ＊Oyster sauce
7.  Prepare 1 tbsp Vegetable oil

##### Steps to make Salmon Cooked In Mayonnaise - For Bento:

1.  Cut the salmon into bite sized pieces. Sprinkle with a little salt. Dust with katakuriko.
2.  Heat some oil in a frying pan over low heat, and pan fry the salmon.
3.  While the salmon cooks, combine the ＊ ingredients in a large bowl (large enough to add the salmon later) and mix well.
4.  Put the cooked salmon in the bowl with the sauce and mix.
