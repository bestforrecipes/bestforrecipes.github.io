##### Instructions to make Steam egg:

1.  Crack the egg into a plate.
2.  Stir it with a fork until it is all yellow.
3.  Add water and stir it with a fork too.
4.  Add sasimi oil and salt.
5.  Cut the imitation crab sticks into cubes
6.  Add the imitation crab cubes
7.  Steam it about 10 minutes.
8.  Take it out.
9.  Add soya sauce
10.  Finish!
