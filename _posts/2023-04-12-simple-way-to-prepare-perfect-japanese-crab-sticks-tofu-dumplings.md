##### The ingredients needed to make Japanese Crab sticks Tofu Dumplings:

1.  Prepare 200 g hard type Tofu
2.  Get 50 g Crab sticks
3.  Make ready 1 dried mushroom
4.  Get 30 g young bamboo
5.  Make ready 20 g onion
6.  Make ready 1 teaspoon grind ginger
7.  Get 2 tablespoon starch(any starch)
8.  Prepare to taste Salt

##### Steps to make Japanese Crab sticks Tofu Dumplings:

1.  Drain Tofu
2.  Ingredients. Now this is the season of young bamboo(April).
3.  Cut vegetables very small.
4.  Mix all and seasonings with hand. And make small balls
5.  Fry in 2cm deep oil till good brown.
6.  Eat with your favorite sauce.
