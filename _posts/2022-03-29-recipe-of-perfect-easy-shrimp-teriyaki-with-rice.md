##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Shrimp Teriyaki with Rice:

1.  Take vegetable stock
2.  Make ready soy sauce
3.  Make ready mirin
4.  Prepare honey
5.  Make ready scallions
6.  Take shrimp (deveined)
7.  Get rice
8.  Take pepper

##### Steps to make Easy Shrimp Teriyaki with Rice:

1.  Mix together stock, soy sauce, mirin and honey. Bring to a boil over medium heat and let reduce, about 15 minutes. Prepare rice
2.  Finely dice scallions.
3.  Coat shrimp with teriyaki sauce and bake at 350 F for ~8 minutes
4.  Sprinkle shrimp with scallions, season with pepper and serve with rice
