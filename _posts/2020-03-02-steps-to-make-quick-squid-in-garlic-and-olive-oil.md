##### The ingredients needed to make Squid in Garlic and Olive Oil:

1.  Make ready 250 g Squid
2.  Take 3 Tbsp Olive Oil
3.  Prepare 8 cloves Garlic
4.  Prepare 2 pcs. Bay leaf
5.  Make ready 1/4 Tsp pepper
6.  Make ready 1 pc. Chili finger, sliced
7.  Take 1 Tbsp Liquid Seasoning
8.  Take 1 Tbsp Calamansi

##### Instructions to make Squid in Garlic and Olive Oil:

1.  Clean squid, remove transparent backbone, squid ink, eyes and mouth
2.  Heat oil in a pan, saute garlic
3.  Add chili
4.  Put squid, saute
5.  Add bay leaf and pepper
6.  Add liquid seasoning
7.  Put Calamansi and turn off the heat. Do not overcook the squid
