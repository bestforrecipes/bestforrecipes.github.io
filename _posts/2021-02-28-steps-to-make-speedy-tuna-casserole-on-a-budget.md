##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Casserole on a budget:

1.  Take Cream of mushroom soup
2.  Take Tuna in water
3.  Make ready Bread
4.  Prepare Egg noodles

##### Steps to make Tuna Casserole on a budget:

1.  Preheat oven to 375.
2.  Open and drain tuna. Set aside.
3.  Cook egg noodles as directed on the box.
4.  Mix soup, tuna, and noodles together and put in a 2 quart casserole dish.
5.  Toast bread lightly in a toaster out toaster oven.
6.  Crumble toast on top of the tuna noodle mixture.
7.  Place in the oven and bake for 5 minutes. Take out and eat warm.
