##### The ingredients needed to make Brad's Cajun tuna steaks:

1.  Prepare 1 tuna steak per serving needed
2.  Take Tony Chachere's creole seasoning
3.  Make ready 1 tbs butter
4.  Make ready sauce from my new Orleans prawn recipe

##### Steps to make Brad's Cajun tuna steaks:

1.  Place tuna steaks on a cutting board. Sprinkle with creole seasoning. Let sit 10 minutes.
2.  Melt butter in a frying pan over medium high heat.
3.  Place steaks in pan, seasoning side down. Sprinkle other side with seasoning.
4.  Fry 2 minutes on each side. Keep steaks rare.
5.  Plate steaks. Drizzle sauce over top. Serve. Enjoy.
