4.  Make ready Little bit of white onion
5.  Get Little bit of green onion
6.  Get 1 tbps soy sauce
7.  Prepare 1 tbps oyster sauce
8.  Get 1 tbps mushroom sauce
9.  Take 1-2 eggs
