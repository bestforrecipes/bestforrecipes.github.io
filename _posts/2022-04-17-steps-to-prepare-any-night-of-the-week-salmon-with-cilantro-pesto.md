##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon with Cilantro Pesto:

1.  Take Sauce
2.  Get 1 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
3.  Make ready 6 minced garlic cloves
4.  Take 1/3 cup toasted pine nuts
5.  Take 2/3 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
6.  Prepare 1/2 tsp pepper
7.  Make ready 3/4 cup olive oil
8.  Take Fish
9.  Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
10.  Get 1/2 tsp white pepper
11.  Make ready 4 each 5oz salmon fillets

##### Instructions to make Salmon with Cilantro Pesto:

1.  To make the SAUCE, follow steps 2 & 3
2.  combine all of the ingredients in a food processor except the oil, blend until well incorporated
3.  slowly add the oil until a thick mayonnaise type sauce is formed, reserve until required
4.  To make the FISH, follow steps 5-7
5.  brush the fillets with oil and season with pepper
6.  grill the fillets over medium heat for 5-6 minutes each side
7.  serve with a dollop of sauce on each fillet
8.  ENJOY
