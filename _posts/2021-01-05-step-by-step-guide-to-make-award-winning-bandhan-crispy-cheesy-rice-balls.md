2.  Get 2 tsp Adrak mirch lahsun paste
3.  Get Salt swadnusar
4.  Make ready Oil for frying
5.  Make ready 50 gm Cheese
6.  Get 1 Fried onion
7.  Prepare Bread crumbs awashktanusar
