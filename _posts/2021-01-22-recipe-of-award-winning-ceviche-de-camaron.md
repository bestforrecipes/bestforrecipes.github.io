##### The ingredients needed to make Ceviche de camaron:

1.  Prepare 1 lb shrimp
2.  Take 1 medium white or red onion
3.  Make ready 3 large tomatoes
4.  Get 1 tbsp minced garlic
5.  Get 1/2 cup lime juice
6.  Make ready 1/4 cup Louisiana style hotsauce
7.  Get 1 bunch cilantro
8.  Make ready 1 large jalapeno (optional)

##### Steps to make Ceviche de camaron:

1.  mix lime juice, garlic, and hotsauce in large mixing bowl
2.  cut shrimp, tomatoes, and onion into 1/4inch size pieces and add to bowl
3.  mix to cover
4.  finely chop the cilantro and add to shrimp, tomatoes, and onion
5.  mix well
6.  salt and pepper to taste
7.  add 1finely chopped jalapeno, deseeded (optional)
8.  refrigerate for 2 hours and enjoy!
