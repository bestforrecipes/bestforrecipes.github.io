##### The ingredients needed to make TINOTUAN / BUBUR MANADO (Manado Vegetable Porridge):

1.  Get 250 gram rice, washed and drained
2.  Make ready 200 gram pumpkin, Chopped
3.  Get 100 gram sweet potato, chopped
4.  Get 1 cob sweetcorn, extract the kernels
5.  Get 50 gram spinach, washed
6.  Prepare 50 gram water convolvulus, washed (optional)
7.  Prepare 5-6 string beans, cut into 2 cm pieces
8.  Get 2 teaspoon salt
9.  Take 2 lemon grass, bruised
10.  Make ready Sambal dabu-dabu :

17.  Get Grilled fish:
18.  Get Wash tuna fish and rub with 1/2 teaspoon salt
19.  Prepare 1/2 teaspoons ginger and 2 tbsp vegetable oil
20.  Get Grilled until cooked

##### Steps to make TINOTUAN / BUBUR MANADO (Manado Vegetable Porridge):

1.  Add water level to a level 3 - 5 cm above the rice level. Boil until almost cooked.
2.  Add pumpkin, sweet potato and corn. When they are cooked, add vegetables and salt.
3.  Cook until the cereal thickens. Serve with sambal and fried salted fish.
