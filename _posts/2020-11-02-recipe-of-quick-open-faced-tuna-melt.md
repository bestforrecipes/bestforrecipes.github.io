##### The ingredients needed to make Open faced tuna melt:

1.  Prepare 2 can (5 oz) chicken of the sea tuna
2.  Make ready 1 medium shallot,minced(approx.2 tbsp)
3.  Make ready 2 tbsp plain non-fat greek yogurt
4.  Make ready 1 tbsp lemon juice
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp minced parsley
6.  Take 1/8 tsp sea salt
7.  Get 4 slice 100% whole-wheat bread, toasted
8.  Prepare 2 fresh tomatoes, sliced
9.  Make ready 1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese

##### Steps to make Open faced tuna melt:

1.  Preheat broiler
2.  Combine tuna,shallot,Greek yogurt,lemon juice,parsley, and salt in a medium bowl. If desired you can add some black pepper or hot sauce for additional flavor.
3.  Spread 1/4 cup of the tuna mixture on each slice of toast; top with cheese and tomato.
4.  Place sandwiches on a baking sheet and broil until the cheese is bubbling and golden brown, 3-5 min
