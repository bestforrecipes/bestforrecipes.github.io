##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Tuna Pasta:

1.  Take 1 can Chicken soup
2.  Take 1 can tuna (small)
3.  Prepare 1 Pasta

##### Instructions to make Chicken Tuna Pasta:

1.  Bring water to a boil on the hob and cook the pasta until fully done
2.  Using a collander, drain the pasta then pour back into the pan
3.  Pour in the chicken soup and the drained tuna
4.  Stir well and serve
5.  Enjoy :D
