##### The ingredients needed to make Salt and pepper squid:

1.  Prepare 2 full squids (500g)
2.  Prepare Plain flour (200g)
3.  Prepare Semolina (200g)
4.  Make ready 1 green chilli
5.  Make ready 3 spring onions
6.  Get 1/2 a lemon
7.  Take 500 ml vegetable oil
8.  Take 1 tablespoon shaoxing wine
9.  Prepare 1/2 tablespoon sesame oil
10.  Take to taste Salt and pepper

##### Steps to make Salt and pepper squid:

1.  Prepare the squid by removing all the body and guts
2.  Slice into bite size pieces and marinade in sesame oil and shaoxing wine
3.  Roll squid in flour and semolina
4.  Heat oil in pan and then add squid for 2mins, dry squid in paper towels
5.  Chop chilli and spring onions. Lemon, salt and pepper to taste.
