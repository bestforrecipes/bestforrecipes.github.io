##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Popcorn Shrimp Parmesan:

1.  Get shrimp
2.  Prepare small, peeled and deveined shrimp
3.  Get egg coating

##### Instructions to make Fried Popcorn Shrimp Parmesan:

1.  Mix the coating all together in a ziploc style baggie. This makes it so easy and clean up is a breeze.
2.  In a bowl, beat the eggs and add the shrimp.
3.  Add egg dipped shrimp into the ziploc bag. Close top tightly and shake to coat all the shrimp.
4.  In a deep fryer or deep pot, add oil and bring to 325°F Fahrenheit.
5.  Add shrimp in batches. Fry several minutes until golden brown.
6.  Drain on paper toweling.
7.  Serve with any dipping sauce. Use as a side to steaks or I love putting them over a salad. Just enjoy!
