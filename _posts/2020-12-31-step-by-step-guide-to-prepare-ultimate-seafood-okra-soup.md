##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood Okra Soup:

1.  Get 4 cups Okra
2.  Prepare 1 big Catfish (cut into 5 piece)
3.  Take 4 Fresh habanero
4.  Take 2 cps shrimps
5.  Prepare 1 bunch Vegetable(ugu) Sliced
6.  Prepare 1 cp Calamari
7.  Prepare 1 cksp Palm oil
8.  Make ready 2 tbs Crayfish Blended
9.  Prepare to taste Salt
10.  Get 2 cube Bouillion
11.  Prepare 1/2 bulb Onions

##### Instructions to make Seafood Okra Soup:

1.  Grate okra and set aside
2.  Dice pepper and onions and set aside
3.  Clean fish, calamari and shrimp set aside
4.  Dice vegetable and set aside
5.  Pour red oil in a pot and heat up add diced pepper, onions, salt and crayfish,
6.  Add calamari and shrimps allow it to fry for about 3mins then add catfish allow it cook for 5mins, add vegetables, and stir properly and turn off heat
7.  Serve with Eba, Semo or any kinda swallow; I decided to lick the soup without any swallow.
