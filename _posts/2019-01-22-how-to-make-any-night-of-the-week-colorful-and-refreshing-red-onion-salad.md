##### The ingredients needed to make Colorful and Refreshing Red Onion Salad:

1.  Make ready 1 Red onion
2.  Get 1 can Canned tuna
3.  Prepare 2 Green onions or scallions
4.  Make ready A
5.  Take 1/2 lemon Lemon juice (fresh or bottled)
6.  Take 3 tbsp Mayonnaise
7.  Take 1 tbsp Mentsuyu
8.  Get 1/2 tsp Coarsely ground black pepper

##### Steps to make Colorful and Refreshing Red Onion Salad:

1.  Peel the onion, cut into thin slices, and soak in water.
2.  After letting it soak, thoroughly drain the water and any excess moisture.
3.  Drain the oil from the canned tuna and combine the tuna with the onion.
4.  Combine the A ingredients. Add to the onion and tuna for flavor.
5.  Arrange on a dish. Top with finely chopped green onion to finish!
