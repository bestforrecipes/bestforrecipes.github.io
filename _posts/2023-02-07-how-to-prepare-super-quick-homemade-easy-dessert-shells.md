##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Dessert Shells:

1.  Prepare dessert shells (usually comes in a package of 6)
2.  Get oreos
3.  Take vanilla frosting
4.  Get nutella
5.  Make ready sprinkles
6.  Get cherries
7.  Make ready whipped cream

##### Instructions to make Easy Dessert Shells:

1.  unpack your dessert shells and add a layer of vanilla frosting on tge bottom
2.  clear a circle in the middle and add a teaspoon of nutella
3.  spray whipped cream over the top
4.  add sprinkles around it
5.  break an oreo cookie into 4, take a quarter of it and put it on top of the dessert shell
6.  add the cherry on the side
7.  you can let it chill for 10 minutes before serving so the nutella hardens
