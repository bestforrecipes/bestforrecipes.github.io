##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Shrimp Scampi Pasta:

1.  Make ready peeled uncooked shrimp (fresh or frozen)
2.  Prepare Mccormicks shrimp scampi sauce
3.  Make ready red pepper flakes
4.  Take pasta
5.  Take parmesan cheese

##### Steps to make Easy Shrimp Scampi Pasta:

1.  Cook desired type of pasta per package directions.
2.  Pour sauce in hot skillet on medium heat and add shrimp. Cook shrimp for 4 minutes flipping halfway through. Add pasta to skillet and toss ensuring sauce and shrimp are evenly distributed.
3.  Plate and add parmesan to taste.
