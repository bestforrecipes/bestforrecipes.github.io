##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon With Soy Sichuan Sauce:

1.  Make ready salmon head or any fish
2.  Get dried Chili
3.  Make ready Of oil
4.  Make ready garlic
5.  Take onion
6.  Get Of dried Sichuan pepper corn
7.  Prepare light soy sauce
8.  Get dark soy sauce
9.  Make ready Of sugar
10.  Prepare Of water
11.  Make ready dice sour vegetables

##### Steps to make Salmon With Soy Sichuan Sauce:

1.  Baked salmon 200 c for 20 to 25 minutes depending on the thickness of the salmon then set aside
2.  With oil pan fry the dried Chili, dried Sichuan pepper corn for 2 minutes
3.  Add in the garlic and onion and stir fry for another 2 minutes
4.  Add in the sauce and mix well, add in the water then add in the sugar together with the dice sour mustard and mix well for another 2 minutes
5.  Thicken the sauce with the cornstarch mixture and mix well for another 1 minutes then off heat and enjoy this sauce with salmon
