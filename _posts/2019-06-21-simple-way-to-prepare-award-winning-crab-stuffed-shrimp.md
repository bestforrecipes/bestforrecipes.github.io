##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Stuffed Shrimp:

1.  Take 12 Jumbo Shrimp, peeled and a slit cut to hold stuffing
2.  Take 8 oz jumbo lump crab meat
3.  Make ready 1/2 stalk celery, finely minced
4.  Make ready 1 small shallot, finely minced
5.  Get 1 clove garlic, minced
6.  Make ready 1/4 cup mayonnaise
7.  Prepare 2 tbsp sour cream
8.  Make ready 1 tsp lemon juice
9.  Take 1/2 tsp dijon mustard
10.  Prepare 1/2 tsp cajun seasoning
11.  Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper and salt to taste
12.  Take 1 tsp hot sauce such as franks brand
13.  Make ready 2 tbsp butter, melted
14.  Get 1/4 cup chicken broth
15.  Prepare 4 slice bacon, cut into pieces
16.  Take 1/2 cup Shredded cheese, I used mix of pepper jack, sharp cheddar and italian four cheese blend

##### Instructions to make Crab Stuffed Shrimp:

1.  Preheat oven to 425
2.  Pour butter into baking pan arge enogh to hold shrimp on one layer. Place shrimp pocket side up in dish.
3.  MAKE STUFFING
4.  In a bowl mix mayonnaise, sour cream, celery, shallot, garlic,lemon, mustard, cajun seasoning, pepper and red hot, whisk to combine. Put crab meat in large bowl and add sauce and very gently combine well.Fill shrimp evenly with crab. Top evenly with cheese, pour over chicken broth. Top with bacon.
5.  Bake for 25 to 30 miutes until shrimp is cooked through.
6.  Serve with rolls amd coleslaw or a salad!
