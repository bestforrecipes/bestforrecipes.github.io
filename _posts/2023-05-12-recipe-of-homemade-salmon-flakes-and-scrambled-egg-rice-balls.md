##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Flakes and Scrambled Egg Rice Balls:

1.  Prepare bowl Plain cooked rice
2.  Get Egg
3.  Make ready plus Salmon flakes (ready made)
4.  Take Sesame seeds
5.  Get Sugar
6.  Take Salt

##### Instructions to make Salmon Flakes and Scrambled Egg Rice Balls:

1.  Spread oil in a non-stick pan and add the egg (seasoned with salt and sugar). Cook on medium heat to scramble the egg. Mix it well with chopsticks so it becomes crumbly as shown.
2.  Put the rice, the egg from step 1, the salmon flakes, and sesame in a bowl and mix.
3.  Form into your desired shape and they're done.
