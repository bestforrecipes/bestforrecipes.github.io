##### The ingredients needed to make Chopsuey Soup (Ho To Tai):

1.  Make ready Vegetable/cooking oil
2.  Make ready 1/4 kg pork cutlets - chopped to smaller pieces
3.  Get 1/4 kg shrimp - remove whiskers/ or better yet remove shell
4.  Prepare 8 pcs fish balls
5.  Get 4 clove garlic- crushed
6.  Get 1 pc small onions- chopped
7.  Make ready 2 pcs small tomatoes - chopped
8.  Get 1 bunch Pechay/Napa Cabbage - remove hard stalks and chop
9.  Take 1/4 kg Green Cabbage - remove hard stalks and chop
10.  Prepare 1 small bunch of broccoli - remove hard stem and separate
11.  Take 1 small bunch of cauliflower - remove hard stem and separate
12.  Prepare 1 small carrot - cut to circles
13.  Get 1/2 part of small chayote -chopped
14.  Take 1/8 kg flat beans - fiber removed
15.  Take 1 handful bean sprouts - cleaned
16.  Prepare 1 stalk celery - chopped
17.  Prepare Chopsuey Sauce:
18.  Take 2 tbsp oyster sauce
19.  Prepare 2 tbsp soy sauce
20.  Get 1 cup chicken broth
21.  Take 1 tbsp cornstarch
22.  Make ready 1 tsp sesame oil
23.  Make ready 1 tsp grounded black pepper
24.  Get Pork Marinade:
25.  Take 2 tbsp oyster sauce
26.  Take 2 tbsp soy sauce
27.  Get 1 tsp black grounded pepper
28.  Make ready 1 tsp chopped ginger

##### Steps to make Chopsuey Soup (Ho To Tai):

1.  Mix the pork marinade and let it for at least 20 min
2.  Heat vegetable/cooking oil in a wok on medium heat. Cook the marinated pork.
3.  Add the shrimp and cook until shrimp is pink.
4.  Drop in onions, garlic and tomatoes. Stir fry on high heat.
5.  Pour the sauce. Drop in the vegetables and fish balls when sauce starts boiling. Mix well
6.  Cook until sauce is reduced and vegetables are cooked.
