##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Salmon with Creamy Avocado Sauce:

1.  Get 8 oz Thawed Salmon filet
2.  Make ready 1 tsp Garlic Powder
3.  Get 1 tsp Garlic Salt
4.  Make ready 1/2 tsp White Pepper
5.  Take 1 tsp Onion Powder
6.  Make ready 100 grams Avocado (half)
7.  Take 3/4 tbsp Vanilla Greek Yogurt
8.  Get 1/2 Lime

##### Steps to make Grilled Salmon with Creamy Avocado Sauce:

1.  Heat small amount of oil in a frying pan. Add salmon and cook for 3 minutes on each side. Remove salmon and set aside on a plate with a paper towel.
2.  Mix remaining ingredients until a creamy consistency is reached.
3.  Spoon half of sauce mix onto top of each salmon filet. Enjoy!
