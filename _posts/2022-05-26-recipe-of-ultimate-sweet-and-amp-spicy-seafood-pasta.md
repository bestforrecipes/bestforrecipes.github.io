##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Spicy Seafood Pasta:

1.  Make ready cooked Spaghetti
2.  Get Shrimp, deveined
3.  Get Squid, cleaned and cut into rings
4.  Prepare Mussels, precooked and removed from shell
5.  Take water (half from mussel & halfpasta water)
6.  Take Olive Oil
7.  Prepare Onion, diced
8.  Get Garlic, minced
9.  Prepare Tomato/Spahetti Sauce
10.  Make ready Oyster Sauce
11.  Get Sugar
12.  Make ready Sriracha, adjust according to preference
13.  Get Pepper
14.  Prepare Spring onion for garnish
15.  Take Note 1: precook mussel with 2 Tbsp grated ginger
16.  Prepare Note 2: I use Filipino style spaghetti sauce

##### Instructions to make Sweet & Spicy Seafood Pasta:

1.  In a bowl mix together tomato sauce, oyster sauce, sugar and sriracha. Mix until sugar is dissolved.
2.  Heat oil, saute onion & garlic
3.  Add shrimp
4.  Add sauce mixture, let it simmer
5.  Add squid, after 1 minute add mussel. Mix and simmer
6.  Put spaghetti noodles, season with pepper. Mix thoroughly.
