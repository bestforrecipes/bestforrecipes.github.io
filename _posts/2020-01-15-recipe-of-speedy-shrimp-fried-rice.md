##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Fried Rice:

1.  Get Mix vegetables
2.  Take rice
3.  Get shrimp
4.  Prepare whole onion
5.  Make ready garlic
6.  Get eggs
7.  Take pinch of Salt
8.  Make ready Pepper
9.  Take Soya sauce
10.  Get Cooking oil
11.  Get Water

##### Instructions to make Shrimp Fried Rice:

1.  Cook the rice and set aside
2.  Saute onion then garlic
3.  Add the shrimp cut into cubes
4.  Then add the mix vegetables
5.  When the mix vegetables is cooked add the rice
6.  Put around two table spoon of soya sauce to taste
7.  In the other pan..fry the beaten eggs and cut into cubes
8.  Then add the egg into the rice
9.  Put a pinch of salt and a little bit of pepper
