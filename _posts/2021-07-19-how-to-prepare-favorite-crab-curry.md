##### Steps to make Crab Curry:

1.  Fry onions, ginger and garlic chillies, garlic, curry leaf, methi seeds, jeera seeds, mustard seeds in oil.
2.  Once fried, add chilli powder, garum masala, jeera powder, turmeric powder and braise for a few seconds.
3.  Add cleaned crab, mix, lower heat and allow water to come out of crab. Once the water has dried out a bit, add in tamarind and mix.
4.  Then add tomatoes and salt. Mix and allow to cook on medium heat. Once chutney thickens, remove from stove. - - Enjoy
