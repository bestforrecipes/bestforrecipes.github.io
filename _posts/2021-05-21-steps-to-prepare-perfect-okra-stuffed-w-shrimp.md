##### The ingredients needed to make Okra Stuffed w/Shrimp:

1.  Take Few pieces of okra
2.  Get shrimp
3.  Get white pepper
4.  Get Parsley

##### Instructions to make Okra Stuffed w/Shrimp:

1.  Wash okra then cut both sides of it. Remove seeds and set aside
2.  Finely chop the shrimp, parsley then sprinkle a little bit of salt and white pepper.
3.  Stuffed the shrimp mixture inside the okra and steam for 4 mins.
