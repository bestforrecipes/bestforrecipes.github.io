2.  Prepare 1/3 cup Hellmans Olive Oil Mayonnaise
3.  Take 1/3 cup shredded swiss cheese
4.  Take 1/4 cup minced red onion
5.  Take Juice of 1 small orange
6.  Make ready 1 teaspoon Chef Paul Prudhomme's Blackened Redfish Magic
7.  Get 1 teaspoon fresh minced parsley
8.  Make ready Serve with Ritz Crackers and lemon wedges

##### Steps to make Crab Salad Magic:
