##### The ingredients needed to make Cake Salé: Tuna and Olive:

1.  Make ready 100 g flour
2.  Take 3 eggs
3.  Make ready 100 ml (soy) milk
4.  Make ready 2 Tbs olive oil
5.  Take 2 1/2 g baking powder
6.  Prepare salt and pepper
7.  Prepare 160 g canned tuna (non-oil)
8.  Get 80 g carrot
9.  Prepare some black/green olives

##### Instructions to make Cake Salé: Tuna and Olive:

1.  Sift the flour and baking powder together. Add the eggs and mix well.
2.  Pour the milk and olive oil and mix together. Then add some salt and pepper, the tuna, carrot (cut in small cubes) and sliced olives.
3.  Pour in a cake mold and bake in the oven preheated at 180 C for about 30 minutes.
4.  Enjoy!
