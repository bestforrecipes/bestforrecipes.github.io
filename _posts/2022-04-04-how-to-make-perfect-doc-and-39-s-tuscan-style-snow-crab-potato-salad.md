##### The ingredients needed to make Doc's Tuscan Style Snow Crab Potato Salad:

1.  Get 6 medium sized potatoes
2.  Prepare 4 large hard boiled eggs
3.  Make ready 1 cup snow crab meat (leg and body meat)
4.  Prepare 1/4 cup chopped artichoke hearts (trimmed)
5.  Get 1/2 roasted red pepper (chopped)
6.  Get 1 tablespoon chopped shallots
7.  Take 1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
8.  Prepare 1 finely chopped basil leaf (fresh)
9.  Take 1 teaspoon chives
10.  Get 1 tablespoon parsley
11.  Take 1/4 cup chopped black olives
12.  Get 1 teaspoon paprika
13.  Take 1 teaspoon salt (add more to taste if desired)
14.  Make ready 1 teaspoon pepper
15.  Take 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
16.  Get 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
17.  Prepare 1 tablespoon yellow mustard

##### Instructions to make Doc's Tuscan Style Snow Crab Potato Salad:

1.  Mix egg yolk, all seasoning, and all wet ingredients in a blender or a mixing bowl. - Let settle in the refrigerator until potatoes are boiled and cooled.
2.  Boil potatoes until desired softness is reached. (poke with a small fork to check) - Let cool to room temperature.
3.  Dice potatoes and fold in all other ingredients.
4.  Chill in refrigerator for 2 hours
5.  Serve cold
