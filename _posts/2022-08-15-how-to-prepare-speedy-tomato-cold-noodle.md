##### The ingredients needed to make Tomato Cold Noodle:

1.  Prepare Soup
2.  Get 100 ml (3.38 fl oz) Vinegar
3.  Get 80 ml (2.70 fl oz) Soy sauce
4.  Get 50 ml (1.69 fl oz) Tomato juice \*unsalted
5.  Prepare 3 tbsp Agabe syrup
6.  Take 1.5 tsp Sesame oil
7.  Get Ingredients
8.  Take 200 g (7.05 oz) Tomato
9.  Prepare 2 Cucumbers
10.  Make ready 1/2 Onion
11.  Get 200 g (7.05 oz) Shrimp
12.  Make ready 4 portions Raw noodles \*thin type
13.  Get to taste Roasted sesame seeds

##### Steps to make Tomato Cold Noodle:

1.  (In advance) Mix these soup ingredients in a container. Keep the container in the refrigerator for a few hours.
2.  Chop tomato, cucumber, onion and shrimp (wash well and lightly boiled to reduce the fishy smell).
3.  Boil the noodles for 60-90 sec. and then drain the water. Soak the noodles in icy water right away to make them firm.
4.  Sprinkle the toppings on the noodles. Pour the soup over the noodles.
