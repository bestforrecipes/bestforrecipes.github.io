1.  Prepare 200 g salmon fillet
2.  Get 160 g Chinese noodles
3.  Take 40 g dried shiitake mushroom
4.  Get 60 g mix bellpeppers
5.  Prepare 20 gm beansprouts
6.  Get 20 gm carrots
7.  Get 80 ml BBq sauce
8.  Make ready 30 g leeks
