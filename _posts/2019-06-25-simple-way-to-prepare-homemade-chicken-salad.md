##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Salad:

1.  Take 1 rotisserie chicken cubed (no skin)
2.  Prepare 1 lb medium shells
3.  Prepare 1/2 lb colby jack cheese, shredded by hand
4.  Make ready 4 cups red seedless grapes (crunchy)
5.  Take 4 stalks celery, chopped
6.  Take 1 cup green onions, chopped
7.  Take 1 cup mayo (until creamy)
8.  Get To taste salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Chicken Salad:

1.  Cook pasta according to the package. Strain pasta.
2.  While pasta is cooking, in a large bowl, add cubed chicken, shredded cheese, grapes, celery and green onion.
3.  Combine all ingredients besides mayo. Set in refrigerator to cool for 30 minutes
4.  Add in mayo once pasta has cooled. Mix together. Add in salt and pepper for taste.
