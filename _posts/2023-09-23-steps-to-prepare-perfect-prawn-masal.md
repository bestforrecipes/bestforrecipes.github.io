##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn Masal:

1.  Get 500 gm prawns
2.  Make ready 2 cup Vegetables of your choice
3.  Get 1 tbsp red chilli and garlic paste
4.  Make ready 1 cup coconut milk
5.  Get Salt
6.  Get 1 tbsp Oil

##### Steps to make Prawn Masal:

1.  First fry prawns in butter and pepper and pinch of salt
2.  Now take oil in pan and add all vegetables and toss it and half cook it.
3.  Add red chilli and garlic paste and coconut milk and mix it.
