##### The ingredients needed to make Lobster Manwich:

1.  Take lobster
2.  Make ready Old Bay Seasoning
3.  Make ready pico de gallo
4.  Prepare hoagie
5.  Get real mayo
6.  Make ready crab boil
7.  Prepare green onion

##### Steps to make Lobster Manwich:

1.  bring crab boil in a large pot to a boil
2.  boil 4 large lobster tails ( i get the rock tails from Sam's)
3.  once tails are fully cooked, allow the tails to cool to room temperature
4.  pull the meat from shell
5.  dice tail meat into large chunks
6.  add old bay seasoning to taste
7.  chop green onions and combine with chopped lobster in a large mixing bowl
8.  slowly add Mayo, making sure to lightly coat lobster
9.  toast hoagies in the oven at 350°
10.  use 8-10 oz of lobster salad in each bun
11.  top sandwich with pico de gallo, and sprinkle old bay over the top
12.  enjoy
