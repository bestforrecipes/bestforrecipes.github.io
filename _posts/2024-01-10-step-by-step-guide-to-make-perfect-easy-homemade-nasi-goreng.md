##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Homemade Nasi Goreng:

1.  Take cooked rice
2.  Prepare eggs
3.  Prepare mince garlic
4.  Get Chilli red sliced
5.  Prepare spring onion
6.  Make ready prawns, can be changed to meat
7.  Prepare Vegetables oil
8.  Get Oyster sauce
9.  Take Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Easy Homemade Nasi Goreng:

1.  Cut and slice vegetables.
2.  Crack all eggs and mix with rice in a bowl.
3.  Heat up pan with oil, add in garlic and spring onion. saute with no colour until smells good. Then add in chilli.
4.  Then add rice that mixed with egg and stir. Add in oyster sauce.
5.  After stir for 1 minute, cook in prawn and season with salt and pepper.
6.  After everything cooked, taste. And ready to be served, Enjoy the meal!
