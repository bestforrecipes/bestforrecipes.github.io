4.  Take 1 cup caesar dressing

6.  Prepare 1/4 cup parmesan
7.  Make ready Tsp black pepper
8.  Prepare Tsp salt
9.  Make ready Tsp garlic powder
10.  Prepare Tsp onion powder

##### Steps to make Stuffed Shells:

1.  Preheat oven to 375. Brown hamburger with the onion and spices, drain grease
2.  In medium bowl, combine dressing, sour cream and parmesan, mix well to combine
3.  Add meat, mix well
4.  Pour the spaghetti sauce in bottom of dish. Stuff shells with meat mixture and place in baking dish. Sprinkle top with parmesan if desired.
5.  Bake 20 minutes until hot and bubbly. I sprinkled top with parsley for presentation.
