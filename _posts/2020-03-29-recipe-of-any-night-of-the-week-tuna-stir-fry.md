##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Stir-fry:

1.  Get 4 oz tuna
2.  Prepare 1/3 cup rice
3.  Make ready 1/4 cup shredded pepperjack cheese
4.  Get 1/4 cup broccoli
5.  Get 2 tbsp green onion
6.  Take 2 tbsp tomato
7.  Get 1 tbsp peanut oil
8.  Make ready 1/2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
9.  Take 1/2 tbsp honey
10.  Get 1 tsp sesame oil
11.  Get 1/2 tsp garlic powder
12.  Take 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
13.  Get 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
14.  Make ready 1/8 tsp salt

##### Steps to make Tuna Stir-fry:

1.  Bring pot of water to a rolling boil. Add rice, and let boil for 15-20 minutes.
2.  Wash all veggies and chop. Combine with tuna in frying pan. Add oil, and bring to medium heat. Cook for 8-10 minutes.
3.  When veggies are soft and tuna is at 150°F, season with black pepper, cayenne pepper, salt, and garlic. Reduce heat and cover.
4.  When rice has reached desired texture, drain water. Stir in honey, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Add cheese and stir til melted.
5.  Plate veggies and tuna on top of rice.
