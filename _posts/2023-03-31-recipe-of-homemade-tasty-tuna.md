##### The ingredients needed to make Tasty Tuna:

1.  Get 3 cans tuna in water
2.  Prepare 4 boiled eggs (chopped)
3.  Prepare salt
4.  Prepare pepper
5.  Get 1 cup mayo
6.  Get 2 tbs. yellow mustard
7.  Make ready paprika
8.  Take 2 tbs. white sugar

##### Steps to make Tasty Tuna:

1.  Add everything together and mix well, put in fridge for about 1 hour then, serve with crackers or, make sandwiches.
