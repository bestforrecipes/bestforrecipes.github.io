##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Shrimp Mushroom Corn Fritters:

1.  Take creme corn
2.  Get Crab meat
3.  Take shrimp
4.  Prepare green onion
5.  Make ready chopped mushrooms
6.  Make ready corn meal
7.  Get mayo
8.  Take salt
9.  Make ready cayenne pepper
10.  Prepare olive oil

##### Steps to make Crab Shrimp Mushroom Corn Fritters:

1.  mix all the ingredients together in large bowl adding the corn meal last.
2.  Add olive oil to a pan and fry a large patty size scoop on medium heat until golden brown on each side. Don't turn the heat up too high and keep an eye on them.
3.  when complete place on napkin to soak oil and enjoy.
4.  you might have to adjust the amount of cornmeal according to taste.
