##### The ingredients needed to make Easy spicy Prawn with Tomato:

1.  Make ready 500 grams Prawn (small)
2.  Make ready 1 cup Tomato ( finely chopped)
3.  Take 1 cup Spring onions ( chopped)
4.  Prepare 1 tbsp Soy sauce
5.  Take 1 tsp Oyster sauce
6.  Prepare 2 tbsp Tomato ketchup
7.  Make ready pinch Black pepper powder
8.  Take salt
9.  Prepare 2 tbsp vegetable oil
10.  Prepare 1 tsp granulated sugar
11.  Prepare 2 piece green chillies( slit)
12.  Make ready 1/2 tsp garlic
13.  Get 2 tsp cornstarch
14.  Make ready 1/2 cup water
15.  Prepare 1/2 box bean vermicelli

##### Instructions to make Easy spicy Prawn with Tomato:

1.  Cook bean vermicelli in water with little salt ,strain and keep aside.
2.  Heat oil in saucepan, add garlic and then spring onions,salt and tomato. Cook for 2-3 min.
3.  Add prawns and other ingredients (except cornstarch) and cook until prawns are cooked. Add a little water if getting dry while cooking.
4.  Add cornstarch to 1/2 cup water and dissolve. Then add this mix to prawn and cook for 2-3 minutes more. Serve hot with cooked bean vermicelli.
