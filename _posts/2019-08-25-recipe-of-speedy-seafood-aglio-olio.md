6.  Make ready Olive Oil

##### Instructions to make Seafood Aglio Olio:

2.  Add garlic, chilli, seafood, salt and pepper
3.  Add in cooked pasta and a little bit of pasta water
4.  Stir the pasta together with the olive oil
