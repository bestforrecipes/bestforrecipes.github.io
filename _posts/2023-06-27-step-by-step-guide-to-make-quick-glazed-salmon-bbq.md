##### The ingredients needed to make Glazed Salmon BBQ:

1.  Prepare 200 g salmon fillet
2.  Take 160 g Chinese noodles
3.  Take 40 g dried shiitake mushroom
4.  Make ready 60 g mix bellpeppers
5.  Prepare 20 gm beansprouts
6.  Make ready 20 gm carrots
7.  Take 80 ml BBq sauce
8.  Prepare 30 g leeks

##### Instructions to make Glazed Salmon BBQ:

1.  Sered the salmon till the skin crispy then when its cook glazed with barbecue sauce in the same pan
2.  Saute all the vegetables with shitake mushroom then add the noodles with BBQ sauce spring onion then put on the plate
3.  Serve the salmon on the noodles glaze again BBQ sauce
4.  Garnish with fried leeks and sesame seeds
