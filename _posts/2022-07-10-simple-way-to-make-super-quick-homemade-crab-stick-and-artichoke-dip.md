##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Stick and Artichoke Dip:

1.  Get 4 stick Imitation crab sticks
2.  Prepare 150 grams Marinated artichokes
3.  Prepare 100 grams ★Cream cheese
4.  Get 1 tbsp ★Sour cream
5.  Make ready 3 tbsp ★Mayonnaise
6.  Take 1 tsp ★Garlic powder
7.  Get 1 dash ★Black pepper
8.  Take 2 clove ★Grated garlic
9.  Take 1 dash Finely chopped parsley
10.  Make ready 1 Baguette
11.  Prepare 1 Tortilla chips

##### Steps to make Crab Stick and Artichoke Dip:

1.  Cut the crab sticks in half and shred them into fine pieces.
2.  Roughly chop the artichokes.
3.  Prepare 100 g of cream cheese.
4.  Put the crab sticks and artichokes in a bowl with the ★ ingredients.
5.  Mix together thoroughly.
6.  Spoon the mixed dip onto the baguettes and garnish with parsley.
7.  Lightly bake in an oven at about 200℃ until slightly golden.
8.  Plate, serve, and enjoy!
