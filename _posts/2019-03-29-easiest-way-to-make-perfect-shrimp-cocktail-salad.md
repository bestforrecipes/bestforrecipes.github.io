##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Cocktail Salad:

1.  Get 3/4 pound cooked shelled shrimp
2.  Get 1 celery stalk, chopped1 small carrot, grated
3.  Take 1 tablespoon grated sweet onion
4.  Take 1 tablespoon zesty cocktail sauce
5.  Take 3-4 tablespoons mayonnaise
6.  Make ready 1 teaspoon sriracha sauce
7.  Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
8.  Get Lettuce leaves for serving
9.  Get Lettece and celery tops for serving

##### Instructions to make Shrimp Cocktail Salad:

1.  Combine all ingredients except shrimp and letttece and celery tops in a biwl
2.  Fold in shrimp, chill until ready to serve
3.  Serve on a bed of lettece, garnish each with a celery too
