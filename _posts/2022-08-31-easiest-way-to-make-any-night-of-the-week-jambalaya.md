##### The ingredients needed to make Jambalaya:

1.  Get 1 tsp olive oil
2.  Prepare 1 onion
3.  Take 1 red bell pepper
4.  Get 1 green bell pepper
5.  Take 2 celery stalks
6.  Prepare 1 lb boneless skinless chicken, cubed

8.  Make ready 2 tbs garlic
9.  Get 2 tbs tomato paste
10.  Get 2 cups chicken or veggie broth
11.  Make ready 1 (14.5 oz) can crushed tomatoes

13.  Prepare 2 tsp old bay seasoning
14.  Prepare 1 lb shrimp peeled and deveined
15.  Make ready 2 green onions thinly sliced

##### Instructions to make Jambalaya:

1.  In a large pot heat olive oil on medium heat and add in green and red peppers, onion, and celery. Season with salt and pepper. Cook 5 minutes.
2.  Stir in chicken and season with salt, pepper, and oregano. Cook 5 minutes.
3.  Stir in smoked sausage, garlic, and tomato paste. Cook 1 minute.
4.  Add chicken or veggie broth, crushed tomatoes, rice, and old bay seasoning. Reduce heat to medium low, cover with lid, and cook 20 minutes.
5.  Add shrimp, cook 3-5 minutes. Stir in green onions and serve.
