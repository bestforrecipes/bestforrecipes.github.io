##### The ingredients needed to make Yakiniku Restaurant-Style Easy Wakame Seaweed Soup:

1.  Make ready 2 tbsp Wakame seaweed (dried or salted)
2.  Prepare 1/3 Japanese leek
3.  Take 3 tsp Dashida (or beef stock)
4.  Prepare 1 dash Salt
5.  Prepare 1 dash Pepper
6.  Prepare 1/2 tsp Sesame oil (for finishing)
7.  Make ready 1 tsp Roasted sesame seeds
8.  Take 650 ml Water
