##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp tacos:

1.  Make ready 1 pound raw cleaned shrimp
2.  Prepare 1 teaspoon garlic powder
3.  Make ready 1 teaspoon paprika
4.  Take 1 teaspoon onion powder
5.  Make ready Juice to 1 lime
6.  Make ready 1/4 teaspoon salt
7.  Prepare 2 table evoo
8.  Make ready Corn tortillas
9.  Take fixings: sliced cucumber red onions pico de Gallo sour cream
10.  Get Lime

##### Steps to make Shrimp tacos:

1.  Take seasonings and evoo and evenly coat all shrimp
2.  Lay shrimp flatly on a baking sheet
3.  Squeeze juice to lime over shrimp
4.  Bake at 375 till shrimp has curled and pink
5.  Put small amount shrimp on warm tortilla
6.  Top with taco fixings like cucumbers sliced onions sour cream shredded cabbage lime juice.
