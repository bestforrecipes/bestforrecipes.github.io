##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Sour Cauliflower:

1.  Get 1 small head Cauliflower \*about 300g, cut into small florets
2.  Prepare Salt
3.  Get <Sweet & Sour Marinade>
4.  Make ready 1/4 teaspoon Chilli Flakes
5.  Make ready 3 tablespoons Rice Vinegar
6.  Make ready 2 tablespoons Sugar
7.  Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Salt
8.  Get 5 cm Kombu (Kelp) \*optional, cut into thin strips

##### Steps to make Sweet & Sour Cauliflower:

1.  In a large bowl, combine Sweet & Sour Marinade ingredients.
2.  Add Cauliflower florets into boiling salted water and bring back to the boil. Cook for 1 minutes, then drain well.
3.  Add the drained hot Cauliflower florets to the marinade, and gently toss and combine evenly. Set aside for 30 minutes or longer, occasionally tossing. After cooled down, keep in the fridge before serve.
