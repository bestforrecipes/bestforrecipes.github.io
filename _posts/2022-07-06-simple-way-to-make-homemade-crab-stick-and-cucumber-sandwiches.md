##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Stick and Cucumber Sandwiches:

1.  Make ready 2 White bread
2.  Make ready 2/3 Cucumber
3.  Prepare 3 Imitation crab sticks
4.  Get 1 tbsp ※Mayonnaise
5.  Prepare 1/2 tsp ※Honey
6.  Take 1/4 tsp ※Mustard
7.  Take (Or 1 1/2 tablespoons of Honey Mustard Recipe ID: 327442)
8.  Take 1 Margarine

##### Steps to make Crab Stick and Cucumber Sandwiches:

1.  Combine ※ marked ingredients. Spread the margarine on one side of each bread slice. Slice the cucumber thin vertically and pat dry with paper towels.
2.  Place the cucumber slices on one of the slices of bread, overlapping slightly, then spread on 1/2 of the honey mustard.
3.  Shred the crab sticks by hand and place on top of the cucumber, and spread on the rest of the honey mustard sauce. Cover with another slice of bread and cut off the crusts. Cut the sandwich any way that you like.
