##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Noodles soup:

1.  Make ready 1 piece salmon
2.  Take 1 carrot
3.  Get 100 gram dried rice vermicelli
4.  Make ready 2 onions
5.  Prepare 100 gram pork chops
6.  Take 3 tomatoes
7.  Make ready Salt, black pepper powder, chopped onion leaves, 1 teaspoon of rice vinegar
8.  Get 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
9.  Make ready 1 teaspoon crunchy fried sliced Asian onion
10.  Make ready 100 gram chopped fresh dill

##### Steps to make Salmon Noodles soup:

1.  Make the rock:
2.  Add chopped tomatoes, rice vinegar, turmeric powder into the broth
3.  Boil raw salmon with the hot broth
4.  Set rice vermicelli (after boil in 2 minutes and wash again by cold water) in to the bowl. Add salmon, fresh dill, chopped onions leaves, black pepper powder then pour the hot broth
5.  Add fried chopped Asian onion, a little bit lemon juice & Enjoy (You can also eat chopped fresh lettuce)
