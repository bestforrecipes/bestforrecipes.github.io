##### The ingredients needed to make Peppery brown sugar salmon:

1.  Get 1 1/2 lb salmon fillets (boneless)
2.  Make ready 1 tbsp butter, melted
3.  Make ready 4 tbsp brown sugar
4.  Take 1/2 tsp onion powder
5.  Prepare 1/2 tsp lemon pepper seasoning
6.  Make ready 1/4 tsp cracked pepper

##### Instructions to make Peppery brown sugar salmon:

4.  on lightly greased backing sheet or foil bake 20-25 mins
