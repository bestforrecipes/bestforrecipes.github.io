##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood Paella:

1.  Get 250 gms paella Rice
2.  Prepare 15 pcs. U 10 prawns
3.  Get 200 gms chicken thigh
4.  Make ready 1 tsp. Paprika
5.  Get 1 onion large
6.  Get 1 tomato riped
7.  Get 1 spring spring onion
8.  Get 2 gm saffron
9.  Make ready 200 ml chicken stock
10.  Make ready 100 gm squid
11.  Get 100 gm mussels
12.  Make ready 50 gm parsley
13.  Get 1 lime
14.  Get Salt and pepper
15.  Take 30 gm Tomato paste
16.  Get 50 gm Tomato sauce
17.  Prepare 2 Clove garlic
18.  Make ready 3 tsp olive oil

##### Instructions to make Seafood Paella:

1.  Cleans all vegetables and seafood. Wash rice keep aside.
2.  Saute prawns, chicken, onion, garlic. Tomato paste, tomato sauce and fresh tomato. Add pakrika, saffron. Seasoning.
3.  Add chicken stock, cook until rice is tender. Garnish with aloili and herbs oil.
