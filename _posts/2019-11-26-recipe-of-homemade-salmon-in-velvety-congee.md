4.  Take garnish
5.  Take 1 bunch tomato and roasted seeweed (nori)
6.  Prepare velvety congee
7.  Get 1 cup rice
8.  Prepare 1 cup oil
9.  Get 7 cup water
10.  Get 2 tsp salt and a dash of white pepper
