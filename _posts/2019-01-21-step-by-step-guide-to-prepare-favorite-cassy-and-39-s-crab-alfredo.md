##### The ingredients needed to make Cassy's Crab Alfredo:

2.  Get 2 can alfredo sauce
3.  Get 1 packages frozen broccoli and cauliflower
4.  Take 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
5.  Make ready 3 tbsp garlic powder
6.  Make ready 1 salt and pepper to taste
