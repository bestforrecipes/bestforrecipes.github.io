##### The ingredients needed to make Stir fry broccoli and prawns:

1.  Prepare 1 pack uncooked prawns
2.  Prepare 1 bunch broccoli (chunky chopped)
3.  Make ready 2 carrots (sliced)
4.  Get Garlic oil
5.  Prepare 1 tbsp Oyster sauce
6.  Get 1 tsp Fish sauce
7.  Take 1 tsp Soy Sauce

##### Instructions to make Stir fry broccoli and prawns:

1.  Wok on medium heat. Add garlic oil
2.  Added prawns for a few mins, half cooked.
3.  Add carrot, broccoli, stir well
4.  Season with soy sauce, fish sauce and oyster sauce. Mix well.
5.  Can be served with rice or noodles
