##### The ingredients needed to make Cheese Spring Rolls:

1.  Prepare Spring Roll wrappers
2.  Get Cheese block, sticks or slices
3.  Take Crab sticks (optional)
4.  Get 1 egg - lightly beaten

##### Steps to make Cheese Spring Rolls:

1.  Cut cheese into few inches long. Or if using cheese slices, stack them in two, then, cut into preferable size. Cheese slices kinda better as some cheeses are extra creamier & melt beautifully. Next, Cut & slice crab stick the same length & size of the cheese - if using crab stick. Kids love plain creamy cheese with fried crispy wrapper skins. Very often, we use only cheese as the filling.
2.  Wrap cheese & crab stick (if using) in a wrapper. Seal the edge/end of the wrapper with beaten egg.
3.  Deep fry over medium heat until lightly brown & serve with cheese sauce.
