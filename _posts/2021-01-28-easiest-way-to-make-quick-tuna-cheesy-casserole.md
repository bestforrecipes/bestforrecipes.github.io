2.  Make ready 2 . 2 cups frozen mix vegetables
3.  Get 3 . 1 cube chicken or vegetable knorr
4.  Get 4 . 1 can mushroom soup
5.  Make ready 5 . 1 can tuna on brine
6.  Take 6 . 4 slices Cheddar cheese
7.  Get 7 . Pinch pepper
8.  Make ready 8 . Spray butter

##### Instructions to make Tuna Cheesy Casserole:

1.  Boil pasta and mix vegetables
2.  After the pasta boiled, strain the water and mix together with mushrooms soup, tuna and dissolve the chicken cube. Stir well and add a pinch of pepper.
3.  Transfer the mixture into a glass tray which already spray with butter. Half the tray, add the cheese slices. And cover with the rest of the mixture.
4.  Put inside the oven 220- 250c for 30 / 35 minutes please check before taking out.
