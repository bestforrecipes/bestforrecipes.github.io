##### The ingredients needed to make Pinoy Chili Tuna Salad:

1.  Take 55 g Hot Tuna flakes, Drained
2.  Prepare 2 tablespoons minced red onion
3.  Make ready 1 teaspoon black pepper mill
4.  Take 1 teaspoon Chili powder
5.  Get 4 large hard boiled eggs
6.  Make ready 1/2 cucumber
7.  Get 2 teaspoon seasalt
8.  Prepare 3 head romaine lettuce, shredded
9.  Take 2 calamansi
10.  Take 2 cherry tomatoes

##### Steps to make Pinoy Chili Tuna Salad:

1.  In a mixing bowl, break up the tuna with a fork.
2.  Toss the shredded lettuce, minced onions, cuts of hard boiled eggs and tomatoes
3.  Add pepper, chilli powder and salt.
4.  Stir to combine.
5.  Add calamansi juice, to taste if needed.
