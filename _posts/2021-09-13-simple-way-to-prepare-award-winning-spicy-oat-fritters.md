##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Oat Fritters:

1.  Prepare 1 piece bread
2.  Take 100 ml palm milk or coconut milk
3.  Get 80 g instant oats
4.  Make ready 150 ml hot water
5.  Take 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
6.  Prepare 140 g wheat flour
7.  Prepare 1 egg
8.  Take 150 – 170ml water
9.  Make ready 20 g dried shrimp - soaked and mashed
10.  Get 1 red chilli - cut
11.  Make ready 4 leeks - sliced thinly
12.  Get oil - to fry
13.  Prepare salt

##### Instructions to make Spicy Oat Fritters:

1.  Shred the bread and soak with coconut milk. Leave for 10 minutes.
2.  Mix the oats with hot water and leave for 10 minutes.
3.  Mix all the ingredients until well incorporated.
4.  Scoop the mixture into hot oil.
5.  Once golden brown, remove it from the oil.
