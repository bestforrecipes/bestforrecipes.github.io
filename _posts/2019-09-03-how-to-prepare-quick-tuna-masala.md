##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Masala:

1.  Prepare 1 can tuna of
2.  Make ready 1 onion diced
3.  Prepare 2 tomatoes medium size chopped
4.  Take 1 tbsp garlic paste
5.  Take 1 tbsp ginger paste
6.  Get 1 chile slit lengthwise (optional)
7.  Make ready 1/4 tsp cumin seeds
8.  Make ready 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
9.  Get 1/2 tsp coriander powder
10.  Take 1/4 tsp chile powder
11.  Prepare Handful coriander leaves chopped
12.  Make ready to taste Salt
13.  Take Oil

##### Instructions to make Tuna Masala:

1.  To the heated oil, add the cumin seeds and onion. Once the onions has softened add the garlic and ginger pastes. Add the chile if you want. I have mentioned slit lengthwise, you can chop it real fine too.
2.  Next add the spices: turmeric, coriander and chile. Allow this to cook until the raw smell of the spices becomes fragant. Add the salt and the tomatoes.
3.  Give it all a quick stir and I let it cook on medium low. About 10-ish mins later, you will have a mostly cooked, fragrant, tomato-onion mixture.
4.  As the final step, I add the can of tuna. Add the freshly chopped coriander leaves, stir and transfer to a serving bowl.
5.  Serving suggestions: This goes great with rotis, or bread/buns like I did on this occasion. Adding potatoes to this mix and serving it with rice is a great combo too!
