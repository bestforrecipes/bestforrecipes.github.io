##### The ingredients needed to make Simple 3-Minute Tofu Steak:

1.  Make ready Tofu
2.  Make ready Egg
3.  Take Butter or margarine
4.  Take Bonito flakes
5.  Get Soy sauce

##### Steps to make Simple 3-Minute Tofu Steak:

1.  Heat the butter in a frying pan. Slice the tofu, then add it to the pan.
2.  Once the tofu has browned turn them over, and add the egg.
3.  Once the egg has cooked, it's good to go! Transfer to a serving plate, top with bonito flakes, and it's complete! Enjoy with a bit of soy sauce to taste.
