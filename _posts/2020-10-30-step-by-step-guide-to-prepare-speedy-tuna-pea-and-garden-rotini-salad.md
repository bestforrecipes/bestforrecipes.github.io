##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna,pea and garden rotini salad:

1.  Take 1/2 box garden rotini pasta, cooked
2.  Prepare 3 cans tuna, drained
3.  Take 3 hard boiled eggs, chopped
4.  Make ready 1/4 C purple onion, chopped
5.  Take 1 1/2 C mayo
6.  Take 1 Tbsp celery salt
7.  Get 1 Tbsp lemon juice
8.  Take 1 1/2 tsp salt
9.  Get 1 tsp pepper
10.  Make ready 1/2 tsp garlic powder
11.  Take 1 C frozen peas
12.  Make ready 1/2 C pickle relish, finely chopped
