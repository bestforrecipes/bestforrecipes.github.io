2.  Make ready 8 oz three 8ounce packages of cream cheese
3.  Take 2 cup mayonnaise
4.  Prepare 1 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
5.  Get 12 tbsp minced green onions (white and green part)
6.  Make ready 8 clove garlic (minced)
7.  Get 8 tsp worcestershire sauce
8.  Prepare 6 tbsp lemon juice
9.  Make ready 8 tsp hot sauce
10.  Prepare 4 tsp old bay seasoning
11.  Make ready salt and pepper
