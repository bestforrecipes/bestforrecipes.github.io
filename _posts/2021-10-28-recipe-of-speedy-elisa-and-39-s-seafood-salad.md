##### The ingredients needed to make Elisa's Seafood Salad:

1.  Make ready 1 lb Shrimp, cooked and peeled
2.  Make ready 1 lb Octopus, cooked, cleaned and diced
3.  Make ready 1 lb Conch, cooked, cleaned and diced
4.  Prepare 1 Onion, chopped
5.  Prepare 1 Green Bell Pepper, chopped
6.  Get 1 Yellow Pepper, chopped
7.  Make ready 1 Red Pepper, chopped
8.  Get 4 tbsp Garlic, finely chopped (Can be garlic powder)
9.  Take 3/4 cup Olive Oil
10.  Get 2 tsp Vinegar
11.  Prepare 1 Salt and pepper
12.  Prepare 1 bunch Cilantro (Coriander leaves) finely chopped… (personally, I like a lot of it)

##### Steps to make Elisa's Seafood Salad:

1.  In a large bowl, mix all the seafood, onions, cilantro and peppers.
2.  In another bowl, mix the olive oil, vinegar, garlic, salt and pepper.
3.  Pour over the seafood all the oil mix and mix well.
4.  Let it rest on the fridge overnight, mixing occasionally.
5.  Serve and enjoy!
