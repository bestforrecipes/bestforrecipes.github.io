##### The ingredients needed to make Mike's Creamy Seafood Spread On Bagels & Crostinis:

1.  Prepare ● For The Seafood \[all seafood fine chopped\]
2.  Prepare 1 Cup King Crab Leg Meat
3.  Prepare 1 Cup Lobster Tail Meat
4.  Get 1 Cup Flaked Salmon
5.  Prepare 1 Cup Shrimp
6.  Prepare 1/4 Lemon Juiced
7.  Prepare ● For The Creams \[all room temp\]
8.  Get 2 (8 oz) Packages Philadelphia Cream Cheese
9.  Take 1 tsp Fresh Dill Weed
10.  Make ready 2 tbsp Fresh Sour Cream
11.  Make ready ● For The Vegetables \[all vegetables fine chopped or minced\]
12.  Make ready 1/3 Cup Celery
13.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Fresh Dill
14.  Make ready 1/4 Cup Fresh Chives
15.  Make ready 1/4 Cup Fresh Parsley
16.  Prepare 1/2 tsp Fine Minced Garlic
17.  Get ● For The Seasonings
18.  Get 1/2 tsp Old Bay Seasoning
19.  Make ready 1/4 tsp Lemon Pepper
20.  Take 1/4 tsp Celery Seeds
21.  Prepare ● For The Breads
22.  Get Assorted Fresh Toasted Bagles
23.  Take Stonefire Naan Breads
24.  Take Assorted Quality Crackers
25.  Take Fresh Quality Crostinis
26.  Make ready Sliced Lemon Wedges

##### Instructions to make Mike's Creamy Seafood Spread On Bagels & Crostinis:

1.  Fine chop your seafood.
2.  Fine chop your vegetables and add to seafood. Mix well.
3.  Mix everything else together well and chill for a few hours.
4.  Smear on bagles, crostinis or, quality crackers.
5.  Fresh Stonefire Naan Breads pictured.
6.  Fresh crostinis pictured.
7.  Garnish with fresh chives and lemon wedges. Enjoy!
