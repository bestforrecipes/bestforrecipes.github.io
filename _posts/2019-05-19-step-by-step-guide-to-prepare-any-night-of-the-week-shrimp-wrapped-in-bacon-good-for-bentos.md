##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Wrapped in Bacon - Good for Bentos:

2.  Prepare Bacon
3.  Make ready Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Shrimp Wrapped in Bacon - Good for Bentos:

1.  Peel and devein the shrimp and straighten them. Cut the bacon in half and wrap around the shrimp so the end of the wrap is facing down.
2.  With the end of the bacon wrap facing down, put on a heatproof dish and sprinkle salt and pepper. Cover with plastic wrap or with a lid and cook in the microwave for 1 minute!
3.  All done!
