##### Steps to make Chicken & shrimp cheese steak:

1.  Clean chicken & slice each breast as thin as possible.
2.  Use 1/2 stick of butter to sauté sliced onions
3.  Season chicken, & when onions are well softened…..add chicken
4.  Slice shrimp & cut off tail if u have to. Add shrimp to chicken but only when chicken is cooked. (Basically, when there’s no liquid left in pan….chicken is done)
5.  When shrimp turn pink and are done….top with cheese. Pour a little water in pan & cover. (to help melt the cheese) Place on ur bread, & toppings….and enjoy!!!
