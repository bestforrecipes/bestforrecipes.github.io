##### The ingredients needed to make Sambal Squid (Sambal Sotong):

1.  Get 200 g Squid (cut into bite size pieces)
2.  Take 2 Tbsp Lime Juice
3.  Get 1/2 tsp Salt
4.  Take Sambal Paste (processed in a blender);
5.  Take 50 g Shallots
6.  Take 20 g Garlic
7.  Get 25 g Fermented Shrimp Paste
8.  Prepare 20 g Dried Chilli (deseed, rehydrate)
9.  Get Pre-Sauce (mix in a bowl);
10.  Prepare 1 Tbsp Oyster Sauce
11.  Take 1 Tbsp Sweet Dark Soy
12.  Make ready 1-2 Tbsp Palm Sugar
13.  Make ready Other Ingredients;
14.  Take 2 pc Tamarind Slice
15.  Prepare 1 cup Coconut Milk
16.  Get 3 Tbsp Cooking Oil

##### Steps to make Sambal Squid (Sambal Sotong):

1.  MARINADE; marinate the squid in lime juice and salt for minimum 30 minutes. The Lime will remove any fishy scent.
2.  COOK THE PASTE; Heat the cooking oil in a wok until hot. Fry the Sambal paste until the oil separates.
3.  PRE-SAUCE; Once the oil separates, add the Pre-Sauce, Tamarind Slices, Coconut Milk and squid. Cook until the liquid has reduced to your desired consistency. Season with salt and sugar if necessary.
