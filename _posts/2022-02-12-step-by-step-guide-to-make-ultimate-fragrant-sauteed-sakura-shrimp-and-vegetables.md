##### The ingredients needed to make Fragrant Sauteed Sakura Shrimp and Vegetables:

1.  Prepare 30 grams Asparagus
2.  Prepare 20 grams Onion
3.  Get 2 grams Sakura shrimp - tiny dried shrimp
4.  Get 1 grams Dried shiitake mushrooms
5.  Prepare 2 grams Vegetable oil
6.  Prepare 1/2 grams Chicken soup stock granules
7.  Make ready 1/10 grams Salt
8.  Get 1/100 grams Pepper

##### Steps to make Fragrant Sauteed Sakura Shrimp and Vegetables:

1.  Cut the asparagus diagonally to a thickness of 1 cm. Julienne the onion. Re-hydrate the shiitake mushrooms and julienne.
2.  Heat the oil in a frying pan and stir-fry the asparagus, onion, sakura shrimp, and shiitake mushrooms together.
3.  Add the chicken soup stock granules, salt, and pepper to season.
