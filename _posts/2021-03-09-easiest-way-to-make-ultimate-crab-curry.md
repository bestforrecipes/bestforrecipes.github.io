##### The ingredients needed to make Crab curry:

1.  Take 250 gm crab
2.  Make ready As per taste salt
3.  Prepare As per taste red chilli
4.  Get 1 tsp turmeric powder
5.  Prepare 1 tbsp ginger garlic
6.  Make ready 1 onion chopped
7.  Prepare 1 tomato chopped
8.  Prepare 2 tbsp coriander powder
9.  Make ready 1 tsp sugar
10.  Get As needed water
11.  Get 1 tsp cumin powder
12.  Prepare 1 tsp garam masala

##### Steps to make Crab curry:

1.  Wash and cut crab in pieces
2.  Heat mustard oil…add sugar..onion and ginger garlic paste saute them well
3.  Add All spices and now add tomato cook for 10 minutes
4.  Fry crab..add in masala…cook for some minutes..it's ready
