##### The ingredients needed to make Malai Prawn Curry:

1.  Make ready 250 gms Prawn
2.  Get 1 Onion
3.  Get 3 tomatoes
4.  Take 1 Small pieces of ginger
5.  Take 1 green chilli
6.  Get 1 stock of coriander leaf
7.  Make ready 4 cloves garlic pods
8.  Make ready 200 ml Coconut milk
9.  Prepare 2 tablespoons turmeric powder
10.  Get 1 tablespoon salt
11.  Get Pinch sugar
12.  Get 1 tablespoon coriander powder
13.  Prepare 16 tablespoons Mustard Oil
14.  Make ready Pinch cumin seeds

##### Instructions to make Malai Prawn Curry:

1.  Clean the prawn properly then marinate in little salt and turmeric for 15 mins. Heat the wok and add mustard oil. Fry the marinated prawn in medium heat till 5 mins. Take it out in another container after it's cooked.
2.  Make a paste of 3 tomatoes, 1 onion, ginger, 1 chilli, chopped coriander leaf, 4 garlic cloves in a blender. Heat the pan and add the leftover mustard oil. Add the complete paste, add turmeric, coriander powder to the oil and saute well till the oil separates out from the masala. Add half cup water to the masala and then add complete coconut milk. Cook it well for 10-15 mins.
3.  Add the prawns to the gravy after it starts boiling. Add salt to taste and little sugar. Cook for another 5 mins. Your malai prawn curry is ready. Garnish with coriander and chilli. Serve with hot rice.
