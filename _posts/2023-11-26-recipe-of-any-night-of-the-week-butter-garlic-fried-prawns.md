4.  Take 2 teaspoons sesame oil
5.  Take 6 cloves garlic, minced
6.  Get 1/2 inch ginger, pounded
7.  Get 3 green onions, finely chopped
8.  Get 1 teaspoon soy sauce
9.  Make ready 1 teaspoon kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
