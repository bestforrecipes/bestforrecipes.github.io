##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna, Avocado and Pasta Salad:

1.  Get 300 grams Rigatoni Pasta
2.  Make ready 1 Red Onion
3.  Make ready 200 grams Cherry tomatoes
4.  Prepare 250 grams Canned Corn
5.  Take 300 grams Greek Yoghurt
6.  Take 150 ml Balsamic Salad Dressing
7.  Prepare 1 large Avocado
8.  Make ready 370 grams Canned Tuna

##### Instructions to make Tuna, Avocado and Pasta Salad:

1.  Cook pasta according to packet instructions. Rinse with cold water.
2.  Finely chop onion, slice cherry tomatoes into quarters and the avocado into small cubes.
3.  Drain the corn, tuna and add to the avocado, onion and tomatoes. Mix together.
4.  Once the pasta has cooled add to the rest of the mix.
5.  Mix the Greek yogurt and balasmic dressing to taste. Then mix in with the rest of the ingredients
6.  Now ready to serve by itself or as a side.
