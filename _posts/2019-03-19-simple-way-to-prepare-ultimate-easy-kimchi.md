##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Kimchi:

1.  Prepare Napa cabbage
2.  Get Salt
3.  Take Chilli powder - if you like spicy, can put 2 cups or 2 n half cups
4.  Take Fermented shrimp
5.  Get Fish sauce
6.  Prepare Garlic
7.  Prepare (inch) Ginger
8.  Get Onion
9.  Get Porridge
10.  Make ready Water
11.  Prepare Rice flour
12.  Prepare Sugar
13.  Prepare Vege
14.  Prepare Radish - jullien cut
15.  Take Carrot - jullien cut
16.  Make ready Green onions (chopped)

##### Instructions to make Easy Kimchi:

1.  Cut the cabbage bite size. Wash it and drained the water.
2.  Sprinkled salt into cabbage. Leave it to sit for 30 minutes. Turn the cabbage for every 30 minutes, this is to ensure the cabbage are 'marinated' well. I do it 3 times so total is 2 hours.
3.  Wash the cabbage thoroughly - like 3 times wash and rinse. Drained the cabbage
4.  For the porridge: Mixed water and rice flour on stove with medium heat. Cooked for 9 minutes. Add sugar and reduce the heat. Cook until it become shiny. Off the heat and let it cool.
5.  For the garlic paste : blend garlic, ginger, onion, fish sauce and fermented shrimp together.
6.  Mix garlic paste with chilli powder and cooled porridge. Then add the veges and mix till incorporate.
7.  Add in cabbage. Mix well and put it in container. Store it in refrigerator. Make sure to push the cabbage down from time to time so that it will soaked with kimchi sauce. DONE!
