##### The ingredients needed to make Left over Rice balls:

1.  Make ready 1 bowl leftover rice
2.  Get 1/2 cup besan
3.  Prepare 2 green chilli chopped
4.  Take 1 chopped onion
5.  Prepare 1/2 tsp garam masala
6.  Prepare 1 cup bread crumbs
7.  Get Salt to taste
8.  Make ready oil for frying

It is the most popular appetizer in Italy. You can make these from leftover last night rice or risotto as well. This is a great recipe for leftover aborio rice. This is Italian rice balls gone South!

##### Instructions to make Left over Rice balls:

1.  Add ingredients in a mixing bowl,
2.  Make small balls with the mixture
3.  Coated with bread crumbs
4.  Heat oil in a wok add balls deep fry till golden in colour
5.  Serve with sauce/green chutney
