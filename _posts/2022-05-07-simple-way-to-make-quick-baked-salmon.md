##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Salmon:

1.  Make ready Salmon Filet
2.  Prepare Butter
3.  Take Lemon Pepper Seasoning or Lemon Juice

##### Instructions to make Baked Salmon:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F
2.  Line the inside of a baking sheet with aluminum foil
3.  Coat the foil with slightly warmed butter
4.  Lay the filet into the butter and season the top with the lemon seasoning or juice
5.  Bake the salmon for 15 minutes or until salmon is pale and flaky
6.  Enjoy!
