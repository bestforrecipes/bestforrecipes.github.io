##### The ingredients needed to make stuffed shells:

1.  Make ready 1 box jumbo shells
2.  Make ready 1 lb ground beef
3.  Get 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
4.  Get 15 oz cottage cheese
5.  Get 1 egg
6.  Make ready 2 tsp basil–dried
7.  Get 1 salt & pepper to taste
8.  Take 2 clove garlic (minced)
9.  Take 1/3 cup onion (minced)
10.  Make ready 1 cup mozzarella cheese
11.  Make ready 24 oz about 2 jars ; spaghetti sauce (any kind you prefer)

##### Steps to make stuffed shells:

1.  cook shells according to box directions
2.  preheat oven to 350; cook ground beef, onion and garlic until lightly browned.
3.  drain excess fat
4.  combine mozzarella, cottage and parmesan cheese along with, Basil,salt&pepper, and egg in a bowl. Add the beef, onion and garlic mixture to this
5.  in a 13X9 dish spread about 1 cup of the sauce on the bottom. fill the shells and put in the pan. if you need another pan a 8X8 held the extras
6.  pour the extra filling over shells then pour the remaining sauce over shells.
7.  bake for about 1 hour 15 minutes or until cooked through
