##### The ingredients needed to make Onion and Crab Sticks Salad:

1.  Take 1 new onion
2.  Make ready 1 pack (75 g) crab stick
3.  Get 5 green onion
4.  Make ready 1 tablespoon olive oil
5.  Get 1/3 teaspoon pepper
6.  Get 1/4 teaspoon or to taste salt
7.  Make ready 1/2 lemon

##### Steps to make Onion and Crab Sticks Salad:

1.  Slice onion very thin and add salt 1/3~1/2 teaspoon salt. Soften sliced onion.
2.  Split crab sticks.
3.  Wash sliced onion and squeeze a little.
4.  Make dressing and mix with vegetables and crab sticks.
5.  Enjoy💝💓🌸
