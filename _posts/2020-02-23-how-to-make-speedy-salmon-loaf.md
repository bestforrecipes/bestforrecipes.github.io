##### Steps to make Salmon Loaf:

1.  Boil the fish and debone it. Keep the flesh aside.
2.  Combine all the ingredients in a food processor till incorporated. Preheat the oven at 180 degree.
3.  Grease a loaf pan and line it with parchment paper.
4.  Put the fish mixture in the greased pan and brush the top with Olive oil. Bake for 45-50 mins.
5.  Once baked, increase the temperature to 200 degrees and broil for another 20 mins. Cool completely before slicing.
6.  Once cooled slice the pieces and shallow fry in butter before serving. Enjoy!
