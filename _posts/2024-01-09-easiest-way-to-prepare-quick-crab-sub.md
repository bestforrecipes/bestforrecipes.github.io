##### The ingredients needed to make Crab sub:

1.  Get 1 packages crab meat
2.  Take 1 packages cream cheese
3.  Make ready 1/2 clove garlic
4.  Make ready to taste hot sauce
5.  Make ready to taste salt & pepper
6.  Make ready 1 packages Swiss cheese
7.  Make ready 1 packages Italian sub

##### Instructions to make Crab sub:

1.  Mix cream cheese until soft mix in crab and stir
2.  Pour in hot sauce and garlic. Mix until completely blended
3.  Put your bread on a baking sheet. Scoop crab mix onto the bread, add salt and pepper and layer cheese on too
4.  Bake at 400 for about 5-8 minutes depending on how you like your bread
