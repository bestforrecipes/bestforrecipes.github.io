##### Instructions to make Salmon:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°
2.  Wash Salmon Fillets, Pat dry.
3.  grease baking pan.
4.  Sprinkle Salt & Pepper over Salmon Fillets.
5.  Cut lemon in half, squeeze lemon juice over fillets.
6.  Drizzle about 1 teaspoon of olive oil over each of the fillets.
7.  Bake until easily flaked or until desired degree of doneness.
8.  Enjoy.
