##### The ingredients needed to make Mike's Shrimp Cocktail:

1.  Make ready FOR YOUR PROTEINS
2.  Make ready 2 Pounds Large Raw Shrimp \[de-veined - shucked - tails removed\]
3.  Make ready FOR YOUR VEGETABLES
4.  Make ready 1/4 Cup Purple Onions \[thin sliced\]
5.  Take 1/4 Cup Celery \[fine chop with leaves\]
6.  Make ready 1/4 Cup Cucumbers \[seeds removed - fine chop\]
7.  Take 2 LG Jalapeños \[fine chop\]
8.  Make ready 2 Roma Tomatoes \[small diced\]
9.  Prepare 1/4 Cup Radishes \[thin sliced\]
10.  Get 2 Pickled Carrots \[thin sliced\]
11.  Get 1 Bunch Cilantro Leaves \[rough chop\]
12.  Take 1 Avocado \[sliced - added only upon serving\]
13.  Take FOR YOUR RED SHRIMP MARINADE SAUCE
14.  Make ready 1 Cup Chilled Clamato Juice
15.  Prepare 1 Cup Chilled V8 Juice
16.  Get 2 tbsp Tapitio Hot Sauce \[or more - i use much more!\]
17.  Get 1/3 Cup Heinz Ketchup
18.  Take 1/2 Cup Fresh Lime Juice
19.  Make ready 1/4 tsp Mexican Oregano
20.  Prepare to taste Sea Salt And Black Pepper
21.  Prepare 1 Dash Worchestershie Sauce
22.  Prepare 2 tbsp Orange Juice
23.  Take SIDES/GARNISHES
24.  Take Tapitio Hot Sauce
25.  Make ready Quality Crackers
26.  Take Lime Wedges
27.  Get Fresh Cilantro
28.  Make ready Green Onions

##### Instructions to make Mike's Shrimp Cocktail:

1.  Make your RED SAUCE MARINADE. Place your raw shrimp in it and mix. Refrigerate for 10 minutes.
2.  In the meantime, chop all of your chilled vegetables.
3.  Add everything together - except for Avocado. Save that for individual serving. Otherwise you'll have an icky looking, murky red sauce.
4.  Marinate for 10+ minutes longer in fridge.
5.  Garnish with chopped cilantro, green onions, lime wedges and Tapitio Hot Sauce. Serve with quality crackers.
6.  Enjoy!
