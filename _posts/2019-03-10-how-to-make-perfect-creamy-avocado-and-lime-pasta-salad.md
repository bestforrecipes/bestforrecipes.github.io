##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy avocado and lime pasta salad:

1.  Take 1/2 packages elbow macaroni
2.  Take 1 can flaked white tuna
3.  Get 1 avocado
4.  Prepare 1/2 lime
5.  Take 2 stick celery
6.  Make ready 1/4 Sweet onion
7.  Get 1/2 green bell pepper
8.  Prepare fresh dill weed
9.  Get mayonnaise
10.  Get dijon mustard
11.  Prepare 1 water
12.  Get 1 salt
13.  Get 1 ground black pepper

##### Steps to make Creamy avocado and lime pasta salad:

1.  Cook macaroni as desired, strain and run under cold tap water.
2.  Chop onions, celery and green pepper
3.  Mash 1 whole avocado, add the juice of half a lime.
4.  Mix all ingredients with the pasta. Add as much mayonnaise as needed. Mix in with pasta.
5.  Add fresh dill, salt and pepper.
6.  Serve and enjoy!
