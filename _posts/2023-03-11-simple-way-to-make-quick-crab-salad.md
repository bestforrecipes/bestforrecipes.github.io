##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Salad:

1.  Make ready 4 tbsp mayonnaise
2.  Prepare 4 tbsp ranch dressing
3.  Prepare 1 can 6oz olives
4.  Make ready 2 pinch allspice
5.  Make ready 1 dash SMALL dash cayenne pepper
6.  Prepare 2 cup crab or immatation crab
7.  Prepare 4 tbsp parsley flakes
8.  Prepare 4 tsp lemon juice
9.  Take 1/2 tsp salt
10.  Make ready 1 tsp ground black pepper
11.  Get 5 each green onions
12.  Get 1 dash ground nutmeg
13.  Prepare 1 dash ground ginger
14.  Get 2 dash asian seasoning
15.  Prepare 2 dash basil
16.  Get 1 dash onion powder
17.  Take 2 dash oregano
18.  Take 2 pinch thyme
19.  Prepare 1 dash crushed rosemary

##### Instructions to make Crab Salad:

1.  Tear crab to bite sizes and place in mixing bowl
2.  Dice olives and add to crab
3.  Dice or take a pair of scissors and snip green onions and add to crab
4.  Stir
5.  Add lemon juice and cayenne pepper to bowl
6.  Stir very well
7.  Add crushed rosemary, thyme, oregano, onion powder, basil, Asian seasoning, ground ginger, nutmeg, ground black pepper, salt, parsley, allspice
8.  Mix well
9.  Add ranch dressing, mayonnaise
10.  Stir well
11.  Chill before serving
