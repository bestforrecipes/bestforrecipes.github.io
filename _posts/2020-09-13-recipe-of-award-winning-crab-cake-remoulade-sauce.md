##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Cake Remoulade Sauce:

1.  Get 1 cup mayonnaise
2.  Get 3 tbsp lemon juice
3.  Take 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
4.  Make ready 2 tbsp Cajun mustard
5.  Prepare 1/4 tsp horseradish sauce
6.  Get 2 splashes hot sauce
7.  Prepare 1 tsp relish
8.  Take 1/4 tsp minced garlic
9.  Get 1/2 tsp paprika

##### Instructions to make Crab Cake Remoulade Sauce:

1.  Put all ingredients in a medium size bowl.
2.  With a wire whisk, mix ingredients until a smooth consistency.
3.  Refrigerate and serve with crab cakes.
