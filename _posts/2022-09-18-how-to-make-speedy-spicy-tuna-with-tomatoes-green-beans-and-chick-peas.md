##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy tuna with tomatoes, green beans, and chick peas:

1.  Prepare 1 onion
2.  Take T minced garlic
3.  Make ready 1 t Cumin
4.  Take 1/2 t Coriander
5.  Get 1/2 t Red pepper flakes
6.  Take Can tomatoes
7.  Take Frozen or fresh green beans
8.  Make ready Chick peas
9.  Take Can tuna in olive oil
