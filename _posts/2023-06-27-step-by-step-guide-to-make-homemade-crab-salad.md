##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Salad:

1.  Prepare 16 Oz Imitation Crab Meat
2.  Get 4 Oz Chopped White Onion
3.  Prepare 6 Oz Tomato
4.  Get 1 Oz Chopped Cilantro
5.  Make ready 3/4 C Mayonnaise
6.  Get 1 Tsp Salt
7.  Take 1 Tsp Pepper
8.  Prepare 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
9.  Make ready 1 Tsp Lemon Juice

##### Instructions to make Crab Salad:

1.  Chop Onion, Crab Meat, And Cilantro Mix Together In Mixing Bowel
2.  Mix Mayo And Seasonings And Lemon Juice In Separate bowl
3.  Mix Everything Together
4.  Eat With Saltines, Crakers, Or Tostada's!
