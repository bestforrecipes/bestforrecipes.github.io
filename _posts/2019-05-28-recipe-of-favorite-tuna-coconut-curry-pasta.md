##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Coconut Curry Pasta:

1.  Prepare 250 g wholewheat fusilli pasta
2.  Get 1 small onion
3.  Make ready 1 glove garlic
4.  Take 2 tablespoons spring onion
5.  Get 1 tablespoon olive oil
6.  Take 1 teaspoon salt
7.  Prepare 1 teaspoon curry powder
8.  Make ready 1 teaspoon garam masala
9.  Take 1 teaspoon paprika
10.  Make ready 1 teaspoon parsley
11.  Take 1 can coconut milk
12.  Prepare 2 cans tuna shredded (drained)

##### Steps to make Tuna Coconut Curry Pasta:

1.  Boil water in a pot and cook pasta for 15-20 minutes.
2.  Finely chopped the onions, garlic and spring onion or chop in a food processor.
3.  Heat oil in a pot and fry the onion mix with 1 teaspoon of salt till soft, not brown.
4.  Mix the curry powder, garam masala, paprika and parsley in a cup.
5.  Add to onion and fry for 30 seconds. Be careful not to burn the curry powder mix.
6.  Add coconut milk, turn heat to medium heat and cook for 10 minutes.
7.  Add tuna and cook for 5 minute on low heat.
8.  Drain pasta once cooked and mix in tuna sauce.
9.  Serve hot.
