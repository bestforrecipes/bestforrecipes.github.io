##### The ingredients needed to make Rice balls:

1.  Take 1 serving left over rice
2.  Take 1 slice grated butternut
3.  Get 1 tbsp all purpose flour
4.  Take 2 eggs (1 for dipping)
5.  Make ready baking powder
6.  Make ready all purpose flour for dusting
7.  Get oil for deep frying

##### Steps to make Rice balls:

1.  Mix the rice with the grated butter nut
2.  Add in the egg
3.  Season and mix to a compact form
4.  Divide and make balls. dip in flour
5.  Dip in the beaten egg
6.  Dust again with flour
7.  Tap off excess flour and fry the balls in medium heated oil to a sweet aroma. remove and place on an underlined dish
8.  Serve
