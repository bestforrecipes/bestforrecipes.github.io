##### Instructions to make Sashimi Transformed! Easy Non-fried Tuna Fries:

1.  Make the sauce. Combine the Worchestershire-style sauce, ketchup, and the sugar in a heatproof container, and microwave for 15 seconds at 500 W. Mix well.
2.  Lightly pepper one side of the sashimi.
3.  Put the tuna and mayonnaise in a plastic bag. Massage over the bag to mix thoroughly.
4.  Add panko and shake the bag so that the panko will coat the tuna evenly.
5.  Heat the frying pan and spread the mayonnaise for pan-frying. When the mayonnaise begins to melt, align the panko-coated tuna without overlapping.
6.  Cook on both sides over medium heat. Take care not to burn. When golden brown, transfer onto a serving dish.
7.  Dish it up, and it's done. Scatter on some parsley to make it colorful. Sprinkle a little lemon juice to give it a refreshing flavor.
