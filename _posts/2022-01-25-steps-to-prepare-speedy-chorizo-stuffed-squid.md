##### The ingredients needed to make Chorizo Stuffed Squid:

1.  Get 1 as needed Fresh Whole Squids
2.  Make ready 1 as needed Chorizo Sausage
3.  Make ready 1 dash Franks Red Hot Sauce

##### Instructions to make Chorizo Stuffed Squid:

1.  Heat the bbq
2.  Stuff the body of the squid with the tails and slices of chorizo sausage
3.  Grill on bbq
4.  Serve tossed with franks red hot sauce
