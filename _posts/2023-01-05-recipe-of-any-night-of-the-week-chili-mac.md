##### The ingredients needed to make Chili Mac:

1.  Prepare 1 lb ground beef
2.  Take 1 lb small shell noodles
3.  Make ready 1/3 cup hot water
4.  Prepare 1 1/2 each jar Ragu cheddar cheese sauce
5.  Take 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
6.  Take 1 can chili beans
7.  Take 1 tbsp hot sauce of your choice

##### Instructions to make Chili Mac:

1.  In a large pan, brown the ground beef . Add the hot water to help it cook faster.
2.  Drain and return to the pan. Reduce heat and stir in chili beans, half the shredded cheese, and hot sauce. Set aside.
3.  Bring water to a boil and add the noodles. Boil until the noodles are soft, about 6-10 minutes. Drain and stir into chili mixture.
4.  Stir in the cheese sauce. Sprinkle the top with the rest of the shredded cheese.
