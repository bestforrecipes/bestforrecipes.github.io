2.  Take 10 raw king Prawns
3.  Get 1 Red jalapeño
4.  Make ready 2 cloves garlic finely sliced
5.  Take 1 tsp Paprika
6.  Take Zest & juice of 1 lime
7.  Prepare Handful chopped coriander

9.  Prepare Olive oil

##### Steps to make Calamari & King Prawns in Chilli Garlic Lime & Coriander:
