##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Shrimp Pinkish Dumpling:

1.  Make ready 150 g minced chicken
2.  Get 10 cm spring onion
3.  Get 1/2 cup minced shrimp
4.  Make ready 2 tablespoon starch
5.  Take 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon powder
6.  Make ready 1 cup sticky rice
7.  Prepare Red food stain
8.  Take 7 shrimps
9.  Prepare Chicken bouillon powder for chicken soup

##### Instructions to make Chicken and Shrimp Pinkish Dumpling:

1.  Soak sticky rice in pink water.
2.  Ingredients for dumplings
3.  Mix well by hand
4.  Drain the rice
5.  Powder shrimp with starch
6.  Make ball and put pink rice
7.  Push the center with finger and place shrimp
8.  Steam in high pressure pan for 5 minuets after boiling
9.  Make chicken soup and thicken with starch water.
10.  Garnish with green onion

12.  Today’s dinner
