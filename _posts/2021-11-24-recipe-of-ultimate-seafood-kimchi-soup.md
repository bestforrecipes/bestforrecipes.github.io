##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood Kimchi Soup:

1.  Get 2 cup kimchi
2.  Take 2 cup seafood stock or any stock
3.  Prepare 3 clove dice garlic
4.  Get 2 tbsp vegetable oil or sesame oil
5.  Make ready SEAFOOD OPTION
6.  Make ready 3 cup mix baby scallop ,clams, mussel, squid and shrimp
7.  Make ready GARNISH OPTION
8.  Prepare 1 cup spring onion or scallion
9.  Get 4 small bird eye chilli or red chili pepper
10.  Prepare 2 clove dice garlic
11.  Make ready SPICY PASTE OPTION 01
12.  Prepare 2 tbsp korean red pepper paste with 1 tsp of korean red chili pepper
13.  Prepare SPICY PASTE OPTION 2
14.  Get 1 tbsp peri peri chili sauce
15.  Make ready 1/2 tsp fine chili pepper
16.  Get 1 cube of chinese red fermented bean curd

##### Instructions to make Seafood Kimchi Soup:

1.  With oil Saute garlic with kimchi for 2 minute then add mix seafood
2.  Add stock and bringbit to a boil with lid cover
3.  Add spicy paste and mix well and let it simmer for another 5 minute
4.  Off heat and top with garnish and serve immediately
