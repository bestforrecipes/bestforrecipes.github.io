##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Fried Shrimp:

1.  Get Flour
2.  Get medium to large deveined shrimp
3.  Prepare Seasoning salt or lemon pepper seasoning
4.  Get eggs
5.  Prepare container of canola oil

##### Steps to make Easy Fried Shrimp:

1.  Add flour and seasoning with 1 egg and mix lightly
2.  Add shrimp to flour mix and coat shrimp.
3.  Heat oil
4.  Once oil is popping…add flour coated shrimp and fry for 5 minutes turning slightly. Might have to cook longer depending on size of shrimp
