##### The ingredients needed to make Smokey Seafood Chowder:

1.  Make ready 1 can Chopped clams and juice
2.  Get 1 large Package of lump crab meat
3.  Get 1 large Potato diced
4.  Make ready 1 can Cream corn
5.  Take 1 medium Shallot chopped
6.  Prepare 1/4 cup Diced celery
7.  Prepare 4 slice Of prosciutto diced and crispy
8.  Make ready 1 tbsp Builion
9.  Make ready 1 dash Hot sauce
10.  Make ready 1 dash Worcestershire
11.  Make ready 1 dash Salt to taste
12.  Take 1 dash Old bay season to taste
13.  Take 1/4 cup Olive oil
14.  Prepare 1/4 cup Flour
15.  Take 1/4 cup Cream dairy or substitute
16.  Prepare 1 Wine.. For drinking.
17.  Take 1 Smoked paprika

##### Instructions to make Smokey Seafood Chowder:

1.  First crack open some wine, pour a glass, and relax.. Cooking is fun!
2.  In a pot heat up oil and add diced potato, and shallot. Stir in celery and flour. Add about five cups water. Enuff to fully cover vegetables. Simmer on medium…add spices. In a pan sauté the thin slices of pork or bacon, until crispy. Add the bacon to the chowder. Add clam juice, can of cream corn. And milk. When it starts to get thick and potatoes are soft add clams and crab. Season to taste and enjoy!
