##### Steps to make Roasted Salmon Fillet:

1.  Add all of marinate ingredients with the salmon in a ziplock bag and let it marinate for 1 hour.
2.  Next cut the salmon into 6 pieces then lined the parchment paper on the baking sheet and place the pieces of salmon on the pan.
3.  Add the lime juice, honey and the minced jalapeno on top of the salmon
4.  Put the salmon in the oven for 10-15 mins.
5.  Then cover the salmon with aluminum foil for 5 mins.
6.  Take salmon out of the oven and serve to your guest with lime slices.
