##### The ingredients needed to make Greek Salmon Panini:

1.  Take x4 cut of La Segunda Cuban Bread butter toasted
2.  Make ready portion of Faroe Island Salmon Grilled
3.  Take tzatziki (varies by yield recipe of choice)
4.  Prepare Shredded Carrot
5.  Take Shaved Hydroponic Seedless Cucumber
6.  Prepare Feta Crumbled
7.  Get Pickled Red Onion (varies by recipe)

##### Instructions to make Greek Salmon Panini:

1.  Cut the bread into a 4x4 square
2.  Grill Salmon. I prefer medium so the texture fits in with the rest of the sandwich.
3.  Meanwhile, toast your cuban bread. I prefer the oven because you will not sacrifice the softness of the bread.
4.  After, place salmon on bottom of the bread.
5.  Then top with the remainder of the ingredients. I didnt include a recipe for the tzatziki and pickled red onion because people usually prefer to do their own to properly balance the tzatziki and the strong acidic characteristic of the pickled red onion.
6.  Enjoy.
