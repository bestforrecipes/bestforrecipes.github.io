##### The ingredients needed to make Lobster Neva:

1.  Prepare lobsters
2.  Get lemons
3.  Prepare water
4.  Take white wine
5.  Take ginger
6.  Make ready rosemary
7.  Prepare butter

##### Steps to make Lobster Neva:

1.  Boil lobster with white wine, ginger, rosemary, 1 lemon cut in to half for 10 minutes. Set on a plate.
2.  Slice lemon in half inch slices and saute in a butter. Then brush the butter over the lobster
