##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp scampi:

1.  Get 1 tomato chopped
2.  Make ready 1 1/4 cup green onion (chives )
3.  Take 2 garlic cloves minced
4.  Take 1 1/4 cup beer
5.  Prepare 1/2 stick of butter or margarine
6.  Get 3 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
7.  Get 2 lb Shrimp. ( I used peeled, devained without tail )

##### Instructions to make Shrimp scampi:

1.  In deep skillet combine the chives, beer, butter, tomato and garlic. Bring to boil. Reduce heat and add shrimp. Cover and simmer just until shrimp start to turn pink.
2.  Remove from heat and drain most of the liquid. Add parmesan cheese and serve.
