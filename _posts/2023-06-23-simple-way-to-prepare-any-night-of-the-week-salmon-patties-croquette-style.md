##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Patties (croquette style):

1.  Make ready cup Canola Oil
2.  Get 15 oz. can Pink Salmon (Wild Caught!!)
3.  Take cup minced Onion
4.  Take cup minced Celery
5.  Get cup minced Pepper
6.  Get cup Italian Bread Crumbs
7.  Take large Eggs
8.  Take tbl. Mayo
9.  Prepare tsp. Lemon Juice
10.  Make ready tbs. Parsley
11.  Prepare tsp. each Salt and Pepper

##### Steps to make Salmon Patties (croquette style):

1.  Heat oil in frypan on med.high heat.
2.  Remove large bones and skin from Salmon.
3.  Mix rest of ingredients into fish. Mixture will be wet.
4.  Fry about 5 min. each side, until browned. Drain on towels.
