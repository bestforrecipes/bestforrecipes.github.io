##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Take 2 packages Pasta
2.  Take 4 can Tuna
3.  Make ready 2 can Cream soup
4.  Take 1/2 cup Milk

##### Instructions to make Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°
2.  Put the pasta in a pot, then cook
3.  Two large mixing bowls, put one can of cream soup in each
4.  Put 1/4 cup of milk in each mixing bowl, then mix
5.  Drain out tuna juice
6.  2 cans of tuna in each bowl, then mix
7.  Add noodles, then mix more
8.  Spray 2 seperate 13x9 pans with oil
9.  One bowl of casserole in each
10.  Peas, pepper and relish are optional (;
