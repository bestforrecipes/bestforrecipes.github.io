##### The ingredients needed to make Mexican Shells:

1.  Make ready Jumbo shells
2.  Prepare Chorizo sausage
3.  Take Chopped onion
4.  Get Minced garlic optional
5.  Make ready 16oz black beans rinsed
6.  Take Your favorite Mexican cheese
7.  Make ready your favorite Mexican cooking sauce ( I use Herdez brand)
8.  Make ready 16oz tomatoes w green chile

##### Instructions to make Mexican Shells:

1.  Cook pasta as directed
2.  Cook onion and garlic add chorizo cook until done drain any grease ( pat dry if want)
3.  Add black beans cook for about 2 min
4.  Add about 1/4 or 1/2 cup of cheese
5.  Put a layer of sauce on bottom of 13x9 baking dish
6.  Stuff shells with meat mixture
7.  Spoon tomatoes over top of shells along with rest of sauce.
8.  Add rest of cheese on top
9.  Bake 350 until cheese is melted
