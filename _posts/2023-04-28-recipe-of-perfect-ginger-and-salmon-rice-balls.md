##### The ingredients needed to make Ginger and Salmon Rice Balls:

1.  Make ready bowl worth Plain cooked rice
2.  Prepare Salmon flakes (store bought variety)
3.  Make ready Sesame seeds
4.  Get Ginger (2 x 2 cm piece)

##### Instructions to make Ginger and Salmon Rice Balls:

1.  Julienne the ginger.
2.  Mix the rice, salmon flakes, and ginger together. Add sesame seeds if you desire.
3.  Shape into triangles and enjoy!
