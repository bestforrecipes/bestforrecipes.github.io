##### The ingredients needed to make Amazingly Delicious California Rolls:

1.  Take 520 grams Cooked rice
2.  Prepare 1 Sesame seeds
3.  Prepare 1 Imitation crab sticks
4.  Get 1 Egg
5.  Prepare 1 Cucumber
6.  Make ready 1 Salmon
7.  Make ready 1 Nori seaweed for hand-rolled sushi

##### Steps to make Amazingly Delicious California Rolls:

1.  Cook the rice and make into sushi rice. Cool well.
2.  Once cooled, place a sheet of nori on a sushi rolling mat, lay over a thin layer of sushi rice over the top and sprinkle with some sesame seeds.
3.  Flip everything over and line the fillings up on top of the nori. Roll everything up tightly.
4.  Cut with a knife and serve.
