##### Steps to make Tuna pasta salad:

1.  Cook noodles and drain
2.  Cut up celery and shred carrots. Add to drained noodles.
3.  Add in tuna
4.  Sprinkle in the bacon bits
5.  Boil 4 eggs for 15minutes. Run cold water over the pan of eggs for a bit and drain. Fill pan of eggs with cold water again, crack eggs(don't Peel), let cracked eggs sit in the cool water for a bit. Peel eggs, cut eggs in half, and take out hard yolk. Dice up white parts of the eggs in small pieces. In a small bowl, take a fork and smash up egg yolks.
6.  Add the diced eggs and smashed yolks to the pasta pan
7.  Add in mayonnaise and ranch dressing. I put a little more mayo than ranch.
8.  Add in the shredded cheese and seasonings, stir it up (optional)
9.  Best served cold\*
