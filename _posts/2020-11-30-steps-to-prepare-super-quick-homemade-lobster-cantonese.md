2.  Get 2 cup chopped celery
3.  Prepare 8 oz water cheastnuts
4.  Prepare 1 large onion chopped
5.  Take 1 stick butter
6.  Prepare 1 quart pasta water
7.  Take 1/4 cup water
8.  Get 1/4 cup cornstarch
9.  Prepare 1 inch ginger sliced thinly
10.  Get 1 tsp salt
11.  Prepare 1 tsp white pepper
12.  Prepare 1 1/2 tsp granulated garlic powder

6.  chop water cheastnuts
