##### The ingredients needed to make Mozzarella Tuna Pasta:

1.  Take 1 can Tuna
2.  Take 2 servings Pasta
3.  Get 1 Red onion
4.  Make ready to taste Salt
5.  Prepare to taste Pepper
6.  Get 1/2 Pesto
7.  Prepare Garnish:
8.  Take Mozzarella balls
9.  Prepare Sundried tomatoes
10.  Prepare Grated mozzarella cheese
11.  Prepare Rucola

##### Instructions to make Mozzarella Tuna Pasta:

1.  Boil pasta. Fry sliced red onion and when fried, add tuna. Spice with some salt and pepper.
2.  Drain pasta and pour in a bowl. Add onion, tuna and pesto. Mix.
3.  Place mixture on a plate and add mozzarella balls and sundried tomatoes.
4.  Decorate with grated mozzarella and rucola. Enjoy.
