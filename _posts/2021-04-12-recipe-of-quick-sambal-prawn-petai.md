##### The ingredients needed to make Sambal prawn petai:

1.  Prepare to taste prawns
2.  Prepare to taste petai (bitter bean)
3.  Make ready to taste sambal (Indonesian chili paste)
4.  Get to taste minced tomato
5.  Make ready 1 tsp salt
6.  Get 1 tsp sugar
7.  Make ready 1 drop sweet sauce
8.  Take to taste potato
9.  Prepare to taste garlic

Petai beans are highly popular in Southeast Asia. It has a distinct smell and it is said to cure diseases. Mix the tamarind pulp with the water and strain the mixture. Add the prawns and tamarind water and bring to a quick boil.

##### Steps to make Sambal prawn petai:

1.  Pan fry garlic. Add petai. Fry till fragrant.
2.  Add sambal. Add tomato. Salt and sugar. Add water.
3.  Put in prawns. Add ONE TINY DROP of sweet sauce
4.  Dice potatoes. Air-fry potato. Add into the frying pan and mix.
