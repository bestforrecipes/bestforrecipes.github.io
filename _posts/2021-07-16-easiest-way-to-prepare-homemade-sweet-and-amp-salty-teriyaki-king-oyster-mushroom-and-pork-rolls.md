##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Salty Teriyaki King Oyster Mushroom and Pork Rolls:

1.  Make ready Thinly sliced pork
2.  Get mushrooms King oyster mushrooms
3.  Get Salt and pepper
4.  Prepare Katakuriko
5.  Prepare Vegetable oil
6.  Prepare Sake
7.  Take ◆Soy sauce
8.  Take ◆Mirin
9.  Take ◆Sugar
10.  Make ready Mirin
11.  Take Shichimi spice

##### Instructions to make Sweet & Salty Teriyaki King Oyster Mushroom and Pork Rolls:

1.  Cut the mushrooms vertically into 4 pieces.
2.  Cut the pork in half, season both sides with salt and pepper. Place the mushrooms on the pork and wrap diagonally. Dust lightly with katakuriko.
3.  Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan. Place the wrapped mushrooms with the seam down. Make sure the seam gets cooked well.
4.  Occasionally turn to brown each side. Add the sake, cover with a lid, and steam for 3 minutes.
5.  Add the combined ◆ ingredients and simmer to infuse the flavors. Stir in the mirin, and once lustrous, optionally add shichimi spice, they're done.
