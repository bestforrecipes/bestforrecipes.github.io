##### The ingredients needed to make Okonomi Yaki:

1.  Get 480 grams Cabbage
2.  Prepare 400 grams Sliced Pork Berry
3.  Prepare 8 tbsp Chopped Spring Onion
4.  Take <Dough >
5.  Make ready 110 grams Flour
6.  Make ready 80 grams Chinese Yam
7.  Make ready 130 ml Hot Water
8.  Take 1/2 tsp Japanese Soup Stock
9.  Get 4 pieces Eggs
10.  Prepare 8 tbsp Fried Ball (Deep fried dough)
11.  Prepare <Seasoning>
12.  Get As needed Okonomiyaki Sauce
13.  Make ready As needed Mayonnaise
14.  Take As needed Bonito flakes
15.  Get As needed Aosa seaweed

##### Instructions to make Okonomi Yaki:

1.  Cut the cabbage 1 cm square size. Chop the spring onion.
2.  Make the dough. Granted the Chinese yam. Put all dough ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
3.  Take one quarter each of the vegetables & dough to another bowl. Mix the vegetables & dough well by a spoon.
4.  Pour the dough into an oiled pan. Then cover the surface of the dough with a slice of pork belly.
5.  Adjust the shape to a round shape with a flyback. Bake for 4 minutes.
6.  Turn over after 4 minutes and bake for another 4 minutes. After that, turn over again and bake for another 3 minutes. Do the same for the remaining 3 servings.
7.  Place on a plate, sprinkle with Okonomiyaki sauce, Mayonnaise, Bonito flakes, and Aosa seaweed.
