##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Salad:

1.  Make ready 1 can(5oz) Tuna in Olive Oil (drain oil)
2.  Make ready 1 Tbsp Chopped Onion
3.  Prepare 2 Tbsp Chopped Celery
4.  Make ready 1 Tbsp Chopped Raisin
5.  Get 3 Tbsp Mayonnaise
6.  Get 1 tsp Mustard
7.  Get salt&black Pepper

##### Steps to make Tuna Salad:

1.  Put all ingredients into the bowl and mix well.
2.  Make it sandwich or eat with cracker. Enjoy!
