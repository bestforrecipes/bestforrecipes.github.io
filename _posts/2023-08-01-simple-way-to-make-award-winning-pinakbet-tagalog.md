##### The ingredients needed to make Pinakbet Tagalog:

1.  Get Few pieces of pork, diced
2.  Prepare Knorr shrimp cube
3.  Get Sitaw, cut
4.  Prepare kalabasa, cubed
5.  Make ready Okra, sliced half
6.  Make ready eggplant, sliced
7.  Prepare tomato, cubed
8.  Make ready ampalaya, sliced
9.  Take kamote, cubed
10.  Make ready onion, diced
11.  Get garlic, diced
12.  Prepare alamang (barrio fiesta), 2 tsp if palengke alamang
13.  Get water
14.  Make ready Cooking oil

##### Instructions to make Pinakbet Tagalog:

1.  Heat cooking oil in a pot. Saute onion and garlic. Add ginger and continue to cook until the onion softens.
2.  Add pork. Saute for 3-5minutes.
3.  Pour water. Let boil. Add Knorr Shrimp Cube. Cover the pot and cook in medium heat for 20 minutes.
4.  Add tomato and bagoong alamang. Stir. Cover the pot. Cook for 3 minutes.
5.  Put the kalabasa and kamote into the pot. Cook for 7 minutes.
6.  Add sitaw, okra, ampalaya, and eggplant. Stir. Cover the pot and cook for 5 minutes.
7.  Season with ground black pepper. Cook for minutes.
8.  Tranfer to a serving plate. Serve. Share and Enjoy!
