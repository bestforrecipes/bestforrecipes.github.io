2.  Make ready 2 tbs blackpepper powder
3.  Take 1 tbs whitepepper powder
4.  Make ready 3 birds eye chili (sliced)
5.  Take 1 sweet onion (sliced)
6.  Make ready 3 cloves garlic (chopped)
7.  Take 2 tsp light soy sauce
8.  Make ready 2 tsp oyster sauce
9.  Take 2 tbsp dark sauce

##### Steps to make Homemade Spicy Blackpepper Beef with a 'lil Indonesian twist:

1.  Heat a cup of cooking oil in a wok over low heat
2.  Stir fry garlic for about 1 min
3.  Add in onion and stir fry til the ingredients light browned
4.  Add in the beef stir fry for about 5 mins
5.  Add in light soy sauce and oyster sauce, followed by blackpepper and white pepper.
6.  Add in dark sauce and put on the lid cover to tender the beef. Leave it for another 15 mins.
7.  After the beef is well browned and tendered, add in the extra birds eye chili and the cornstarch, stir fry it for 1 min then it's ready to serve.
