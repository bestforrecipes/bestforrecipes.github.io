##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna kimchi:

1.  Prepare 300 g tuna
2.  Get 1 avocado
3.  Get 30 g kimchi
4.  Prepare 3 tabls spoons soy sauce
5.  Get 3 tablespoons Japanese sweet sake
6.  Get Lemon liquid
