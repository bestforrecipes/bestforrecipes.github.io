##### The ingredients needed to make Japanese Miso Mayo Salmon:

1.  Prepare 3 pieces salmon
2.  Make ready 2 tablespoon mayonnaise
3.  Make ready 2 tablespoon Miso
4.  Get 1/2 teaspoon grind garlic

##### Steps to make Japanese Miso Mayo Salmon:

1.  Salt salmon a little
2.  Make sauce. Mix all seasoning well.
3.  Put mayo sauce on salmon.
4.  Bake in preheated oven for 12 minuets (200°c)
5.  Dish with vegetables
