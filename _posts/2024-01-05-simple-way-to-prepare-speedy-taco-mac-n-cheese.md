##### The ingredients needed to make Taco Mac n Cheese:

1.  Take 16 oz - Cheddar Cheese Sauce
2.  Get 2 Tbs - Taco Seasoning
3.  Take 1 Tsp - Garlic Powder
4.  Make ready 1 Tbs - Mild Hot Sauce
5.  Get 16 oz - Rigatoni Pasta
6.  Get 3 Crushed Hard Taco Shells

##### Steps to make Taco Mac n Cheese:

1.  Boil box of noodles in water until soft
2.  Crush 3 hard taco shells in plastic bag
3.  Sprinkle garlic powder into plastic bag of crushed shells
4.  Put 1 Tbs of taco seasoning into plastic bag and shake bag up
5.  Put 16 oz of cheddar cheese sauce and drained noodles back onto burner
6.  Stir until cheese sauce is thinned
7.  Stir 1 Tbs of mild sauce into pasta
8.  Stir in bag of crushed and seasoned taco shells
9.  Let sit for 10 minutes and serve
