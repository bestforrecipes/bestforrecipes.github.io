##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Somen Noodles in Hot Soup:

1.  Make ready Dashi stock (kombu and bonito based)
2.  Prepare bundles Somen noodles
3.  Make ready Shiitake mushrooms
4.  Take Mitsuba or young green scallion
5.  Get Sake
6.  Get Usukuchi soy sauce

##### Steps to make Simple Somen Noodles in Hot Soup:

1.  Boil the somen noodles to al dente. Drain in a colander and wash the noodles under water.
2.  Pour the dashi stock and sake into a pot. Once it comes to a boil, add usukuchi soy sauce, a pinch of salt and sliced shiitake mushroom.
3.  Add the somen noodles, bring it to a boil again, and it is done.
