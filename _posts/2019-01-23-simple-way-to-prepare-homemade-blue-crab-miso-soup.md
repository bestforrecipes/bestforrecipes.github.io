##### The ingredients needed to make Blue Crab Miso Soup:

1.  Take 8 to 12 Blue crab
2.  Take 1 Dashi stock granules
3.  Make ready 1 Miso
4.  Get 50 ml Sake
5.  Take 900 ml Water

##### Instructions to make Blue Crab Miso Soup:

1.  Wash the crabs and drain.
2.  Put 900 ml of water in a pot and bring to a boil. The liquid on the lower right of the picture is 50 ml of cooking sake.
3.  When it comes to a boil, add the crabs. Skim off the scum. Add a little dashi stock granules.
4.  Dissolve in whatever type of miso you prefer, and adjust the taste to your liking.
5.  You could also top with sliced scallions if you prefer.
