##### The ingredients needed to make Homemade Spicy shrimp curry:

1.  Take 500 gm small shrimps
2.  Prepare 500 gm chopped onions
3.  Take 7/8 chills
4.  Prepare 1 tb spoon of garlic & ginger paste
5.  Make ready 1/2 tb spoon of turmeric powder
6.  Make ready 1/2 tb spoon of chilli powder
7.  Prepare 1/2 tb spoon of coriander powder
8.  Prepare Salt
9.  Take 5-8 tb spoon of oil
10.  Take 2 tb spoon
11.  Take Lemon juice

##### Instructions to make Homemade Spicy shrimp curry:

1.  First wash the shrimps & marinate them with little bit of salt and lemon juice. Marinate for 15-30min.
2.  On a pan heat the oil, and fry the onions. Till they get brown in colour. Then add all the spices in that. Add a little bit of water.
3.  When the mix is coming to boil add the shrimps. Let it cook for 10min. Add the chills into it. Wait for 5min.
4.  Put the curry into a serving dish & enjoy the spicy home made shrimp curry.
