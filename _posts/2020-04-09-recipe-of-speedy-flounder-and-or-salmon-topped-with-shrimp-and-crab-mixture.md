##### The ingredients needed to make Flounder and/or Salmon topped with shrimp and crab mixture.:

2.  Take 2 tbsp Butter
3.  Prepare 1 small onion
4.  Take 2 tbsp minced garlic
5.  Take 1/2 cup red bell peppers
6.  Make ready 1/2 cup green bell peppers

8.  Make ready 1 tbsp old bay seasoning

10.  Prepare 4 tbsp italian breadcrumbs

12.  Make ready 1/2 cup mayonaise
13.  Get 4 Flounders
