##### The ingredients needed to make Lemon Baked Salmon:

1.  Prepare salmon
2.  Make ready 1/4 stick butter sliced
3.  Prepare 1 large lemon sliced
4.  Take 2 tsp seasoning salt
5.  Make ready 1 large salmon fillet

##### Instructions to make Lemon Baked Salmon:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°
2.  Lay fillet down on a cookie sheet skin side down. I suggest on a sheet of foil.
3.  Sprinkle with seasoning salt, place slices of butter along fish. Squeeze lemon slices on to fillet and place additional slices on top.
4.  Bake for 40 minutes @ 350°
