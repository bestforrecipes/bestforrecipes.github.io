##### Instructions to make Tuna salad:

2.  Next add the tuna to your salad
3.  Add the sweet corn to the mixture
4.  Chop up some black olives
5.  For the sauce add 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
6.  Then add 2 teaspoons of olive oil
7.  Add salt and pepper to taste
8.  Add the sauce to your salad
