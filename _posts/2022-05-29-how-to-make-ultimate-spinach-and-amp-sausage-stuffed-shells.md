##### The ingredients needed to make spinach & sausage stuffed shells:

1.  Make ready 12 oz box of jumbo shells
2.  Prepare 1/4 tsp parsley
3.  Take 1/2 tsp salt & pepper
4.  Prepare 15 oz container of ricotta cheese
5.  Make ready 4 cup mozzerella cheese
6.  Take 10 oz package of frozen spinach; thawed and drained
7.  Take 1 26 ounce jar of spagetti sauce
8.  Take 1 lb sausage; cooked, broken up and drained

##### Instructions to make spinach & sausage stuffed shells:

1.  perpare shells as directed on box; strain and let cool
2.  in a medium sized skillet throughly cook and break up sausage; drain; and let cool down
3.  In a large bowl mix cheeses, spices, spinach and sausage; mix throughly
4.  preheat oven to 350°F
5.  add a thin layer of spagetti sauce in a 13" x 8" cassarole pan
6.  depending on size scoop about 2 tablespoons in shells or desired amount and arrange in the pan
7.  pour some sauce over shells; top with more cheese if desired cook for 30-35mins
