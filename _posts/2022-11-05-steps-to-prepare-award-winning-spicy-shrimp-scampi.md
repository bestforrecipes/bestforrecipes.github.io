##### The ingredients needed to make spicy shrimp scampi:

1.  Get 2 box large shrimp
2.  Get 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
3.  Take 2 tbsp crushed garlic
4.  Take 2 tbsp lemon juice
5.  Take 1 cup fresh parsley
6.  Get 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
7.  Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
