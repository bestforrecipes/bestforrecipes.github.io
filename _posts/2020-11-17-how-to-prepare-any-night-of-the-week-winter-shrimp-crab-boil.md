3.  Make ready 2 lbs. of small red potatoes
4.  Get 8 ears sweet corn
5.  Take 1 onion…cut in half
6.  Get 4 Tblsp salt
7.  Make ready 2 garlic gloves

##### Steps to make Winter Shrimp Crab Boil:
