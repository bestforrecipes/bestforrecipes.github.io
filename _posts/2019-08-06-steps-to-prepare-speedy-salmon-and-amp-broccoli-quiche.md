##### Steps to make Salmon & Broccoli Quiche:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°
2.  Cook frozen pie shell for 8 mins.
3.  Reduce oven temp to 325°.
4.  Combine milk, mayonnaise, cornstarch, and eggs. Mix thoroughly.
5.  Add drained salmon, broccoli, cheese, dill, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
6.  Pour mix into pie shell.
7.  Cook at 325° for 45 mins.
8.  Let stand 5 mins before serving.
