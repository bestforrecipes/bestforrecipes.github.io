##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Scampi:

1.  Take 2 lb Raw Jumbo Shrimp
2.  Take 1/2 clove Fresh Pressed Garlic
3.  Make ready 1 stick Butter
4.  Take 1/4 cup Lemon Juice
5.  Make ready 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
6.  Make ready 1 box Spaghetti Noodles
7.  Make ready 1 as needed Parmesan Cheese

##### Steps to make Shrimp Scampi:

1.  Clean, peel and butterfly all raw shrimp.
2.  In large skillet, combine butter, garlic and lemon juice until melted.
3.  Add shrimp to skillet to sauté and cook until light pink.
4.  Bring noodles to a boil, and cook until desired tenderness.
5.  Drain noodles, combine all contents from skillet with noodles and mix thoroughly
6.  Stir in Parmesan cheese, to noodles and shrimp while stove heat is low.
