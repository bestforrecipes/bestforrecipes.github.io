##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon & Ikura Salmon Roe Temari Sushi:

1.  Make ready 300 ml Cooked white rice
2.  Prepare 5 slice Smoked salmon
3.  Make ready 5 leaves Shiso leaves
4.  Prepare 2 tbsp Salmon roe
5.  Make ready Sushi vinegar
6.  Make ready 3 tbsp Vinegar
7.  Prepare 2 tbsp Sugar
8.  Prepare 1 tsp Salt

##### Instructions to make Salmon & Ikura Salmon Roe Temari Sushi:

1.  Combine the ingredients for the sushi vinegar in a mug or something similar.
2.  Mix the sushi vinegar with some warm cooked rice. Fan the rice whilst folding the vinegar in.
3.  Feel free to use store-bought sushi vinegar and mix things into the rice.
4.  Place around 50 g rice onto a piece of cling film and lightly shape into a ball. Place a slice of salmon (cut in half) and half a shiso leaf over the rice and mould together into a ball.
5.  If you make the top and bottom of the sushi roll a bit flat, it will be easier to rest the salmon roe on top.
6.  You can make about 8-10 sushi rolls from this recipe. Keep the sushi rolls wrapped whilst working.
7.  Just before serving, peel off the cling film and present on a tray. Place some salmon roe on top and they're ready.
