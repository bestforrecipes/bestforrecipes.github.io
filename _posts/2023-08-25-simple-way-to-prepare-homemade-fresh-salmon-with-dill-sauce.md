##### The ingredients needed to make Fresh Salmon with dill sauce:

1.  Get 180 gm Fresh salmon
2.  Prepare 20 gm olive oil
3.  Make ready 50 gm fresh cream
4.  Take 1/2 tsp salt
5.  Make ready 1/2 tsp white pepper
6.  Make ready 3 bunch dill leaves
7.  Prepare 1 tsp parmesan cheese
8.  Get 1/2 tsp tabasco sauce
9.  Prepare 100 gm artechok

##### Instructions to make Fresh Salmon with dill sauce:

3.  Saute artechook on pan and keep aside.
