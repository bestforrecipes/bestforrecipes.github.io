##### The ingredients needed to make Warmed Pasta Salad:

1.  Make ready Favorite pasta; I use shells, wheels, or rotini
2.  Get sliced black &/green olives
3.  Prepare large tomatoes, diced
4.  Get large onion, chopped (optional)
5.  Prepare Favorite Italian Dressing
6.  Make ready Favorite cheese, cubed (mozzarella, cheddar)
7.  Take choice of cold cut; salami, ham, etc

##### Instructions to make Warmed Pasta Salad:

1.  Cook pasta according to directions.
2.  While pasta is cooking, slice vegetables. Put into serving dish.
3.  Cut up Deli meats. Put into serving dish.
4.  Throw all ingredients in serving dish.
5.  When pasta is cooked, drain, & toss with ingredients in serving dish.
6.  Enjoy!
