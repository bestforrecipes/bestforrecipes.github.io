##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Soup Pasta:

1.  Make ready 1 can Canned tuna
2.  Make ready 1/2 Onion
3.  Prepare 2 bunches Mizuna greens
4.  Get 1 Shredded nori seaweed
5.  Prepare 1 Wasabi
6.  Get 3 tbsp Chinese chicken stock powder
7.  Make ready 1 Butter
8.  Make ready 1 tbsp Soy sauce

##### Instructions to make Tuna Soup Pasta:

1.  Fry the tuna and onion in the oil from the tuna can, and add butter for fragrance.
2.  Add 5 ladles full of the pasta boiling water and the stock powder. Add the mizuna and boil until wilted.
3.  Add 1 tablespoon of soy sauce. If the flavour is too weak, add a little more. You can add white wine too, if you'd like.
4.  Transfer the boiled pasta to serving plates, and pour the soup on top. There's no need to mix or stir-fry everything in the frying pan.
5.  Top with some shredded nori and wasabi to finish Mix everything well on the plates, and enjoy.
