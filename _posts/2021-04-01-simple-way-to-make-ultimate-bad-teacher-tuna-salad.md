##### The ingredients needed to make Bad Teacher Tuna Salad:

1.  Prepare 3 Boiled eggs, CD&S (chopped diced & sliced)
2.  Prepare 2 Stalks of celery ,CD&S
3.  Make ready 1 large tomatoes peeled,CD&S
4.  Make ready 1 medium cucumber, CD&S
5.  Make ready 3 dash Sea salt to taste
6.  Make ready 2 dash Coarse black pepper to taste
7.  Make ready 2 12 cans of tuna in water
8.  Take 3 dash Tiger seasoning to taste (very important)
9.  Prepare 1 1/2 cup Mayo
10.  Get 1 1/4 cup Spicy mustard
11.  Get 4 dash Texas Pete (add more if you like more heat)

##### Steps to make Bad Teacher Tuna Salad:

1.  Add everything except tomatoes together in a bowl and roll with fork , after all is mixed add tomatoes and gently mix in tomatoes chill in refrigerator 3 hours and enjoy
