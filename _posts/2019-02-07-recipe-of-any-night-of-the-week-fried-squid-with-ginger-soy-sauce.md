##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Squid with Ginger Soy Sauce:

2.  Get small Squid
3.  Get ground black pepper
4.  Prepare olive oil, extra virgin
5.  Take hot paprika powder
6.  Make ready roughly chopped coriander
