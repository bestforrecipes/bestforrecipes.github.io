##### The ingredients needed to make Super Easy Tuna Potato Salad:

1.  Make ready 1/2 packages Noodles of choice
2.  Get 6 Small red potatoes
3.  Get 2 can Tuna
4.  Get 1 can Peas and Carrots
5.  Get 1 can Corn
6.  Get 1 Mayonnaise or Miracle Whip
7.  Make ready Salt & Pepper

##### Instructions to make Super Easy Tuna Potato Salad:

1.  Boil noodles and potatoes until cooked (cook separately)
2.  Cut potatoes into small pieces
3.  Add pasta, potatoes, tuna, and canned vegetables into bowl
4.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste
5.  Add mayonnaise to taste
6.  Mix and enjoy
