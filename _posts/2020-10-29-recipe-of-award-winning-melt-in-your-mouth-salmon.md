##### The ingredients needed to make Melt In Your Mouth Salmon:

1.  Prepare salmon filets (boneless, skinless)
2.  Prepare soy sauce
3.  Take juice from 2 lemons
4.  Get brown sugar
5.  Make ready water
6.  Take salt free lemon pepper seasoning

##### Instructions to make Melt In Your Mouth Salmon:

1.  Pre heat oven to 350°
2.  Mix soy sauce, lemon juice, brown sugar and water in a baking dish.
3.  Put salmon filet in baking dish with mixture and sprinkle with lemon pepper.
4.  Bake in oven for 30-45 minutes based on how well you like it cooked.
5.  Remove from juices and serve with white rice or whatever you like to go along with it.
