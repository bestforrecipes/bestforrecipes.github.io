##### The ingredients needed to make Slightly Sweetened Glazed Pork Rice Bowl:

1.  Prepare 2 to 3 Pork loin slices
2.  Make ready 1 tsp Light brown sugar
3.  Make ready 2 tbsp Mentsuyu (5x concentrate)
4.  Make ready 1 tbsp Mirin
5.  Make ready 1 Grated garlic (from a tube or powdered garlic)
6.  Make ready 1 dash Sesame oil
7.  Get 1/3 sheet Nori seaweed
8.  Get 1 bowlful Plain cooked rice
9.  Get 1 White sesame seeds

##### Steps to make Slightly Sweetened Glazed Pork Rice Bowl:

1.  Cut the pork into easy to eat pieces. Make small cuts in the fat.
2.  Heat sesame oil in a frying pan and fry the meat. Sprinkle the sugar over the meat, and stir fry while coating the meat with the sugar until the meat changes color.
3.  Add 2 tablespoons of mentsuyu, 1 tablespoon of mirin and grated garlic to the pan and rapidly reduce the glaze over high heat.
4.  Serve rice in a bowl and top with torn nori seaweed. Lay the meat slices on top and pour over the leftover glaze. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and it's done.
