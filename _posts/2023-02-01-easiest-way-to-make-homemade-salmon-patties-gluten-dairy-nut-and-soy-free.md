##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Patties- Gluten, Dairy, Nut And Soy Free:

1.  Take Potatoes
2.  Get Tinned salmon (preferably red)
3.  Prepare Salt and pepper
4.  Make ready Egg, beaten

##### Instructions to make Salmon Patties- Gluten, Dairy, Nut And Soy Free:

1.  Boil potatoes until tender
2.  Mash potatoes
3.  Remove all bones and skin from salmon
4.  Mix salmon and egg through mashed potato
5.  Form mixture into patties and fry for a few minutes each side until golden brown
6.  Serve with salad, delicious with a drizzle of vinegar on top
