5.  Make ready 1 tbsp paprika
6.  Prepare salad
7.  Make ready 3 oz spring mix greens
8.  Prepare 1 tbsp white balsalmic
9.  Make ready 1 tsp olive oil
10.  Get 1 oz julienne red pepper
11.  Get 1 oz feta
12.  Prepare 1 oz kalamata olives
13.  Make ready dressing
14.  Get 1 tbsp mayo
15.  Take 1/2 tsp sambol
16.  Prepare 1/2 tsp lime juice

##### Steps to make Fried Calamari Salad:

1.  Preheat frier to 360degrees
2.  mix salad ingredients together
3.  mix dressing together
4.  mix dry ingredient and dredge calmari in it
5.  sift calamari and fry to golden
6.  mix dressing with salad and top with calamari serv withe lemon
