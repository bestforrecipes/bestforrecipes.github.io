##### The ingredients needed to make Marinated Octopus and Broccoli:

1.  Make ready 200 grams Octopus (Boiled)
2.  Prepare 1 half a bunch Broccoli
3.  Prepare 1 medium Red bell pepper (Optional)
4.  Prepare 1 Cucumber (Optional)
5.  Take 1 ★ Marinade ("Sayaa☆'s". Tasty homemade low calorie marinade)

##### Steps to make Marinated Octopus and Broccoli:

1.  Boil the broccoli (in slightly salted water.)
2.  Cut the octopus, cucumber and red bell pepper into bite-sized pieces.
3.  Chill the boiled broccoli and cut into bite-sized pieces.
4.  Place everything in a bowl, and toss with "Sayaa 's" marinade. Done!
