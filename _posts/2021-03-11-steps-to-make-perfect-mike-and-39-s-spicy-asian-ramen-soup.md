##### The ingredients needed to make Mike's Spicy Asian Ramen Soup:

1.  Get 3 Packages Oriental Ramen Noodles \[with seasoning packets\]
2.  Make ready 1 Pound Presteamed Shrimp \[deshelled - added last\]
3.  Get 3 Cans Seafood Or Vegetable Stock \[i prefer seafood stock\]
4.  Take 4 Cups Water \[more of needed\]
5.  Take 1.5 Cups Fresh Cabbage \[sliced thin\]
6.  Make ready 1/2 Cup Fresh Chopped Cilantro Leaves
7.  Prepare 1/8 Cup Fine Minced Jalapenos
8.  Make ready 1 Small Can Bamboo Shoots \[drained\]
9.  Take 1 Small Can Water Chestnuts \[drained\]
10.  Get 1 (15 oz) Can Baby Corn \[drained\]
11.  Get 1/8 Cup Fine Minced Jalapenos
12.  Make ready 2 tbsp Siracha Sauce
13.  Take 1/2 tsp Crushed Thai Chillies
14.  Take 1/2 Can Bean Sprouts
15.  Take 1/3 Cup Green Onions \[+ reserves for garnish\]
16.  Take 1/2 Cup Dried Shitake Mushrooms
17.  Make ready 1 tsp HonDashi
18.  Get 1/2 tsp Fresh Ground Black Pepper
19.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Dried Thai Peppers
20.  Prepare 2 tbsp Siracha Sauce \[added last\]
21.  Take 1 tbsp Fine Minced Pickled Ginger
22.  Get 1/4 tsp Chinese 5 Spice
23.  Take ● For The Sides
24.  Make ready Siracha Hot Sauce
25.  Make ready Sesame Seeds

##### Steps to make Mike's Spicy Asian Ramen Soup:

1.  Here's the bulk of what you'll need. Ginger and mushrooms not pictured.
2.  Put everything, except for shrimp, in a large pot. Including Ramen packets. Boil for 15 minutes.
3.  Dried Asian mushrooms pictured.
4.  In the meantime, boil your noodles in water in a separate pot as per manufactures directions. Drain and add to vegetable pot.
5.  Add your shrimp and Siracha and boil for 2 minutes longer.
6.  Serve with Siracha Sauce to the side and garnish with green onions, cilantro and sesame seeds. Like I said, this recipe is stupid easy! Enjoy!
