##### The ingredients needed to make Avocado Toast:

1.  Make ready Sandwich bread (white/whole wheat)
2.  Make ready Cherry tomato, halved
3.  Take Cucumber, seeded and diced
4.  Make ready Cooked shrimp, cut a bite size
5.  Make ready Red onion
6.  Take For Guacamole :
7.  Prepare Avocado
8.  Get Onion, diced

10.  Make ready Garlic, minced
11.  Get Cayanne pepper
12.  Take Cumin
13.  Take Salt
14.  Take Black pepper
15.  Get Lime juice

17.  Take For Salad Dressing :
18.  Get Red wine vinegar
19.  Make ready Honey
20.  Get Dijon mustard
21.  Make ready Salt
22.  Get Black pepper

##### Instructions to make Avocado Toast:

1.  For guacamole : mix all the ingredients and mash it up.
2.  For salad dressing : mix all the ingredients. Pour over the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, shrimp and red onion. Stir it until well combined.
3.  Toast the bread. Spread the guacamole. Top with the salad.
