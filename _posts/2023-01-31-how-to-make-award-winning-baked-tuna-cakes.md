##### The ingredients needed to make Baked tuna cakes:

1.  Make ready 3 cans solid white tuna in water
2.  Prepare 1/4 cup italian bread crumbs
3.  Take 3 tablespoons dijon mustard (or mayo if mustard isn't your thing)
4.  Take 2 eggs
5.  Take 1 teaspoon old bay seasoning
6.  Get 1 teaspoon garlic salt
7.  Prepare 1 teaspoon dill
8.  Get 1 tablespoon minced onion (i used dried but fresh is fine too)
9.  Take 1/4 cup panko
10.  Prepare cooking spray (pam)
