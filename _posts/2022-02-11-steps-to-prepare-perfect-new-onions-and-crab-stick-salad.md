##### The ingredients needed to make New Onions and Crab Stick Salad:

1.  Prepare 1/2 New season onion
2.  Get 1/2 Cucumber
3.  Make ready 1 packet Crab sticks
4.  Prepare 1/2 tsp ✩Salt
5.  Get 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
6.  Make ready 1 tsp Vinegar
7.  Prepare 1 tsp Sugar

##### Steps to make New Onions and Crab Stick Salad:

1.  Slice the onion and cucumber thinly and put in a bowl. Add salt and rub gently. Leave to rest for about 10 minutes.
2.  Squeeze out water from the vegetables. Shred or slice the crab sticks diagonally. Add the crab sticks, mayonnaise, vinegar and sugar to the bowl and toss.
