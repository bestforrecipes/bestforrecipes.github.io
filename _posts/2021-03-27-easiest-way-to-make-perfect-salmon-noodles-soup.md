##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Noodles Soup:

1.  Take 1 slice SALMON
2.  Make ready 1 can cut baby corn
3.  Make ready 2 cup soy noodle
4.  Get SOUP BASE
5.  Take 2 tbsp thai red curry paste
6.  Get 1 liter stock of your choice
7.  Get 1 pinch brown sugar
8.  Make ready 1 dash lemon juice
9.  Prepare GARNISH
10.  Get 1 bunch spring onion

##### Instructions to make Salmon Noodles Soup:

1.  Bring a pot of water to a boil then add soy noodle for 3 minutes then drain and set aside
2.  Season salmon with salt then bake or grill salmon and flake salmon flesh
3.  Put stock in pan and add curry paste then mix well and bring it to boil
4.  Then add cooked soy noodle then add light soy sauce and sugar with baby corn and bring it to a boil
5.  off the heat then add lime juice
6.  serve and top with cooked salmon flake and top with spring onion
