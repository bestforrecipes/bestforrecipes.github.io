##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Patties:

1.  Get 2 can 14 3/4oz Pink Salmon
2.  Prepare 2 Eggs
3.  Get 1 1/2 cup Bread Crumbs
4.  Get 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
5.  Get 1 tsp mustard powder
6.  Make ready 1 tsp basil
7.  Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
8.  Get 1/3 cup onion

##### Instructions to make Salmon Patties:

1.  Drain salmon and flake in a 2qt. mixing bowl
2.  add all other ingredients and mix together.
3.  Heat oil in skillet.
4.  press salmon mixture into 3-4" patties.
5.  Place patties in hot oil and cook approximately 3-4 minutes per side or until slightly golden brown.
