##### The ingredients needed to make Octopus fry with sautéed bean sprouts:

1.  Prepare Octopus fry
2.  Get 1 Octopus leg 🐙
3.  Make ready 2 tbsp ☆ soy sauce
4.  Take 2 tbsp ☆ cooking sake
5.  Make ready 1 tsp ☆ ginger, grated
6.  Make ready 1 tsp ☆ garlic, minced
7.  Get 1/2 tsp ☆ dried green seaweed powder
8.  Take 2 TBSP potato starch
9.  Make ready 2 TBSP flour
10.  Prepare Bean sprouts sauté
11.  Get 1 bag bean sprouts (255g)
12.  Prepare Dash salt
13.  Make ready Dash pepper
14.  Take Dash bonito soup stock (hondashi)
15.  Prepare 1 tsp soy-sauce

##### Instructions to make Octopus fry with sautéed bean sprouts:

1.  Cut octopus into bite sized pieces.
2.  In a small bowl, mix all the ingredients ☆ and add octopus. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
3.  Add flour and mix well. Then add potato starch. This way, you can limit the possibility of an explosion of the oil. (it's a smart idea to hold the lid of a pan in front of your face to defend yourself 😂)
4.  Cook it for 3 minutes and set it aside. 👍
5.  Now it’s time for bean sprouts. In a large pan, high heat sauté the bean sprouts for 3 minutes.
6.  Add salt, pepper, hondashi, soy sauce. Turn off the heat.
7.  Add fried octopus and mix it together. Sprinkle more dried seaweed flakes (Aonori) at the end for flavor.
