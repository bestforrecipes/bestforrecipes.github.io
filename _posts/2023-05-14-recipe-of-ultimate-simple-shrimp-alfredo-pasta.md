##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Shrimp Alfredo Pasta:

1.  Make ready Start
2.  Get 1 tbsp Diced Onions
3.  Take 1 tbsp Green Pepper
4.  Prepare 1 tsp Jalapeno
5.  Prepare Minced Garlic
6.  Take tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
7.  Prepare 1 box Penne Whole Grain
8.  Make ready 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
9.  Take Thowed Shrimp
10.  Get tbsp Butter
11.  Take cup Spinach
12.  Get pinch salt and pepper
13.  Get boiling water
14.  Prepare 1 box pasta whole grain

##### Instructions to make Simple Shrimp Alfredo Pasta:

1.  Boil your water: i had some minced Garlic and salt in my water
2.  Start to slow simmer your veggies
3.  Add minced garlic and shrimp. low simmer for 4 mins
4.  Add 1 1/2 cans of alfredo sauce to shrimp and spinach i like mines at the end to taste a little raw
5.  Serve with pasta on plate then spoon alfredo sauce on top. Sprinkle chesse ontop :)
