##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Salad:

1.  Prepare 1 lb Mock crab, chopped or flaked
2.  Prepare 1/2 cup Celery, diced small
3.  Get 1/2 cup Sweet onion, diced small
4.  Get 1/2 cup Red bell pepper, diced small
5.  Get 1/2 cup Black olives, sliced
6.  Take 1 cup Shredded cheddar cheese
7.  Make ready 3/4 cup Mayonnaise
8.  Take 1/4 cup Milk OR water (to thin mayo a bit)
9.  Get 1/2 tsp Each, salt & pepper
10.  Take 1 tsp Dill weed
11.  Take 3 tbsp Sriracha sauce, optional

##### Instructions to make Crab Salad:

1.  Dice crab and veggies. Place in a large mixing bowl with the cheese and olives.
2.  In a separate bowl, mix the remaining ingredients for dressing. Add a bit more milk or water as needed to thin the mayo.
3.  Mix dressing with crab mixture. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
