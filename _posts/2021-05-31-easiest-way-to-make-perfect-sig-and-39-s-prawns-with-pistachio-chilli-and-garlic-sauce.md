##### The ingredients needed to make Sig's Prawns with Pistachio, Chilli and Garlic Sauce:

1.  Prepare prawns
2.  Make ready 30 large prawns ready cooked
3.  Get 1 lemon,divided into sections or slices for drizzling over
4.  Take sauce
5.  Take 4 clove garlic,chopped roughly
6.  Make ready 1 large very ripe tomato, quartered and seeded
7.  Make ready 2 small long red chilli, seeded,roughly chopped
8.  Prepare 4 tbsp unsalted pistachio nuts
9.  Make ready 60 grams chargrilled peppers in oil,,chopped
10.  Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
11.  Make ready 1 tbsp rasberry or red wine vinegar

##### Steps to make Sig's Prawns with Pistachio, Chilli and Garlic Sauce:

1.  Wash prawns arrange on plates. Divide the lemon
2.  Put all other prepared ingredients into blender and work into a semi smooth sauce.
3.  Serve prawns with mixed lettuce, sauce, lemon slices and scatter a few pistachio over top.
4.  If you want to.make this extra special heat the unpeeled garlic, wrapped in foil and tomato and chilli in preheated oven about 200C/400 f for about 10-12 minutes,towards 9 minutes add nuts to just heat them through.
5.  Squeeze the garlic out when slightly cooled and add all into blender with rest of sauce ingredients.
