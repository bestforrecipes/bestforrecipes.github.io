##### The ingredients needed to make Pan Seared Salmon with Avocado Tomato Cucumber Salad:

1.  Prepare 4 pcs (@170 gr) salmon fillet, around 2 cm thick (with or without skin)
2.  Make ready 1 Tbsp olive oil
3.  Get 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
4.  Get 1/4 tsp ground black pepper or taste

##### Instructions to make Pan Seared Salmon with Avocado Tomato Cucumber Salad:

1.  Season the salmon with salt and black pepper powder. Set aside.
2.  Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium high heat until shimmering. Place the salmon in the skillet, skin side up. Cook for 4 minutes until crisp.
3.  Carefully flip the salmon. Reduce the heat to medium. Cook for another 5-6 minutes. Remove from the heat. Serve the salmon with the avocado tomato cucumber salad. Yum! ðŸ˜‹
