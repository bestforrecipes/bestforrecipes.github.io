1.  Take 12 oz sushi grade ahi tuna
2.  Get 1 tbs soy sauce
3.  Make ready 1 tsp rice wine vinegar
4.  Prepare 1 tsp olive oil
5.  Get 1/2 tsp white and black sesame seeds
6.  Take 1/2 tsp ground mustard and ginger
7.  Take to taste Sea salt
8.  Make ready Garnishment
9.  Take Crystallized ginger
10.  Prepare leaves Radicchio
