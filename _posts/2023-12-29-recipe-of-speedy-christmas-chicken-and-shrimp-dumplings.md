##### The ingredients needed to make Christmas Chicken and Shrimp Dumplings:

1.  Prepare 300 g minced chicken
2.  Make ready 100 g shrimp cut roughly
3.  Prepare 4 tablespoon starch
4.  Get 10 cm spring onion minced
5.  Make ready 1 teaspoon grind ginger
6.  Take 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon powder
7.  Get 2 teaspoon soy sauce
8.  Prepare Wantan skin
9.  Take Vegetable or sesame oil
