##### The ingredients needed to make Sundried Tomato and Basil Tuna Dip:

1.  Take 1 can Clover Leaf Flaked Light Tuna Sundried Tomato and Basil
2.  Prepare 1 tbsp Light Cream Cheese
3.  Prepare 2 slice Fresh Tomato
4.  Get 2 slice White Onion
5.  Get 1 pinch Salt
6.  Prepare 1 pinch Black Pepper

##### Instructions to make Sundried Tomato and Basil Tuna Dip:

1.  Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and blend together.
2.  Serve on toast or as a dip for crackers. I particularly enjoyed it with Triscuit Cracked Pepper and Olive Oil crackers.
