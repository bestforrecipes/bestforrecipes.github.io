##### The ingredients needed to make Yum Woon Sen / Vermicelli and Seafood Spicy Salad:

1.  Prepare 1/2 cup my spicy sauce ,only 1 - 2 tbsp if you dont like spicy
2.  Prepare 2/3 cup mix seafood; squid,shrimps,mussels,fish
3.  Get 50 grams vermicelli
4.  Prepare 1 bunch spring onion and coriander
5.  Make ready 1 chopped mix red and yellow pepper to color the dish
6.  Make ready 1 slice of carrot and red cabbage ,not much,only to give color to the dish
