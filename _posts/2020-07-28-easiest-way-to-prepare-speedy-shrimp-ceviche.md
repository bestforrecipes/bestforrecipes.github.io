##### The ingredients needed to make shrimp ceviche:

1.  Make ready 2 lb shrimp
2.  Make ready 8 each lemons
3.  Take 1 large onion
4.  Prepare 1/2 bunch cilantro
5.  Take 4 each tomatoes
6.  Make ready 3 each serano chiles
7.  Take 3 each avacados
8.  Take 1 packages tostadas
9.  Prepare 1 dash hot sauce
10.  Take 1 dash salt

##### Instructions to make shrimp ceviche:

1.  wash and peel shrimp
2.  cut shrimp in half. place in deep bowl.
3.  cut lemons squeeze over shrimp.
4.  shrimp should start to turn pink, now you can cut the rest of the vegetables place in bowl.
5.  If you want it less spicy you can seed the Serrano Chile
6.  add some salt, avocado, refrigerate for a few hrs
7.  enjoy over a tostada with some hot sauce
