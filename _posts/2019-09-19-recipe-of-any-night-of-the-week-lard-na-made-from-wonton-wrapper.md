##### The ingredients needed to make Lard Na Made from Wonton wrapper:

1.  Get Noodles & imitation crab
2.  Get 1 packages wonton wrapper
3.  Get 1 tbsp Maggie's soy sauce
4.  Make ready 1 lb imitation crab meat
5.  Take veggies & shrimp
6.  Prepare 1/2 large sweet onion
7.  Prepare 1 clove minced garlic
8.  Take 1 tsp minced ginger
9.  Take 1 bunch broccoli
10.  Prepare 1 dozen baby Portobello mushrooms
11.  Take 2 cup chicken broth
12.  Get 2 tbsp Maggie's soy sauce
13.  Prepare 1 lb shrimp
14.  Take 1 tbsp canola oil
15.  Take 1 tbsp sweet soy sauce
16.  Take 1/2 bunch cabbage
17.  Get gravy
18.  Get 2 tbsp corn starch
19.  Prepare 1/2 cup chicken broth

##### Instructions to make Lard Na Made from Wonton wrapper:

1.  Boil hot water in medium pot with tablespoon of canola oil
2.  Cook for about 10-12 min. Do the pinch and taste test to make sure it's cooked completely
3.  remove from heat and strain, run cold water to stop the cooking
4.  heat a wok or large pan, add oil, pinch of garlic and pinch of ginger
5.  Add noodles & crab, 1tbs of Maggi's soy sauce
6.  let the noodles fry until a little dark brown color. Stir, remove from heat immediately and add to your serving plates
7.  heat wok on medium high, Add canola oil, pinch of garlic and ginger
8.  Add all veggies except for the mushrooms
9.  Add 2 cups chicken broth, 2 tbs Maggie's soy sauce, 1 tbs sweet soy sauce. stir
10.  in a separate bowl, mix 2 tbs corn starch and chicken broth
11.  Add chicken broth and corn starch mix
12.  let sauce thicken. Pour over noodles once it's a nice thick consistency like gravy. Add siracha if u like a little kick
