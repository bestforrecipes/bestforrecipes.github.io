##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimps Fry Rice:

1.  Make ready 2 cup of cook rice
2.  Take 1 large eeg
3.  Prepare 1 tbsp of cooking oil
4.  Prepare 2 tbsp of choped onions
5.  Prepare 1 tsp of choped garlic
6.  Make ready 8 large Shrimp, peeled and take of heads .slits on the center
7.  Get 1 tbsp choped green onions and red bell pepper
8.  Get 1/2 tsp of chicken stocks powder
9.  Make ready 1 tsp of seasoning soy sauce
10.  Take 1 tsp of fish sauce
11.  Prepare 1/2 tsp of sugar
12.  Prepare 1 tsp of oyster sauce
13.  Prepare 1 tsp of black peppers

##### Instructions to make Shrimps Fry Rice:

1.  In the heating pan add oil when the oil hot add choped garlic and onions cook for 30 second
2.  Add erg beat and cook through then add shrimps stir for 1 minutes.
3.  Add cooked rice mixing well
4.  Add red bell pepper and greens onions , add fish sauce and chicken stock powders then add soy sauce mixed throughly
5.  Add sugar , oyster sauce finishing with black peppers.
