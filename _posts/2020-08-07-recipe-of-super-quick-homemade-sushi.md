4.  Prepare ginger paste
5.  Prepare salt
6.  Take sliced half boiled carrots
7.  Make ready sliced tossed capsicum
8.  Make ready cheese spread
9.  Take big plastic sheet to roll the sushi
10.  Get dark soya sauce
11.  Take black sesame seeds (optional)

##### Instructions to make Sushi:

1.  For the rice, take any small grain starchy rice & cook them till they become sticky, then cool them completely, then add white vinegar or rice vinegar (I added white vinegar), salt, ginger paste & mix well.
2.  Now take nori sheet, place it on a plastic sheet, now spread flavoured rice on half of the nori sheet, then in the centre spread cheese spread, place 3 slices of carrots, 6 slices of tossed capsicum and slowly roll the sushi with help of plastic sheet into a firm roll.
3.  Cut the edges with a sharp knife & cut sushi into clean & meat pieces, then sprinkle black sesame seeds over each piece & serve it with soya sauce.
4.  Note -you can also serve sushi with pickled ginger & wasabi, I however do not like wasabi, so I served it with only soya sauce.
5.  Note- you can find nori sheets on Amazon
