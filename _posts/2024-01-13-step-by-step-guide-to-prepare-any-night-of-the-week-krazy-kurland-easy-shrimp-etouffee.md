##### The ingredients needed to make Krazy Kurland Easy Shrimp Etouffee:

1.  Prepare salted butter
2.  Make ready kosher salt
3.  Prepare bell pepper (optional); can use bottled, marinated peppers
4.  Make ready large onion, diced
5.  Prepare ribs celery
6.  Take garlic, minced
7.  Get chicken broth
8.  Take shrimp (or crawfish)
9.  Take table salt
10.  Prepare cayenne
11.  Prepare black pepper
12.  Take paprika
13.  Get parsley flakes
14.  Make ready lemon juice (approximately 1 lemon)
15.  Prepare sherry (or port or simply red wine)
16.  Take cornstarch in water (slurry) for thickening

##### Steps to make Krazy Kurland Easy Shrimp Etouffee:

1.  Soften vegetables in butter and kosher salt (shown with marinated red peppers included)
2.  Add remaining spices and cook for 3-4 minutes
3.  Add chicken broth and shrimp; cook semi-covered on medium heat approximately 30-40 min
4.  Add cornstarch slurry; cook approximately 5 minute untill desired consistency
5.  Done! Serve over rice (yellow rice shown)
