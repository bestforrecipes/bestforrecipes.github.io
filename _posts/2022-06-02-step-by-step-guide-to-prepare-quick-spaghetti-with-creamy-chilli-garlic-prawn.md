##### The ingredients needed to make Spaghetti with Creamy Chilli Garlic Prawn:

1.  Make ready 300 gr prawn
2.  Take 200 gr spaghetti
3.  Get 200 ml cream
4.  Prepare 4 cloves garlic
5.  Prepare 1 tbsp chilli flake
6.  Prepare 1 tbsp salt
7.  Make ready 1 tsp pepper
8.  Prepare 4 tbsp olive oil
9.  Get parsley

##### Steps to make Spaghetti with Creamy Chilli Garlic Prawn:

1.  Fry the garlic and chilli flake with olive oil on low heat about 3 minutes, season with salt and pepper
2.  Add the cream, mix, and add the prawn, meanwhile cook the pasta to al dente
3.  After the pasta cooked, mix well with the creamy garlic chilli prawn and add parsley for freshness
