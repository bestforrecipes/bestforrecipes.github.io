##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy seafood aglio olio:

1.  Get 250 gram spaghetti
2.  Take 2 tbs olive oil
3.  Get 8 clove garlic (chopped)
4.  Make ready Seafood mix on choice (squid, prawns or mussels)
5.  Take Chicken sausage (chopped into small bites)
6.  Make ready 1/3 cup water
7.  Get 2 tsp chilli flakes
8.  Prepare to taste Salt and blackpepper
9.  Get Spices mix on choice (oregano, rosemary, parsley and basil)
10.  Make ready 1/2 cube anchovies stock (\*optional)
11.  Prepare 1/2 cup grated parmigiano-reggiano cheese (\*optional)
