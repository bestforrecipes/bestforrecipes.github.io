##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna pizza:

1.  Take 1 sheet pizza dough
2.  Make ready 80 gm tuna fish
3.  Prepare 4 tbsp tomato sauce
4.  Make ready 2 tbsp tomato cubes
5.  Get 1/3 onion
6.  Take 1 tbsp mozzarella
7.  Prepare 2 tbsp grated cheese
8.  Take 1 Tsp olive oil
9.  Make ready 1 cup cheese
10.  Prepare 1 tsp basil

##### Steps to make Tuna pizza:

1.  Preheat the oven to the highest temperature (minimum 250 ° C). - Unroll the pizza dough with the baking paper on the oven plate.
2.  Divide the tomato sauce and the tomato cubes over the dough. - Peel the onion and cut into rings. Cut the mozzarella into pieces.
3.  Divide the tuna, the onion and the mozzarella over the pizza. - Sprinkle grated cheese over it.
4.  Place the pizza in the oven for about 15 minutes. - Brush with the cheese and finish with a few drops of olive oil and shredded basil leaves.
