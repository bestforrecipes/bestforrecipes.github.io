##### The ingredients needed to make Easy! Cute Pikachu Bento:

1.  Get 1 use as much as you'd like Plain cooked white rice
2.  Get 1 Decofuri rice seasoning (yellow)
3.  Get 1 Nori seaweed
4.  Make ready 1 Imitation crab meat sticks

##### Instructions to make Easy! Cute Pikachu Bento:

1.  Mix the yellow "decofuri" rice seasoning into the rice. Wrap with plastic cling wrap and form into a shape as shown in the picture. Wrap the ear tips with nori seaweed .
2.  Place facial features on top, and you're done. Use nori seaweed for the eyes, mouth, and nose, then cut-outs of imitation crab meat sticks for the cheeks.
