##### The ingredients needed to make Laurinda's Shrimp Etouffee:

1.  Get Green onion chopped
2.  Get Bell pepper chopped
3.  Make ready minced garlic
4.  Make ready butter
5.  Make ready Sweet onion chopped
6.  Take boneless skinless chicken breast, cubed
7.  Prepare cream of chicken soup
8.  Get raw peeled and veined shrimp
9.  Prepare diced tomatoes undrained
10.  Prepare diced tomatoes seasoned with basil, garlic & oregano
11.  Take Cayenne pepper or season to taste.

##### Instructions to make Laurinda's Shrimp Etouffee:

1.  Sautee green onions, bell pepper & minced garlic in butter. set aside
2.  Sautee sweet onion & chicken until chicken is white
3.  add cream of chicken soup & mix til smooth
4.  add all remaining ingredients
5.  simmer 20 minutes stirring occasionally
6.  serve over rice if desired
