##### The ingredients needed to make Irmgards Bacon Tuna Pasta Melt:

1.  Get 40 grams tomato puree
2.  Get 100 grams pasta of your choice I used asda gluten free
3.  Make ready 100 grams tuna in water drained
4.  Make ready 26 grams bacon lardons /pancetta
5.  Prepare 1/2 tsp thyme
6.  Prepare 1/4 tsp asafoetida
7.  Get sauce
8.  Take 50 grams crumbly feta cheese
9.  Prepare 30 ml almond milk if low fodmap

##### Steps to make Irmgards Bacon Tuna Pasta Melt:

1.  Cook pasta according to instructions and bacon lardons in an air fryer or frying pan, I air fried mine
2.  While pasta is cooking put feta cheese in bits in a bowl over a pan of hot water stirring occasionally to mix until melted
3.  Mix tuna, tomato puree asafoetida & thyme together in a bowl.
4.  When cheese is almost melted stir in milk
5.  Drain pasta, add lardons, tuna mix and mix well
6.  Put pasta mix on large plate and pour on sauce
7.  Enjoy
