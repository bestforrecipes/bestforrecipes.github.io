##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy King Prawn and Whole-wheat Spaghetti:

1.  Take Spinach
2.  Prepare Mushrooms
3.  Take Asparagus Spears
4.  Get Purple sprouting broccoli spears
5.  Make ready Sugar Snap Peas
6.  Take Courgette
7.  Make ready Cherry Tomatoes
8.  Take King Prawns
9.  Take Olive oil (optional)
10.  Take Knorr Chicken Stock Cube
11.  Get Salmon Philadelphia
12.  Get Whole-wheat Spaghetti (dry)
13.  Make ready Cayenne Pepper (optional to taste)
14.  Get Salt and Pepper

##### Steps to make Creamy King Prawn and Whole-wheat Spaghetti:

1.  Add spaghetti to a pan or boiling salted water.
2.  Roughly Chop all veg, add to a pan with the stock cube and olive oil. Add a dash of water or 2 and cook until still firm. Add the raw prawns and spinach until spinach has wilted.
3.  Add the Salmon Philadelphia and more water if required. Add cayenne and seasoning to taste.
4.  Once Philadelphia has melted add the Al Dente Spaghetti to the sauce and stir through. Serve and Enjoy!
