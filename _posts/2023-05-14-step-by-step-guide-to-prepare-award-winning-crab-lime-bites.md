##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Lime Bites:

1.  Take 1 can crab meat (6 oz)
2.  Get 3 green onions, chopped
3.  Get 1 tbsp capers, chopped
4.  Prepare 1 tsp dill
5.  Get 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
6.  Take 1/4 tsp lemon pepper
7.  Make ready 1 tbsp lime juice
8.  Make ready 2 tbsp greek yogurt
9.  Prepare 3 tbsp real mayonnaise
10.  Prepare 1 spinach leaves
11.  Get 1 vegetable ritz crackers
12.  Get 1 fresh thyme leaves for garnish

##### Instructions to make Crab Lime Bites:

1.  Mix all ingredients in a medium sized bowl. Chill until ready to serve.
2.  Top crackers with spinach leaves and then crab mixture. Garnish with fresh thyme leaves and serve.
