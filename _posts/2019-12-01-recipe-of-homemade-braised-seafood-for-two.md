##### The ingredients needed to make Braised Seafood for Two:

1.  Get 6 Shrimps
2.  Take 6 Mussels
3.  Prepare 6 Clams
4.  Prepare 6 Octopus
5.  Prepare 1 Chicken Thigh (diced)
6.  Take Onion & garlic (thinly sliced) Sake
7.  Take Peanut oil
8.  Prepare salt
9.  Take “Sazon” from Goya

##### Steps to make Braised Seafood for Two:

1.  Heat Peanut oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Saute the Onion & Galic until browned, Add Chicken Thigh Cook for 2 minutes. Then, add Seafoods, Sake, Sazon & Salt, Cover and cook in the pan for about 10 minutes.
