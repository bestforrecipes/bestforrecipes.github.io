##### The ingredients needed to make Instant Prep, Easy Chirashi-zushi:

1.  Make ready 1 two bowls' worth Sushi rice
2.  Get 1 slab Skipjack tuna (bonito)
3.  Make ready 1/2 Cucumber
4.  Make ready 1 tsp Finely grated ginger
5.  Take 1 Soy sauce
6.  Take 1 White sesame seeds
7.  Prepare 1 Pickled ginger (optional)

##### Instructions to make Instant Prep, Easy Chirashi-zushi:

1.  Thinly slice the bonito. Transfer the slices to a bowl, pour soy sauce over them just until it covers the top of the bonito, then add grated ginger, and set aside to macerate.
2.  Thinly slice the cucumber.
3.  Fill rice bowls with sushi rice, and top with bonito from Step 1 and cucumber slices from Step 2, sprinkle with sesame seeds, and add a touch of picked ginger.
