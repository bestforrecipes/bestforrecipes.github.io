##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Lettuce Tacos:

1.  Take 1 lb. Peeled Gumbo Shrimp
2.  Take 2 tsp. Minced Garlic
3.  Get 3 TBSP. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
4.  Make ready 2 tsp. Parsley
5.  Take 2 TBSP. Ground Fresh Chili Paste
6.  Get Juice Of 1 Lemon
7.  Make ready Pinch Of Sea Salt
8.  Get Pinch Of Black Pepper
9.  Make ready Corn (Topping)
10.  Make ready Mozzarella Cheese (Topping)
11.  Get Romaine Lettuce (For Serving)

##### Instructions to make Shrimp Lettuce Tacos:

1.  In a large bowl, stir together shrimp, parsley, lemon juice, chili paste, garlic, and 2 tablespoons oil and season with salt and pepper. Toss until combined, then let marinate in the fridge 10 minutes.
2.  In a large skillet over medium heat, heat remaining tablespoon oil. Add shrimp and marinade cook until pink, 2-3 minutes per side.
3.  Assemble wraps: Add shrimp to lettuce, drizzle with sour cream, and garnish with corn and mozzarella!
