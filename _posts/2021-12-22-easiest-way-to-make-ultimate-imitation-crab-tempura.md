##### The ingredients needed to make Imitation Crab Tempura:

1.  Take 5 stick Imitation crab meat
2.  Get 5 tbsp Plain white flour
3.  Make ready 3 1/2 tbsp Water
4.  Prepare 2 cm in the bottom of the pot Frying oil

##### Steps to make Imitation Crab Tempura:

1.  Prepare the ingredients.
2.  Whisk the flour in water.
3.  Add in the imitation crab, and swirl it around, it will break up all by itself. Deep fry at 180°C / 360°F until golden brown.
4.  If you don't want it completely shredded, just break up the ends a bit. You can easily do this with a pair of cooking chopsticks.
5.  Dip each side into the batter. They will soften and fall apart easily, so be careful.
6.  Deep-fry until golden brown, at 180°C.
7.  This is what they should look like after frying.
8.  The ones that have been completely broken up are much more springy in texture. I recommend making these when you feel that you do not have enough for your beer snack, or are lacking enough side dishes.
