2.  Take 4 6 oune fresh tuna steaks
3.  Get 1 large egg
4.  Get 2 tbsp mayonnaise
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
6.  Make ready PEANUT SESAME CRUST
7.  Get 1 tbsp white seasame seeds
8.  Prepare 4 tbsp finely chopped peanuts, I use dry roasted
9.  Take 1 tbsp black seasame seeds
10.  Make ready 1 tsp kung pao dry seasoning mix
11.  Make ready WASABI CREAM SAUCE
12.  Take 1 recipe wasabi cream sauce, recipe attached in direction step #5
