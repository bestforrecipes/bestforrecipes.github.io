##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Stick Omelette:

1.  Make ready 3 eggs, beat
2.  Get 2 green onions, finely chopped
3.  Get 6 pieces crab stick, diced
4.  Get salt and pepper
5.  Make ready 1/2 tbs butter

##### Instructions to make Crab Stick Omelette:

1.  Mix eggs, crab sticks, green onions, salt, and pepper until well combined.
2.  Heat the butter on a pan. Pour the eggs mixture.
3.  Fry until both sides golden brown.
