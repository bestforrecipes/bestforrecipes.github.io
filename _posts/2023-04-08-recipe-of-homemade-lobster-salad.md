##### The ingredients needed to make Lobster Salad:

1.  Prepare 2 8oz packages of Imitation Lobster
2.  Take 3 Chopped Celery Stalks
3.  Get 1/4 cup Chopped Green Onion
4.  Get 1/4 cup Chopped Cilantro
5.  Get 1/2 stick Melted Butter
6.  Prepare 4 clove Garlic Crushed
7.  Get 1 tsp Lemon Juice
8.  Prepare 1 tsp Sea Salt
9.  Make ready 1 tsp Cracked Pepper
10.  Get 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
11.  Take 2 tbsp Sour Cream
12.  Get 1 tbsp Softened Cream Cheese
13.  Prepare dash White Wine

##### Instructions to make Lobster Salad:

1.  Mix all ingredients well in large bowl
2.  Chill for 30min
