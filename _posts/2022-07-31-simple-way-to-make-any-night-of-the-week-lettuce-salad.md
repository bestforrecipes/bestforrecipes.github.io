##### The ingredients needed to make Lettuce Salad:

1.  Make ready 1/2 head lettuce (as much as you want)
2.  Take red leaf lettuce (as much as you want)
3.  Prepare 1/2 pack tomyo (pea sprouts) (cut into 3 cm length)
4.  Prepare 4 crab sticks (※) (tear into appropriate pieces)
5.  Take 2 Tbsp mayonaisse (to taste)
6.  Get 1 pickled ume (remove the seed, chop finely) (optional)
7.  Prepare salt and pepper

##### Steps to make Lettuce Salad:

1.  Bring the water and salt to a boil. Put 2 kinds of lettuce and tomyo and cook for 10~20 seconds, immediately soak in cold water and let them cool, then drain very well.
2.  Just before eating, combine the drained vegetables and mayonaise and chopped ume(optional). Season with salt and pepper.
