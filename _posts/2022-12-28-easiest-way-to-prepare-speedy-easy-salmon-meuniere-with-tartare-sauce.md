##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Salmon Meunière with Tartare Sauce:

1.  Take pieces King salmon, fresh salmon or cod
2.  Take Salt and pepper
3.  Make ready Flour (cake flour)
4.  Make ready Oil for pan frying (grapeseed, vegetable etc.)
5.  Make ready to 10 grams Butter
6.  Get Tartar sauce
7.  Prepare Boiled eggs
8.  Take of an onion Onions (finely chopped)
9.  Make ready just 1 dash for color!! Parsley (dried parsley is fine)
10.  Take Mayonnaise
11.  Make ready Lemon (or Italian dressing)
12.  Get Side vegetables
13.  Make ready cm Carrot
14.  Make ready to 15 pods Snow peas
15.  Get Boiling water
16.  Take Butter

##### Steps to make Easy Salmon Meunière with Tartare Sauce:

1.  Salt and pepper and the salmon, and dust lightly with flour.
2.  Spread the oil in a frying pan, and put in the flour dusted salmon.
3.  Cover with a lid, and cook over low heat until the salmon is browned on one side.
4.  Turn the salmon over, and brown the other side. Drop the butter into the pan. That's it. I believe in keeping cooking easy and delicious!!
5.  To make the tartare sauce, finely chop the boiled egg, onion and parsley, and mix with mayonnaise and lemon. Use as much as you like!
