##### Steps to make shells stuffed with sausage, spinach & garlic:

2.  mince garlic, chop spinach add to sausage. use about as much spinach as you want. we will add more spinach later.
3.  once meat is finished add to a mixing bowl. be sure to chop any chunks of sausage. add ricotta more chopped spinach sale pepper both cheeses and 1/2 spaghetti sauce
4.  preheat oven 350 . now drain shells and grease large baking dish and add spaghetti sauce to The bottom about 3 tablespoons.
5.  let shells cool. now mix together your ingredients in the mixing bowl. once shells have cooled start stuffing. your shells with your mixture. and then add to the baking dish.
