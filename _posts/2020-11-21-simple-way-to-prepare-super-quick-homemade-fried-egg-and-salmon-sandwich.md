##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Egg and Salmon Sandwich:

1.  Make ready sauce————
2.  Make ready 1-1/2 tablespoons prepared Horseradish
3.  Get 1/3 cup favorite barbecue sauce
4.  Make ready sandwich———-
5.  Take 4 slices bread
6.  Take 6 ounces canned boneless and skinless pink salmon
7.  Take 3 large eggs
8.  Take as needed sliced dill pickle chips

##### Steps to make Fried Egg and Salmon Sandwich:

1.  Fry the salmon and Eggs in a skillet
2.  Cover one slice of bread with pickle chips
3.  Add the Horseradish and barbecue sauce to a bowl
4.  Mix well
5.  Add eggs and Salmon to top of pickles
6.  Drizzle sauce over egg and Salmon
7.  Add slice of bread on top
8.  Cut in half and serve. I hope you enjoy!
