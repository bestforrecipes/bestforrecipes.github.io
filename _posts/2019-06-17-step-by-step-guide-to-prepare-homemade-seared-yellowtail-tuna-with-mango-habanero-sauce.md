##### The ingredients needed to make Seared Yellowtail tuna With mango habanero sauce:

1.  Prepare 2 block sushi grade tuna
2.  Take 3 tablespoons bagel mix seeds
3.  Make ready 3 tablespoons black pepper
4.  Get 3 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
5.  Prepare 3 tablespoons black sesame seeds
6.  Get 1 tablespoon oil
7.  Prepare 5 tablespoons homemade mango habanero salsa
8.  Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
9.  Take 1/2 cup rice vinegar
10.  Make ready 1 tablespoon cornstarch
