##### The ingredients needed to make Mike's Lobster & King Crab Claw Cheese Spread:

1.  Make ready 1 Pound Rock Lobster Tails \[meat weight - fine chopped\]
2.  Make ready 1 Pound Pre-steamed King Crab Claws \[meat weight -fine shread\]
3.  Get as needed Quality Dry White Wine
4.  Make ready 4 (8 oz) Packages Philadelphia Cream Cheese \[room temp\]
5.  Get 1/2 Cup Chives \[fine chopped\]
6.  Take 1/2 tsp Lemon Pepper
7.  Get 1 Dash Lemon Juice
8.  Get 1/2 tsp Old Bay
9.  Get ● Options
10.  Prepare Toasted Bagles
11.  Make ready Chilled Vegetables
12.  Take Lemon Wedges
13.  Prepare Crackers

##### Steps to make Mike's Lobster & King Crab Claw Cheese Spread:

1.  Place shelled seafood on a cookie sheet with white wine. Bake seafood at 350° for 20 minutes. Pull from oven and crack shells once cooled. Rough chop meat or shread. Mix everything together and chill.
2.  Serve spread on toasted Bagles, crackers or dip vegetables in. Also, with lemon wedges.
3.  This spread makes for great gifts!
4.  Enjoy!
