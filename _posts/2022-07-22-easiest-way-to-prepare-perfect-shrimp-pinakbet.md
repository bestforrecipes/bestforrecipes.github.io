##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Pinakbet:

1.  Prepare Shrimps
2.  Get Onions chopped
3.  Get garlic cloves chopped
4.  Take Ginger chopped
5.  Get Ampalaya sliced
6.  Take Kalabasa peeled and chopped
7.  Make ready Sitaw cut
8.  Prepare Tomatoes chopped
9.  Make ready Shrimp paste or bagoong
10.  Get Eggplant sliced diagonally

##### Steps to make Shrimp Pinakbet:

1.  In hot pan with oil sauté garlic until fragrant onions translucent ginger TIL fragrant and tomatoes then add bagoong and stir then add water
2.  Bring to a boil then lower heat bring to a simmer add remaining Vegetables cover and simmer
3.  Add shrimps then cover and simmer when shrimp is done turn off heat serve
