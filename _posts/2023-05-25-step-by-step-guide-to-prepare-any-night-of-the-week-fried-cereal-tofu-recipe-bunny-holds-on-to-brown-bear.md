##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Cereal Tofu Recipe \[Bunny holds on to brown bear\]:

2.  Prepare 1 packet tofu

4.  Get Corn flour
5.  Prepare Some butter

9.  Make ready Seaweed

11.  Prepare Brown Bear

##### Instructions to make Fried Cereal Tofu Recipe \[Bunny holds on to brown bear\]:

1.  Beat the egg, add some salt to the egg mixture.
2.  Cut the tofu to the shape you like, I cut it into triangle shape to represent the mountains.
3.  Coat it with the egg, then corn flour and shallow fried till golden brown.
4.  Melt the butter in the pan, stir-fry the cereal till golden brown. Add the fried tofu back to the pan and coat well with the cereal mixture.
5.  Create the bunny shape with cooked rice. Add features using cut seaweed. Add the heart-shaped ham as the parachute with finely cut seaweed as the string.
6.  Create the brown bear by mixing the cooked rice with some soya sauce. Mould it into a bear shape. Add the cheese and seaweed as the features.
