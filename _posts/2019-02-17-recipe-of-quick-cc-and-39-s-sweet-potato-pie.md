2.  Prepare 3 large sweet potatoes
3.  Make ready 3 large eggs
4.  Prepare 1 cup milk
5.  Get 1/2 cup sugar
6.  Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
7.  Take 1 tbsp vanilla extract
8.  Take 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
9.  Get 1 tsp ground nutmeg
10.  Make ready 2 tbsp lemon juice
11.  Get 1 stick butter or margarine
12.  Make ready 2 frozen pie shells (i used pet ritz)
13.  Prepare 5 medium sheets of aluminum foil

##### Instructions to make CC'S SWEET POTATO PIE:

2.  Let potatoes cool , cut them in half take off skin and put in a large bowl mash and set aside
3.  Put lemon juice in milk and set aside for about 3-5 minutes
4.  Add eggs slightly beaten to sweet potatoes
5.  Add melted butter, milk, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar to sweet potatoes mix well
6.  With a hand mixer, mix until well blended and smooth no lumps.
7.  ATTENTION: before you pour into pie shells now would be the time to taste your sweet potatoe mix adjust spices and sugar if necessary.
