##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Tuna Pasta Salad:

1.  Get 1 box elbow noodles
2.  Prepare 2 cans tuna drained
3.  Prepare 4 oz frozen peas
4.  Take 1 small onion diced
5.  Prepare 1 1/2 cup mayo
6.  Prepare 2 tsp red wine vinegar
7.  Take 1 cup shredded cheddar
8.  Take 1/2 cup dill pickle relish

##### Instructions to make Easy Tuna Pasta Salad:

1.  Boil noodles according to package.
2.  Cook peas and cool.
3.  Mix all ingredients and chill. Serve cold.
