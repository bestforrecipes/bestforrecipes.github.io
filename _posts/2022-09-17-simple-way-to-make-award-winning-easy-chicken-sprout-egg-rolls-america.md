##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Chicken- Sprout Egg Rolls #america:

1.  Get Egg wraps, (usaully in your produce department)
2.  Make ready Fresh bean sprouts, rinsed, drained, and pat dried
3.  Prepare Broccoli Slaw, remove all broccoli and large pieces of cabbage or cut them up into same size pieces as the slaw
4.  Make ready large onion, chopped
5.  Take each; soy sauce and oyster sauce
6.  Take Chinese five spices
7.  Take salt and white pepper
8.  Take "You can add cut up Shrimp or Chicken if you like as well; to this mixture."

##### Instructions to make Easy Chicken- Sprout Egg Rolls #america:

1.  In a large bowl mix all ingredients together. Set aside.
2.  You take one wrapper, add 1to 2 tbsp. full of mixture; then wrap from corner first….
3.  Pull corner over mixture and roll and then fold the two sides in over mixture and roll all the way… brush with water to seal last corner. (Refer to attached picture)…Set in frying basket and fry @ 350° for 2 to 3 minutes; remove and drain on paper towels..,then enjoy!
