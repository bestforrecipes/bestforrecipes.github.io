##### The ingredients needed to make Mixed seafood fried rice noodles:

1.  Prepare 10 Dry shiitake mushrooms
2.  Take 1 Carrot
3.  Prepare 1 Leek
4.  Prepare 1 Tablespoon Dry shrimps or Brown shrimp
5.  Make ready 225 g Bamboo shoots
6.  Make ready 1 Tablespoon Shallot sauce
7.  Prepare 300 ml Water
8.  Get 1 Tablespoon Fried crispy onion
9.  Make ready 1 Tablespoon Fried crispy garlic
10.  Take 2-3 Tablespoons Soy sauce
11.  Prepare 1 Tablespoon Black vinegar
12.  Prepare 1 Tablespoon Sesame oil

##### Steps to make Mixed seafood fried rice noodles:

1.  Soak the dry mushrooms in a warm water until they turn soft. And then slice the mushrooms and add a tiny bit salt.
2.  Heat the frying pan and add a bit oil. Fry the mushrooms, carrot and leek until they become softened.
3.  Add in some dry shrimps or brown shrimp.
4.  Add in some bamboo shoots.
5.  Add in the shallot sauce.
6.  Pour in water to cover the ingredients and cook for 2-3 minutes.
7.  Add in the rice noodles. Let the noodles soak in the soup and put the lid on for 4 minutes.
8.  Add in mixed seafood, soy sauce, fried onion and garlic.
9.  Add in a bunch of coriander and mix well. Finally just add in black vinegar and sesame oil. Enjoy.
