##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Pasta:

1.  Make ready 1 box pasta
2.  Prepare 1 cup shrimp
3.  Make ready 1/2 heavy cream
4.  Prepare 1 cup any kind of cheese
5.  Take 2 Tbsp cream cheese
6.  Prepare 2 cup brocoli and yellow pepper
7.  Take Salt and pepper
8.  Make ready 1/2 onion
9.  Prepare 4 garlic clove
10.  Make ready 1 teaspoon garlic and onion powder

##### Instructions to make Shrimp Pasta:

1.  Cut the onion and garlic very small. Cook in the pan with olive oil
2.  Cook the pasta al dente
3.  Cook the pepper and brocolli and shrimp. Mix all the ingredient together
