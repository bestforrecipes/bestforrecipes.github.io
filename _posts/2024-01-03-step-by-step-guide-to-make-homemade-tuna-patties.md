##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Patties:

1.  Take 4 can Tuna (in water)
2.  Take 1 cup Whole Wheat Bread Crumbs
3.  Take 4 tbsp Omega-3 Mayo Light
4.  Take 1/4 cup Sunflower Seeds
5.  Make ready Chopped Onion
6.  Get Garlic Powder

##### Instructions to make Tuna Patties:

1.  Mix all ingredients into large mixing bowl
2.  Form mixture into patties
3.  Grill in pan on medium heat
