##### The ingredients needed to make Spaghetti With Tuna:

1.  Take Spaghetti 1 long packet
2.  Get 1 Tin tuna
3.  Get Black olives any amount
4.  Prepare 2 Tomatoes
5.  Prepare Hot pepper any amount
6.  Prepare Tomato sauce
7.  Prepare to taste Salt

##### Instructions to make Spaghetti With Tuna:

1.  Boil the spaghetti with Tuna
2.  Blend tomatoes and add in the spaghetti
3.  Add hot pepper and salt to taste
4.  Let the spaghetti gets dry, serve on a plate and spread olives and sauce. Serve with any preferred juice!
