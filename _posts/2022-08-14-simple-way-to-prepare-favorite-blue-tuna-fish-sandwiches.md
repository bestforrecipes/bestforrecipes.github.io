##### The ingredients needed to make Blue Tuna Fish Sandwiches:

1.  Prepare 1 can tuna in water
2.  Prepare 3 tbsp chopped onion
3.  Take 1 tbsp chopped scallions
4.  Get 3 tbsp Blue cheese dressing
5.  Make ready salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Blue Tuna Fish Sandwiches:

1.  Drain tuna.
2.  Add all the ingredients to tuna.
3.  Serve on bread or lettuce. Rye bread tastes best. Enjoy! :-)
