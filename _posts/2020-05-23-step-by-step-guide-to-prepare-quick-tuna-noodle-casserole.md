##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Make ready 1 lb egg noodles
2.  Take 3 cans cream of mushroom soup
3.  Get 4 cans tuna in oil
4.  Get 1/2 cup milk
5.  Prepare 1/4 cup butter
6.  Take 8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
7.  Prepare Italian bread crumbs

##### Steps to make Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Boil water, add egg noodles. Cook until tender. Drain.
2.  Put noodles back into pan.
3.  Add butter, cream of mushroom soup and tuna. Stir till well combined. Add milk and stir. Continue to cook until mixed well. Add cheese and bread crumbs. Let sit on low heat until cheese melts.

Fold in the tuna, followed by the cooked egg noodles and shredded cheddar cheese.
