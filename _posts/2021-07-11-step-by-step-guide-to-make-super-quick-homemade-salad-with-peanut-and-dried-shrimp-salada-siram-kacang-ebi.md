##### The ingredients needed to make Salad with Peanut and Dried Shrimp (SALADA SIRAM KACANG EBI):

1.  Make ready 100 gr sweet potato greens, use leaves and boil
2.  Get 100 gr kale, separate leaves from ribs then boiled
3.  Get 150 gr cucumber, halved and thinly sliced in rounds
4.  Make ready 100 gr cabbage, finely sliced
5.  Make ready 200 gr potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks and fried
6.  Get 100 gr cassava crackers
7.  Make ready 2 round eggplants, cut into pieces
8.  Get 1 block fried tofu, cut into cubes
9.  Make ready 2 piece fried tempeh, cut into cubs
10.  Get 4 eggs, boiled
11.  Take SAUCE
12.  Prepare 200 gr peanuts, fried/roasted
13.  Get 1 tsp salt
14.  Make ready 4 tsp fine sugar
15.  Get 3 pcs curly red chili
16.  Get 2 pcs red chili (bird's eye)
17.  Take 2 Tbsp toasted ebi (small shrimp)
18.  Take 450 ml hot water
19.  Prepare 1 tbsp vinegar

##### Instructions to make Salad with Peanut and Dried Shrimp (SALADA SIRAM KACANG EBI):

1.  SAUCE : Mix peanuts, salt, sugar, and chilis. Blend until smooth, add the dried shrimp.
2.  Pour hot water and stir well. Add vinegar and stir.
3.  Place the sweet potato leaves, kale, cucumber, cabbage, potatoes, eggplant, on the base plate.
4.  Add the tofu, tempeh and eggs.
5.  Sprinkle seasoning and sprinkle on top cassava crackers and serve.
