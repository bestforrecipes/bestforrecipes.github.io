##### The ingredients needed to make Rice Balls with Soy Sauce, Bonito Flakes and Sesame Salt:

1.  Prepare 180 grams Plain cooked rice
2.  Prepare 1 packet ☆ Bonito flakes
3.  Take 1/3 tsp ☆ Sugar
4.  Get 1 1/2 tsp ☆ Soy sauce
5.  Make ready For garnishing
6.  Take 1 Gomashio (salt with sesame seeds)

##### Steps to make Rice Balls with Soy Sauce, Bonito Flakes and Sesame Salt:

1.  Mix the ☆ ingredients in a bowl.
2.  Add the rice and mix well.
3.  Shape into whatever you like, sprinkle gomashio on top, and it's ready.
4.  Serve.
