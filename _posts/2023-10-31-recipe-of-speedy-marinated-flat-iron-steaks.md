##### The ingredients needed to make Marinated Flat Iron Steaks:

1.  Get 3 tbsp. Honey
2.  Make ready 3 tbsp. Balsamic vinegar
3.  Take 3 tbsp. Olive oil
4.  Make ready 1 tbsp. Fresh lemon juice or pineapple juice
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
6.  Prepare 1/2 tsp. Dried tarragon
7.  Take 1/2 tsp. Dried rosemary
8.  Prepare 1 tsp. Minced garlic
9.  Make ready 1/2 tsp. White pepper
10.  Take 1 pinch salt
11.  Make ready 1 1/2 pound flat iron steak
