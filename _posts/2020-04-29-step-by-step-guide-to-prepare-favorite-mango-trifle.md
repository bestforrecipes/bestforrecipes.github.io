##### The ingredients needed to make Mango trifle:

1.  Get 4 tbsp Mango custurd powder
2.  Prepare 1 kg Milk
3.  Make ready 1 packet Mango jelly
4.  Prepare 1 cup Mango puree
5.  Make ready Mango for decoration
6.  Make ready 1 packet Cream
7.  Prepare 1 cup Marie biscuits crushed
8.  Prepare 100 gms Butter melted
9.  Prepare 1 tbsp Agar agar powder
10.  Take 8 tbsp Sugar

##### Steps to make Mango trifle:

1.  First melt butter and crushed Marie biscuits. Now mix them both. Put them in dish and pressed with the help of your hand.
2.  Refrigerate to set.
3.  Now make mango custurd dissolve 4 tbsp custard powder and add sugar in 1 kg milk, when sugar dissolves add dissolve custurd powder and make custard.
4.  Now dissolve agar agar powder in some water and add them in custard mix well. Cook for a while.
5.  Now put them in dish above biscuits layer. Refregirate for about 2 hours. Now the third layer is whiped cream.
6.  Fourth layer :Now dissolve mango jelly in 2 tbsp water and pour them in mango puree. Mix well.
