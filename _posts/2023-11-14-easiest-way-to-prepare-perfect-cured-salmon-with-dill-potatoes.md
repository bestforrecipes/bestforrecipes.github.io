##### The ingredients needed to make Cured Salmon with Dill Potatoes:

1.  Prepare gravlax (cured salmon)
2.  Take butter
3.  Take flour
4.  Take milk
5.  Prepare cooked potatoes
6.  Make ready chopped dill
7.  Take crushed white pepper
8.  Take salt
9.  Make ready sliced lemon

##### Instructions to make Cured Salmon with Dill Potatoes:

1.  Cube the cooked potatoes. Keep aside.
2.  Melt butter and add flour.
3.  Dilute with milk while whisking to form a smooth sauce
4.  Boil for 3-5 minutes.
5.  Mix in the potatoes and chopped dill.
6.  Season with salt and pepper.
7.  Serve on plates with the salmon and garnish with dill stick and a slice of lemon.
8.  Enjoy.
