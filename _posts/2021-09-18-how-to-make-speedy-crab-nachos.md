##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Nachos:

1.  Get 1 bag of tortilla chips
2.  Get 1 bag of Mexican mix cheese
3.  Take 1 can lump crab
4.  Take 1 pico de gallo
5.  Get 1 Avocado
6.  Get 1 bunch cilantro
7.  Get 1 dash old bay

##### Instructions to make Crab Nachos:

1.  Layer the tortillas with the cheese and crab
2.  Place the Nachos in the oven on 375 until the cheese melts
3.  When the Nachos come out the oven sprinkle the Nachos with the old bay
4.  Top the Nachos with pico de gallo and avocado
