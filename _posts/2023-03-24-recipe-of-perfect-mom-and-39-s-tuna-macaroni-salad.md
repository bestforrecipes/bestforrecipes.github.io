##### The ingredients needed to make Mom's Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Prepare 1 packages elbow macaroni
2.  Take 1 can tuna
3.  Make ready 3 hard boiled eggs
4.  Prepare 1/2 cup mayonnaise or miracle whip
5.  Take 2 tbsp olive oil
6.  Prepare 1/4 cup diced onion
7.  Make ready 1/4 cup pickle relish
8.  Take salt and pepper (approximately 1 tsp each)
9.  Make ready 1 tsp paprika

##### Instructions to make Mom's Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Cook macaroni according to packag, drain

3.  In a large bowl, mix all the other ingredients
4.  Add macaroni to bowl and top with sliced eggs.
5.  Sprinkle with paprika
6.  Chill for 1 hour
