##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna marinade:

1.  Get 250 g tuna
2.  Get 1/2 onion
3.  Prepare 3 cm green onion
4.  Prepare For dressing
5.  Make ready 10 ml oil
6.  Get 40 ml vinegar
7.  Prepare 1 teaspoon honey
8.  Take Minced garlic
9.  Make ready 10 ml soy sauce with citrus juice (ajipon)

Preheat the grill to HIGH heat. Remove the steaks from marinade (discard any marinade) and place on a plate. Season lightly with freshly ground black pepper on both sides. Shake excess moisture from the steaks.

##### Steps to make Tuna marinade:

1.  Slice tuna into bite pieces.
2.  Slice onion. Mince the green onion.
3.  To make the Japanese dressing, combine all the seasonings, oil, vinegar, honey,minced garlic and soy sauce with citrus juice.
