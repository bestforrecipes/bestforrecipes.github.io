2.  Take 2 Tbs olive oil
3.  Get 100 g butter
4.  Make ready 4 cloves crushed garlic
5.  Make ready 1 tsp chives
6.  Take 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
7.  Get 1/2 tsp chopped parsley
8.  Prepare cracked pepper
9.  Prepare 3 Tbs chopped spicy chorizo (or any spicy salami)
