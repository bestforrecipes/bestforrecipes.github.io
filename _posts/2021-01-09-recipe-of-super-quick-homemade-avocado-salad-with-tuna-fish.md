##### Steps to make Avocado Salad with Tuna Fish:

1.  Cut the avocados in two parts and remove the seeds.
2.  Cut the avocados in cubes in a clean bowl
3.  Add the tuna fish
4.  Diced the onion in
5.  Add the black pepper,salt and olive oil
6.  Chop the lettuce leaf very small and add to the salad
7.  Stir all together and put in fridge to chill
8.  Then serve with any meal of your choice
