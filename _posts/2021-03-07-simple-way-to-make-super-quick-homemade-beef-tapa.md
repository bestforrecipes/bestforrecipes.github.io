##### The ingredients needed to make Beef tapa:

1.  Take 1 1/2 lb Beef sirloin thinly slice
2.  Prepare 5 tbsp soy sauce
3.  Prepare 4 tbsp oyster sauce
4.  Prepare salt
5.  Get pepper
6.  Take 2 tsp. brown sugar
7.  Prepare 5 cloves minced garlic

##### Instructions to make Beef tapa:

1.  Mix all the ingredients and marinade the beef for 10hrs to 15hrs then place on the refrigerator.
2.  In a frying pan place the beef and cover it with lid to simmer. This will help the beef to be tender. Slowly add the remaining marinade sauce until it evaporates. Then add a little bit of oil to fry.
3.  Serve the beef with brown rice and egg.
