##### The ingredients needed to make Stacked Baked Crab Sticks, Corns and Cheese:

1.  Take 2 tbsp or less Frozen corns
2.  Get 2 Crab sticks
3.  Make ready 1 Sliced cheese
4.  Take 1 as required Salt and pepper
5.  Prepare 1 as required Dried parsley

##### Steps to make Stacked Baked Crab Sticks, Corns and Cheese:

1.  Cut the crab sticks into small pieces and quarter the sliced cheese.
2.  Fry the crab sticks and corn kernels and season with salt and pepper. Divide into 4 portions.
3.  Put 1/4 of Step 2 into a paper cup and lay a sliced cheese on top. Put another 1/4 of Step 2 and lay another sliced cheese on. Repeat.
4.  The residual heat from the fried crab sticks and corn will melt the cheese.
