##### The ingredients needed to make Grilled Eggplant x tuna:

1.  Make ready 1 eggplant
2.  Prepare 1 tbsp tahini
3.  Take 1 tinned tuna
4.  Prepare 1 pinch salt
5.  Make ready 1 clove garlic
6.  Get 1 lime juice if u like

##### Steps to make Grilled Eggplant x tuna:

1.  Cut eggplant and garlic. Grill in the oven till it's soft
2.  Put salt, tahini and drained tinned tuna. Mix together.
3.  It's ready for serve! Add lime juice if you like:)
