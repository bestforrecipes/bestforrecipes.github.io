##### The ingredients needed to make Zucchini with Bonito Flakes, Mayonnaise, and Wasabi:

1.  Take 1 Zucchini
2.  Get 1/2 tsp Salt
3.  Take 1 tbsp ●Mayonnaise (light mayonnaise is also fine)
4.  Take 2 cm plus ●Grated wasabi
5.  Prepare 1 half a pack ●Bonito flakes
6.  Make ready 1 ●Grated garlic
7.  Make ready 1 ●Black pepper
8.  Prepare 1 few drops Dashi soy sauce (or mentsuyu)

##### Instructions to make Zucchini with Bonito Flakes, Mayonnaise, and Wasabi:

1.  Slice the zucchini into 1-2 mm rounds. Mix the salt and set aside for 10 minutes. Afterwards, rinse off the salt and squeeze the zucchini with your hands to drain excess water.
2.  Combine all ● ingredients in a bowl. Taste, and season with dashi soy sauce. (It would be too strong if you added it from the beginning).
