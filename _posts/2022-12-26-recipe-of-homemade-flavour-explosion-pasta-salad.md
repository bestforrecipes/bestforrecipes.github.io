##### The ingredients needed to make Flavour explosion pasta salad:

1.  Take 2 cups pasta
2.  Prepare Boiling water
3.  Take Salt
4.  Take 1/2 small bell pepper
5.  Get Green part of one large spring onion
6.  Prepare 1 small tomato
7.  Get 1/2 cup pomegranate
8.  Make ready 2/3 cup sweetcorn
9.  Prepare 6 heaped tbsp mayonnaise
10.  Make ready Pinch salt and pepper
11.  Take 1 can tuna

##### Instructions to make Flavour explosion pasta salad:

1.  Put the pasta in a pan and cover with boiling water and sprinkle salt. Cook for about 10 minutes or until pasta is soft then drain.
2.  Chop up half the bell pepper and the green part of the spring onion
3.  Chop up one small tomato
4.  Add the chopped ingredients to the bowl with pasta along with the sweetcorn and pomegranate and mix
5.  In a separate bowl mix together the mayonnaise and tuna along with a pinch of salt and pepper. Then add to the large bowl. Enjoy!
