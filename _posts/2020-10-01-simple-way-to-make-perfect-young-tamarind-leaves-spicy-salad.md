##### The ingredients needed to make Young Tamarind Leaves Spicy Salad:

1.  Get 150 grams young tamarind leaves ,cleaned
2.  Make ready 5 roasted dried chili
3.  Prepare 1 tbsp cloves of Thai garlic
4.  Take 2 rocks of seasalt
5.  Take 3 shallots
6.  Prepare 1 tsp shrimps paste
7.  Prepare 1 onion,sliced
8.  Get 1 tomato sliced
9.  Get 2 can canned sardines
10.  Prepare 1 crispy pork skin,or crispy bacon
11.  Take 1 fishsauce to taste
12.  Prepare 1/3 tbsp oil to fry chili paste
13.  Get 1 tbsp water

##### Instructions to make Young Tamarind Leaves Spicy Salad:

1.  Unknown special young tamarind leaves spicy salad ,my favorite dish :) start from clean and drain fresh young tamarind leavez,set aside
2.  Make chili paste ,by pestle and mortar grind roasted chili with little clove of garlic,sea salt,add shrimps paste,shallots ,
3.  Fry chili paste with oil on medium heat,stir,add water,let it cool
4.  In mixing bowl ,add tamarind leaves,sliced tomato,onion,add sardines,crispy pork,cool chilli paste,fish sauce,then softly stir to mix ..serve as simple salad or serve with rice :)
