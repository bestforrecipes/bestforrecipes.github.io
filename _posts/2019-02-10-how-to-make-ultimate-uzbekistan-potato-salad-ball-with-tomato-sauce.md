##### The ingredients needed to make Uzbekistan Potato Salad Ball with Tomato Sauce:

1.  Get 2 potatoes
2.  Prepare 2 small carrots
3.  Prepare 1 Salmon (i replaced it with 1/4 gr chicken breast)
4.  Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
5.  Prepare 1 tsp soy sauce
6.  Take 1 cup yoghurt
7.  Make ready Cheese
8.  Get 1 parsley
9.  Prepare 1 boiled egg
10.  Make ready Sesame/tomato sauce

##### Steps to make Uzbekistan Potato Salad Ball with Tomato Sauce:

1.  Boil or steam potatoes and carrots
2.  Boil salmon or chicken breast
3.  Shred boiled potatoes, carrots, egg and cheese. Chop-chop salmon or chicken breast, and parsley
4.  Mix step 2 with 1 tsp soy sauce, 1 cup yoghurt, 1/2 tsp salt
5.  Make a ball
6.  Pour it with sesame. I don't have sesame stock at home, so i use tomato sauce
7.  Serve it
