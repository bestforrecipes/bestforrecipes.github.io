##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Sautéed Shrimp:

1.  Make ready Shrimp
2.  Make ready Spanish Ñora Peppers Powder
3.  Prepare Salted Butter
4.  Make ready Pisco, Grapa, Orujo or any other liquor made from distilled grape juice
5.  Prepare Salt

##### Steps to make Easy Sautéed Shrimp:

1.  Wash the Shrimp with plenty of water.
2.  Preheat a pan and add Butter, Salt and the Shrimp.
3.  Add the Ñora Peppers.
4.  Let the Shrimp cook for 5 min at medium heat.
5.  Add the Pisco and let cook for another 5 min.
6.  Serve and enjoy!
