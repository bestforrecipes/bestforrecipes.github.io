##### The ingredients needed to make Japanese Pancake ‘Okonomiyaki ‘:

1.  Get 1/2 cup flour
2.  Prepare 1 cup water
3.  Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Dashi powder (Japanese fish bouillon powder)
4.  Prepare 4 pieces cabbage shredded
5.  Make ready 1 egg
6.  Make ready 1/2 cup Tempura flake (Tenkasu)
7.  Take 1 tablespoon bonito flake
8.  Get 1 tablespoon dried small shrimp
9.  Take 1 tablespoon minced chicken or other meat
10.  Make ready Okonomiyaki sauce
11.  Prepare Mayonnaise

##### Instructions to make Japanese Pancake ‘Okonomiyaki ‘:

1.  Mix flour, Dashi powder and water
2.  Today I don’t have Tempura flakes, so I use vegetable Tempura and cut small pieces
3.  Pour flour mixture on hot pan with salad oil. And make like crepe.
4.  Put cabbage and other ingredients on it.

6.  Egg in center
7.  Pour over the rest of flour mixture
8.  Bake the other side.
9.  Surface is crispy when it baked.
10.  Eat with Okonomiyaki sauce mayonnaise or soy sauce
11.  Bonito flake on Okonomiyaki
