##### The ingredients needed to make Beef Florentine Stuffed Shells:

1.  Get ground beef
2.  Make ready jumbo shells
3.  Prepare raw leaf spinach
4.  Take Tblsp Italian seasoning
5.  Make ready ricotta cheese
6.  Get spaghetti sauce
7.  Make ready shredded mozzarella cheese

##### Steps to make Beef Florentine Stuffed Shells:

1.  Brown ground beef in a sauce pan over medium-high heat breaking into pieces. Drain excess grease.
2.  Toss spinach in sauce pan with browned ground beef. Cover stirring frequently until spinach shrinks down.
3.  Stir ricotta cheese and Italian seasoning in to ground beef until blended.
4.  Pour half the spaghetti sauce into a lightly greased 2-quart cake pan.
5.  Fill shells with a spoon until full. Place stuffed shells, stuffing side up, in spaghetti sauce. Until filling is gone.
6.  Pour remaining spaghetti sauce over stuffed shells.
7.  Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350°F for 30 to 35 minutes or until pasta is soft. Uncover sprinkle with cheese and bake an additional 5 minutes or until the cheese melts.
