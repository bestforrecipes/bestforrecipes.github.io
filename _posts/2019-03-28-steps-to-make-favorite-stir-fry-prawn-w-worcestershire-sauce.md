##### The ingredients needed to make Stir Fry Prawn w/ Worcestershire sauce:

1.  Make ready 1 kg prawn
2.  Prepare 1 yellow onion (slices)
3.  Prepare 2 cloves garlic (minced)
4.  Make ready 2 stalks spring onion (chopped)
5.  Get 1 tbsp light soy sauce
6.  Prepare 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
7.  Prepare 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce
8.  Take 1 cup water
9.  Make ready Cooking oil
10.  Make ready Salt
11.  Prepare Pepper
12.  Get Sugar

##### Instructions to make Stir Fry Prawn w/ Worcestershire sauce:

1.  Clean the prawn.
2.  Heat oil. Add yellow onion, garlic and half of chopped spring onion and cook until fragrant (onion turns translucent).
3.  Add soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, sweet soy sauce, salt, pepper, sugar and water. Adjust the taste.
4.  Add the prawn, stir and cook until the prawn have cooked through.
5.  Garnish with the leftover spring onion. Ready to serve.
