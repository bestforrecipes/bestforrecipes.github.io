##### The ingredients needed to make Crab meat cakes:

1.  Take 1 pound crab meat
2.  Take 1/4 cup cream sauce
3.  Get 2 eggs
4.  Make ready 8 ounces mushrooms
5.  Take 1/2 cup butter
6.  Take 4 tablespoons flour
7.  Take 2 cups cream
8.  Make ready to taste Salt,pepper paprika
9.  Prepare 1/2 cup cooking sherry

##### Steps to make Crab meat cakes:

1.  Form crab meat into cake by mixing with little cream sauce
