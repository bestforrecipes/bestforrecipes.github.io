##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Beetroot nicoise:

1.  Get 1 boiled egg
2.  Take 1/2 jar good quality tuna
3.  Take Handful spinach
4.  Get Few chopped radish
5.  Get 1/4 chopped cucumber
6.  Prepare 1/2 Tin sweetcorn
7.  Prepare 2 anchovies
8.  Prepare Handful any cooked pasta shapes
9.  Take 2 small pickled beets sliced
10.  Get Glug of the beetroot vinegar to flavour and colour the cooked pasta

##### Instructions to make Tuna Beetroot nicoise:

1.  Add the tuna, anchovies, sweetcorn and beetroot juice to the cooked pasta and pop into a bowl.
2.  Add the spinach, radish, cucumber and beetroot to the bowl along with the boiled egg. Season and serve.
