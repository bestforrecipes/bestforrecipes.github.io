##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Rice with Salmon Flakes:

1.  Take rice bowls' worth Plain cooked rice
2.  Take Salmon flakes
3.  Make ready Eggs
4.  Make ready Green onions or scallions
5.  Make ready White sesame seeds (optional)
6.  Make ready Sesame oil
7.  Make ready Salt and pepper
8.  Take Soy sauce

##### Steps to make Fried Rice with Salmon Flakes:

1.  Finely chop the green onion. Add some salt (not listed) to the egg and beat it.
2.  Heat up the sesame oil in a frying pan and pour in the beaten egg from Step 1. Add the rice immediately and cook over high heat.
3.  Mix and stir-fry the egg and rice, then add the salmon flakes and the green onion. Continue cooking over medium heat.
4.  Once Step 3 has been lightly cooked, add the white sesame seeds and the soy sauce. Once the flavors have blended, add salt and pepper to taste. Cook over low heat and serve.
