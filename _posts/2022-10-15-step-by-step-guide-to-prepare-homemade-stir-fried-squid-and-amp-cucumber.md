##### The ingredients needed to make Stir-fried Squid & Cucumber:

1.  Get 1 squid (gutted, cleaned, cut into 5~7mm round slices)
2.  Get Small quantity of salt, pepper, flour for squid
3.  Prepare 1 cucumber (cut in half lengthwise, cut into 5~7mm slices, sprinkle salt and leave to drain moisture out well)
4.  Make ready 1 stick celery (cut into 3cm length)
5.  Prepare 1 tsp ginger (chopped finely)
6.  Take 1/2 ~1 tsp garlic (chopped finely)
7.  Get 1 pinch crushed red pepper
8.  Get 2 tbsp \* sake
9.  Prepare 1 tbsp \* soy sauce
10.  Take 1/2 ~1 tsp \* chicken broth(chicken stock powder)
11.  Take 1 tbsp vegetable oil
12.  Take 1 tsp sesame oil

##### Steps to make Stir-fried Squid & Cucumber:

1.  Remove water from the squid well, sprinkle salt, peppe and flour, shake off excess.
2.  Heat the vegetable oil in a pan, stir-fry ginger and crushed red pepper with low heat. Add squid, celery, cucumber and cook for 1~2 mins over high heat. Add seasonings marked with \* and keep stir-frying until the flavor blended.
3.  To finish it off, pour sesame oil from the edge of the pan to inside of it, stir once.
