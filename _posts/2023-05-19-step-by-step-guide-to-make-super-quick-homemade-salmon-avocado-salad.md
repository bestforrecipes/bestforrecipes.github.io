##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Avocado Salad:

1.  Prepare Leaves
2.  Make ready 2 salmon oven 20mins 180C
3.  Take 2 avocados
4.  Get Salt, pepper, olive oil, parsley, 1/2 lemon, balsamic vinaigrett
5.  Prepare 1 slice salmon fume
6.  Make ready Cucumber / courgette
7.  Get Onions (1/4)

##### Steps to make Salmon Avocado Salad:

1.  Season salmon with salt pepper parsley. Over 180C for 20mins
2.  Cut cucumber into thin slices, cut avocado, onions, salmon fume
3.  Sauce of olive oil, salt, pepper and parsley, balsamic vinaigrette (1 tsp), lemon juice. Heat over quickly
4.  Put 1/2 sauce on cooked salmon. Mix salmon with everything else. Put the rest sauce and mix
