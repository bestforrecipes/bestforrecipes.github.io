##### The ingredients needed to make Kanya's Spicy Prawns Pasta / Champion Pasta :):

1.  Get 70 grams pasta
2.  Prepare 4 prawns
3.  Make ready 1 tsp finely chopped ginger
4.  Take 3 finely chopped hot chili
5.  Make ready 1 clove garlic,finely chopped
6.  Make ready 1 shallots,thinny sliced
7.  Prepare 1 bunch mints
8.  Prepare 3 cherry tomato
9.  Get 1/2 tbsp lime juice
10.  Prepare 1 tsp fish sauce
11.  Get 1/4 tsp sugar to taste
12.  Make ready 1 salt and pepper to taste prawns

##### Steps to make Kanya's Spicy Prawns Pasta / Champion Pasta :):

1.  Cook pasta in salted water about 7 mins
2.  Season prawns with salt and pepper and drizzles some lime juice,then grill total about 1 min per each side ,or to your preference,optional i use bamboo skewers to grill prawns..
3.  Finely chop other ingredients ,except tomato and mints,then seasoning by lime juice,fishsauce,little salt and sugar to taste
4.  Softly mix all ,serve :)
