##### The ingredients needed to make Really Yummy Macaroni Salad:

1.  Take 1 lb Elbow Macaroni
2.  Get 14 oz (2 cans) Albacore tuna, drained
3.  Make ready 2 Celery stalks, chopped
4.  Get 1 Carrot (grated)
5.  Get 1/2 Sweet Vidalia onion, chopped
6.  Make ready 1 cup Mayonnaise
7.  Get 1 as needed Salt

##### Instructions to make Really Yummy Macaroni Salad:

1.  Cook elbow macaroni until al dente.
2.  Mix in all other ingredients. You may want to reserve some of the shredded carrots to sprinkle on top as a garnish.
3.  Chill for about one hour before serving.
