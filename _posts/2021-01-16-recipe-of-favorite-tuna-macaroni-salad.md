##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Get 4-6 servings of your favorite shaped pasta noodles cooked
2.  Take 1 can tuna
3.  Make ready 1 can sweet green peas
4.  Prepare 1 cup miracle whip
5.  Get 1/2 onion finely chopped
6.  Make ready 1 teaspoon fresh chopped parsley
7.  Make ready 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella or grated parmesan
8.  Take Desired Amounts of the following;
9.  Get Dash Mrs
10.  Take Black or white garlic
11.  Prepare Salt
12.  Make ready Pepper
13.  Take Celery seed

##### Instructions to make Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Boil noodles to soften. Strain and pour into a large mixing bowl. Fold and gently mix in with all other ingredients.
2.  Add seasonings and spices to taste. Then cool in the fridge.
