##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp and Tofu Rice-Paper Fry:

1.  Take 100 g hard type tofu
2.  Prepare 15-20 small shrimp
3.  Make ready 30 g leek
4.  Make ready 1 tablespoon starch
5.  Prepare 1.5 teaspoon oyster sauce
6.  Take 1 egg white
7.  Take Rice paper

##### Instructions to make Shrimp and Tofu Rice-Paper Fry:

1.  Ingredients: cut shrimp
2.  Mix tofu, shrimp and seasoning.
3.  Put Tofu mixture on rice paper. Stick with flour-water removing air inside.
4.  Fry in 1cm deep oil.
5.  Serve with sweet chili sauce. Enjoy. Today’s dinner
