2.  Take 2 lemons…quartered
3.  Get 2 lbs. of small red potatoes
4.  Make ready 8 ears sweet corn
5.  Take 1 onion…cut in half
6.  Make ready 4 Tblsp salt
7.  Make ready 2 garlic gloves
8.  Prepare 2 packs sausage (preferably HillShire)
9.  Take cayenne pepper (optional)

##### Instructions to make Winter Shrimp Crab Boil:

2.  Add crab legs…boil 5 minutes
