##### The ingredients needed to make Kani Mango Roll:

1.  Prepare 9 Rice Paper
2.  Make ready 12 crab sticks, cooked and shredded
3.  Prepare 1 cucumber, seeded and sliced into thin strips
4.  Prepare 18 green ice lettuce leaves
5.  Prepare 3 tbsp Japanese mayonnaise
6.  Prepare 1 mango, sliced into thin strips
7.  Get Pinch salt
8.  Prepare Peanut Sauce:
9.  Make ready 2 tbsp peanut butter
10.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
11.  Get 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
12.  Make ready 1/2 tbsp honey
13.  Get 1/2 tbsp sesame oil
14.  Get 1/2 tbsp garlic, minced
15.  Get 3 tbsp hot water

##### Instructions to make Kani Mango Roll:

1.  Combine crab sticks and mayonnaise in a bowl. Season it with salt. Set aside
2.  Dip a piece of rice paper in a big bowl of water to soften it. Lay the softened wrapper on a clean chopping board.
3.  Line 2 lettuce leaves on the rice paper. Add 2 spoonful of crab mixture on top of the lettuce leaves. Arrange 2 cucumber sticks and top with 2 mango slices.
4.  Fold it on both side to seal each ends and roll the rice paper tightly to secure the filling. Slice it the center. Repeat to make a total of 9 rolls.
5.  For Peanut Sauce, combine all the ingredients and mix it until the sauce is thick.
6.  Arrange on a serving platter and serve with peanut sauce, Enjoy!
