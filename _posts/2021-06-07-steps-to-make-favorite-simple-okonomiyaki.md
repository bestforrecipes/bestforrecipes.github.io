##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Okonomiyaki:

1.  Make ready 1/4 cabbage (shredded coarsely)
2.  Take 2 beaten eggs
3.  Take 1 tsp salt
4.  Take 1 tbsp olive oil or vegrtables oil
5.  Make ready For toppings (as much as you like):
6.  Prepare Dried bonito flakes
7.  Take Green laver/aonori
8.  Prepare Mayonnaise
9.  Get Worcester sauce
10.  Prepare pickled ginger

##### Instructions to make Simple Okonomiyaki:

1.  Combine cabbage, salt, oil well in a bowl, after mixed, add beaten egg and stir once. Cook it over low heat in a pan for about 2~3 mins with a lid. (adjust the hardness of the eggs to you taste)
2.  Pour the sauce over it (and add mayonnaise if you like it). Top with dried bonito flakes, aonori, pikled ginger to your taste.
