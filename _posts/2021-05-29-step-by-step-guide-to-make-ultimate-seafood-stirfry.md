##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood Stirfry:

1.  Take shrimps
2.  Make ready calamari
3.  Prepare green pea
4.  Get diced carrot
5.  Take mixed veg
6.  Get olive oil
7.  Prepare salt
8.  Make ready knorr cube
9.  Make ready oregano
10.  Take fresh pepper diced
11.  Prepare onions diced

##### Instructions to make Seafood Stirfry:

1.  Wash shrimp and calamari and marinate with any spice of your choice and set aside
2.  Heat up frying pan and once its hot, add olive oil, stir in onions after a minute stir in shrimp and calamari
3.  Stir continuously for 3 - 5 minutes so it can evenly cook through, then add salt, pepper, knorr cube, oregano
4.  Stir in all mix vegetable, carrot and green peas, stir and remove from heat after 2 minutes
5.  Serve with quinoa for healthy eating
