##### The ingredients needed to make Cheese Pizza Quesadillas:

1.  Make ready 1 bunch Quesadilla Shells
2.  Take 1 cup Pizza Sauce
3.  Make ready 1 packages Mozzarella Cheese (or which ever you prefer)
4.  Prepare 1 Grated Cheese

##### Instructions to make Cheese Pizza Quesadillas:

1.  Place 1 shell in a skillet and warm it
2.  After warming it place it on a plate and toss another 1 into the skillet for the topper.
3.  Place a desired amount of sauce on the shell and spread it around
4.  Sprinkle the desired amount of cheese on told sauce evenly
5.  Shake on grated cheese
6.  Place the top shell on top
7.  Put back into skillet and warm up till the cheese glues everything together per say.
8.  Cut up I use a pizza cutter it's super easy! Plate and enjoy
