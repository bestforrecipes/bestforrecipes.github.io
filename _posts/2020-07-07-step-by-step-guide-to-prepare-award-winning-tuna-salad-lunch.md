##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Salad Lunch:

1.  Take 6 lettuce
2.  Make ready 4 large eggs
3.  Make ready 4 large carrots
4.  Prepare 1 large cucumbers
5.  Prepare 2 can tuna
6.  Get 1 large avocados
7.  Prepare 2 large tomatoes
8.  Get 4 tsp salt
9.  Get 4 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin

##### Instructions to make Tuna Salad Lunch:

1.  Chop the lettuce leaves and add to container. Set the eggs to boil.
2.  Add thinly sliced carrots.
3.  Add thinly sliced cucumber.
4.  Add two tunna cans and mix it to dissolve.
5.  Add the avocado and sliced tomatoes.
6.  Add the boiled eggs, salt and olive oil. Mix everything again and serve.
