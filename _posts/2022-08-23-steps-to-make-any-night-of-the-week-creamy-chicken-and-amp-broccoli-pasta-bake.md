##### The ingredients needed to make Creamy Chicken & Broccoli Pasta Bake:

1.  Prepare 1 lb medium pasta shells
2.  Take 6 tbsp butter
3.  Take 1 medium onion
4.  Prepare 2 tsp minced garlic
5.  Get 1 packages cream cheese, cubed
6.  Make ready 1 1/2 cup milk
7.  Prepare 1/2 cup chicken stock
8.  Take 3/4 cup parmesan cheese
9.  Make ready 1 pinch nutmeg
10.  Take 2 cup cooked chicken, chopped
11.  Get 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
12.  Prepare 2 cup broccoli

##### Instructions to make Creamy Chicken & Broccoli Pasta Bake:

1.  preheat oven to 350°F. cook pasta per package instructions. drain & set aside.
2.  melt butter with onion& garlic in a large saucepan over medium heat. saute until onions are translucent.
3.  add cream cheese and stir until smooth. whisk in milk & chicken stock; stir until the mixture starts to thicken. add 1/2 cup of parmesan, nutmeg, chicken & broccoli. when mixture simmers, add pasta & fold until well-coated.
4.  transfer the pasta into casserole dish & top with parmesan cheese & breadcrumbs. bake for 30 minutes or until top has browned.
