1.  Get 1 can (28 ounce) diced tomatoes
2.  Make ready 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
3.  Take 1 lb andouille sausage (polska kilbasa, or summer sausage work)
4.  Make ready 1 large white onion, chopped
5.  Make ready 2 clove garlic, minced
6.  Take 2 tsp dried oregano
7.  Get 1 large green bell pepper, chopped
8.  Prepare 1 cup celery, sliced
9.  Make ready 1 cup chicken broth
10.  Make ready 2 tsp blackened, or cajun seasoning
11.  Make ready 1 tsp cayenne pepper
12.  Prepare 1/2 tsp dried thyme
13.  Prepare 1 lb small cooked shrimp, no tails
