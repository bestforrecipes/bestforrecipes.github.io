##### Instructions to make baked salmon:

1.  preheat oven to 350
2.  on a baking sheet with edges lay out aluminum foil.
3.  place salmon fillets on baking sheet and salt.
4.  slice tomato, onion, and jalapeno and layer on salmon.
5.  place fresh cilantro on both fillets , drizzle with olive oil.
6.  cover with foil and bake about 30-45 minutes.
