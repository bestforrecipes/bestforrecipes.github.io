##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken and broccoli stuffed shells:

1.  Take 2 cups cooked chicken cut into small chunks
2.  Get 1 1/2 cups cooked broccoli cut into small chunks
3.  Prepare 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese
4.  Take 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
5.  Get 12 jumbo shells
6.  Get 2 cups milk
7.  Make ready 2 tbs flour
8.  Prepare 1/2 cup mozzarela cheese
9.  Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
10.  Prepare 2 tbs butter

##### Instructions to make Chicken and broccoli stuffed shells:

1.  Cook jumbo shells 10 min and rinse and set aside.
2.  Mix cooked chicken, broccoli,cream cheese and cheddar cheese together. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
3.  Heat milk in a pan and add butter. When it starts to boil slowly add flour until it thickens. Then add mozzarella cheese salt and pepper and stir.
4.  Place 1/2 cup of sauce mixture in bottom of pan and then stuff shells with chicken mixture. When all shells are stuffed and in pan pour the rest of the sauce over the shells.
5.  Bake in oven at 350 for 30 min and enjoy.
