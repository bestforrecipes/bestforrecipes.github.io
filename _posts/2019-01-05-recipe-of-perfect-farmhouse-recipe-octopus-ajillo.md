##### The ingredients needed to make \[Farmhouse Recipe\] Octopus Ajillo:

1.  Take 3 clove Garlic
2.  Get 50 grams Octopus
3.  Get 100 ml Olive oil
4.  Prepare 1 tsp Salt

##### Instructions to make \[Farmhouse Recipe\] Octopus Ajillo:

1.  Thinly slice the garlic into about 2 mm thick and the octopus into 5 mm thick.
2.  Put the olive oil and garlic in a saucepan. Cook over a medium heat.
3.  When the garlic releases its aroma, add the octopus and salt. Simmer for about 1 minute. It's ready to serve.
4.  Dip the baguette in this oil and enjoy.
