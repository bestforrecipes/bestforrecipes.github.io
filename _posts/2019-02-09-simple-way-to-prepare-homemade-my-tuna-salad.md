##### The ingredients needed to make My Tuna Salad:

1.  Make ready 2 cans tuna (in water)
2.  Take 2 hard boiled eggs
3.  Make ready 1 large stalk celery, diced (optional)
4.  Prepare 2 tbsp sweet relish or diced dill pickles
5.  Prepare 2 tbsp white onion (minced)
6.  Make ready 2 tsp yellow mustard
7.  Take 2 tsp dried parsley
8.  Take to taste Salt and pepper
9.  Prepare 1/2 tsp dried dill weed
10.  Make ready 1/2 tsp celery seed
11.  Get 1/2 tsp hot sauce (I use Frank's Red Hot Sauce)
12.  Prepare 1 tsp lemon juice

##### Steps to make My Tuna Salad:

1.  In a medium pot boil 2 large eggs.
2.  Add drained tuna to bowl and gently break up large chunks.
3.  Add eggs, mayonnaise, celery, pickles, onion, mustard, parsley, salt, pepper and/or optional dill, celery seed, hot sauce, and lemon juice.
4.  Gently stir everything together and serve in a sandwich, with crackers or on a salad. Or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator until ready to use. Enjoy!
