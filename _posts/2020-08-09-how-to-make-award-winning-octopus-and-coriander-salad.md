##### The ingredients needed to make Octopus and Coriander Salad:

1.  Make ready 1 octopus, whole
2.  Prepare 3 cloves garlic, chopped finely
3.  Take 1 pinch salt
4.  Prepare 1 handful chopped coriander
5.  Take 1 drizzle olive oil, extra virgin
6.  Prepare 1 drizzle vinegar

##### Instructions to make Octopus and Coriander Salad:

1.  In a pressure cooker, add water, salt and whole octopus. Cook the octopus for 10-15 minutes or for 45 minutes at boiling point in a normal pot.
2.  Remove from the water and let the octopus cool down completely.
3.  Cut the octopus into pieces.
4.  Add the chopped garlic, coriander and a pinch of salt.
5.  Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar.
6.  Stir well and place in fridge until serving time.
