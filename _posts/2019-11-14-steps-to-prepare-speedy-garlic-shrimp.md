##### The ingredients needed to make Garlic Shrimp🍤:

1.  Prepare Large shrimp
2.  Make ready Garlic clove
3.  Get Shallot(sub onion if you don’t have)
4.  Take Lemon
5.  Make ready Salt
6.  Get Pepper
7.  Get Red Pepper Flakes
8.  Get Olive oil

##### Steps to make Garlic Shrimp🍤:

1.  Gather Ingredients
2.  Chop up garlic and shallots
3.  Season shrimp with salt, pepper & red pepper flakes
4.  Sauté garlic and shallots with olive oil on low heat
5.  Add Shrimp and lemon juice and cook on low for 5 mins
6.  Garlic Shrimp🍤🍤
