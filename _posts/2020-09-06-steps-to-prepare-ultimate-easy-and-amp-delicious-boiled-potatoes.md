##### The ingredients needed to make Easy & Delicious Boiled Potatoes:

1.  Take 3 large Potatoes
2.  Take 2 tsp Salt
3.  Get 1 tbsp Margarine (or butter)
4.  Prepare 1 dash Parsley or aonori

##### Instructions to make Easy & Delicious Boiled Potatoes:

1.  Peel the potatoes and cut into bite-sized pieces. Place into a pot filled with water. (Once you've added all the potatoes, refresh with clean water until the potatoes are just barely covered.)
2.  Turn the heat to medium-high, and add the salt. Once it comes to a boil, turn the heat to low, cover with a lid, and cook for 6-7 minutes. Once a chopstick or skewer can pass through easily, turn off the heat.
3.  Lift the lid a little bit and drain the water.
4.  Add the margarine (or butter). Put on the lid and hold it shut as you shake the pot to coat the potatoes with the butter. Then, it's done! Transfer to a dish and garnish with parsley.
