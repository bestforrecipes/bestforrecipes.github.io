##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Crab Dip:

1.  Take 1 packages cream cheese (8 oz.) @ room temp
2.  Get 2 cup sour cream ( 16 oz )
3.  Make ready 3 can crabmeat, drained (6 oz. cans)
4.  Take 2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
5.  Prepare 3 green onions, finely snipped
6.  Get 1 paprika or old bay - to taste
7.  Get 1 lemon - sliced

##### Instructions to make Baked Crab Dip:

1.  Preheat oven to 350º
2.  In a medium mixing bowl, beat cream cheese till smooth. Add sour cream, mix well.
3.  Fold in crab, shredded cheese & finely snipped green onions.
4.  Place mixture in baking dish. Sprinkle with paprika or old bay.
5.  Bake uncovered for 45 minutes.
