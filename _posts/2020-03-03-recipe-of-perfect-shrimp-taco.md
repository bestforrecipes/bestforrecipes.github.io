##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Taco:

1.  Take 1 lb Cleaned & Rinsed Shrimp
2.  Make ready 1 packages Taco seasoning
3.  Make ready 6 Hard taco shells
4.  Prepare 6 Small tortillas
5.  Make ready 2 cup Chopped lettuce
6.  Prepare 1 Chopped tomato
7.  Get 2 cup Grated cheddar cheese
8.  Take 1 cup Sour cream
9.  Prepare 2 Chopped green onions
10.  Take 1/2 cup Water

##### Steps to make Shrimp Taco:

1.  Saute shrimp in a pan for about 2 minutes.
2.  Then add the taco seasoning and the half cup of water and simmer for about 7 minutes.
3.  Once the shrimp and sauce thickens remove from heat.
4.  Assemble.. lay the soft tortilla down and rest the hard taco inside.
5.  Place 1-2 spoonfuls of shrimp mixture in the bottom of shell, then top with some lettuce, green onion, tomato & cheese.
6.  Add a spoonful of sour cream on top & Enjoy!!!
