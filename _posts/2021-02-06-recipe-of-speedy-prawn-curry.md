##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn Curry:

1.  Make ready Shrimps
2.  Make ready Chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilies
3.  Get Coconut milk (or cooking cream)
4.  Get Spices: Paprika, turmeric, garlic ginger paste
5.  Take Spices: Cumin, Masala powder, coriander powder

##### Steps to make Prawn Curry:

1.  Frying chopped onions till brown then adding green chillies, garlic ginger paste till soft, then adding tomatoes till soft
2.  Add paprika and a bit of water
3.  Add shrimps then add all spices except for coriander powder
4.  Add milk then coriander powder
5.  Add coriander herb and a bit of cooking cream (or no cooking cream if used that instead of coconut milk)
