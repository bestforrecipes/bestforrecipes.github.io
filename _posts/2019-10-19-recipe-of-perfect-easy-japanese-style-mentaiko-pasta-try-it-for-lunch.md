##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Japanese-Style Mentaiko Pasta - Try It For Lunch!:

1.  Prepare 200 grams Pasta
2.  Prepare 60 to 80 grams ● Mentaiko (or tarako)
3.  Prepare 10 to 15 grams ●Butter
4.  Get 1 tbsp ● Soy sauce
5.  Take 1/2 lemon ● Lemon juice
6.  Take 4 shakes ● Umami seasoning
7.  Get 1 Nori seaweed and shiso leaves

##### Steps to make Easy Japanese-Style Mentaiko Pasta - Try It For Lunch!:

1.  Cook the pasta following the package instructions, or cook them al dente.
2.  While the pasta is cooking, mix the ingredients marked ● in a bowl. Take the membrane off the mentaiko first.
3.  Drain the cooked pasta and quickly mix in the sauce from Step 2. Transfer to serving plates, sprinkle with nori seaweed and shiso leaves, and it's done!
