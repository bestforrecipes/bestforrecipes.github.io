##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Seafood Gumbo:

1.  Take 1 cup and a half oakra
2.  Take 1/2 lb Quality shrimp tails and shell removed and reserved
3.  Get 6 oz of crab claw meat
4.  Make ready 1 large tomato diced
5.  Get 1 large bell pepper diced
6.  Prepare 2 clove of garlic diced
7.  Make ready 1 One onion diced
8.  Get 1/2 cup Flour
9.  Make ready 1 Salt to taste
10.  Take 1 Cajun spices about a teaspoon or to taste
11.  Make ready 1 Wine to drink.. Do I need to tell you how much?
12.  Take 1 Hot sauce a dash or two to taste
13.  Prepare 1 Worcestershire a splash or two
14.  Make ready 1/2 cup olive oil
15.  Make ready 6 cup water

##### Steps to make Easy Seafood Gumbo:

1.  Make a seafood veggie stock with veggie scraps and shells… Drink some wine
2.  Make a roux by cooking flour and olive oil until it starts to brown. Add peppers, onions, and garlic. Cook until soft and opaque. Strain the broth and add to the roux. It will start to thicken. Add more water or broth if needed. Add tomato and okra. Season with spices, salt , hot sauce etc to taste. Add shrimp and crab, cook on low for 10-15.
