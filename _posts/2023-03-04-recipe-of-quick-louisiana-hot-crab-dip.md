##### The ingredients needed to make LOUISIANA HOT CRAB DIP:

1.  Take 2 lb jumbo lump crab meat
2.  Get 8 oz three 8ounce packages of cream cheese
3.  Make ready 2 cup mayonnaise
4.  Prepare 1 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
5.  Prepare 12 tbsp minced green onions (white and green part)
6.  Prepare 8 clove garlic (minced)
7.  Take 8 tsp worcestershire sauce
8.  Make ready 6 tbsp lemon juice
9.  Take 8 tsp hot sauce
10.  Make ready 4 tsp old bay seasoning
11.  Make ready salt and pepper
