##### The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Salty Green Pepper Stir-Fry with Bonito Flakes:

1.  Prepare 4 to 5 Green bell peppers
2.  Get 1 tsp Vegetable oil
3.  Take 1/2 tbsp Sake
4.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp Mirin
5.  Take 1 tbsp Mentsuyu (3x concentrate)
6.  Prepare 2 packs Bonito flakes

##### Steps to make Sweet and Salty Green Pepper Stir-Fry with Bonito Flakes:

1.  Cut the green peppers in half and remove the insides. Cut the peppers into 2 cm cubes.
2.  Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan and cook the green pepper. Once the pepper is coated in oil, add the sake, mirin and mentsuyu, then stir-fry.
3.  Once the green pepper has been cooked and the flavors have been absorbed, sprinkle the bonito flakes. Toss the ingredients and turn off the heat.
