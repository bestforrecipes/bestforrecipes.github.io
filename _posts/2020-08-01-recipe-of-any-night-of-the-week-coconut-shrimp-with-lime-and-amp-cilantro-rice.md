##### The ingredients needed to make Coconut Shrimp with Lime & Cilantro rice:

1.  Get jumbo shrimp peeled and deveined
2.  Prepare coconut flakes Toasted
3.  Get eggs beaten with a pinch of salt and pepper
4.  Get of Panko
5.  Get Minute Rice
6.  Take lime
7.  Take cloves of garlic
8.  Make ready salt
9.  Make ready handful of cilantro
10.  Make ready cooking spray

##### Steps to make Coconut Shrimp with Lime & Cilantro rice:

1.  Toast Coconut, allow to cool and mix with panko in a baking dish
2.  Coat shrimp in egg mixture
3.  Remove and coat shrimp in coconut and panko crumbs
4.  Broil on sprayed a baking sheet medium high until golden brown( about 4 mins each side)
5.  Tear desired cilantro leaves and chop
6.  Smash and mince garlic
7.  salt minced garlic and smear into a paste
8.  cook rice and stir in cilantro and garlic salt paste
