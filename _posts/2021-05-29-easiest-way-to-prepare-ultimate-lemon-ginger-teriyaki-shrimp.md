##### The ingredients needed to make Lemon Ginger Teriyaki Shrimp:

1.  Get 1 lb Cooked shrimp
2.  Get 1/2 chopped red pepper
3.  Take 1 tsp olive oil
4.  Make ready 3 tbsp soy sauce
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp honey
6.  Get 1 tsp ground ginger
7.  Take 1 tsp garlic powder
8.  Make ready 1 tbsp lemon juice

##### Steps to make Lemon Ginger Teriyaki Shrimp:

1.  Mix olive oil, soy sauce, honey, ginger, garlic powder, and lemon juice in small bowl
2.  Place shrimp in mixture and let marinade in refrigerator for 25 to 30 minutes
3.  Heat olive oil in large skillet over high heat then add red pepper. Cook for about 4 to 5 min.
4.  Add shrimp and Cook shrimp for about 10 min or until cooked through to your liking
5.  Serve shrimp over quinoa or rice. Enjoy!
