##### The ingredients needed to make Yakiniku (Japanese BBQ) Restaurant-style Egg Drop Soup:

1.  Make ready 800 ml Chicken stock
2.  Get 1 tbsp Usukuchi soy sauce
3.  Get 1 dash Salt and pepper
4.  Take 2 Eggs
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp of katakuriko + 20 ml of water Katakuriko slurry
6.  Take 1 Green onions
7.  Take 1 Roasted white sesame seeds
8.  Get 1 tbsp Sesame oil
9.  Take 1 Shredded nori seaweed

##### Steps to make Yakiniku (Japanese BBQ) Restaurant-style Egg Drop Soup:

1.  Beat the 2 eggs and add 30 ml of water. Bring the chicken stock to a boil, and pour the beaten eggs slowly. Chicken stock. - - [https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/149128-very-useful-chicken-stock](https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/149128-very-useful-chicken-stock)
2.  Turn off the heat, pour the katakuriko dissolved in water to thicken the soup. Turn on the heat again, drizzle 1 tablespoon of sesame oil to finish.
3.  Garnish with chopped green onions, roasted white sesame seeds and shredded nori seaweed and it's done.
