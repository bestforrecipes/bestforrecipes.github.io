##### The ingredients needed to make Almond Tarts:

1.  Get 6 Phyllo pastry sheets sheets
2.  Take 2 tbsp Butter
3.  Prepare For filling;
4.  Make ready 15-20 Almonds roughly chopped
5.  Prepare 2 tbsp Honey
6.  Get Tart shells or muffin tin/cups

##### Steps to make Almond Tarts:

1.  Take 1 phyllo sheet apply butter.Place another sheet on top.
2.  Repeat till all the sheets are used
3.  Cut the sheets into 4 × 4 squares and arrange them in tarts. Place them tightly in the shells
4.  If you do not have tart shells you can use muffin tins
5.  Bake at 200 The Celsius preheated oven for 10 to 15 min or till they golden
6.  Take the almonds and add the honey.Spread on a baking tray and bake for 10 mins
7.  Once the tarts are ready.fill the tarts with honey roasted almonds
8.  Serve warm
