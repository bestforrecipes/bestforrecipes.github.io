##### The ingredients needed to make Oyster Stew:

1.  Make ready 4 Tbs Butter
2.  Make ready 1 medium Celery Stalk, finely diced
3.  Get 1 medium Garlic Clove, minced
4.  Make ready 2 Tbs White Flour
5.  Take 2 cups Milk
6.  Prepare 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
7.  Make ready 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
8.  Take 1/2 tsp minced Fresh or Freeze Dried Parsley Flakes, plus some for dusting
9.  Take 1/2 tsp Kosher Salt
10.  Get 2 cup fresh Oysters packed in Oyster liquor
11.  Prepare 1 grind or two of Ground Black Pepper
12.  Prepare Cook
13.  Get About an hour before cooking, measure out the milk, cream, and oysters to get to room temperature. Prepare the rest of the ingredients
14.  Prepare Melt the butter in a soup pot over medium heat. Stir in the celery and garlic and cook until soft
15.  Get Add the flour and stir until the roux gets a light tan in color
16.  Prepare Slowly add the milk and cream to the roux, stirring constantly
17.  Prepare Stir the Onion Powder, Parsley, and Salt into the stew. Reduce heat to medium-low; continue cooking and stirring until the mixture bubbles and thickens, 5 to 7 minutes. Reduce heat to barely simmer
18.  Get Add the oysters with the liquid from the container to the mixture; cook until the edges of the oysters curl, and slightly thickened, about 5 minutes more. Season with pepper and serve immediately
19.  Get Ladle into bowls, dust with parsley, and serve along with crusty bread
