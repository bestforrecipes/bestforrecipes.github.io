##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Udon:

1.  Get 1/2 Onion \*sliced
2.  Take 1 clove Garlic \*finely chopped
3.  Make ready 1 tablespoon Canola OR Olive Oil
4.  Make ready Chilli Flakes OR Fresh Chilli as required
5.  Take 1 (\*95g) can Tuna \*drained
6.  Take 2 servings Udon Noodles \*OR Spaghetti
7.  Prepare 1 Spring Onion \*finely chopped
8.  Take <Sauce Ingredients>
9.  Make ready 90 ml Hot Water \*Hot tap water is good
10.  Take 1/4 teaspoon Dashi Powder
11.  Prepare 2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
12.  Prepare 1 tablespoon Mirin \*OR 1 teaspoon Sugar
13.  Prepare \*Note: If you have ‘Mentsuyu’, use 1/2 cup of ‘Mentsuyu’ instead

##### Instructions to make Tuna Udon:

1.  Make sauce by mixing all the sauce ingredients in a small bowl.
2.  Prepare Udon Noodles according to the instruction on the package. If you use already cooked Udon noodles, soak in hot water to loosen, then drain well. \*Note: Frozen cooked Udon are the best!
3.  Heat Oil and Garlic in a frying pan, add Onion and Chilli, and cook until Onion is getting soft. Add Tuna and the sauce, and bring to the boil, stirring to mix.
4.  Add drained Udon Noodles and toss and mix all well.
5.  Sprinkle chopped Spring Onion on top and enjoy.
