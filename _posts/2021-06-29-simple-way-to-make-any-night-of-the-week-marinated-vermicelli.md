##### The ingredients needed to make Marinated Vermicelli:

2.  Take cucumber (slice thinly)
3.  Take crab sticks (or tube‐shaped fish paste cake)(to taste)
4.  Get (mixed in advance)
5.  Get vinegar
6.  Get sugar

##### Instructions to make Marinated Vermicelli:

1.  Soak cucumber in cold salted (1 tsp) water for 5 minutes. Drain and squeeze well.
2.  Bring the water to a boil, add bean‐starch vermicelli and cook according to the directions on the package. Cut into bite sized pieces.
3.  Mix all ingredients and marinate.
