2.  Get 2 tbs olive oil
3.  Prepare 8 clove garlic (chopped)
4.  Take Seafood mix on choice (squid, prawns or mussels)
5.  Get Chicken sausage (chopped into small bites)
6.  Take 1/3 cup water
7.  Take 2 tsp chilli flakes
8.  Get to taste Salt and blackpepper
9.  Make ready Spices mix on choice (oregano, rosemary, parsley and basil)
10.  Take 1/2 cube anchovies stock (\*optional)
11.  Get 1/2 cup grated parmigiano-reggiano cheese (\*optional)
