##### The ingredients needed to make Trev's Tuna Ceviche:

1.  Get 12 oz Tuna Sushi Grade
2.  Take 1 Avocado
3.  Take 1 red onion
4.  Prepare 1 bunch scallions
5.  Prepare 2 oz jalapenos
6.  Get 2 lemons
7.  Get 1/4 cup champagne vinegar
8.  Prepare 1/4 cup sesame oil
9.  Get 4 oz soy sauce
10.  Take 1 cup cilantro
11.  Take 1 salt & pepper

##### Steps to make Trev's Tuna Ceviche:

1.  Chop Tuna, onion, scallion, avocado, and cilantro
2.  Avocado & Tuna should be the same size. 1/4 inch cubes is about right. the onion and jalapenos can be chopped smaller.
3.  After chopping gently mix everything together in a large bowl. GENTLY! lol
