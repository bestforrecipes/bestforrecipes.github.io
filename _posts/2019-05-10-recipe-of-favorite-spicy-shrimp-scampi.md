##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Shrimp Scampi:

1.  Prepare 2 pounds large raw shrimp, shells removed and deveined
2.  Make ready 4 Tablespoond garlic butter, my recipe is in my profile and found in search for Garlic Butter
3.  Take 2 to 4 pickled hot cherry peppers , sliced, seeds removed for a milder taste
4.  Take 1 pint grape tomatoes
5.  Take lemon wedges for serving

##### Instructions to make Spicy Shrimp Scampi:

1.  Have peppers sliced and ready

3.  Heat garlic butter and peppers in a large skillet until melted
4.  Add shrimp and cover, cook on medium low until shrimp start to turn pink turn once, about 3 to 4 minutes

6.  Add tomatoes and increase heat to medium and cook just until tomatoes start to soften, about 2 to 3 minutes
7.  Serve with rice as an entree or as an appetizerwith french bread slices, pita wedges, crackers or flatbread. Serve with lemon on the side.
