##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn and Pork Toast:

1.  Get 1/4 k Ground Pork
2.  Take 1/4 k Prawn, chopped
3.  Make ready 8-110 cloves garlic, chopped
4.  Prepare 1 Egg white
5.  Take 3 Tbsp Fish Sauce
6.  Get 2 Tbsp Soy sauce
7.  Get Pepper
8.  Take White bread, cut into four
9.  Get Oil for frying

##### Steps to make Prawn and Pork Toast:

1.  In bowl combine 1st 7 ingredients. Mix
2.  Toast bread for 1 min.
3.  Put enough amount of mixture into bread
4.  Heat oil.
5.  Fry bread mixture down then turn it over once golden brown
6.  Serve with sweet chilli sauce or as it is
