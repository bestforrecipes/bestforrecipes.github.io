##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Salmon with Herbs:

1.  Take 1 1/2 kg salmon fillet, arround 8 fillets
2.  Prepare 2 1/2 cups bread crumbs, soft, (around 5 slices)
3.  Get 4 cloves garlic, finely minced
4.  Prepare 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese, grated
5.  Take 2 tablespoons butter, melted
6.  Prepare 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
7.  Get 2 teaspoons parsley, dried
8.  Get 1 teaspoon thyme, dried
9.  Prepare 1 teaspoon rosemary, crushed
10.  Make ready 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
11.  Get 1/2 teaspoon salt

##### Instructions to make Baked Salmon with Herbs:

1.  Preheat oven to 350 F.
2.  Cover the bottom of a baking pan with aluminum foil and brush it with oil.
3.  Put the salmon fillets on the aluminum foil in the baking pan, and coat it with oil.
4.  Mix the bread crumbs, garlic, cheese, parsley, thyme, rosemary, salt and pepper in a bowl. Add in the melted butter and mix until well combined.
5.  Spread the mixture evenly over the salmon filets. Make sure all salmon filets are well covered.
6.  Bake in the oven for about 35 to 40 minutes, until the fish is done and can be easily flaked with a fork.
