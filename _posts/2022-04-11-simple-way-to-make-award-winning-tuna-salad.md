##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Salad:

1.  Prepare 4 cans tuna fish liquid drained
2.  Get 1/2 onion chopped
3.  Get 3 chopped tomatoes
4.  Get 1 grated carrot
5.  Prepare 1 chopped bell pepper any color
6.  Get Dried basil
7.  Prepare Dried oregano
8.  Take Mayonnaise
9.  Prepare Salt and pepper
10.  Take Bread,crackers or wraps

##### Steps to make Tuna Salad:

1.  In a bowl mix all ingredients together then transfer to a plastic container then chill.
2.  The spread on bread crackers or wraps
