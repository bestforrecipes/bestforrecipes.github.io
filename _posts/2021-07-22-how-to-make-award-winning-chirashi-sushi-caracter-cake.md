##### The ingredients needed to make Chirashi Sushi Caracter Cake:

1.  Take Hot cooked rice
2.  Prepare Chirashi sushi mix
3.  Prepare Nori
4.  Take Sakura Denbu
5.  Make ready Salmon flakes

##### Instructions to make Chirashi Sushi Caracter Cake:

1.  Mix the chirashi sushi mix into the freshly cooked rice.
2.  Take out 1/4 of the rice and mix with the sakura denbu to create pink colored rice.
3.  Place half of the remaining rice into a round cake pan. Use a wooden spoon to pack in.
4.  Place the pink rice on top of Step 3 and pack it down tightly and evenly.
5.  Place the remaining rice on the very top and pack it down tightly and evenly.
6.  Cover the cake pan with a plate and flip it all over. Remove from the pan.
7.  After you have designed the character using nori and salmon flakes, it's done.
