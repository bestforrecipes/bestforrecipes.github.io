##### The ingredients needed to make For Bentos! Leafy Vegetable, Yuzu and Bonito Flake Rolls:

1.  Take 1 Leafy vegetables (Cabbage, Chinese cabbage etc.)
2.  Make ready 1 dash Cut yuzu skin (Japanese citron fruit)
3.  Prepare 1 Bonito flakes

##### Instructions to make For Bentos! Leafy Vegetable, Yuzu and Bonito Flake Rolls:

1.  Blanch the leafy vegetables in plenty of hot water for 3 minutes. Cool the leaves in cold water, and drain well.
2.  Place a a large leaf on a sushi rolling mat then layer on the smaller leaves. Add the bonito flakes and yuzu and roll it up. Cut into easy to eat pieces.
