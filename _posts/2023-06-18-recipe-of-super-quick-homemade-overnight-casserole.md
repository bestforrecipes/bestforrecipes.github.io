##### The ingredients needed to make Overnight Casserole:

1.  Take 1 small onion finely chopped
2.  Get 1 small green pepper finely chopped
3.  Prepare 1 cup uncooked macaroni
4.  Prepare 3-4 cups meat, cooked (your choice chicken, turkey or tuna) finely chopped
5.  Take 1 can cream of mushroom soup
6.  Take 1 can cream of celery soup
7.  Take 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
8.  Make ready 3 eggs hard boiled and chopped
9.  Make ready 2 cups milk
10.  Get to taste Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Overnight Casserole:

1.  Mix all ingredients together in 13x9 baking dish. Cover and refrigerate over night.
2.  Next day, preheat oven to 350 and cook for 1 hour.
