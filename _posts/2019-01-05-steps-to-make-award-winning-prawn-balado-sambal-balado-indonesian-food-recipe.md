##### The ingredients needed to make Prawn Balado/Sambal Balado, Indonesian food recipe:

1.  Make ready 1 kg medium prawn
2.  Prepare 10 pcs shallots
3.  Prepare 5 pcs garlic
4.  Get 3 medium tomato
5.  Prepare 20 pcs hot chili for medium spice (optional)
6.  Make ready 2 pcs lime juice
7.  Take 1 tsp sugar
8.  Make ready 2 pcs bay leaf
9.  Get 2 pcs lemon grass (optional)
10.  Make ready Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Prawn Balado/Sambal Balado, Indonesian food recipe:

1.  Clean the shell of the prawn and leaving the tail intact.
2.  Blender the shallots, garlic, chili, tomato all together.
3.  Heat it up little bit oil on the pan and pour it the blended ingredients, add bay leaf, lemongrass, cook it 8-10 minutes to reduce the water and add the prawn, lime juice, salt and pepper.
4.  Prawn no need to cook any longer, 3 minutes with medium fire its enough.
