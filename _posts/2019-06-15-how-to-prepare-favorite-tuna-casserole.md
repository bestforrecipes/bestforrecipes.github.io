##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna casserole:

1.  Take 2 cans tuna in water
2.  Get Salt and pepper
3.  Get 1 can peas
4.  Take 1 stick butter or 1/2 cup butter
5.  Make ready 1 c shredded cheddar jack
6.  Make ready 2 boxs any preferred mac and cheese
7.  Get Hot sauce\*optional
8.  Prepare 1/2 c milk

Tuna casserole is a casserole primarily made with pasta (or rice) and canned tuna, with canned peas and corn sometimes added. ##### Instructions to make Tuna casserole:

1.  Remove the cheese powder packets from the macaroni and set aside.
2.  Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the noodles and stir. Cook the noodles for 7-9 minutes. Add the peas during the last 2 minutes that the pasta cooks. Drain.
3.  Return the pasta and peas to the pan and add the butter, milk, cheese powder, Cheddar and tuna. Stir well.
4.  Serve and enjoy. I add salt and pepper and hot sauce to my bowl when making my plate.
