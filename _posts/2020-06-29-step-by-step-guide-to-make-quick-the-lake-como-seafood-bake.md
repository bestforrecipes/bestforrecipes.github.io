##### The ingredients needed to make The Lake Como Seafood Bake:

1.  Prepare Seafood
2.  Make ready Littlenecks Clams
3.  Get P.E.I. Mussels, scrubbed, beard removed
4.  Get U-8 Sea Scallops
5.  Prepare 15-20 Jumbo Shrimp, peeled, deveined, tails on
6.  Prepare 3 - 4 oz. Lobster Tails
7.  Get Sweet White Corn on the Cob Shucked
8.  Take each Italian Sausage (Sweet or Hot)
9.  Get Fingerling Potatoes
10.  Prepare large Onion, Chopped
11.  Make ready Pinot Grigio Wine
12.  Take Fresh Chopped Basil Leaves
13.  Take Butter, cut into 1/4
14.  Take Chopped Garlic
15.  Take Sea Salt
16.  Make ready Lemons

##### Steps to make The Lake Como Seafood Bake:

1.  Scrub the Clams & Mussels to remove any Sandy Grit & Rinse off the Potatoes.
2.  Place the Sausage, Potatoes, Corn, Clams & Mussels in a Large Roasting Pan.
3.  Then place Lobster Tails, Shrimp & Scallops on Top of the Clams & Mussels.
4.  Add the Wine, Butter, Lemon Juice
5.  Place Roasting Pan in the Oven or on the Grill & Bake till all of the Clams & Mussels are Open.
