##### The ingredients needed to make Balsamic Salmon:

1.  Prepare 1 Salmon Filet
2.  Take 3 tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
3.  Take 2 tbsp Olive Oil
4.  Get 1 clove Garlic
5.  Take 2 tbsp Honey
6.  Take 2 tbsp Dijon Mustard

Here's a quick and easy balsamic glazed salmon recipe for salmon lovers. This Honey Balsamic Instant Pot Salmon may become a family favorite! Sprinkle salt and pepper on salmon filets. In a small bowl, mix together honey and balsamic vinegar.

##### Steps to make Balsamic Salmon:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F.
2.  Brown garlic in olive oil.
3.  Then add balsamic vinegar.
4.  Then add honey and mustard.
5.  Brush glaze over the salmon.
6.  Bake for 5 minutes.
7.  Take it out and add more glaze then bake for another 10 minutes.
8.  Garnish with parsley and serve.
