##### The ingredients needed to make Spam With Wasabi Fried Rice:

1.  Get 1 can spam
2.  Get 2 cup cooked white rice
3.  Take WASABI SOY MIXTURE
4.  Take 2 tbsp light soy sauce or teriyaki soy sauce
5.  Make ready 1 tbsp wasabi paste
6.  Make ready GARNISH OPTION
7.  Take 2 tbsp otona no furikake / ready to eat seaweed topping

##### Steps to make Spam With Wasabi Fried Rice:

1.  Pan fried black pepper flavoured spam or any spam till its lighly crispy and brown then drain and set aside
2.  Put the cooked white rice onto a non stick pan then pour the wasabi soy sauce mixture and stir fry to mix
3.  Add the fried spam and stir fry to mix for another 2 minutes then off heat and add seasoning and stir fry to mix
