##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese Salmon:

1.  Get salmon fillets
2.  Prepare soy sauce
3.  Take honey
4.  Make ready ginger
5.  Prepare garlic powder
6.  Prepare garlic cloves
7.  Prepare water
8.  Prepare olive oil
9.  Get rice
10.  Get eggs
11.  Get corn flour
12.  Take sesame seeds
13.  Prepare snap peas

##### Instructions to make Chinese Salmon:

1.  Cut up the salmon into chunks
2.  Make your marinade by mixing soy sauce, olive oil, water, garlic, ginger and honey
3.  Place the salmon in the marinade for 20 mins or more if you wish
4.  When the salmon is done marinating, place the rice into boiling water and then simmer the rice till ready
5.  Heat a pan with olive oil and cook the salmon on both sides till it has a nice golden brown colour
6.  When the salmon is cooked on both sides add the snap peas and cook for a few minutes
7.  Next add the rest of your marinade and mix the cornflour with water then add to the pan to create a sauce
8.  Next drain the rice and on a different pan scramble your eggs and add the rice and fry for a few minutes till all combined
9.  Serve your salmon on top of the rice, place some sesame seeds and enjoy !
