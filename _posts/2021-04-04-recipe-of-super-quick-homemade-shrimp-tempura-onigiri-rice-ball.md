##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Tempura Onigiri (Rice Ball):

1.  Make ready Frozen shrimp for Tempura
2.  Get Tempura flour
3.  Get 1 bowl cooked rice
4.  Get Salt
5.  Get 1/2 Nori
6.  Make ready 1 tablespoon soy sauce
7.  Take 1/2 tablespoon Dashi powder
8.  Get 1/2 tablespoon sugar
9.  Make ready 50 ml water

##### Instructions to make Shrimp Tempura Onigiri (Rice Ball):

1.  Frozen shrimp
2.  Tempura flour
3.  Mix tempura flour with water and put 1 ice cube.
4.  Fry shrimp
5.  Mix Tempura sauce ingredients
6.  On plastic film put rice and shrimp tempura with sauce
7.  Make Onigiri form with wrapping film.
8.  Put salt to taste
9.  Cover Onigiri with nori
