##### The ingredients needed to make Bbq Chilli Prawns:

1.  Prepare Garlic, finely chopped
2.  Take Peeled prawns
3.  Get Dried chilli flakes
4.  Make ready Lemon juice
5.  Make ready Handful fresh coriander, finely chopped
6.  Prepare to taste Sea salt
7.  Make ready to taste ground black pepper
8.  Make ready Drizzle of Olive oil
9.  Prepare 1 good pinch of smoked paprika

##### Instructions to make Bbq Chilli Prawns:

1.  Marinade the prawns with all the ingredients atleast 30 minutes before grilling. Then pop them on a skewer ready for the bbq.
2.  Grill on each side for 2-3 minutes or until cooked. Easy as that.
