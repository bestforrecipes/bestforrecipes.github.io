##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Teriyaki:

1.  Make ready 2 salmon fillets
2.  Prepare 100 ml Teriyaki sauce
3.  Prepare 1 carrot
4.  Take 1 cougette
5.  Get 2 spring onions
6.  Prepare Half a red pepper
7.  Make ready Handful mangetout
8.  Make ready Long stem brocolli
9.  Get Spinach

##### Instructions to make Salmon Teriyaki:

1.  Cut the carrot, courgette, pepper and spring onion into strips
2.  Place the salmon, vegetables and teriyaki sauce into baking foil and wrap up, leaving space above the ingredients
3.  Place in oven at 180C for 15-20 minutes
4.  Steam the brocolli for 5 minutes and the spinach for 1 minute
5.  Unwrap parcels and serve with rice and vegetables
