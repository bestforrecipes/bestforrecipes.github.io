##### The ingredients needed to make Sunny Side Up Toast Crab Stick Version:

1.  Make ready 1 slice Sliced bread
2.  Get 4 Imitation crabsticks
3.  Get 1 Egg
4.  Prepare 1 Mayonnaise

##### Instructions to make Sunny Side Up Toast Crab Stick Version:

1.  Place the bread slice on aluminum foil and make a frame with crab sticks.
2.  Crack the egg in the frame and fold up the aluminum foil. Squeeze the mayonnaise on the bread edges.
3.  Place the bread gently in the oven and toast about 5 minutes. When the egg is set, it's done!
