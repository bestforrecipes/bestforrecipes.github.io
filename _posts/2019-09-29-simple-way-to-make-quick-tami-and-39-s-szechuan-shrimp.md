##### The ingredients needed to make Tami's Szechuan Shrimp:

1.  Make ready 1 lb shrimp, peeled & deveined
2.  Make ready 2 tbsp ketchup
3.  Make ready 2 tbsp chili sauce
4.  Prepare 2 tbsp rice wine or sherry wine
5.  Take 2 tbsp soy sauce
6.  Take 1 tbsp honey
7.  Take 1 tsp salt
8.  Prepare 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
9.  Get 1 tbsp cornstarch
10.  Make ready 4 oz mushrooms, sliced
11.  Prepare 1/2 each red & yellow pepper, sliced
12.  Get 2 green onions, chopped
13.  Prepare 2 tbsp sesame oil
14.  Get 1 tsp Chinese 5 spice

##### Steps to make Tami's Szechuan Shrimp:

2.  In a bowl add all ingredients except the shrimp, green onions Chinese 5 spice, set sauce a side.
3.  Preheat wok or large saute pan, add sesame oil, heat oil for a moment then add peppers & mushrooms, stir constantly add shrimp, chinese 5 spice and green onions stir constantly about 5-10 minutes til shrimp are cooked add sauce, toss the shrimp to coat evenly. Serve over rice or noodles. Enjoy :)
