##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Salad:

1.  Make ready 1 tin Tuna in oil
2.  Prepare 50 g Chopped red or white onion
3.  Get 50 g Diced tomato
4.  Prepare Chopped fresh coriander/chinese parsley
5.  Get 1 Chili seeded and minced
6.  Get To taste Salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Tuna Salad:

1.  In a bowl stir together the tuna, onion, tomatoes and chili. Season with salt and pepper.
