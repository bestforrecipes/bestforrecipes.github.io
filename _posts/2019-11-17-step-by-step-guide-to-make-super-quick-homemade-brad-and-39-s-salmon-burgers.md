##### Steps to make Brad's salmon burgers:

1.  Mix flour and seasonings, set aside
2.  Flake salmon into a bowl.
3.  Mix in mayo, bread crumbs, and egg. Stir in well
4.  Form into patties. Dredge well in flour on both sides. Let sit for five minutes.
5.  Heat oil to cover the bottom of a large skillet. When hot fry on both sides until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
6.  Toast buns. Add desired toppings and condiments. Eat and enjoy
