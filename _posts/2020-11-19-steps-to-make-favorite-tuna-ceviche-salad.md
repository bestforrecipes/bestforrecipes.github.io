##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Ceviche Salad:

1.  Get 3 (12 oz) cans of tuna (of your choice)
2.  Take 2 1/2 cups Persian cucumber, unpeeled (regular cucumber, peel
3.  Prepare 3 fresh jalapeños (optional, or to your liking)
4.  Make ready 2 cups tomato
5.  Take 1 1/2 cups red onion (or regular onion)
6.  Make ready 1 cup cilantro
7.  Get 2 cups fresh squeezed lemon juice
8.  Get to taste Salt and pepper

##### Steps to make Tuna Ceviche Salad:

1.  Drain your tuna
2.  In a large mixing bowl add chopped cucumbers (I like mine in small bits, but you may chop them bigger)
3.  Add the tomatoes (I usually chop them the same size as the cucumber)
4.  Add in your jalapenos (optional)
5.  Add in your cilantro
6.  Add in your tuna, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Mix well
