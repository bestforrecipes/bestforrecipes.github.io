##### The ingredients needed to make Thai Salad with Shrimp and Fried Rice Noodles:

1.  Make ready Sauce
2.  Take Sriracha
3.  Make ready fish sauce
4.  Get soy sauce
5.  Get molasses
6.  Take lime juice- 3 small limes
7.  Take seasoned rice wine vinegar
8.  Take sugar
9.  Take sesame oil
10.  Prepare vegetable oil
11.  Get sweet onion – Paper thin sliced
12.  Take each garlic cloves – chopped
13.  Make ready lemon grass – ultra fine chopped
14.  Make ready ginger root – peeled and minced
15.  Take shrimp – finely chopped
16.  Get each egg white – mix with chopped shrimp
17.  Take sauce base
18.  Take Salad
19.  Get each red bell pepper – 1" fine julienne
20.  Get each carrots – 1" fine julienne
21.  Make ready napa cabbage – fine chiffionade
22.  Get basil – chiffonade
23.  Take mint – chiffonade
24.  Make ready cilantro – lightly chopped
25.  Make ready rice noodles (fried @400)
26.  Make ready each limes zest with a micro-plane or finely chopped zest

##### Instructions to make Thai Salad with Shrimp and Fried Rice Noodles:

1.  Whisk together first 8 ingredients in a bowl. Taste and adjust for acidity, sweetness and heat. Set aside
2.  In a non stick saute pan, heat oil and saute onion, lemon grass, ginger and garlic until evenly colored to a light golden brown.
3.  Stir in shrimp mixture cook through out adding 1/4 cup of the sauce and coking for 1 minute then remove from that heat.
4.  Combine peppers, carrots, herbs,nappa cabbage and fried noodles in salad bowl
5.  Toss in warm shrimp with lime zest and extra sauce as needed.
6.  Toss to mix thoroughly and quickly and serve
7.  Note…..If you like more spice add to your hearts content.
