##### The ingredients needed to make Thai King Prawn Penang:

1.  Take King prawns (I used cooked for quickness but raw is probably better)
2.  Make ready 1 tsp chopped ginger
3.  Prepare 1 tsp chopped garlic
4.  Prepare 1 lemongrass stalk (break with back of the knife)
5.  Get 1 lime zest & juice
6.  Make ready 2 lime leaves
7.  Get 1 red chilli
8.  Get 2 tsp red curry paste
9.  Get 3 spring onions
10.  Get Chopped coriander
11.  Prepare 1/2 pint almond milk
12.  Take Salt
13.  Make ready 2 tsp peanut butter

##### Instructions to make Thai King Prawn Penang:

1.  Cook the garlic lemongrass ginger chilli & lime leaves over low heat for 5 mins. I used coconut oil.
2.  Add curry paste cook for further 5 mins
3.  Add milk & simmer for 10 mins just till the sauce starts to thicken
4.  Add all other ingredients cook for 2 mins. Stir in peanut butter & lime juice. serve
