##### The ingredients needed to make AMIEs Polpette di Tonno (Tunaballs):

1.  Get 250 grams can tuna in olive oil, drained
2.  Make ready 2 eggs
3.  Take 3 slice white bread, soaked in 3 tablespoon milk
4.  Prepare 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
5.  Get 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh parsley
6.  Get 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
7.  Prepare salt and ground black pepper
8.  Get 1 cup breadcrumbs
9.  Prepare 1/4 cup vegetable oil, for frying

##### Steps to make AMIEs Polpette di Tonno (Tunaballs):

1.  Combine the tuna and bread then add the parsley, parmesan,garlic and eggs. Mix to combine. Season with salt and pepper. Shape tablespoonfuls into firm balls then shape to make a polpette.
2.  Heat the oil in a large frying pan and fry the polpette in batches for 3-4 minutes, turning, until browned all over and cooked.
3.  Serve with your choice of side dishes like "mista insalata" (mixed vegetable salad). Share and enjoy.
