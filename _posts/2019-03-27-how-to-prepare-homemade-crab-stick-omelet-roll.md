##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Stick Omelet Roll:

1.  Make ready 1 Egg
2.  Get 2 Imitation crab sticks
3.  Prepare 1 less than 1 tablespoon Dashi stock
4.  Make ready 1 dash Salt
5.  Make ready 1 dash Umami seasoning
6.  Take 1 Olive oil

##### Instructions to make Crab Stick Omelet Roll:

1.  Break a egg into a bowl. Add salt, umami seasoning, and dashi stock. Especially beat the egg white well, and incorporate.
2.  Cut the imitation crab sticks to fit in a tamagoyaki pan.
3.  Heat olive oil in a tamagoyaki pan. When the pan is warm, pour in half of the egg mixture, and turn the heat down to low.
4.  Lay the crab sticks and roll it towards you.
5.  When it's done, push it to the other side of the pan, and pour the rest of the egg mixture in the empty space.
6.  Roll again towards you. Place the roll joint-side down, and fry for a little while.
7.  Cut into 4 even pieces, and it's done.
