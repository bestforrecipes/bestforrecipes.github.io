##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna kimchi:

1.  Make ready 300 g tuna
2.  Make ready 1 avocado
3.  Get 30 g kimchi
4.  Make ready 3 tabls spoons soy sauce
5.  Make ready 3 tablespoons Japanese sweet sake
6.  Get Lemon liquid

##### Steps to make Tuna kimchi:

1.  Slice tuna into small size.Season tuna with soy sauce and Japanese sweet sake.
2.  Cut avocado. Dip lemon liquid over avocado. Combine tuna and avocado and kimchi in a bowl.
3.  Place lettuce on a plate and put mixed tuna on it.
