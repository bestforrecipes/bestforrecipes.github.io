##### The ingredients needed to make TINOTUAN / BUBUR MANADO (Manado Vegetable Porridge):

1.  Get 250 gram rice, washed and drained
2.  Get 200 gram pumpkin, Chopped
3.  Take 100 gram sweet potato, chopped
4.  Make ready 1 cob sweetcorn, extract the kernels
5.  Get 50 gram spinach, washed
6.  Take 50 gram water convolvulus, washed (optional)
7.  Take 5-6 string beans, cut into 2 cm pieces
8.  Take 2 teaspoon salt
9.  Make ready 2 lemon grass, bruised
10.  Take Sambal dabu-dabu :
11.  Take Finely slice 3-4 red chilies, 6 bird's eye chilies & 3 shallots
12.  Prepare Mix well with 1 chopped tomatoe
13.  Prepare 1 tablespoons lime juice and 1/2 teaspoon salt
14.  Make ready Fried salted fish:
15.  Prepare Cut salted fish into 2 cm pieces and soak with boiling water
16.  Get Let it stand until cooled, wash pat it dry, then fry
17.  Prepare Grilled fish:
18.  Prepare Wash tuna fish and rub with 1/2 teaspoon salt
19.  Get 1/2 teaspoons ginger and 2 tbsp vegetable oil
20.  Make ready Grilled until cooked

##### Steps to make TINOTUAN / BUBUR MANADO (Manado Vegetable Porridge):

1.  Add water level to a level 3 - 5 cm above the rice level. Boil until almost cooked.
2.  Add pumpkin, sweet potato and corn. When they are cooked, add vegetables and salt.
3.  Cook until the cereal thickens. Serve with sambal and fried salted fish.
