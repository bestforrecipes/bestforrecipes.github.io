3.  Get 100 gr Broccoli
4.  Take 1 whole onion
5.  Take 2 cloves garlic
6.  Get 1 tbsp margarine or salted butter

9.  Prepare 1 tsp sweet soy sauce
10.  Make ready 1 tsp soy sauce (shoyu)

##### Steps to make Eringi \[エリンギ\] Mushroom, Prawns & Broccoli in Sweet Oyster Sauce:

1.  First, prepare prawns to be fried with margarine. After frying it, set aside. For the sauce, mix all in a bowl together.
2.  Cut the broccoli. Boil the broccoli with water until soft. Set aside
3.  Cut the mushrooms, set aside. Then cut onion and chop the garlic.
4.  In frying pan, put 1tbsp of margarine or butter, then pour onion & garlic, mix until the color turns golden brown.
5.  After that, put the fried prawns, mushrooms and broccoli into the frying pan. Add the sauce and mix until all the ingredients covered.
6.  And that’s it! Enjoy your meal🤤
