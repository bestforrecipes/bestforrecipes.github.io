##### The ingredients needed to make Healthy Thick and Silky Tofu Salad:

1.  Take 1 block Silken tofu
2.  Take 10 cm Yamaimo yam
3.  Take 1 Chirimen jako
4.  Prepare 1 packet Bonito flakes
5.  Prepare 1 Lettuce or any leafy greens
6.  Take 1 Sliced onions
7.  Get 1 Tomato
8.  Get 1/2 pack Daikon radish sprouts
9.  Prepare Dressing
10.  Make ready 3 tbsp Ponzu
11.  Take 2 tbsp Olive oil
12.  Make ready 3 cm Wasabi paste
13.  Make ready 1 dash Salt and pepper

##### Steps to make Healthy Thick and Silky Tofu Salad:

1.  Lightly drain the tofu, and cut into portions according to the number of people being served. If the jako sardines are dry, use as is, but if they are moist, dry-fry in a frying pan.
2.  Scatter the leafy greens and onions on a plate, then the tofu on top! Grate the yamaimo yam on top. Scatter the tomato, bonito flakes, daikon radish sprouts, and jako sardines on top.
3.  Pour the well-mixed dressing, and it's done.
