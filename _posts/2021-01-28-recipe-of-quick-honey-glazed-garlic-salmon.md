##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Glazed Garlic Salmon:

1.  Get 4 salmon
2.  Get 8 tbsp honey
3.  Prepare 4 tbsp soy sauce
4.  Make ready 2 tbsp white vinegar
5.  Get 4 large garlic clove
6.  Take olive oil
7.  Get salt and pepper
8.  Make ready sesame seeds
9.  Prepare chives or shallots or green onions

##### Instructions to make Honey Glazed Garlic Salmon:

1.  Take salmon out of fridge 20 minutes before cooking. Pat salmon skin dry with paper towel and sprinkle with salt and pepper
2.  Take a small bowl and mix sauce honey, soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic together
3.  Put olive oil in a non stick pan over medium heat. Place salmon in the pan and cook each side for 4 minutes
4.  Pour sauce over salmon and cook for 1 minute or until it starts to thicken slightly. Check the side of the salmon to see how cooked through the middle is. If sauce thickens too much before your salmon is cooked to your taste, just add water 1 tbsp at a time
5.  Remove onto serving plates and drizzle with sauce, and sprinkle with sesame seeds and chives or shallots if desired or green onions
6.  Best served with some vegetables on the side like broccoli or green beans. Enjoy!
