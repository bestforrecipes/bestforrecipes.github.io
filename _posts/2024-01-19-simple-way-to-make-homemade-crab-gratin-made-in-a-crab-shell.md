##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Gratin Made in a Crab Shell:

1.  Get 130 grams Flaked crab meat
2.  Take 50 grams Macaroni
3.  Prepare 300 ml Milk
4.  Get 50 ml Heavy cream
5.  Prepare 1/2 Onion
6.  Prepare 20 grams Butter
7.  Prepare 25 grams Cake flour
8.  Prepare 1 tsp Soup stock granules
9.  Take 1 dash Salt and pepper
10.  Make ready 1 Mayonnaise
11.  Get 1 Pizza cheese
12.  Take 1 Fresh parsley
13.  Get 3 Crab shells

##### Steps to make Crab Gratin Made in a Crab Shell:

1.  Slice the onion thinly. Boil macaroni in salted water.
2.  Put butter in a pan, and sauté the onion. When it has wilted, scatter cake flour, mix together lightly, and turn the heat down to low.
3.  When the flouriness is gone, add the milk little by little while mixing. Continue mixing until the milk is thickened, then add the soup stock granules.
4.  When the sauce comes to a boil add the cream, crab meat and macaroni. Season with salt and pepper.
5.  Put the mixture into the crab shells. Make lines on top with the mayonnaise, and scatter on pizza cheese.
6.  Put the crab shells on a baking sheet, and bake in a 480°F/250°C oven until browned.
7.  Scatter with parsley and it's done.
