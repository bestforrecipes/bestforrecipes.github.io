##### The ingredients needed to make Shirataki Noodles Boiled with Pickled Plums and Bonito Flakes:

1.  Take 1 packet Shiratak or konnyaku noodles
2.  Make ready 1 packages Bonito flakes
3.  Make ready 2 Salted plum
4.  Prepare 1 to 2 tablespoons Sugar
5.  Get 2 tbsp Soy sauce
6.  Make ready 1 tbsp Mirin

##### Instructions to make Shirataki Noodles Boiled with Pickled Plums and Bonito Flakes:

1.  Rub the konnyaku noodles with a little salt in a bowl of water, then drain in a colander. Cut into bite-sized pieces. Make sure to remove the seeds from the pickled plums.
2.  Put the konnyaku noodles into a pot, and boil down the water. When the water has evaporated, add sugar and mix over a medium heat.
3.  Once the sugar dissolves, add mirin and soy sauce and turn up to high heat. Adding the pickled plums and bonito flakes and boiling it down until most of the juice has evaporated.
