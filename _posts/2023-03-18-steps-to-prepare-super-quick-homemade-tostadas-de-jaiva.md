##### The ingredients needed to make Tostadas de jaiva:

1.  Prepare 2 lb imitation crab
2.  Get 1/2 chopped onion
3.  Take 2 tomatoes, chopped
4.  Prepare 1 bunch cilantro, finely chopped
5.  Prepare 4 juicy limes
6.  Take optional
7.  Prepare 1 avocado
8.  Prepare 1 serrano chile, finely chopped
9.  Take 1 cucumber, chopped
10.  Make ready 1 hot sauce, I like Tapatio

##### Instructions to make Tostadas de jaiva:

1.  Shred imitation crab with hands
2.  Add chopped onion, tomato and cilantro
3.  Squeeze lime juice all over
4.  Mix well and let sit for at least 30 minutes for better flavor
5.  I top mine with avocado and hot sauce
6.  You can add Serrano if you like spice. Add cucumber off you like crunch
