##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Pasta Salad:

1.  Get 1 box shell pasta
2.  Get 1 can diced water chessnuts
3.  Get 1 cup Dukes mayonnaise
4.  Get 1 tsp lemon pepper seasoning
5.  Prepare 1 salt
6.  Prepare 1 pepper
7.  Make ready 1 can drained peas
8.  Take 1 can drained tuna

##### Instructions to make Tuna Pasta Salad:

1.  Boil pasta
2.  Drain & rinse with cold water
3.  Mix all other ingredients in a seperate bowl
4.  Add pasta
5.  Refrigerate for an hour
