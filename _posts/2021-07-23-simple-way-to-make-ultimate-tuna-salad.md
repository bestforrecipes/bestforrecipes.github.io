##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Salad:

1.  Get 230 gm Canned tuna (packed in water) after draining
2.  Get 1/4 cup Celery stalks diced
3.  Get 4 tbsp Onion diced
4.  Prepare 3 tbsp Green capsicum diced
5.  Make ready 1/4 cup Carrot grated
6.  Make ready 3 tbsp Cucumber (deseeded) diced
7.  Get 1 tsp Red chilli finely minced
8.  Take Coriander leaves - handful, finely chopped
9.  Take 2 tsp Sweet mango chutney
10.  Prepare 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
11.  Take 2. 1/2 tbsp Parmesan cheese
12.  Prepare 1 tsp Red chilli flakes
13.  Take 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
14.  Prepare 3/4 tbsp Whole grain mustard
15.  Prepare 6-8 tbsp Mayonnaise
16.  Get 2 tbsp Lemon juice
17.  Get to taste Salt

##### Steps to make Tuna Salad:

1.  Using a fork, break the tuna into flakes.
2.  In a mixing bowl add all the ingredients and mix until well combined.
3.  Spoon into a salad bowl.
4.  Cover with cling wrap and refrigerate until needed.
