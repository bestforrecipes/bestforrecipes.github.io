##### The ingredients needed to make Easy and Delicious Octopus Rice:

1.  Prepare 200 grams Boiled octopus
2.  Prepare 20 grams Ginger
3.  Get 1 Aburaage
4.  Get 700 grams White rice
5.  Prepare 2 tsp Sake
6.  Take Flavoring ingredients:
7.  Prepare 4 tbsp ★ Mentsuyu (2x concentrate)
8.  Take 1 tsp ★ Dashi stock granules
9.  Make ready 1 tsp ★ Mirin

##### Instructions to make Easy and Delicious Octopus Rice:

1.  Wash the rice, and strain for 30 minutes.
2.  Slice the octopus into bite sized pieces and coat with sake (rest for around 10 minutes).
3.  Peel the ginger and julienne. Soak the aburaage in boiling water, drain, and cut into bite-sized pieces. Mix the ★ ingredients.
4.  Put the rice in a rice cooker and mix the ★ seasonings. Pour water up to the 2 cup line and stir lightly.
5.  Add aburaage and octopus on on top and scatter the ginger.
6.  Cook as normal, or if you have a mixed rice setting on your cooker, use that. When it's cooked, mix from the bottom and serve.
