##### The ingredients needed to make Sambal Goreng Kentang Tempe (Indonesian Potato and Tempeh in Spicy Chili Sauce):

1.  Prepare 300 gr potatoes, washed, peeled, small diced
2.  Make ready 1 pc tempeh/soy bean cake (around 300 gr), small diced
3.  Take 500 ml vegetable oil for deep frying potatoes and tempeh
4.  Prepare 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
5.  Prepare 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
6.  Get 4 cloves shallots, thinly sliced
7.  Prepare 3 green chili peppers, diagonally sliced
8.  Prepare 3 red chili peppers, diagonally sliced
9.  Take 5 red Thai bird eye chili peppers (optional according to taste)
10.  Prepare 1,5 Tbsp oyster sauce
11.  Make ready 4 Tbsp sweet soy sauce
12.  Get 1 tsp salt or to taste
13.  Make ready 1/4 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
14.  Get 150 ml water
15.  Make ready 2 bh tomatoes, cut into pieces
16.  Take 100 gr stinky beans/sator beans

##### Steps to make Sambal Goreng Kentang Tempe (Indonesian Potato and Tempeh in Spicy Chili Sauce):

2.  In the same fry pan, fry tempeh until cooked and golden brown. Remove from the heat. Set aside.
