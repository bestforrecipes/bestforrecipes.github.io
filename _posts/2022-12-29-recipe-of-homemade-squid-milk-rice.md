##### The ingredients needed to make Squid Milk Rice:

1.  Take Squid
2.  Make ready Butter
3.  Prepare Milk
4.  Make ready Rice
5.  Prepare Dashi
6.  Make ready Onion
7.  Take Sesame seed oil

##### Instructions to make Squid Milk Rice:

1.  Prepare the ingredients
2.  Cut the onion and fry it.
3.  Cut the squid and add it
4.  Add rice
5.  Add a gollop of butter
6.  Add Dashi and mix everything together
7.  Add milk
