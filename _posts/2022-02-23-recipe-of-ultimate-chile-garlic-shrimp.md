1.  Take 2 lb medium to jumbo shrimp
2.  Make ready 1 tbsp chipotle powder
3.  Take 1 tsp garlic powder
4.  Take 2 tsp cayenne pepper
5.  Take 1 tsp salt
6.  Take 1/4 olive oil
7.  Take 4 slice bacon
