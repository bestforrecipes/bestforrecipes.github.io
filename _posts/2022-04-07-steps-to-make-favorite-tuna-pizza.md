##### Steps to make Tuna pizza:

1.  Add flour, yeast and sugar to bowl and mix. Slowly add milk and water and mix then knead to form a dough.
2.  Add olive oil to dough and knead again. Leave to rise somewhere warm for around 30 mins.
3.  While the pizza dough is rising, prepare the sauce. Add oil to pan and fry onions and garlic until soft (5 mins or so) add tuna, chopped tomatoes, herbs and pepper to taste. Stir and simmer for around 20 mins on a low heat. Meanwhile, heat oven to 180 degrees.
4.  The dough should have roughly double in size. Knead again then stretch out onto a pizza tray - this is the fun part! You could also split the dough to make mini pizzas.
5.  Spread the cooked sauce evenly onto the dough.
6.  Top with grated cheese…
7.  Add your delicious toppings and bake for 20 mins! It’s so easy and is delicious cold too. Serve with jacket potatoes and green veg.
