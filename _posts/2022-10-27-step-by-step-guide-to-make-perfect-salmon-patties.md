##### Steps to make Salmon patties:

1.  Start with the vegetables. Mix sugar, vinegar and water in a bowl. Cut the cucumber in half and remove the seeds with a spoon. Make thin slices of the cucumber and carrot with a potato peeler and marinate in the vinegar mixture.
2.  Put the salmon in a blender with curry paste, parsley and soya sauce and pulse together. Add the flour and work it in there with a spoon. (If you dont have a blender just chop the salmon finely with a knife and mix everything in a bowl).
3.  Shape the mixture into two patties and cover with sesame seeds. Let rest in the fridge for 10 minutes.
4.  Fry the patties on medium heat for about 4 minutes on each side. Serve with the pickled vegetables and anything else you fancy. Enjoy!
