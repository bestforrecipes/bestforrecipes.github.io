##### The ingredients needed to make Filipino pizza:

1.  Prepare 1 one pizza dough
2.  Prepare 1/2 cup sweet chilli sauce
3.  Take 1/2 cup dried squid dried and pounded to small pieces
4.  Get 100 grams pork belly thinly sliced and fried
5.  Get 1 cup pork crackle pounded to small pieces
6.  Take 200 grams mozzarella cheese grated 100 grams for base 100 grams for final topping
7.  Make ready 1/2 cup lechon sauce
8.  Make ready 1 cup sliced mushrooms
9.  Make ready 1 sachet magic sarup
10.  Make ready 200 grams sliced ham into small pieces
