##### The ingredients needed to make 🧑🏽‍🍳🧑🏼‍🍳 Garlic Pepper Prawns Recipe •Thai Style "Goong Pad Kratiem Prik Thai":

1.  Prepare 3 cloves Garlic (crispy garlic)
2.  Take 1 pc. Coriander root
3.  Get 3 cloves Garlic
4.  Take 1 tsp. Black pepper corn
5.  Take 8 pcs. Prawn
6.  Make ready 2 tbsp. Vegetable oil
7.  Get 1/2 tbsp. Oyster sauce
8.  Take 1/2 tbsp. Soy sauce
9.  Prepare 1/2 tbsp. Fish sauce
10.  Take 1 tbsp. Water

1.  Chopped garlic and fried with low heat until crispy • clean and peel prawns remove black string •and fried crispy Egg in medium heat
