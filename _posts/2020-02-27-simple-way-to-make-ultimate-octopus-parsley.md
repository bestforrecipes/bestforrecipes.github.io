##### The ingredients needed to make Octopus Parsley:

1.  Make ready 2 legs Boiled octopus
2.  Get 1 Parsley
3.  Prepare 1 clove Garlic
4.  Prepare 2 tbsp Olive oil
5.  Get 1 Salt, pepper, black pepper

##### Instructions to make Octopus Parsley:

1.  Thinly cut the octopus along the shape of it's leg. Finely chop parsley and garlic. Drizzle olive oil in a circular motion and adjust the taste with salt and pepper. Lastly, season with a shake of black pepper.
