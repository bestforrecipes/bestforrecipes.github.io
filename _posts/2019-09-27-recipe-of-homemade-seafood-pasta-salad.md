##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood Pasta Salad:

1.  Make ready 1 Bag Pre-Cooked Small Shrimp
2.  Make ready 1 (1 lb) package of Imitation Crabmeat
3.  Get 1 Box Rotini
4.  Get 1 sliced and diced cucumber
5.  Prepare 1 Sliced and diced tomato
6.  Take 1 (24 oz) bottle Kraft of Italian Dressing
7.  Make ready 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
8.  Make ready 1 tablespoon sugar

##### Instructions to make Seafood Pasta Salad:

1.  Cook Rotini according to package
2.  Slice and diced tomato and cucumber
3.  Drain pasta, rinse with cold water in the drainer, add to big pasta bowl
4.  Add cut up crab meat, shrimp, cucumber, tomato to pasta.
5.  Pour 24 oz of Italian dressing on top. Mix together
6.  Add seasoning and sugar to taste
7.  Let it sit in the refrigerator and chill. Tastes better if it sits overnight but can be eaten after chilling for an hour or so.
