##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon In Honey Peach Sauce:

1.  Get SALMON OPTION
2.  Prepare 1 salmon fish head or salmon fillet
3.  Make ready HONEY PEACH SAUCE
4.  Make ready 3 tbsp honey
5.  Take 1/2 tbsp mustard powder
6.  Prepare 1 tbsp orange juice
7.  Take 1 tbsp melted butter
8.  Take 1 dash white pepper
9.  Make ready 1 can peach slice
10.  Get glazing
11.  Take 2 tbsp brown sugar
12.  Prepare 1 tbsp orange zest
13.  Get 1 tbsp ginger powder

##### Steps to make Salmon In Honey Peach Sauce:

1.  For the honey peach sauce …mix all ingredient and blend till smooth set aside
2.  FOR SALMON broil salmon skin up for 20 minute then turn over
3.  broil for 10 minute then baste with peach sauce and add peach at the side on broil for 5 minute
4.  top glazing on salmon and broil for another 5 minutes then serve
