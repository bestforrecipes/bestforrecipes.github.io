##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Tomato Melt:

1.  Get 1 can tuna
2.  Get 1 slice provolone cheese
3.  Get 1 Italian dressing of your choosing
4.  Prepare 1 broccoli (optional)
5.  Make ready 1 pinch of basil (optional)
6.  Make ready 1 medium sized tomatoe

##### Instructions to make Tuna Tomato Melt:

1.  Preheat oven to 350°
2.  Mix tuna, zesty italian and basil
3.  Cut out the center of the tomato
4.  Put the tuna mix in the tomato hole
5.  Place a provolone cheese slice or two on the topb
6.  Bake 20 minutes
