##### The ingredients needed to make Salade Niçoise:

1.  Prepare Salad:
2.  Get 2 tins tuna, in oil or spring water
3.  Take 12 small new potatoes, cooked with skin on and quartered lengthways
4.  Prepare 4 vine tomatoes, chopped
5.  Prepare 120 g French beans, topped and cooked al dente
6.  Prepare 1 Romaine lettuce
7.  Prepare 2 red peppers, roasted and seeded
8.  Get 1/2 cucumber, sliced into half circles
9.  Prepare 1/2 red onion finely sliced into half rings
10.  Get 2 free range/organic eggs, boiled and quartered lengthways
11.  Take 8 anchovy fillets, cut in half lengthways
12.  Make ready 20 black olives, pitted
13.  Prepare French dressing/vinaigrette:
14.  Take 120 ml olive oil
15.  Prepare 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
16.  Get 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
17.  Take 1 shallot, finely minced
18.  Take 1 small garlic clove, finely minced
19.  Prepare Sea salt

##### Instructions to make Salade Niçoise:

1.  Dressing: whisk together all the ingredients and season to taste
2.  Salad: Using your hands, tear up the washed and dried romaine lettuce leaves and lay over 4 dishes/dinner plates
3.  Arrange the tuna, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, green beans, potatoes, boiled egg quarters, olives and anchovy strips, equally over the 4 plates
4.  Drizzle over the French dressing and finish with freshly ground black pepper, to taste
