##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Green Aglio Olio:

1.  Take 100 gr Pasta
2.  Get 1 Canned sarden/mackerel/tuna
3.  Get champignon mushroom
4.  Make ready 6 pcs onions
5.  Prepare 5 sheets lime leaves
6.  Prepare Virgin Coconut Oil
7.  Prepare Salt

##### Steps to make Simple Green Aglio Olio:

1.  Put water onto pan with salt and virgin coconut oil. Boiled it. Put in your pasta into it. Cook pasta until al dente
2.  Meanwhile, slice onions, mushrooms and lime leaves. Separate lime leaves from its bone first.
3.  Fry onions into frying pan until fragrant. Put in canned sarden and salt. Stir it and add mushrooms. Stir them well.
4.  If you think you already had your proper taste, mix it with al dente spagheti.
5.  Put your pasta in your favorite plate and sprinkle lime leaves on the top of your aglio olio.
6.  And voila! Your aglio olio ready to serve :) Happy lunch..
