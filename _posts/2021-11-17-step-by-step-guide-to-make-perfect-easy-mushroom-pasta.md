##### The ingredients needed to make Easy mushroom pasta:

1.  Make ready 10 oz Mushroom pasta sauce
2.  Take 1 packages shell noodles
3.  Take 1 bunch Uncooked spinich (washed)
4.  Take 1 tbsp sea salt
5.  Take 1 tbsp black pepper
6.  Take 1 tbsp granulated sugar
7.  Make ready 1 tsp chopped garlic
