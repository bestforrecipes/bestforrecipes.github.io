##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Take 1 lb Elbow Macaroni
2.  Prepare 1 cup Chopped Celery
3.  Make ready 1 cup Finely Chopped Red Onion
4.  Make ready 1/2 cup Sweet Pickle Relish (you can add more to your liking)
5.  Make ready 1/2 cup Hellmann's Mayonnaise (you can add more if needed)
6.  Get 1/2 cup Miracle Whip (you can add more if needed)
7.  Make ready 1 pinch Sea Salt (you can add more to suit your taste)
8.  Prepare 1 pinch White Pepper (you can add more to your taste)
9.  Make ready 1 pinch Old Bay Seasoning (you can add more to your taste)
10.  Prepare 2 cans Chunk Light Tuna in Water (drained)

##### Instructions to make Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Cook Macaroni according to directions - do not over cook - drain & rinse
2.  When Macaroni has cooled, add all ingredients and gently mix together
3.  Tweek seasonings if needed
4.  Refrigerate for four or more hours
5.  Serve chilled
