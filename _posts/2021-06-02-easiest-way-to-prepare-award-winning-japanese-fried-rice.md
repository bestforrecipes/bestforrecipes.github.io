##### The ingredients needed to make Japanese Fried Rice:

1.  Take 1/2 spring onion
2.  Take 3 dried Shiitake mushroom
3.  Prepare 1 pack Kanikamaboko (crab sticks)
4.  Make ready 3 eggs
5.  Make ready 2 bowls cooked rice
6.  Get 1 tablespoon Dashi powder (Japanese fish bouillon)
7.  Get 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
8.  Make ready Black pepper

##### Instructions to make Japanese Fried Rice:

1.  Soak dried Shiitake and cut Shiitake small.
2.  Cut spring onion and Kanikamaboko
3.  Beat eggs with a little salt and fry eggs. Put fried eggs aside in dish.
4.  Heat salad oil in pan and fry spring onion.
5.  Fry Shiitake mushroom and Kanikamaboko and put cooked rice.
6.  Season Dashi powder black pepper and soy sauce then add fried egg.
