3.  Chop all vegetables and seafood.
4.  Add cheeses and creams. Mix well.

9.  Top with all reserves. Bake at 400° for 30 minutes uncovered.
10.  Lightly top at service with warmed Hatch Green Chili Enchilada Sauce.
