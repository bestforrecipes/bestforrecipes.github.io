##### The ingredients needed to make Tiffie's Spicy Gambas:

1.  Get 1/4 cup Olive oil
2.  Get 1 Garlic (1whole peeled and minced)
3.  Make ready 3 Jalapeño (green long chilies-sliced diagonally)
4.  Prepare 2 tsp Pimenton (Spanish paprika-sweet)
5.  Prepare 1 dash Salt (to taste)
6.  Take 1/2 kg Shrimps (drained and shelled, tails preferably intact)
7.  Make ready 1/4 tsp Brown sugar

##### Instructions to make Tiffie's Spicy Gambas:

1.  Heat oil in a non-stick pan and sauté garlic. Add in Jalapeño after 2-3 minutes.
2.  Add in salt and pimenton, simmer for 2 minutes before adding in the shrimps.
3.  Wait for the shrimps to cook/to turn orange. Remove shrimps from the sauce and set aside. Simmer and reduce sauce mixture for about 5 minutes.
4.  Once sauce has reduced, add in sugar and mix well. Simmer for another minute before putting the cooked shrimps back in. Cook for about 2-3 minutes under medium to high heat.
5.  Serve hot. This is best paired with toasted ciabatta, toasted pita bread or plain rice, whichever you prefer.. :)
