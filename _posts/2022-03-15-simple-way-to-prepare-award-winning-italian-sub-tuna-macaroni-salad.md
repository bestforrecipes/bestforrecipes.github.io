##### The ingredients needed to make Italian Sub Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Take 1 pack/box Pasta
2.  Take 1 can tuna
3.  Make ready 1 can peas
4.  Prepare 1 yellow onion
5.  Prepare To taste Green olives
6.  Make ready To taste Salami or similar sandwich meat
7.  Take To taste Cheddar cheese cubes
8.  Get 1 1/2 cups Miracle whip or Mayonnaise
9.  Take 1 tablespoon Pickle relish
10.  Prepare 2 teaspoons Mustard
11.  Make ready 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan cheese
12.  Make ready 1 teaspoon Garlic salt
13.  Make ready 1 teaspoon Pepper

##### Steps to make Italian Sub Tuna Macaroni Salad:

1.  Boil pasta to soften noodles. Drain and set aside to cool. Meanwhile, Chop or Dice onion, olives, salami, & cheese.
2.  Drain tuna & peas and Combine with chopped ingredients in a medium mixing bowl.
3.  Add in all remaining Seasoning & Condiment ingredients and mix well to coat evenly.
4.  Enjoy as a side, a salad, or a snack!
