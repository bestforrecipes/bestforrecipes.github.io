##### The ingredients needed to make Steam Crab 蒸螃蟹:

1.  Get 3 pcs Crabs
2.  Prepare Gingers
3.  Take Pinch Salt
4.  Make ready Spring Onion
5.  Prepare 10 ml Cooking Wine

##### Instructions to make Steam Crab 蒸螃蟹:

1.  Steam Crabs with pinch of salt & gingers about 20 minutes
2.  Remove from steamer add in cooking wine & spring onion, ready to be served.
