##### The ingredients needed to make Crab Pepper Fry:

1.  Prepare 1/2 kg Crab's
2.  Prepare 1 Onion
3.  Prepare 1 Tomato
4.  Take 1 green chili
5.  Take leaves Few coriander
6.  Take 2 tsp ginger garlic paste
7.  Take 1 tsp Fennel seeds
8.  Prepare 1 tsp Turmeric powder
9.  Prepare 1/2 cup water
10.  Take 2 tsp cumin powder
11.  Take 3-4 tbsp sesame oil
12.  Make ready 1 tbsp black pepper powder
13.  Prepare to taste Salt

##### Instructions to make Crab Pepper Fry:

1.  Grind onion and tomato to a fine paste and keep it aside.
2.  Heat oil in a pan, add Fennel seeds and let it splutter. Then, add ginger garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
3.  Add the pureed onion and tomates and sauté for another minute.
4.  Add crab, turmeric, pepper, cumin and mix.
5.  Add water, give it a mix and cover with a lid.
6.  Stir frequently to avoid masala sticking to the pan. - Once Crabs are cooked add chopped green chili, coriander leaves, mix and remove from stove.
7.  Serve hot or warm with Roti or rice.
