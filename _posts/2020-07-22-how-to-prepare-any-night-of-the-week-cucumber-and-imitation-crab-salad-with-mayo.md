##### The ingredients needed to make Cucumber and Imitation Crab Salad with Mayo:

1.  Prepare 1 Cucumber
2.  Prepare 100 grams Imitation crab sticks
3.  Take 2 tbsp ◎Mayonnaise
4.  Prepare 1 ◎Black pepper
5.  Get 1/2 tsp Salt
6.  Take 1 Soy sauce

##### Instructions to make Cucumber and Imitation Crab Salad with Mayo:

1.  Julienne the cucumber, rub with salt and drain. Shred the crab sticks.
2.  Combine the ◎ ingredients with the cucumber and crab and it's done. Serve with a little soy sauce, to taste.
