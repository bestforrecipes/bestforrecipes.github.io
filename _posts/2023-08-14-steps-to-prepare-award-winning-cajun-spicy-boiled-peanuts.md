##### Steps to make Cajun spicy boiled peanuts:

3.  Soak peanuts in lukewarm water for 30-45 minutes.
4.  Place peanuts in large stock pot, fill with water.
5.  Add all spices and water to cover. Peanuts will float to tip, stir.
6.  Bring to boil, stir frequently. Continue for 45-60 minutes.
7.  Transfer to crockpot. Simmer on low for 7+ hours.
