##### The ingredients needed to make Roast Salmon with Salsa:

1.  Prepare medium plum tomatoes, chopped
2.  Get small onion, chopped
3.  Make ready garlic, peeled
4.  Take jalapeno, seeded and chopped
5.  Prepare cider vinegar
6.  Prepare chili powder
7.  Prepare ground cumin
8.  Get salt
9.  Take hot sauce
10.  Prepare salmon, skinned and cut into 6 portions

##### Instructions to make Roast Salmon with Salsa:

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F.
2.  Place all ingredients except salmon into food processor, chop until finely diced and uniform.
3.  Place salmon in large roasting pan and spoon salsa on top.
4.  Roast salmon until flaky on outside but still pink inside, about 15 min.
