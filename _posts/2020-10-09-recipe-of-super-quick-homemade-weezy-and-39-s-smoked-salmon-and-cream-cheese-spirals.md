##### The ingredients needed to make Weezy's Smoked Salmon and cream cheese spirals:

1.  Make ready smoked salmon slices (two strips)
2.  Prepare cream cheese
3.  Prepare chopped corriander
4.  Take spinach leaves, chopped, remove stems
5.  Make ready black pepper
6.  Get lemon juice

##### Steps to make Weezy's Smoked Salmon and cream cheese spirals:

1.  Lay both strips of salmon on plate. Spread cream cheese over each strip evenly.
2.  Sprinkle corriander and spinach evenly over cheese
3.  Add lemon juice and dash of pepper. Carefully role the salmon to make a pinwheel shape and enjoy
