##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Calamari Rings:

1.  Take 1 Calamari
2.  Take 50 grams White flour
3.  Prepare 1 Egg
4.  Make ready 60 ml Water
5.  Take 100 grams Panko
6.  Get 1 Oil for deep frying
