##### The ingredients needed to make Spicy Asian Slaw With Shrimp:

1.  Take 1 packages Coleslaw mix
2.  Get 1 Red bell pepper, sliced thin
3.  Take 8 oz Sugar snap peas, sliced
4.  Make ready 1/2 Red onion, diced
5.  Make ready 1 lb Medium size shrimp, cooked/shelled
6.  Get 1 as needed Peanuts or chow mein noodles, optional topping
7.  Get dressing
8.  Make ready 1/2 cup Soy sauce
9.  Prepare 1/4 cup Or less, sesame oil
10.  Prepare 1 tsp Sugar
11.  Prepare 1 tsp Ginger paste or 1 teaspoon ginger powder
12.  Take 1 tsp Red pepper flakes

##### Instructions to make Spicy Asian Slaw With Shrimp:

1.  Mix the dressing ingredients an set aside
2.  In a large bowl, toss all remaining ingredients, except peanuts
3.  Mix dressing with salad, tossing well to coat all
4.  Allow to marinate in the fridge for a while before serving
5.  Top with peanuts or chow mein noodles if desired
