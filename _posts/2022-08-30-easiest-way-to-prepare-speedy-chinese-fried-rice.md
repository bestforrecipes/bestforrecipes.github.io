##### The ingredients needed to make Chinese Fried Rice:

1.  Get 2 plates of cooked rice
2.  Take 125 grams shrimps (cleaned)
3.  Take 125 grams chicken meats (chopped into small portions)
4.  Prepare 3 tbsp butter
5.  Get 1 cup peas
6.  Make ready 4 eggs (whisked)
7.  Prepare 5 small red chillies (blended)
8.  Take 5 shallots (blended)

##### Instructions to make Chinese Fried Rice:

1.  Using a wok, in a med-heat, melts down the butter.
2.  Next, put in the blended chillies and shallots paste into the butter. Cook until it lets out a beautiful scent, or in other words, until it turns slighly golden or darker than it original colour. Make sure to not overcook it as it is easily burnt.
3.  Put in the shrimps and chickens, and keep on cooking until they are fully cooked.
4.  Then, put in the peas and cook for 10 seconds, and later on put in the rice. Mix them well.
5.  Pour in the whisked eggs into the rice, and cook for another 20 seconds, or until the eggs is half/fully cooked, depends on what you like. But i prefer it to be half cook, so that the rice is a little wet and moist.
6.  Serve hot.
