##### The ingredients needed to make crab Rangoon dip:

1.  Get dips
2.  Prepare 2 cup crab meat
3.  Get 16 oz cream cheese soft
4.  Get 1/2 cup sour cream
5.  Make ready 4 green onions chopped
6.  Get 1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
7.  Get 2 tbsp powered sugar
