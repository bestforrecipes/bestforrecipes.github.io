##### The ingredients needed to make KaShawna's Crab Cakes With Shrimp:

1.  Get Lump Crab meat
2.  Make ready Crushed Cracker (I use Ritz)
3.  Get Green Onions Cut (White part too)
4.  Make ready Egg
5.  Take Shrimp Cut into small pieces
6.  Prepare Worcestershire
7.  Prepare Mayo
8.  Prepare Celery (Chopped Finely)
9.  Prepare Dry Mustard
10.  Take Lemon Juice
11.  Get Garlic Powder
12.  Make ready Salt
13.  Prepare Pepper
14.  Make ready cayenne pepper
15.  Take Flour (For Dusting)
16.  Take Extra virgin olive oil
17.  Prepare Kashawna's Home Made Tarter (see recipe)

##### Instructions to make KaShawna's Crab Cakes With Shrimp:

1.  In a large bowl, mix together all ingredients, except for the flour and peanut oil.
2.  Shape into patties and dust with flour.
3.  Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
4.  When oil is hot, carefully place crab cakes, in batches, in pan and fry until browned, about 4 to 5 minutes. Carefully flip crab cakes and fry on other side until golden brown, about 4 minutes.
5.  Serve Warm with Kashawna's Tarter
