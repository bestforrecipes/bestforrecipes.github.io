##### The ingredients needed to make Fried Calamari Rings:

1.  Take 1 Calamari
2.  Take 50 grams White flour
3.  Take 1 Egg
4.  Prepare 60 ml Water
5.  Make ready 100 grams Panko
6.  Make ready 1 Oil for deep frying
