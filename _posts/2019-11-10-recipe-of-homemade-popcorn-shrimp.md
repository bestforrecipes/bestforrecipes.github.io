##### The ingredients needed to make Popcorn Shrimp:

1.  Get flour
2.  Make ready egg
3.  Prepare milk
4.  Prepare salt
5.  Make ready dried basil
6.  Prepare dried parsley
7.  Prepare garlic powder
8.  Prepare onion powder
9.  Prepare bread crumbs or crushed flavored croutons
10.  Get uncooked medium shrimp, peeled & deveined
11.  Prepare vegetable oil for frying

##### Steps to make Popcorn Shrimp:

1.  Heat oil in deep fryer or medium saucepan to 350°F
2.  Place flour in one bowl
3.  Whisk egg and milk in another bowl
4.  Mix remaining dry ingredients in a 3rd bowl
5.  Coat shrimp in flour shaking off any excess, then dip in milk/egg mixture, then coat with the breadcrumbs
6.  Cook in oil about 3-4 minutes or until a nice golden brown
7.  Dry on a paper towel. I used ranch croutons for the breadcrumbs.
