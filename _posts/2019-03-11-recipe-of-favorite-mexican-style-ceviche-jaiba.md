##### The ingredients needed to make Mexican Style Ceviche (Jaiba):

1.  Get Imitation Crab Meat 🦀
2.  Get Lemons/limes 🍋
3.  Get Cucumbers🥒
4.  Take Tomatoes🍅
5.  Get Cilantro
6.  Prepare 1 Onion
7.  Get Jalapenos🌶️
8.  Take 2 avocados 🥑
9.  Make ready Sprinkle garlic salt w/ Parsley (optional)
10.  Get Tostadas or Tortilla chips

##### Instructions to make Mexican Style Ceviche (Jaiba):

1.  Cut up and shred the imitation crab meat.
2.  Slice, Dice, and Chop all your ingredients. Discarding the unedible parts.
3.  Mix ALL ingredients together in a bowl.
4.  Juice your lemons/ limes and pour over your mixture. The citrus will marinate and cook the meat on its own.
5.  Cover. With topper or plastic wrap.
6.  Chill it. In refrigerator for 2 hours or more. Or Over night.
7.  Serve with your tostadas, and enjoy.
