##### The ingredients needed to make Seafood Tom Yum Soup:

1.  Make ready Tom yum paste (supermarket)
2.  Prepare Lemongrass (slice in to pieces)
3.  Make ready Red Onion (slice into pieces)
4.  Make ready 8 prawns (shell on)
5.  Prepare 1 squid (sliced)
6.  Make ready Cabbage (sliced)
7.  Make ready 500 ml water
8.  Prepare Red chilli (in pieces)
9.  Take Lime juice

Tom yum or tom yam is a type of hot and sour Thai soup, usually cooked with shrimp (prawn). Tom yum has its origin in Thailand. The words "tom yam" are derived from two Thai words. Tom refers to the boiling process, while yam means 'mixed'.

##### Steps to make Seafood Tom Yum Soup:

1.  Stir fry tom yum paste and add in the water to put to boil
2.  Add in lemongrass, red chilli & onion
3.  Add in cabbage, prawns and squid (wash with salt and rise off) (boli for 3 min) add in lime juice (boil another 8min) ready to serve
