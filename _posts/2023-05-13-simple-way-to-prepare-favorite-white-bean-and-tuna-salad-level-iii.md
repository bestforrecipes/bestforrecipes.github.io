2.  Take 1 cup white beans, cooked
3.  Make ready 1 cup green pepper
4.  Make ready 1 cup yellow pepper
5.  Make ready 4 tbsp green onion
6.  Make ready 3 tbsp parsley
7.  Get 1 dash white pepper
8.  Prepare 3 tbsp lemon juice
9.  Take 3 tbsp lemon zest
10.  Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil

##### Steps to make White Bean and Tuna Salad-Level III:
