##### The ingredients needed to make Gluten Free Prawn Pad Thai:

1.  Get 20 tiger prawns
2.  Take 1/2 courgette
3.  Take 1 red pepper
4.  Prepare 1 red onion
5.  Get 1 tsp garlic
6.  Take 1 tsp fresh chopped chilli
7.  Take 1 tsp fresh ginger
8.  Take 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
9.  Take to taste Fish sauce
10.  Prepare 1 lemon juice
11.  Prepare 1 tsp honey
12.  Get Salt and pepper
13.  Take 2 bunches soaked glass noodles
14.  Make ready Chopped peanuts and cashews

##### Steps to make Gluten Free Prawn Pad Thai:

1.  Stir fry your veg until soft, add in your prawns and 3/4 cook.
2.  Add your sauce (chilli, garlic, ginger, soy, fish sauce, lemon and honey) and reduce, cooking the prawns through.
3.  Add your nuts and mix well. Season to taste and add your glass noodles.
4.  Cool for two minutes and serve. Garnish with coriander
