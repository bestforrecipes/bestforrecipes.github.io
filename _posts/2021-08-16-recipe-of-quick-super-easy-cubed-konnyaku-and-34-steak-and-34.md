##### The ingredients needed to make Super Easy! Cubed Konnyaku "Steak":

1.  Make ready Konnyaku
2.  Prepare Sesame oil
3.  Prepare Bonito dashi stock
4.  Take Soy sauce
5.  Take Ground sesame seeds

##### Steps to make Super Easy! Cubed Konnyaku "Steak":

1.  Score both sides of the konnyaku in a lattice pattern, before slicing it into cubes.
2.  Heat the sesame oil in a pan and add the konnyaku. Once browned, add the bonito dashi stock and cook well.
3.  Add the soy sauce and cook another a few minutes before adding the sesame seeds.
