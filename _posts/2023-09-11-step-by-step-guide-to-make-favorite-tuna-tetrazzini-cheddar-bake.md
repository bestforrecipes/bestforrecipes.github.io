##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Tetrazzini Cheddar Bake:

1.  Get 2 can Tuna
2.  Get 2 1/3 cup hot water
3.  Prepare 1 box dry spaghetti noodles
4.  Prepare 1 1/3 cup milk
5.  Get 1/4 cup flour
6.  Take 3 tbsp olive oil
7.  Make ready 1 dash parsley
8.  Take 1/2 onion
9.  Make ready 1 dash red pepper flake
10.  Prepare 4 large mushrooms
11.  Get 1 cup Cheddar cheese
12.  Prepare 1 cup panko bread crumbs
13.  Make ready 1 clove garlic

##### Steps to make Tuna Tetrazzini Cheddar Bake:

1.  Cook noodles to al dente
2.  Preheat oven to 400°F
3.  On stove top, combine mushrooms, tuna, onions, garilc and seasonings and saute to a light brown
4.  Make quick roux with flour and oil and stir in milk
5.  Add noodles, tuna saute and gravy together in 9 X 13 casserole dish
6.  Top with Cheddar cheese and Panko, then dash with parsley, sea salt and pepper
7.  Bake for 30-45 mins. and enjoy!
