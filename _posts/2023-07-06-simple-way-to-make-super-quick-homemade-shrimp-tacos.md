##### The ingredients needed to make Shrimp tacos:

1.  Make ready 1 packages of soft tortilla shells
2.  Get 1/4 bunch cilantro
3.  Get 1 can chipotle peppers in adoba sauce
4.  Prepare 1 cup sour cream
5.  Prepare 1/4 head lettuce
6.  Prepare 1 bag of cooked deveined shrimp
7.  Make ready 1 pinch flour
8.  Get 1 diced tomato

##### Steps to make Shrimp tacos:

1.  In a mixing bowl add shrimp , flour and chipotle peppers and sauce and mix. Remove and discard peppers. Let sit 30 min
2.  Chop cilantro and shred lettuce
3.  Warm shrimp along with sauce in pan on top stove for 5-8 min
4.  Warm tortilla shells per package
5.  Add shrimp and a little sauce to shells and top with cilantro, lettuce , tomato and sour cream
