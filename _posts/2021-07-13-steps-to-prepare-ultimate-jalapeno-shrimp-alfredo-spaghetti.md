##### Steps to make Jalapeno Shrimp Alfredo Spaghetti:

1.  Medium pot bring water to boil with a tsp of olive oil and place in spaghetti for 6 minutes. Remove from heat.
2.  In a large sauce pan on medium high melt butter and tsp of olive oil. Once butter melts add wine, jalapeño and simmer for 2 minutes, then add shrimp and seasoning. Stir and simmer for 3 t 4 minutes and add Alfredo sauce. Simmer till ready to eat! Enjoy
