##### The ingredients needed to make Bacon Wrapped Buffalo Cheese Stuffed Shrimp:

1.  Make ready jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
2.  Make ready thin sliced bacon, cut in half
3.  Make ready to 8 ounces buffalo cheddar cheese, or any spicy cheese
4.  Make ready Lemon pepper seasoning
5.  Make ready granulated sugar
6.  Prepare GARNISH
7.  Prepare sour cream mixed with chopped green onions or chives

##### Steps to make Bacon Wrapped Buffalo Cheese Stuffed Shrimp:

1.  preheat oven to 375, line a baking sheet with foil.
2.  Cut shrimp down the center to open a pocket, making sure not to cut all the way through
3.  cut a piece of the cheese to fit in pocket., sprinkle with lemon pepper
4.  Wrap a piece of bacon tightly around shrimp to encode cheese in. Sprimkle with the sugar. Secure with a toothpick
5.  Cook 10 minutes, then turn shrimp over and cook about 10 minutes more until bacon is crisp and shrimp is cooked. Serve with the sour cream for dipping
