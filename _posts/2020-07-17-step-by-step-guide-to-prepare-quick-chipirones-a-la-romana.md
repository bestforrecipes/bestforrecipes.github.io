##### The ingredients needed to make Chipirones a la Romana:

1.  Make ready x Small Squid
2.  Prepare plain flour
3.  Make ready smoked paprika
4.  Take salt
5.  Take oil for frying

##### Instructions to make Chipirones a la Romana:

1.  Clean and prepare the squids. You can ask your fishmonger to do this for you or alternatively you can buy them already prepared.
2.  Keep the tentacles to one side and slice the heads into small ringlets. You will need to remove the beak from the tentacles as this is hard and not really edible.
3.  In a ziplock bag, put in the flour, salt and smoked paprika and shake well
4.  Add the squid to the bag and shake until it's all coated with the flour mix
5.  Fry in batches until golden brown
6.  Crack open a cold beer and enjoy with a squeeze of lemon
