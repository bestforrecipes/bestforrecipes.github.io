##### The ingredients needed to make Japanese Inari Sushi:

1.  Make ready 5 pieces Aburaage (thin dried Tofu)
2.  Take 2 tablespoon soy sauce
3.  Make ready 2 tablespoon sugar
4.  Prepare 2 tablespoon Mirin(sweet seasoning Sake)
5.  Get 150 ml water
6.  Get Cooked rice or Sushi rice
7.  Make ready Nori (dried laver) for garnish
