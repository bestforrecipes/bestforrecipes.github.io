##### The ingredients needed to make Crab and Spinach Salad:

1.  Prepare 1 lb crab
2.  Take 1 packages Spinach
3.  Take 5 or 6 dollops of tzatziki sauce
4.  Prepare 4 oz yogurt (vanilla adds flavor)
5.  Get 5 slice thin of red onion chopped
6.  Get 1/4 cup chives
7.  Make ready 1 lb cooked shrimp (optional)
8.  Get 1/2 tsp lemon juice
9.  Prepare 3 dollops of light mayo
10.  Prepare 2/3 can garbanzo beans

##### Steps to make Crab and Spinach Salad:

1.  Create the dressing: mix tzatziki sauce, yogurt, mayo and lemon juice in a small bowl. You can add or change the dressing according to your taste preferences.
2.  Mix all other ingredients in a bowl. This is the salad.
3.  Add dressing and toss salad.
