##### The ingredients needed to make Foil Wrapped Salmon with Rice & Asparagus:

1.  Get rice pilof mix(any brand)
2.  Prepare salmon steaks
3.  Get Fresh or frozen asparagus tips
4.  Take Lemon pepper
5.  Take Cooking spray oil

##### Steps to make Foil Wrapped Salmon with Rice & Asparagus:

1.  Prepare rice according to package instructions.
2.  Spray 4 sheets of aluminum foil with cooking spray
3.  Put 1/2c prepared rice into center of foil & pat down into a rectangle
4.  Put a salmon steak on top of rice
5.  Sprinke lemon pepper on top of salmon steak for flavor
6.  Top salmon steak with 4-5 asparagus spears
7.  Fold foil length wise to seal. Then fold or twist ends to seal packet
8.  Put packets seam side up in large skillet filled 1/2 inch deep with boiling water
9.  Cover & steam approximately 10 minutes. Checking water level every few minutes & adding water as needed
10.  Flip packets over & steam another 10 minutes
11.  Remove from skillet. Carefully open packets (hot).
12.  Serve with Homemade Tartar Sauce. Enjoy!
