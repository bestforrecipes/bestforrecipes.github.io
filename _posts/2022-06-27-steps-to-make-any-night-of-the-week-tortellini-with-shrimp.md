##### Instructions to make Tortellini with Shrimp 🍤:

1.  In a large pot sauté in olive oil the chopped onion and green pepper, add the minced garlic when veggies are tender…….
2.  Next add your black pepper and oregano, sauté for several minutes……
3.  Next add in your frozen bag of shrimp, sauté until shrimp turn slightly pink…..I rinsed mine in cold water before adding to pot….
4.  Next add your 2 jars of pasta sauce…..Stir until heated thru…
5.  While sauce is cooking get your tortellini ready by boiling some water, don’t forget to add salt to the water 💧!!!
6.  Drain tortellini and then add to pot with shrimp, stir well……Remove from heat……
7.  Serve and enjoy 😉! Top each serving with Parmesan cheese !! 😋
