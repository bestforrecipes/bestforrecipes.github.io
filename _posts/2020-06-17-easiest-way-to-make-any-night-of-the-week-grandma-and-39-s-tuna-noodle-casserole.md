##### The ingredients needed to make Grandma's Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Prepare 12 oz. extra wide egg noodles
2.  Take 2 cans (4 oz) ea.) tuna; drained
3.  Get 1 can cream of chicken soup
4.  Prepare 1 can cream of mushroom soup
5.  Make ready 1 cup sour cream
6.  Get seasonings of choice:
7.  Take parsley, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder
8.  Get Topping:
9.  Take 8 oz. pkg. shredded Italian cheese blend
10.  Get 1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs
11.  Get 1/2 stick butter, cut into pats

##### Instructions to make Grandma's Tuna Noodle Casserole:

1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
2.  In a large bowl combine soups, sour cream, tuna and seasonings.
3.  Stir in the cooked and drained egg noodles.
4.  Spread mixture evenly into a greased 9x13 baking dish.
5.  Top with cheese, bread crumbs and butter.
6.  Bake for 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
7.  Enjoy!
