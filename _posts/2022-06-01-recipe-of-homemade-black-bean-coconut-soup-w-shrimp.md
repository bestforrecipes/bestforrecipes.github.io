##### The ingredients needed to make Black Bean Coconut Soup w/Shrimp:

1.  Take Stew
2.  Take 2 clove garlic
3.  Take 2 cup water
4.  Take 1/4 cup coconut milk
5.  Get 1 packages sazon vegetable season
6.  Prepare 1 cup chopped broccoli
7.  Make ready 1/4 cup Jamaican curry powder
8.  Get 15 medium thawed shrimp
9.  Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
10.  Take 3/4 can black beans
11.  Prepare 1 1/4 small carrot

##### Instructions to make Black Bean Coconut Soup w/Shrimp:

1.  Heat oil in a pot and saute garlic on high heat.
2.  Add beans, sliced carrots, and curry powder and stir lightly 2 min.
3.  Pour in coconut milk and water then stir lightly
4.  Sprinkle in Sazon, add broccoli, and shrimp. Then lower fire to simmer.
5.  Simmer for about 30 to min.
6.  Option #2: Remove shrimp, puree cooked soup & veggies
7.  Strain soup, then add shrimp back to soup
