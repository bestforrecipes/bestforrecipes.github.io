##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Hello Kitty Character Bento:

1.  Take 1 use as much (to taste) Hot cooked white rice
2.  Make ready 1 Nori seaweed
3.  Make ready 1 kernel Corn
4.  Get 1 stick Imitation crab stick

##### Instructions to make Easy Hello Kitty Character Bento:

1.  Shape the rice into a Hello Kitty shape.
2.  Make the eyes and whiskers by cutting out the nori seaweed. Peel off the red part of the imitation crab stick and make a bow out of it.
3.  Place on the facial features, then you're all done.
