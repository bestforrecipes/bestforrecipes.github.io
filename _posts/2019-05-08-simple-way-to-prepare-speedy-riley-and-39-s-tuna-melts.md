##### The ingredients needed to make Riley's Tuna Melts:

1.  Prepare 1 can White albacore tuna
2.  Take 1/4 cup Mayo
3.  Make ready 1 dash Chili powder
4.  Take 1 dash Natures seasons
5.  Make ready 1 cup Cheddar cheese
6.  Take 2 slice Sourdough bread

##### Instructions to make Riley's Tuna Melts:

1.  Mix tuna mayo and chili powder together.
2.  Place bread on a flat cookie sheet .
3.  Add tuna mixture to bread and top with cheese.
4.  Finally Sprinkle with natures and put under broiler for five to ten mins or until bubbly.
