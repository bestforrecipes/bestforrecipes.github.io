##### The ingredients needed to make Chipotle Salmon:

1.  Make ready salmon
2.  Take adobe chipotles
3.  Take maple syrup
4.  Prepare orange juice
5.  Prepare garlic cloves
6.  Take avocado
7.  Make ready lettuce
8.  Prepare olive oil

##### Instructions to make Chipotle Salmon:

1.  Blend the chipotles, garlic cloves and maple syrup
2.  Add the orange juice to obtain a dense blend
3.  Marinate the pice of salmon with the blend for about 10 minutes
4.  Add the olive oil tablespoon to an already hot pan
5.  Cook each side of the salmon for 5 minutes
6.  Serve in a bed of lettuce and avocado
