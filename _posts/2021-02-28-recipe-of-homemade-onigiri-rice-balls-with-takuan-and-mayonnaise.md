##### The ingredients needed to make Onigiri Rice Balls with Takuan and Mayonnaise:

1.  Prepare 1 Hot cooked rice
2.  Prepare 1 slice Takuan
3.  Get 1 tsp Mayonnaise
4.  Prepare 1 handful Bonito flakes
5.  Make ready 1 Nori seaweed

##### Steps to make Onigiri Rice Balls with Takuan and Mayonnaise:

1.  Chop the takuan roughly.
2.  Combine rice, takuan, mayonnaise, and bonito flakes. Mix thoroughly, and form into rice balls to finish. Wrap up with nori if you'd like.
