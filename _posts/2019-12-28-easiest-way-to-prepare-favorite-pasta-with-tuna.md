##### The ingredients needed to make Pasta with Tuna:

1.  Take 1-2 Tbsp olive oil
2.  Get 1 small chopped onion
3.  Make ready 1/2-3/4 cup chopped baby carrots
4.  Make ready 1/2-3/4 cup chopped celery
5.  Get 1-2 jalapeno peppers seeded and sliced (optional)
6.  Make ready 1-6 ounce can tuna
7.  Get 1-28 ounce can diced tomatoes
8.  Prepare 1/2 can tomato paste
9.  Make ready 1 Tbsp sugar
10.  Get 1 tsp salt
11.  Get 1/2 tsp oregano
12.  Prepare 1 tsp basil
13.  Take Pasta of choice
14.  Make ready Parmesan cheese (optional)
15.  Prepare Crushed red peppers (optional)

##### Steps to make Pasta with Tuna:

1.  Saute olive oil, onion, carrots, celery and jalapeno peppers slowly in a large covered saute pan.
2.  When vegetables are crisp tender, add tuna to pan and saute a bit longer.
3.  Add diced tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, salt, oregano and basil to pan and simmer (covered) for 20 to 30 minutes.
4.  Serve over top of your favourite pasta (I normally use spagetti or penne) and sprinkle with parmesan cheese and/or crushed red peppers if desired.
5.  Enjoy!
