##### The ingredients needed to make Potato Salad:

1.  Take 4 lbs potatoes
2.  Make ready 12 eggs
3.  Take 1 pt mayonnaise
4.  Get 2 tsp salt
5.  Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper
6.  Prepare 1/2 tsp Cajun seasoning
7.  Take 1/8 tsp smoked paprika
8.  Make ready 2 tsp yellow mustard
9.  Take 1/4 c Crab Boil
10.  Take 1 tsp baking soda

##### Instructions to make Potato Salad:

1.  Place eggs in a large pot, add enough cool water to cover them, add the baking soda.
2.  Bring water to a rolling boil over high heat. Once boiling, turn off heat and cover with a lid. Let sit for 10-12 minutes then transfer eggs to an ice bath. Peel once completely cooled.
3.  Peel potatoes. Add crab boil to a large pot of boiling water and add potatoes. Boil until fork tender, usually 15-20 minutes, and drain.
4.  Mash eggs and potatoes. Add remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.
5.  Top with a few dashes of paprika. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes to overnight.
