##### The ingredients needed to make KaShawna's Crab Cakes With Shrimp:

1.  Get 1 lb Lump Crab meat
2.  Make ready 1/3 cup Crushed Cracker (I use Ritz)
3.  Make ready 3 Green Onions Cut (White part too)
4.  Make ready 1 Egg
5.  Get 1/2 Shrimp Cut into small pieces
6.  Make ready 1 tsp Worcestershire
7.  Get 1/4 cup Mayo
8.  Take 1/2 cup Celery (Chopped Finely)
9.  Prepare 1 tsp Dry Mustard
10.  Prepare 1/2 cup Lemon Juice
11.  Prepare 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
12.  Take 1 tsp Salt
13.  Get 1 tsp Pepper
14.  Get 1 dash cayenne pepper
15.  Get 1 Flour (For Dusting)
16.  Make ready 1/2 cup Extra virgin olive oil
17.  Make ready 1 Kashawna's Home Made Tarter (see recipe)

##### Steps to make KaShawna's Crab Cakes With Shrimp:

1.  In a large bowl, mix together all ingredients, except for the flour and peanut oil.
2.  Shape into patties and dust with flour.
3.  Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
4.  When oil is hot, carefully place crab cakes, in batches, in pan and fry until browned, about 4 to 5 minutes. Carefully flip crab cakes and fry on other side until golden brown, about 4 minutes.
5.  Serve Warm with Kashawna's Tarter
