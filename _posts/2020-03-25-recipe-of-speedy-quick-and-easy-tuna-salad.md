##### The ingredients needed to make Quick and Easy Tuna Salad:

1.  Take 4 x 5oz cans tuna (in water), well drained
2.  Take 1 cup mayonnaise
3.  Prepare 1/3 cup finely chopped celery (about 1 rib)
4.  Prepare 2 tablespoons finely chopped red onion (about 2 slices)
5.  Make ready 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish or dill relish
6.  Prepare 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
7.  Get 1 garlic clove, minced
8.  Make ready Salt and freshly ground black pepper
9.  Take Pinch dill

##### Steps to make Quick and Easy Tuna Salad:

1.  Add the first 7 ingredients to a bowl and mix well.
2.  Season to taste with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with a pinch of dill
3.  Can be eaten straight away or for even better results, cover with and chill in the fridge for a couple of hours. Enjoy!
4.  Serving suggestion - with cottage cheese, hard boiled egg, avocado slices and cherry tomatoes.
