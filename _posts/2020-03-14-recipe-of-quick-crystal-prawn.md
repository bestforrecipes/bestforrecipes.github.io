##### The ingredients needed to make Crystal Prawn:

1.  Make ready 500 g prawn
2.  Take 1 tsp Salt (dissolve with 1/2 cup of water)
3.  Get 3 cloves(or more) Garlic (minced)
4.  Get 2 stalks spring onion (chopped)
5.  Take Chilli Padi (chopped)
6.  Prepare leaf Coriander
7.  Get 3 tbsp cooking oil

##### Instructions to make Crystal Prawn:

1.  Peel and devein the prawn, tail on.
2.  Steam or boil the prawn until it turns pink. Place in the plate and mix well with spring onion, chilli padi, garlic and salt water.
3.  Heat the oil. Pour the hot oil onto the prawn.
4.  Use coriander leaf as garnish. Ready to serve.
