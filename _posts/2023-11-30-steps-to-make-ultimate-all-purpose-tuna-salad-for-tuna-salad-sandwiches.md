##### The ingredients needed to make All-Purpose Tuna Salad ☆ For Tuna Salad Sandwiches ♪:

1.  Get 2 can Canned tuna (light) (58 kcal per can)
2.  Prepare 4 tbsp Red onion (minced)
3.  Get 1 tsp Dill (or parsely)
4.  Get 3 tbsp Mayonnaise
5.  Make ready 1 Salt and pepper
6.  Prepare 1 tsp Lemon juice
7.  Make ready 4 Sliced sandwich bread
8.  Make ready 1 Margarine

##### Instructions to make All-Purpose Tuna Salad ☆ For Tuna Salad Sandwiches ♪:

1.  Mince the red onion and dill. Thoroughly drain the oil from the canned tuna, then lightly flake the meat.
2.  Combine the ingredients from Step 1 with all of the seasonings, and stir together until creamy.
3.  Toast the sliced bread until crisp and brown, spread one slice with margarine, sandwich a generous amount of tuna salad using the other slice, then serve.
