##### The ingredients needed to make Pasta Salad:

1.  Get 1 large Pack of spiral or spaghetti pasta
2.  Prepare 1/2 tomato or whole depending on your liking
3.  Make ready 1 Cucumber
4.  Get 1 envelope powder ranch
5.  Make ready 2 bags of salad shrimp
6.  Make ready 1 pack of slice turkey or ham
7.  Prepare 1 1/2 bottle of Zesty Italian or Red wine vinaigrette dressing or as much as you prefer

##### Instructions to make Pasta Salad:

1.  Boil pasta until done
2.  Let pasta cool off
3.  Add pasta to large pan
4.  Cut tomato and cucumber
5.  Wash shrimp
6.  Add and stir tomato, cucumber, shrimp, turkey or ham, powder ranch and dressing together with pasta
7.  Cover pasta salad and refrigerate for at least an hour so ingredients can set in
