##### The ingredients needed to make Salmon Salad:

1.  Make ready Red pepper, diced
2.  Make ready Cucumber, diced
3.  Get Red onion, diced
4.  Make ready Shredded carrots
5.  Take Hard boiled egg, Peeled and crushed
6.  Take Salmon filet (cooked)
7.  Prepare Mayonnaise (or miracle whip)
8.  Take Lemon juice
9.  Take Salt and pepper
10.  Prepare Cayenne pepper seasoning

##### Instructions to make Salmon Salad:

1.  Cook salmon as directed on package. Flake pieces and let it cool.
2.  In a large bowl, combine red peppers, cucumbers, onions, carrots, mayonnaise, salt and pepper. Add a dash of cayenne pepper if desired.
3.  Add cooked salmon and crushed boiled egg to bowl and mix gently. Add lemon juice to taste.
4.  Can be served as is, on a sandwich or over some leafy greens :)
5.  This is just a simple salad recipe, add, leave out and modify ingredients to your liking!!! Enjoy….
