##### The ingredients needed to make Shiokara Fermented Squid Ochazuke:

1.  Get 1 rice bowl's worth Plain cooked rice
2.  Prepare 20 grams Squid shio kara
3.  Get 80 ml Bonito flakes & kombu based dashi stock
4.  Prepare 1 dash Green onions or scallions
5.  Get 1 White sesame seeds
6.  Get 1 Shredded nori seaweed

##### Instructions to make Shiokara Fermented Squid Ochazuke:

1.  Sprinkle white sesame seeds on top of hot rice, then top with the squid shio kara.
2.  Pour the bonito kombu dashi on top of the shio kara. You can also use dashi stock.
3.  Garnish with chopped green onions and shredded nori and enjoy.
