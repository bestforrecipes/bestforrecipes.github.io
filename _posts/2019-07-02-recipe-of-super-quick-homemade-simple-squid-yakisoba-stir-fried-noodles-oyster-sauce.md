##### The ingredients needed to make Simple Squid Yakisoba Stir-Fried Noodles Oyster Sauce:

1.  Take 3 servings Chinese-style noodles
2.  Get 3 Squid
3.  Get 1 Green onion or scallion
4.  Prepare 10 grams Ginger
5.  Prepare 10 grams Garlic
6.  Take 3 tbsp Oyster sauce
7.  Prepare 1 tbsp Mirin

##### Instructions to make Simple Squid Yakisoba Stir-Fried Noodles Oyster Sauce:

1.  Cut the squid into thin rings and mince the garlic and ginger. Chop the scallion.
2.  Poke the bags of the noodles and make 2-3 holes and microwave for 3 minutes. Heat about 1 tablespoon of sesame oil in a pan and add the garlic, ginger and scallions. Then add the squid and cook.
3.  Add the warm noodles, salt, pepper and 2 tablespoons of sake and combine with the squid. Season with oyster sauce and mirin. Done.
4.  Sprinkle on some black peppers if you like.
