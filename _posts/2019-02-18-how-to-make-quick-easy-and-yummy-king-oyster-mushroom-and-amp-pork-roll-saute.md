##### Steps to make Easy and Yummy! King Oyster Mushroom & Pork Roll Sauté:

1.  Tear each king oyster mushrooms vertically into 4 pieces and then wrap from the ends with a pork slice. Even if the meat tears during the wrapping process, squeeze the wrap at the end to secure.
2.  Sprinkle salt and pepper evenly. Turn them over so that both sides are seasoned. Pour olive oil in a hot pan.
3.  Saute over medium heat while turning, so both sides brown. It's better not to cover with a lid so that the water from the king oyster mushrooms will not come out.
4.  Absorb excess oil with paper towels, and they're done. They're best when fresh and hot.
5.  It's also good even when you use 2 tablespoons of yakiniku sauce instead of salt and pepper listed above. Wipe off the excess oil and add the sauce. Stir fry over high heat and mix well.
