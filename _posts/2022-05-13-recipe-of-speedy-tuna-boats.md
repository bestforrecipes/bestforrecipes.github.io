##### The ingredients needed to make Tuna Boats:

1.  Take 1 can tuna (6.5 oz in water)
2.  Get 1 cup cheese (cubed) - I use sharp cheddar but any cheese will do
3.  Take 1/3 cup mayonnaise
4.  Prepare 1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
5.  Prepare 2 tablespoons - green onion (chopped)
6.  Make ready 1/2 teaspoon - prepared mustard
7.  Prepare 4 hot dog buns
8.  Make ready 1 Sliced green olives - optional

##### Steps to make Tuna Boats:

1.  NOTE: if using all 8 hot dog buns… double the recipe.
2.  Preheat oven to 350° Fahrenheit.
3.  Mix first six ingredients together (mix well)
4.  Fill buns with tuna mixture.
5.  Garnish with olives (optional)
6.  Arrange in cooking dish or baking pan. Cook uncovered until cheese melts. (Approx. 30 min).
