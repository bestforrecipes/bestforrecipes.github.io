##### The ingredients needed to make Easy Jerk Chicken, Shrimp and Pasta:

1.  Prepare Fettucini
2.  Make ready Precooked grilled chicken breast slices
3.  Get Steamed Shrimp w/ Old Bay (Grochery store made to save time)
4.  Get Bell Peppers (thin sliced) (your choice of color)
5.  Get Heavy whipping cream
6.  Prepare Butter
7.  Make ready Sour cream
8.  Prepare Soft Cream Cheese
9.  Prepare Parmesan Cheese
10.  Get Shredded Colby & Monterey Jack cheese
11.  Prepare Jerk Seasoning (Add as much or as little as you desire)
12.  Prepare Italian Seasoning (Add as much or as little as you desire)
13.  Make ready Rosemary
14.  Prepare Turmeric (optional)
15.  Make ready Ground Ginger (optional)
16.  Get Flax Seed (optional)

##### Steps to make Easy Jerk Chicken, Shrimp and Pasta:

1.  Cook noodles until they are half cooked. Then drain water.
2.  Mix in all ingredients with the noodles (minus the shrimp). Cook on medium until chicken is tender and bell peppers are soft.
3.  Add shrimp as a topping to your plate.
4.  ENJOY!!! Oh and post your pics!
