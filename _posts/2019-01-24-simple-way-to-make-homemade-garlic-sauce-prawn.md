##### The ingredients needed to make Garlic Sauce Prawn 蒜辣虾:

1.  Get 2 River Prawn
2.  Take 40 g Garlic (Chopped)
3.  Take 5 tbsp Thai Sauce
4.  Make ready 3 tbsp Tomato Sauce (Maggi)
5.  Prepare 3 tbsp Chili Sauce (Maggi)
6.  Prepare Pinch Salt
7.  Make ready 1 tsp Sugar

##### Instructions to make Garlic Sauce Prawn 蒜辣虾:

1.  Cleaning the prawn and cut into half.
2.  Add in 2tbsp vegetables oil into hot wok to fried garlic till brownish…
3.  Add in 30ml of water and follow by all the sauces… when the sauce is boiled, add in salt & sugar to taste.
4.  Lastly, add in the prawn to cook till the color trun into red… then ready to be served.
