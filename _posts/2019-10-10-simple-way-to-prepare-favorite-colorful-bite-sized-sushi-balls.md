##### The ingredients needed to make Colorful Bite-Sized Sushi Balls:

1.  Take Topping:
2.  Prepare 20 cuts total Salmon, tuna, squid, sea bream, shrimp, etc
3.  Make ready 1 Shiso, salmon roe (for white sashimi such as squid)
4.  Get 1 Wasabi
5.  Prepare Sushi Rice:
6.  Make ready 700 grams Freshly cooked rice
7.  Get 3 tbsp Vinegar
8.  Take 2 tbsp Sugar
9.  Prepare 1 tsp Salt

##### Steps to make Colorful Bite-Sized Sushi Balls:

1.  Mix together the vinegar, salt, and sugar and add to the freshly-made rice. Mix together using cutting motions while flapping a fan over the rice.
2.  Put the topping, wasabi, and rice on plastic wrap, and shape into a ball.
3.  If putting squid or other white sashimi onto the rice, first put shiso or wasasbi onto the plastic wrap before the other ingredients, then top with some salmon roe after shaping.
