##### The ingredients needed to make Pasta salad with peas and tomato:

1.  Get 1/3 cup white vinegar
2.  Take 2 tablespoons water
3.  Prepare 2 teaspoons salt
4.  Take 1/2 teaspoon sugar
5.  Make ready 2 teaspoons minced garlic, pasted with some of salt
6.  Make ready 1/2 teaspoon taragon
7.  Take 1/2 cup olive oil
8.  Prepare 1 lb shell pasta
9.  Prepare 1/2 lb peas, abour 1 1/2 cup
10.  Get 2 pints yellow an red cherry tomatoes
11.  Get 1/2 cup shredded basil
