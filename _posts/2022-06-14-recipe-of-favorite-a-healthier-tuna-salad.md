##### The ingredients needed to make A Healthier Tuna Salad:

1.  Make ready tuna salad
2.  Prepare 1 can good quality chunk white tuna packed in water, 5 ounces
3.  Take 1 can great nothern beans, drained and rinsed 15 ounce
4.  Get 1 tbsp white balsamic vinegar
5.  Take 1/2 cup greek yogurt
6.  Get 1/4 cup capers
7.  Take 1/3 cup finely chopped celery
8.  Get 1/3 cup finely chopped red onion
9.  Take 1/2 tsp sea salt
10.  Make ready 1/4 tsp white ground pepper
11.  Make ready 1/2 tsp paprika
12.  Get 1/2 avocado, mashed
13.  Prepare 1/4 cup fresh chopped dill
