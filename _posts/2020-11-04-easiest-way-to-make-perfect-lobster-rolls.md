##### The ingredients needed to make Lobster Rolls:

1.  Make ready Mayonnaise
2.  Prepare Celery, chopped
3.  Prepare fresh Parsely, chopped
4.  Prepare Chives, chopped
5.  Take fresh Lemon juice
6.  Take 4 Lobster Tails
7.  Make ready Rolls, lightly toasted
8.  Prepare melted Butter, for brushing
9.  Prepare salt and pepper

##### Instructions to make Lobster Rolls:

1.  Preheat the oven to Broil HI.
2.  Clean and split Lobster Tails and brush with melted butter. Place in preheated oven for 10-11 minutes, or until cooked to your preferred doneness.
3.  In a bowl, combine the mayonnaise, lemon juice, celery, parsley, chives, and the salt and pepper to taste.
4.  After the lobster tails are cooked, remove from shell and chop into bite - size pieces. Add into the bowl of other ingredients.
5.  Place lobster salad into the refrigerator for 10-15 minutes to chill in order for the flavors to absorb into the lobster.
6.  Toast the rolls then spread melted butter into each side. Then fill with lobster salad. Enjoy!
