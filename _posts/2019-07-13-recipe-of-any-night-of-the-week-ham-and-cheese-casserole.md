##### The ingredients needed to make Ham And Cheese Casserole:

1.  Take 3 cup Ham, cubed
2.  Get 1 packages Frozen chopped broccoli
3.  Take 1 box Pasta, I prefer elbows, shells, or bow tie
4.  Take 1/4 cup Butter
5.  Make ready 3 tbsp Basil pesto (I use Classico brand)
6.  Take 1 can Campbell's cheddar cheese soup
7.  Prepare 1/2 cup Milk
8.  Make ready 1 cup Shredded Italian mix cheese ( use your favorite but this melts great)
9.  Take 1 Salt and pepper to taste

##### Instructions to make Ham And Cheese Casserole:

1.  In large pot boil water and cook pasta
2.  Meanwhile in large skillet over medium heat melt butter
3.  Add pesto, soup, and milk, mixing well until combined and smooth
4.  Add shredded cheese and mix until melted
5.  When the cheese is melted add the ham and cooked broccoli
6.  Drain and add pasta mix well
