##### The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian wonton soup:

1.  Make ready 1 package round wonton wraps
2.  Prepare 1 bag vegetarian morningstar ground beef
3.  Get 1 c. small diced jicama
4.  Make ready 1 c. finely chopped carrots (pulse in chopper)
5.  Take 1 c. small diced celery
6.  Make ready 1 bunch cilantro stem finely chopped
7.  Make ready 1 c. diced cabbage
8.  Make ready 1 tsp ground pepper
9.  Take 2 tbs vegetarian oyster sauce
10.  Get 1 tsp soy sauce
11.  Take 1 large carrot cut like flower
12.  Prepare 5 nappa cabbage leaves cut bite size pieces
13.  Prepare 6 mushrooms sliced
14.  Prepare 1-2 veg. soup bullion or 1 tsp mushroom msg

##### Steps to make Vegetarian wonton soup:

1.  Add ground beef, jicama, celery, cabbage, finely chopped carrots, ground pepper, oyster sauce in frying pan on medium heat. Mix well, add seasoning per your taste. Lastly add chopped cilantro stem. The finer the ingredients, the easier to wrap in wonton wrappers.
2.  Fill a small bowl or dipping cup with water. Put wonton wrapper in left hand, use right index finger to dip in water and wet around the edge. Scoop 1 tbs of ground beef mix into the center of wonton and fold in half sealing it tight. Set aside and repeat this step until all wonton used.
